Greetings Explorers

There have been some BIG changes in Gielinor. Please pay careful attention.

The grand exchange has received a massive upgrade. The Grand Exchangers have found some lost offers under the desk. Any offers you have made in the past that the Grand Exchangers lost will start showing up. When you login with empty offer slots, any previously lost offers will start showing up in these slots.

Some other highlights include an authentic Tutorial Island, a personalised and dynamically adjusted shop system, pyramid plunder minigame rewrite plus a new quest (Tribal Totem). Full changes as follows:
