Introduction

Equipment sets are only available to RuneScape Members. Please subscribe to get this feature.

Some sets of equipment, armour and weapons give the wearer special bonuses or effects when the complete set is worn. These can range from increased damage to affecting your opponents' stats.

These equipment sets are incredibly ancient as they have been lying in the ground for centuries. The armour and weapons will require a bit of occasional servicing to keep them in tiptop condition.

The costs for repairing the items scale according to how damaged they are; prices for repairing totally broken pieces are as follows:

  • Barrows weapons: 100k
  • Barrows bodies: 90k
  • Barrows legs: 80k
  • Barrows helmets: 60k
You can get your armour repaired by Bob in Lumbridge, Dunstan in Burthorpe, Tindel in Port Khazard or the Squire on the Void Knights outpost.

Note: These items are only tradeable when totally fixed or totally broken.
