Dungeoneering - Fishing Tables

Introduction | Fish


Introduction

To catch fish in Daemonheim, you must be raiding a dungeon on the following complexity level:

Complexity level 2+ - Fishing


Fish

Many pools within the dungeons are home to strange fish and crustaceans, all of which can be caught and cooked. You will need a fishing rod and feathers, both of which can be purchased from the smuggler or found in some of the chambers, if you're lucky.
