Introduction

Your sword is sharp, your armour gleaming and the monsters are just waiting for you to come and kill them. At this point you might want to pick up some food, in the event that one of the evil wee beasties manages to hit you through your flurry of blows and magnificent defence. The table below shows the foods that will affect your life points when consumed and how many life points they can heal, so you can choose which delicacies will be best for your needs.
