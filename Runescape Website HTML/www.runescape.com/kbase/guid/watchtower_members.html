Watchtower (Members)

Introduction | Information | Spoilers | Developers
Introduction
The wizards of the Yanille Watchtower have been reporting increased ogre sightings recently.

Use a wide variety of your skills to help the wizards recover their stolen power crystals and stop the advance of the ogre hordes!
Quest Information
Members only: Yes
Start point: Watchtower close to the city gates of Yanille
To Start: Speak to watchtower wizard
Quest Length: Long
Minimum Requirements:
Level 25 Agility
Level 14 Herblore
Level 14 Magic
Level 40 Mining
Level 15 Thieving
Useful:
Must defeat level 68 monsters
