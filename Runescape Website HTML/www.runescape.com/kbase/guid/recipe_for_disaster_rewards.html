Recipe for Disaster Rewards

Introduction | Areas | Food | Items
Experience / Subquest Rewards | Other New Features


Introduction

Recipe for Disaster is only available to RuneScape Members. Please subscribe to get this feature.

Please note: This article shows the items, areas and all rewards for completing the Recipe for Disaster quest. If you do not wish to know or spoil the fun of earning the rewards, please do not look at this guide.

For completing the Recipe for Disaster quest, you will unlock a vast amount of new items, experience, areas and weapons, all of which are covered in this guide.

The more subquests of the Recipe for Disaster quest you complete, the more you will be rewarded in regards to the Culinaromancer's chest. This chest contains gloves, food and weaponry, which will contain more and more items, the further you go into the subquests.

Certain subquests unlock new areas and items too!

Areas

The subquests of the Recipe for Disaster quest will take you all over the RuneScape world, and new areas will be discovered.

For saving Pirate Pete, you will discover a new underwater area, accessible only if you have certain swimming items, and you are not too heavy to swim. Speak to Murphy in Port Khazard after you have completed this part of the quest for more information.

For saving Sir Amik Varze you will unlock a new training area, the Evil Chicken lair, which includes black dragons and a baby black dragon!

As uncovered when saving Awowogei there is a new Agility area, found on Ape Atoll, which requires an Agility level of 48 or higher.

Other new areas you unlock include the Goblin Kitchen, and Evil Dave's basement, which contains hellrats.

Other New Features

If you have completed the ogre subquest, you will have access to a shortcut from the Feldip hills to Karamja via a boat powered by Rantz's children. To take this journey, you have to pay the ogre fare. This may be 'glug glug herbsies' (herbs such as rogue's purse and others you can find on Karamja), or chompy birds, so be prepared to collect these items if you wish to take the trip.
If you have completed the Evil Dave subquest, you will have access to hellcats, which can be trained via killing seven hellrats in Evil Dave's basement!
