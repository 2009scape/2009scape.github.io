Vestments

Truly devout worshippers of Guthix, Saradomin and Zamorak now have the ability to dress themselves in the robes of the highest priests of their order. Vestments come in all three varieties, and are a must for the devoted.

Spirit Shields

The Spirit Beast's Realm is a strange place, filled with a type of magical energy not found anywhere upon the surface of RuneScape, and this can be imbued into items that spend a great deal of time there. When you have completed Summer's End, you will be rewarded with a spirit shield. This can be used on its own, blessed, or combined with a variety of sigils to grant it even more powers. In addition to improved stats, the divine and elysian spirit shields come with special bonuses:
  • Divine spirit shield
    30% - or as much as if possible if 30% is more than your current amount of Prayer points - of any damage you take is removed. Half of this 30% is deducted from your Prayer instead, while the other half is ignored completely.
  • Elysian spirit shield
    70% chance of reducing the damage you receive by 25%.
