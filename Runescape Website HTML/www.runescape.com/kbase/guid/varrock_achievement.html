Varrock armour When worn:
Chance of smelting two bars at once when smelting ores up to (and including) steel in Edgeville
Chance of Mining two ores at once when Mining ores up to (and including) coal
Greater chance of receiving skull sceptre parts in the Stronghold of Security (while you wear the armour). The skull sceptre offers 10 charges rather than the usual 5.
Talk to Rat Burgiss to swap Varrock teleports between the market and south of the Grand Exchange
Opportunity to buy up to 16 battlestaves from Zaff while wearing the armour
Experience lamp - 1,000 experience in the skill of your choice
