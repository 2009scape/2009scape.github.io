Construction - Chapel

Introduction | Key Features | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

Show your allegiance to your god when you reach level 45 Construction and gain the ability to create your very own chapel to help with your Prayer skill.

Key Features

A typical chapel
The main feature of the chapel is the altar and incense burners. You can recharge your Prayer in the chapel as normal.

There is an important extra feature to the altar: depending on your altar and the number of incense burners you have lit, you will be awarded more Prayer experience for the bones you offer.

The Hotspots

Chapel hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are eight types of hotspot in the chapel that you can construct on:

  • Altar
  • Lamp
  • Icon
  • Musical
  • Statue
  • Window
  • Rug
  • Room
