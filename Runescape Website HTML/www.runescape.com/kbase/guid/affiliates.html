About Affiliates

There are a number of sites with which Jagex Ltd has an affiliate agreement. This agreement allows them to serve RuneScape and FunOrb games to their users. There is nothing insecure about playing RuneScape or FunOrb games on these sites, they are just other places to play. Of course, if you like to be absolutely secure, you should always come to www.RuneScape.com or www.FunOrb.com.

This is the official list of sites which are authorised to offer RuneScape and FunOrb games. This list only appears on www.RuneScape.com, do not trust copies of this list you see elsewhere. If a site does not appear on this list, it is not an official affiliate and you SHOULD NOT enter your password on that site.
