Dungeoneering - Slayer Tables

Introduction | Slayer Creatures | Slayer Rewards

Training Slayer in Daemonheim is only accessible to RuneScape Members. Please subscribe to get this feature.


Introduction


Slayer creatures can be found on all complexity levels.

Click here to read more about Slayer in RuneScape.


Slayer Creatures


Occasionally in Daemonheim, you will come across creatures that have a Slayer requirement to defeat. No items will be necessary to defeat them, only the correct Slayer level. You will only gain Slayer experience from these creatures if they are your current slayer task.
