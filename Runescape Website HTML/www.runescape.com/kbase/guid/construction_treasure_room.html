Construction - Treasure Room

Introduction | Key Features | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

When you reach level 75 Construction you will find yourself able to build the treasure room, which holds a reward for players who complete your dungeon, but it is not as simple as it may seem to get into the chest...

Key Features

A typical treasure room
The key feature and central point to this room is the rewards chest. This contains the reward for players who make it to this room during challenge mode.

You will need to construct a 'boss' character in the monster space who will defend this room and chest aggressively. After this boss is conquered, the first person to open the chest will receive the prize.

The Hotspots

Treasure room hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are six types of hotspot in the treasure room that you can construct on:

  • Monster
  • Treasure
  • Decoration (shields)
  • Decoration
  • Lighting
  • Door
