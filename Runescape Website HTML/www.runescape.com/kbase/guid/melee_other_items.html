Dragonfire shield* (m)
(Uncharged)		 75 + 0 + 0 + 0 - 10 - 5 + 20 + 25 + 22 + 10 + 22 + 17 + 7 + 0

Dragonfire shield~ (m)
(Fully charged)		 75 + 0 + 0 + 0 - 10 - 5 + 70 + 75 + 72 + 10 + 72 + 67 + 7 + 0

* The dragonfire shield is a remarkable piece of equipment capable of unleashing the awesome power of a dragon's fiery breath. See Smithing - Extra Features for details about how to make a dragonfire shield.

To charge your dragonfire shield you will need to fight dragons. As the shield still holds all the abilities of an anti-dragonbreath shield, you will be protected from most of the damage of the dragon's flame. Each time the shield protects you, it will charge up, storing the power of the breath to be unleashed later.

A dragonfire shield can hold fifty charges of dragon's breath. To see how many charges your shield has, right-click on it and select 'Inspect'.

To discharge the flame, right-click on the equipped shield while you are in combat and select 'Operate'. The shield will then fire a deadly blast of fiery energy at your opponent, potentially inflicting massive damage. The shield will need to cool down for a while before you can unleash its flames again.

~ The shield can hold up to fifty charges, and each charge stored within it will add +1 to its stab, slash, crush and ranged defence. So, for example, with 30 charges stored it will have + 50 stab defence, + 55 slash defence, + 52 crush defence, + 10 magic defence and + 52 ranged defence.

The shield can be charged by the breath of any dragon (including the King Black Dragon's ice and poison breaths) or skeletal wyvern.
