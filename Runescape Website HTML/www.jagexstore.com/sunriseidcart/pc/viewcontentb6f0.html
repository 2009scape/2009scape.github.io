  • To estimate our audience size; measure aggregate customer traffic patterns.
  • To authorize payment, whether through credit card processing gateways or through other payment methods.

USE OF COOKIES

Cookies are only used to remember web session state, or user preferences, such as user preference for currency of US dollars or Pounds. Personal information is not stored in cookies and cookie data is not shared with any other company except insofar as necessary for the technical operations of the Jagex store site.

CREDIT CARD SAFETY

If any unauthorized use of your credit card occurs from a Sunrise Identity purchase, notify your credit card company in accordance with its reporting rules and procedures. If your credit card company finds credit card fraud, but does not waive your entire liability for unauthorized charges, Sunrise Identity will reimburse you for the remaining liability, up to a maximum of fifty U.S. dollars ($50.00). This guarantee only applies to credit card purchases made using Sunrise Identity's online ordering process.

Steps Sunrise Identity Takes to Ensure the Privacy of our Customers

  • We do not share (other than with Jagex Limited) or sell information that identifies our guests personally or makes it possible for other parties to contact them directly. However we may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) comply with the law or comply with legal process served on Jagex Limited or the site; (b) protect and defend our rights or property, the site, guests or the public.
  • Sunrise Identity backs every credit card purchase by secure service software, which encrypts information using SSL technology, ensuring that Internet transactions stay private and protected.
    Sunrise Identity protects your account information through the use of a password. To protect the confidentiality of your information (such as your credit card number) you MUST keep your password confidential and not disclose it to any other person.
