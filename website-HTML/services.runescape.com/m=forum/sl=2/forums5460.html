Penguins Hide And Seek, 2 Juni til 9 Juni
---------------------------------------------

1. Al-Kharid – Bush - 1 pt
Anywhere around Duel Arena, Mage Training Arena, or Al Kharid

2. Sawmill – Crate – 1 pt
Enter the sawmill north-east of Varrock by squeezing through the western fence.

3. Ice Mountain – Rock – 1 pt

4. Port Phasmatys – Barrel – 2 pts
Requires either 25 ecto-token or completion of the Ghosts Ahoy quest to enter Port Phasmatys. Fastest way to get here is ectophial teleport or Fairy Rings code ALQ.

5. Castle Wars – Rock – 2 pts
Anywhere from Oo'glog to Yanille. Often in Yanille south wall.

6. Witchaven – Rock – 1 pt
Witchaven is located just east of Ardougne.

7. Karamja – Bush - 1 pt
Anywhere on the island of Karamja, and often around Brimhaven and Musa Point.

8. Sophanem – Cactus – 2 pts

9. Lletya – Toadstool – 2 pts
Requires the Regicide quest. It roams up to the tree gates. Teleport here using your Elf crystal, charter a boat, or walk using one of the passes.

10. Wilderness – Rock – 2 pts

11. Polar Bear – Musa well - 1 pt
Requires Hunt for Red Raktuber, The quest. The polar bear is always in a well.

8. Canifis/Haunted Woods - Kasse - 2 pts

9. Mort Myre – Sten - 2 pts
