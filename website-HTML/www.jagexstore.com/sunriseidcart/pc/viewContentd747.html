How do I place an order?
To make a selection, choose the quantity, size, color (where applicable) and click on the 'Add to Cart' button. The items selected will then be added to your virtual shopping cart. To view the contents of your shopping cart, review totals, and/or remove items click on the 'View Cart'.

When you're done shopping, click on the 'Checkout' link below your shopping cart located on the bottom left part of your screen. You will then be directed to a secured server where you will be required to confirm your billing, shipping and payment information.

How do I pay for my order?
Once you are ready to complete your purchase, payment can be made by credit card (Visa, MasterCard & American Express).

Can I ship to my P.O. Box?
For tracking purposes, our fulfillment company only ships via UPS . UPS cannot ship to a P.O. Box.

     International orders?

     Can I order products from the other site to ship to me?
     Not at this time.  Currently stores can ship only to the countries they are designated to service.  The complexities
     of international shipping and taxes makes this an unpleasant process for everyone and we want you to pay the
     lowest fees possible.
 
     What if I want to order something for another person that lives in Europe but I live in Toledo, OH?
     Good idea and they'll love you.  Shop at the store that services the addressee (recipient), in this case the
     Europe store.

Why don't the stores offer exactly the same product?
In order to make sure that the shipping and production costs stay as low as possible, the Jagex store now operates from two regions.  As a result, some items may be a little different, depending on the store you visit.  As time goes on, we will work to make sure that more and more products will be identical on both store sites.
