Full Patches

North of East Ardougne. North of Catherby.
South-east of Falador. North-west of Port Phasmatys.


Bush Patches

South of East Ardougne. South-west of Etceteria Castle.
North of Chemist's house in Rimmington. South-west of Varrock, next to the Champions' Guild.


Hop Patches

South-west coast of Entrana. Across the river, along the route from Lumbridge to Varrock.
North of McGrubor's Wood, north-west of Seers' Village. North of Yanille.


Tree Patches

In Falador Park. North-west behind Lumbridge Castle.
South-east in Taverley. South-east of Varrock Palace, inside the grounds.
West of the Agility Training Area in the Tree Gnome Stronghold.


Fruit Tree Patches

North coast of Brimhaven. South-east of Catherby.
South-east of the Grand Tree in the Tree Gnome Stronghold. North-west of the Tree Gnome Village.
South-east corner of Lletya.


Spirit Tree Patches

South of Etceteria Castle. North-east of Port Sarim.
North-east of Brimhaven.
