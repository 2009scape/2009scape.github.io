Crafting - Pottery

Introduction | Objects Available


Introduction

To craft articles of pottery, follow the method below:

  • Obtain clay ore (either through Mining or by purchasing it from another player).
  • Get a jug or bucket and fill it with water (look out for the following symbol on your minimap).
  • Take the clay ore and use it with the jug/bucket of water to get soft clay.
  • Go to a potter's wheel (Look out for this symbol on your map) and use the soft clay with it.
  • Choose what you wish to make.
  • Take the unfired item to a pottery oven (found near the potter's wheel) and use them together in order to make the finished product.
