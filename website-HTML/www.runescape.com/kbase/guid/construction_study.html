Construction - Study

Introduction | Key Features | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

Every house should have a nice room for quiet study, but with the opportunities to bind magic into tablets and to change the element of your staves, this room could get loud!

Key Features

A typical study
One of the key items in the study is the lectern. Each lectern is capable of creating different magic spells. If you have soft clay, and the runes required for the spell in your inventory, then you will 'craft' the spell. For example, if you had soft clay, a law rune and some air runes in your inventory, then with the correct lectern you could craft a 'Camelot Teleport' tablet.

This becomes a stackable tablet in your inventory that you 'break' (which casts the spell). In this way, you could craft and sell spell tablets that you can cast to those who cannot, or save space in your inventory by carrying the magical tablets.

Notes: You get the Magic experience for making the tablet, not breaking it. Additionally, if you have completed the Love Story quest, you can modify Teleport to House tablets using a chisel, which will allow you to change the teleport location to any POH portal. These modified tablets cannot be traded.

Another key item in the study is the crystal ball. Here you can use an elemental staff on the crystal ball, which will give the option to change the element of the staff. Normal elemental staves can be changed easily. For higher level staves, you will need a higher level crystal ball.

To change elemental battlestaves, you will need 100 of the rune you wish to change to. To change an elemental mystic staff, you will need 1000 of the rune you wish to change to.

The Hotspots

Study hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are seven types of hotspot in the study that you can construct on:

  • Globe
  • Lectern
  • Crystal ball
  • Telescope
  • Wall chart
  • Bookcase
  • Room
