Cooking - Non-Members' Food






If you are not a member, you still have many food options available to you. To get those most desirable rewards, it may help you to know what food you should use during your battles, quests and journeys to help keep you alive. For level requirements to catch the fish in the table below please see the non-members' Fishing table.

The table below shows the free player items that can be cooked using both the Cooking ranges and open fires.
