Dungeoneering - Farming Tables

Introduction | Farming Foods | Farming Textiles | Farming Herbs


Farming in Daemonheim is only accessible to RuneScape Members. Please subscribe to get this feature.


Introduction

To plant seeds, you must be raiding a dungeon on the following complexity level:

Complexity level 5+ - Farming seeds

To harvest textiles, you must be raiding a dungeon on the following complexity level:

Complexity level 4+ - Farming textiles

Click here to read more about Farming in RuneScape.

Farming Foods

Potato seeds and mushroom spores can be gathered from monster drops and then planted at the farming patches you find in the dungeons of Daemonheim.

Note that you need no tools to farm foods in Daemonheim, nor do you need to water your plants or use any potions to protect them from disease. In addition to this, you will find that the magical nature of the dungeon causes your plants to grow very quickly indeed - over the course of minutes.

Farming Textiles

To harvest textiles, you must be raiding a dungeon on the following complexity level:

Complexity level 4+ - Farming textiles

Textiles cannot be grown, but farming patches full of them can be found from complexity 4 onwards. Simply harvest any that you see, gain Farming XP and use the resulting textile at a spinning wheel to make material that can be sewn into garments.
