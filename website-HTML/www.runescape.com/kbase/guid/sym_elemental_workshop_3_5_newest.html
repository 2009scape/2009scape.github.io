The Elemental Workshop III (Members)

Introduction | Information | Spoilers | Developers
Introduction
A number of restored texts have been made available in the Seers' Village chapel, and one stands apart from the others. In a book embossed with a curious attern, the story of Vitruvius is continued, as he attempts to bring his assistant to life using the mysterious properties of the 'body'.

There are lessons to be learned from this new book, as you put Vitruvius's theories into practice again in the Elemental Workshop. Only those with the ability to unravel the most devious puzzles may, perhaps, be able to unlock the workshop's latest secret...
Quest Information
Members only: Yes
Start point: Seers' Village chapel
To Start: Search the bookcases and read a ragged book
Quest Length: Medium
Minimum Requirements:
The Elemental Workshop I
The Elemental Workshop II
Level 20 Mining
Level 33 Defence
Level 33 Smithing
