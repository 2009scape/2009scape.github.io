Agility - Penguin Course

Introduction | Location | The Course | Rewards


Introduction

Inside the Penguin Agility Course
The Penguin Agility Course can only be accessed by starting Cold War. You will also need to be disguised appropriately to gain access to the area.

The Iceberg, far to north of the main land masses of the RuneScape world, is home to a large force of decidedly militant penguins. Penguins, you might think, are ungainly and clumsy creatures, not naturally given to great feats of acrobatics and flexibility. On that point, you would be wrong.

The penguin leaders have been busy training a crack force of ultra-penguins, bent on defeating their foes, and now they are the pinnacle of penguin poise and persistance.

Location

Location of the Agility Course
The Penguin Agility Course can be found north of the Penguin Outpost on the Iceberg. In this cold and unfriendly place the penguins train their armies.

You can reach the course by heading to the Iceberg with Larry the Zookeeper, you will then need to disguise yourself as a penguin in order to gain access to the outpost and, thus, the Agility course.

If you have lost your disguise, you will be able to craft a new one in the workshop of your house.
