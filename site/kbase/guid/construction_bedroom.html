Construction - Bedroom

Introduction | Key Features | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

The bedroom is another useful room that you can use your Construction level to make. With features like free makeovers, changing clothes, and access to servants, the bedroom may end up being vital in helping your Construction level.

Key Features

A typical bedroom
Dressers are found in the bedroom, and with a dresser you will find you can easily (and freely) change your hair's appearance. With higher level dressers, more of the styles and colours are available.

A wardrobe is found on the other side of the bed. With features such as changing your shoes, legs, arms, torso colours and appearance for free (depending on your constructed item), the wardrobe space provides another great addition to the bedroom and free character personalisation.

It is also worth noting that the fireplace does, in fact, work. If you acquire some logs and tinderbox, use the logs with the fire and you will light the fireplace. You will be given a small amount of Firemaking experience for lighting it.

Finally, the most important feature of having a bedroom is the ability it grants: if you build two bedrooms, one for yourself and one for a potential servant, you can head to the Servants' Guild and hire a servant to help you around the house.

The Hotspots

Bedroom hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are eight types of hotspot in the bedroom that you can construct on:

  • Bed
  • Wardrobe
  • Dresser
  • Fireplace
  • Clock
  • Rug
  • Curtain
  • Room
