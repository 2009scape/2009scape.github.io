Slayer - Lumbridge Swamp Dungeon

Introduction | Location

Introduction

Just south of the quiet and peaceful town of Lumbridge, beneath the swamps, is a complex network of tunnels. A few of the less dangerous Slayer monsters can be found here, making it an ideal location for those new to the Slayer path.

Location

Caves' location
South-west of Lumbridge Castle in the Lumbridge Swamp. You will need a lantern to see in the dungeon, but swamp gas makes using torches and candles dangerous.
