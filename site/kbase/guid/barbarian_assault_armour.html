Set Bonuses

By wearing a full set of penance gear, you will benefit from the same strange magicks that the penance deal in. By wearing ALL of the following at once, you will find that your prayer points recover over time, at a rate that is faster than how your other stats replenish:
  • Any penance hat (fighter, healer, runner or ranger)
  • Fighter torso
  • Runner boots
  • Penance gloves
  • Penance skirt

Penance tridents are two-handed. When wielded, they will attempt to 'save' the runes used in any spell. They will do this in the following way:
  • The trident finds out how many different rune types you are using for your spell
  • For each rune type, the trident makes a 1 in 60 roll
  • If successful, the trident will save all runes of that type


For example, an Earth Blast requires 1 death rune, 3 air runes and 4 earth runes. There is a 1 in 60 chance that, once cast, your penance trident will do any one, two or three of these things: save 1 death rune, 3 air runes or 4 earth runes. Your penance trident cannot save only 'some' of the runes of any rune type: for example, it will not save 1 or 2 air runes from the above example, as it will save none or all of any rune type.

To upgrade your penance trident to a master penance trident, visit Commander Connad in Barbarian Assault.
