TzHaar Rewards Page

Introduction | Items

Introduction

The TzHaar City is only available to RuneScape Members. Please subscribe to get this feature.

Deep beneath Karamja Volcano, members will find the underground city inhabited by the men of stone and magma, the TzHaar.

Here, you can participate in activities with a particular focus on combat. These give good rewards if you can prove to the TzHaar that you are a true warrior and survivor. To find out more about the activities available here, look at the TzHaar Fight Pits page. Alternatively, if you want to prove your might, perhaps TzHaar Fight Caves is more your thing...

'TokKul' is the currency used by the TzHaar, and you can obtain it in a number of ways.

Firstly, you can participate in the activities. For winning or making good progress, you will win a variable amount of TokKul.

Alternatively, you can try fighting the many different types of TzHaar inhabitants, from which you may find TokKul drops. Occasionally, a TzHaar inhabitant will drop a large cache of TokKul, so keep an eye out for these. It is also worth noting that some of the monsters found in the TzHaar city with higher combat levels are known to drop the items themselves on rare occasions!

However you make your TokKul, when you have enough, you may be able to buy a variety of items from the TzHaar shops.

Aside from buying runes from the rune shop, gems and ores from the gem shop, you can buy TzHaar-only weapons from the weapon shop located in the heart of the city.
