Construction - Oubliette

Introduction | Key Features | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

Level 65 Construction gives the option to be mean! With level 65, you can construct the oubliette. This can be constructed under the throne room, or just in the dungeon area.

Key Features

A typical oubliette
The oubliette's main features are the cage and floorspace traps.

If a trap is activated and the oubliette is below a Throne room, players will fall through from above into the pit with a trap of your choice!

If it is not under a throne room, players still feel the bite of your traps. If you construct teleport traps in your dungeon and a player gets caught in one, they will be teleported into the oubliette. (If an oubliette is not built you will be teleported to the start of the area you failed.)

To get out of the oubliette you need to find the door section of the cage, which you can try to pick or force open. The higher level cages will be harder to escape!

The Hotspots

Oubliette hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are seven types of hotspot in the oubliette that you can construct on:

  • Floor
  • Prison
  • Guard
  • Ladder
  • Decoration
  • Lighting
  • Door
