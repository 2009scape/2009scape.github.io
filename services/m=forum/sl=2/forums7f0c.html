To celebrate the launch of Miner Disturbance on the iPhone, we're proud to announce the Miner Disturbance Story Competition.

To enter this competition, all you need to do is write a 400-word story inspired by either the iPhone-based or browser-based version of Miner Disturbance. Once you've written your story, send it to competitions@jagex.com, with your account/display name and Miner Disturbance: Story Competition in the subject box.

The deadline for sending us your entry is 23:59 BST on the 30th of June. The judges will then dig through the mountain of entries to pick out their favourite.

Once the judges have decided on their favourite entry, we'll announce the winner and award the author with lifetime membership to both FunOrb and RuneScape, plus some exclusive signed Miner Disturbance artwork. The second-placed author will receive 12 months of membership, and the third placed author will receive 6 months.

We have a dedicated thread for the competition that has full rules, terms & conditions and an FAQ. You'll find this thread on the FunOrb forums (Quick find code: 29-30-987-66650).

The competition entry can be written in either Danish or English.


Feel free to translate the news post and the rules on the forum thread for the Danish forums!
