Po Angielsku:

Overview of Application of Process:
1. Make an application once all of the requirements needed are met.
2. Once accepted you will become a "Recruit" of KO and will have two weeks to meet people, attend events, and prove that you wish to be in the clan.
3. After the two weeks are completed you will receive a PM from an official requesting your information.
4. When the information is gathered you will be placed into voting which requires an amount of 80% yes votes to become a full "Knight".

To be placed into recruit voting you need to do the following beforehand:
1. Attend seven events with five official(wars/pk's) events in your two week trial period.
2. For every event attended please take a screenshot as proof that you were there. Make sure to note what event was and when it took place.
3. PM a HC/Council member with your information.
4. It is strongly recommended to have at least 50 posts before your voting period. Anything less may affect your chances to join the clan.

Novitiate:
The "Novitiate Rank" will be available to applicants that donï¿½t meet our requirements but would still like to be a part of KO. They will have limited access to our forums until they meet the necessary requirements. This is beneficial and we strongly recommend it, as it is a chance to familiarise yourself with the clan and to get to know the members as well get an understanding of what we are all about.

Requirements:
-110+ Combat with two referrals.
-You may only be in one main clan.
-The only exceptions are country clans and teams.

Community:
We are a clan that appreciate and value our members as we are all equal. The mutual respect within KO allows the clan to maximize the fun that can be experienced in the game of Runescape. Having a community where respect is given and earned gives off the comfortable atmosphere where everyone is considered family. Like family, we encourage others to reach their potential, we aid those that are in need of it, and we hold each other up when times are bad. The unique unity is what keeps us up whenever adversary faces us and it's the reason why we suceed.

What can you experience?
Other than the close-knit community we offer, you'll be able to experience warring with one of the better clans at it. We pride ourselves in our warring ability and have captured many victories acting as one cohesive unit. Obtaining swift KO's, keeping our targets binded, and leaving the battlefield with many survivors is the only way of winning for us. We also have many laid-back events, such as God wars, Ventrilo singing contests, barrows, and many more.

Why we stand:
Standing for the past 6+ years is credited to the dedicated, loyal, and diligent members KO is blessed to have. The rich history of falling and getting back up is the main reason why we have become stronger than ever before. The leaders of KO take much pride in leading a group of great people.

Any questions feel free to PM on runescape or post if you'd like.
