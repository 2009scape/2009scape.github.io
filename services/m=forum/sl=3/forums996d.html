�Qu� te pasa? / What's wrong with you?

�Te duele algo? / What is your hurt? (literal translation but used to say 'What's wrong?')

Me duele(n) _______ / My hurt is _______.
**This can be a bit tricky, you will need to add the letter 'n' to duele if the body part you are talking about is plural i.e. hands (las manos.)

Me gusta(n) ______ / My pleasure is ______
** This can be a bit tricky, you will need to add the letter 'n' to gusta if the object you are talking about is plural. Also, This phrase in spanish literally means my pleasure is; but is used to say 'I like ______'

Also, for those of you who are trying to speak spanish remember these two rules,
1) H's in spanish are always silent so hombro (shoulder) is pronounced ombro.

2) If there is a double L in a word, such as la pantorilla (leg), you pronounce the 'll' using a y (which sounds like e in spanish.) So la pantorilla would be pronounced pant-or-e-a.
