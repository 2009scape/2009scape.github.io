I'm Canadian, but I know a little bit about Sweden

The capital is Stockholm
The country belongs to a group of countries known around the world as Scandanavia.
It's neighbours are Finland and Norway, with Germany and Poland to the south.
There is a city north of Stockholm called Uppsala.
Swedish is the most commonly spoken language in Sweden
Swe is the Swedish abbreivation for Swedish, and is actually commonly used in RuneScape Swedish worlds.
The currency is called Crowns in English, yet I forget the Swedish name. I have a Swedish coin actually and it's really cool!
