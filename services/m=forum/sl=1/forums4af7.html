By the way for Alex;

Irregular verb - To be.

-English-.................................-Dutch-

I [ am ]................................. Ik [ ben ]
You [ are ]...........................Jij [ bent ]
He/she/it [ is ]....................Hij/zij/het [ is ]
We [ are ]............................Wij [ zijn ]
You [ are ]...........................Jullie [ zijn ]
They [ are ].........................Zij [ zijn ]

Example:

-English-
+ They [ are ] strong!

-Dutch-
+ Zij [ zijn ] sterk!

I hope this helps!
