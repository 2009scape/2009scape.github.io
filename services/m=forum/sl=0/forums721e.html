History of the Forum Parades:

Back in January of '09, MrL (Ace Lev), formerly known as Ruinedrune7 saw an opportunity to bring attention to a community not many knew about. At the time, mentioning the forums in game was equivalent to advertising an entirely different website.

Ace Lev created the first forum awareness parade a mere 4 days in advance of the event. Within 8 hours of posting the thread was stickied and posted on a new thread created by Mod Howes, The Awesome Events Digest.

Since the inception of the Forum Awareness Parade, we saw the rise of new events communities, which any look around this forum will tell you has been a great success!

The first Forum Awareness parade started in Draynor Village, boasting the attendance of some of the most active forumers in RS. We traveled from Draynor to Lumbridge to Varrock to Falador to Taverley to Camelot to Ardougne all the way to Yanille.

Parades followed in the next month on a less frequent basis. Due to unforeseeable circumstances, Ace Lev was unable to make one of the scheduled parades. Thankfully, Ace was able to contact My Wiz in Me who was ready to take charge, and more than successfully led an awesome parade of his own!

The parades were laid to rest for a short period of time as it had done its job! Of course theres always room for more...
