��� Clan Chat Rules �

1.) Golden Rule - Treat others the way you wish to be treated!

2.) Abide by ALL RuneScape Rules.

3.) Please don't use any language that may be offensive, racist, or profane. This includes abbreviations. Any offensive usernames will also be reported by using the in-game 'Report-Abuse' feature.

4.) If you happen to come across an Evil Tree while gaming, please be sure to report it by using this template: World / Location / Type / % of Health Left. DO NOT report any Evil Trees you find on PvP/BH Worlds and if left unreported you can be kicked from etc.

5.) False Reports are not welcome. Doing so will result in removal from the clan chat.

6.) Off-Topic Chat (discussion that isn't about Evil Trees) is not permitted in the chat. Please refrain from doing so.Unless there has been a update and then is only allowed by rank permission first.

7.) Asking for Evil Trees isn't allowed.

8.) We understand that many people like to be helpful, but please let the ranks do the "modding".

9.) In order to participate in the clan chat, you must be logged into a non-PvP members world. You may not be in the CC while in a lobby.

10.) Please do not use quick chat within the cc unless using to report evil trees.

After completion of the Tree Gnome Village quest, you gain access to Spirit Trees.
Spirit Trees offer hints to where a Sapling is located, Teleports to a grown tree and the general time that the next Evil Tree will appear after completion of Tree Gnome Village.

Spirit Trees are located in the following areas:
- Grand Exchange
- Gnome Stronghold
- Gnome Village (Maze)
- Khazard Battlefield
- Mobilising Armies
- Mountains east of poison waste (Completed Path of Glouphrie)
-(Players can plant a Spirit Tree with level 83 farming)

~ Spirit Tree Time Reports

� Over an hour = 1 hour - 2 hours
� Over a half hour = 30 minutes - 1 hour
� Under a half hour = 15 minutes - 30 minutes
� Under 15 minutes = 5 minutes - 15 minutes
� About to Sprout = 0 minutes - 5 minutes

~ NOTE ~
None of these will have a Location, Type or Percentage as they have not sprouted yet.

~ A list of the Sapling location possibilities is located earlier on this page.
