Alright.
Everyone knows about the dragon crossbow and how range desperately needs a re-balance in the combat triangle.. so first I'm going to just establish something.

1. You cannot have a double special...
Reason: If you had a damage % increase with it...
it would be ridiculous seeing as max d-bolt special is 680. (or higher) or have something like Zanik's crossbow special with dragon bolts..

2. Some people have previously suggested that its just accurate to the extreme.
Not a good idea: It would just become another overpowered weapon like dragon claws.

Sooo I've finally thought of a special attack that is completely unique to runescape that would not be a "double special". I don't think i've seen anyone ever suggest this either.

Think of the rune c'bow. fairly accurate.
The Dragon crossbow should only have about +15 more range attack bonus.

The special itself should be extremely unique.
My idea for the special attack would for it to
Drain 100% of the special, and have your attack rate increased by about 30% for a designated amount of time.
This would make it so it is not overpowered but a good weapon to pk with.

I think it would be an awsome way to proceed with the special.

The only problem that I have thought of as of yet is how eating would affect the special. Would the % of attack rate increase carry over to the next firing of the weapon?

ok first of all let me say my opinion on this whole "combat triangle" look here im not here to flame or anything but rather give a perspective on this whole combat triangle that lots of people typicaly miss or dont consider.

from what i know the combat triangle should look like this

melee> range
range> mage
mage> melee

now let me explain somethings to people, there is hardly a combat triangle it is so faint and hardley is bound to this game.

now consider this, in runescape you are "NOT FORCED to pick a class/role like ranger,melee, or mage" you actually can mix skills as most of rs does now in doing so you have things like these listed below

melee/mage, melee/range
range/melee,range/mage
mage/melee, mage/range

now in doing so, that takes the combat triangle out of existience, and should be out of perspective, if rs was a "CLASS/ROLE" based game where you had to pick melee or range or mage then the triangle would become what it was intended to be.

now i hope people understand my logic and took time to read this, as im sick of people saying range dont need this, melee needs more of that, mage need 100 more items and so forth

@SoCYellow130,

I think the Staff of Light's special was made that way because generally mages don't have particularly high defence armor. That act's as a kind of 'counter measure', where as the armor of rangers can be fairly more protective(such as armadyl armor, or even black dragonhide). Mages also have the disadvantage of losing a large amount of magic attack bonus when putting on higher protective equipment. I'm not trying to say the Staff of Light can't be overpowered in the right situations, but only that mages kind of need something to help them protect themselves.
