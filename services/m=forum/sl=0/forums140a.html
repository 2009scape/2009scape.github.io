~ Runescape Services Directory~

Hello everyone! And welcome to the Runescape Services Directory!

This directory will be like a yellow pages for the businesses in this community. They will be organized by services provided and alphabetically by name along with a brief description and their threads QFC.

I would like to point out this thread is simply like a directory to show players exactly what's out there when they want a particular service. This gives the consumer an idea of who to look at when deciding over all what company to choose. We offer this service free to all businesses in this community for the purpose of aiding both new members and new businesses as well as supporting the more experienced business members in the community.

Thank you.

Welcome to the second version! I have doubled the space for our directory so that we will be able to continue to grow together in our business community. We filled the first thread remarkably fast with eager new businesses springing up all around our community. Now with even more room to grow we can keep welcoming the new companies around us into the Services Forums.

I feel it is important to recognize everyone who has helped with this directory- but there are simply to many to name, however I will take this space and a moment to thank the following players for hard work in helping to keep the directory continuously updated:

Rules of use:

1. All Jagex rules
2. Threads that do not abide by JaGeX's Rules of Runescape and Code Of Conduct will be removed from the Directory.
3. No offensive company names or artwork
4. No provisions of websites, youtube acct names or anything that may cause the player from leaving the Runescape Website to receive the services offered.
5. No scamming
6. No 'personal' ads. This is exclusively for businesses.
7. Any legitimate Runescape Business may be included, whether listed under Clan Community, Services, Events (providing it is a continued service company not just a one time action.) Freeplayer and Member businesses are included and encouraged.
