Impling Spotted!
Hi there. We've made this thread to help you to help others take part in the Impetuous Impulses minigame. It's generally a helpful, caring thread and I'm sure that I'm not the only one who can feel the love.

If you spot an impling and would like to share it's location with other players, then please use this thread.


To make it easier for your fellow spotters, it'd be great if you used the following format:

Impling Type:
World:
Location:

Here are the guidelines for this thread:

● It's a generous, giving thread and we're trying to keep a nice, helpful vibe. So please don't try to charge players for the info.

● The template is for the catchers to quickly find an impling. It is not required to be used, but it helps.

● The only implings allowed to be posted here are Magpie and above, with the exception of Pirate Implings.

● Pirate implings do not give much loot, so please do not post them (even though they require a higher level to catch).

● Trust trades are not discouraged, but all trust trades must be discussed in-game, and not here.

● If your impling has been caught, please edit your post to indicate this.

● Impling logs are allowed in the Monsters forum, providing they are only used as logs and not to find scouts or share information about where implings can currently be found.

● The Miscellaneous forum should be used for employing scouts. This includes asking people to pm you when they spot an Impling. All arrangements in regards to pay should be agreed before hand to ensure fairness for all parties involved.

● Please do not accuse people of making false reports on this thread. There are places in Runescape where Implings can easily escape.

● When reporting Implings, especially Dragon Implings, try and keep your Private chat set to 'On' and try to follow the impling for at least a little bit. A catcher might PM you, asking where it is, and if you help them, they might be more inclined to give you a reward .

● When reporting Implings from a PvP world, please put a warning in your post that it is a PvP world. If you don't, your post will be removed.

● Intentionally posting false or misleading info helps no one and such posts will be considered to be spam and removed.

● If you caught an impling that was reported on this thread, please do not post that you caught it. If you want to record your loots and/or catches, please make an impling log.


Have fun!
