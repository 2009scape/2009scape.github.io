Clan Rules

These are the clan rules and every member of The Divine Legends must abide by them if you do not want to abide please do not fill out an application

Respect
Have respect for all other members of the clan bulling, racial comments, and unnecessary swearing will not be tolerated.

Higher rank authority
Always have respect for a higher rank then you meaning if a Corpral is the highest rank in the clan chat at the time all recruits must fallow his instructions unless it goes against the rules.

Bumping
Every member is expected to bump the clan thread at least twice a week and they do not count as a bump unless they are an hour apart.

Clan sweep
The clan sweep is somthing that happens at the end of each month what happens is the sweep officer gets a list of all the inactive members and gives it to the leader which then the names on the list will be deranked/kicked from clan
