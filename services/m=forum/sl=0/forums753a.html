There are a few simple steps to achieving ultra-fast fairy ring travel.


Step 1:

Do all the Fairy Tale quests. This includes part 1, 2, and 3.

The bare minimum is to unlock fairy ring use in the beginning of part 2, but part 3 is recommended in order to unlock staff-free fairy ring travel.

Step 2:

Get 300 Slayer Points, and unlock Rings of Slaying. This requires the quest Smoking Kills.

Step 3:

Make 50+ Rings of Slaying. If you do not have 75+ crafting, you can use the assist system.

You will need 1 enchanted gem (1gp) and 1 gold bar (about 90gp) to make each ring. You also need a ring mould in your inventory. Use the gold bar at a furnace to make the rings.

The rings are very cheap.

Step 4:

Do the things to unlock more fairy rings after completion of Fairy Tale part 3.

The Ape Atoll fairy ring requires a small ninja monkey gree-gree.

To unlock the Mos le Harmless fairy ring, you will need 8 regular planks, 20+ nails, and a hammer.

Step 5:

Go to every fairy ring location once. This will fill your log up with all the codes and you'll never have to enter them again. This takes a bit of time but it's worth it.

Step 6:

Whenever you need to use a fairy ring, simply withdraw a slayer ring from your bank and use it to teleport to the Relleka slayer dungeon entrance. You will be mere steps away from a fairy ring. Use this fairy ring to go to zanaris, then choose your location from the list.

Congratulations on achieving ultra-fast fairy ring travel !

NOTE: I came up with the idea of using slayer rings myself. I didn't steal the idea from anywhere. I'm pretty sure I was one of the first to think of this.
