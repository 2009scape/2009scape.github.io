Hello and welcome to Effyssist. This clan chat and thread is designed to help those who have effigies in their inventory and who do not have the level of that specific skill.

Join our Clan Chat "Effyssist"

The owner of this clan are:
Danigoy

The co-owner of this clan are:
Lord Magords

If you would like to help others please fill out an application and one of the owners or thread operators will accept your application.

How do you assist?

Someone who has high-level skills will be able to use the Assist system to help you investigate your effigy. To do this, make sure your Assist button is turned on (by the chat box), then right-click a player and select "Req Assist". The person being assisted will get the experience when you investigate it, but your effigy will be charged.

The only skills that can be assisted however are:

Crafting, Construction, Cooking, Farming, Fletching, Herblore, Smithing, and Runecrafting.

How Efigies work?

The effigies have level requirements:

�Starved ancient effigy: Requires level 91 in a particular skill to investigate.
- grants 15,000 experience in the skill that is used and turns into a Nourished ancient effigy

�Nourished ancient effigy: Requires level 93 in a particular skill to investigate.
- grants 20,000 experience in the skill that is used and turns into a Sated ancient effigy

�Sated ancient effigy: Requires level 95 in a particular skill to investigate.
- grants 25,000 experience in the skill that is used and turns into a Gorged ancient effigy

�Gorged ancient effigy: Requires level 97 in a particular skill to investigate.
- grants 30,000 experience in the skill that is used and will turn into a Dragonkin Lamp

The rules and ranks

The rules are quite simple..

1) Don't spam either in the clan chat or on the thread. This is very important because we want to be able to assist others and yourself.

2) Those who want to be assisted, please don't ask for a rank because ranks are for those who have applied and are working to help you.

3) No fighting with each other, eventually it will cause you to be kicked.

4) No swearing please, there are approximately 988,968 English words to describe your frustation why use a swear word.

5) Don't lie, especially about your stats, it will be pretty obvious.

Ranks...

Key next to name - Owner
Gold - Owner or General
Silver - Thread Operator
Bronze - Clan Chat Operator
3 Bananas - People who help
Smily Face - Help to recruit and refer

Those who are helping with the Effygies will earn a 3 banana rank, and can move up if they show their hard working abilities.
