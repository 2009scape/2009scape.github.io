2 - Clan Chat Rules


These rules MUST be followed by everyone in Pvp Houses clan chat:

1. No swearing

2. No inappropriate talk

3. No trading in the chat. (Excluding Marrentill, in case of emergency house openings.)

4. No spam

5. Do not advertise another clan chat.

6. Do not advertize a house of which Pvp Houses clan is not using.

7. DO NOT attack ANYONE using Pvp Houses clan chat.
(If they are a purple dot, don't touch them.)

8. All Jagex rules apply

If you do not follow these rules, you will be given a warning, if you continue you will be promptly kicked.
