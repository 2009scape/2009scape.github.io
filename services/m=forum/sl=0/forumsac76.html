1. Follow all Jagex Rules
2. Follow instructions of our Leaders and Co- Leaders
3. No Multiclaning. Pest Control and Player vs. Player clans are allowed as long as they do not interfere with this clan in anyway.
4. No spamming, flaming or complaining about how the clan is run.
5. Our Leaderteam will make the promotions, do not ask.
6. Bump once every 10 minutes. Any more is consider spam.
7. Please bless the grave of your clan members who die at events.
8. You have to attent 3 Events in 2 Weeks.
9. Real life issues will always come first. Please use the Absence form on page 3 of the forum if you know you will be absent.
10. Check daily our Threads and please don't ask in our clan chat about our current event.
11. Only private message our leaders in an emergency.
12. All kickings from clan chat must be posted on the thread. (Name and Reason).
13. You have to bump the Thread at least once per day.
14. You have to add all Leaders.
15. If you leave the clan or quit you won't have your old position back.
16. Offical Clan Meeting and Clan Wars Events are mandatory. If you are online and miss them, you WILL lose Event Points.
17. Respect every Clan member and follow the orders from ranked members.

5. No checking other Teams levels.
