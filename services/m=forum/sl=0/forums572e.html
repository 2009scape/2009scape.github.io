My RuneScape Character:

I try to avoid combat except for the Quest related fights.

3rd Question
"Why? What made you choose this goal over all the other typical goals?" ~Midnite Cars

Answer:
A very good question! There's no actual answer to that. I guess it was a lot of things that made me wanna do this.
I've allways liked to do quests and I've allways liked to try to complete the hard quests with as low combat lvl as possible. The low combat lvl shows that you can survive and fight though the combat skills are low. There's a lot of people in RuneScape with high combat lvl, but who's not capable of much els besides fighting, which in my oppinion is not what RuneScape is about. So I wanted my character to be like the opposite of them.
So I thougt: If I'm more skiller than fighter I guess my combat level should be lower than my levels in all of my skills
Getting all of the non-cb skills about the combat lvl wouldn't be a challenge if you were a lvl 3 skiller, so to have a reason for the combat skills I got I wanted to have the Quest Cape aswell.

I plan on getting untrimmed Runecrafting Cape, but it won't be anytime soon. I don't know what I'll be doing irl 2010/11. I depends on my irl how much time I will get to play and since Runecrafting is one of the toughest skills in RS it'll take a large amount of time.
