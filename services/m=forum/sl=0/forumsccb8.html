=======================
RuneScape Trading Directory
=======================

Welcome everyone.

The RuneScape Trading Directory will tell you which forum to use when buying or selling your
RuneScape items. Please check this directory before you trade so that you trade in the correct
forum each and every time.

TIP: To quickly find an item press CTRL+F and type in the item name to search for it in this list.

Good luck with your trades.

===============
Armour
===============
Ancient ............ Morrigan's armour, Statius's armour, Vesta's armour, Zuriel's armour.
Barrows .......... Barrows armour (Including full sets) (Ahrim's, Dharok's (DH)).
Barrows .......... Barrows armour (Guthan's, Karil's, Torag's, Verac's).
Basic ............... Chainbody, Shields, Helms, Platebody, Platelegs, Plateskirts.
Boots ............... Boots (Iron, Steel, Mithril, Addy, Rune, Dragon boots), Mystic boots.
Capes ............. Obsidian (Obby) capes, Spotted capes, Spottier capes.

Fremennik ...... Fremennik helms (Archer, Berserker, Farseer, Warrior).
Fremennik ...... Fremennik armour (Rockshell, Skeletal, Spined).
Granite ............ Granite body, Granite helm, Granite legs, Granite shield.
Hunter ............. Gloves of Silence, Hunter gear (Graahk, Larupia, Kyatt, all Camo clothes).
Others ............. Black mask, Focus sight, Hexcrest, Obsidian armour.

Mage Armour ... Infinity armour (Robes, Top, Hats, Gloves, Boots).
Mage Armour ... Dagon'hai armour, Mystic armour, Splitbark armour, Robes of Zamorak.
Shields ............. Arcane, Blessed, Divine, Elysian, Spectral, Spirit shields.
Shields ............. Anti-dragon, Broodoo, Crystal, Dragonfire, Dragon half, Obsidian shields.
Tassets ............ Bandos tassets, Proselyte tassets.
White Armour ... White Knight armour, Initiate armour, Proselyte armour.

* God or Trimmed armour - Use Treasure Trail Items forum.
