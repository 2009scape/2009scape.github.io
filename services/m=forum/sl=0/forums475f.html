Hello! And welcome to ABC's Before YOU Post! Now you might be wondering..How do you play? Well it's pretty simple.

Players take turns saying the alphabet. But if the letter the person said is the same letter as the start of their username you reset the alphabet and start with A again. Here's a example:

Sharp Blade: A
Heyy: B
Cool Guy: C - Let's reset the count!

Wrong Example:

Hello: A
Cat Guy: B Reset!

Which makes no sense

To make it a bit more intresting after you complete the alphabet one time when you start on A again it will become 2A and if you complete it again 3A.. And on and on If you make it to 4A you get a cookie.

You may be wondering what about the people with numbers at the start of thier name?
0 - 5 at the beggining: Can reset anytime during the first round. (A,B,C)
6 - 9 at the beggining: Can reset anytime on the second round (2A,2B,2C)
On the thrid round they're just regular players.

And people with A in their name can reset anytime

Highest Count we got to so far: 2R


Rules
1. Follow the letters IN ORDER
2. Follow Jagex rules
3. Don't post twice in a row
