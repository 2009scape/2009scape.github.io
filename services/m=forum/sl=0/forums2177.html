The Clans Sign Up



Clans/Groups Sign Up

�Teamwork is the fuel that allows common people to attain uncommon results.� - Andrew Carnegie




This thread is here for clans/businesses/corporations/groups to join one of the organisations. Signing a clan up to an organisation does not mean that all members of that clan must be part of that organisation. Individuals are free to join any organisation they wish as clan and individual influence points are separate.

How to sign up


Before you even think of signing up make sure you have a majority agreement from your clan. If you sign up without their agreement you are likely to cause more harm than good. Also, only Clan leaders and high ranking clan members should sign their clan up.

To sign your clan up simply fill in the following template:


Clan Name: Your clans name
Clan Leader: Who leads your clan
Number of Members: How many people are in your clan (Don't list names, the total number will do)
Type of clan: Are you PvPers, skillers, a corporation etc
Organisation: Do you pledge yourselves to the Lords, Judges or Reavers?


YOU MUST HAVE AT LEAST 10 PEOPLE IN YOUR CLAN TO SIGN UP


What Next?


Once a moderator has added you to the clans list of an organisation you are now eligible to earn influence for that organisation through events, challenges and competitions.

At first you'll only be able to accept challenges approved by a moderator but once your clan progresses through the ranks of an organisation you'll be able to set your own challenges.

A moderator will always need to confirm the amount of influence a challenge will give. This is to ensure things run smoothly.

FAQ


My clan is split on which organisation to join. What do we do?
You'll need to reach a consensus with your clan in order to join an organisation. There is no rush to do this though and you're always welcome to sign up to an organisation as an individual.

Do we have to join an organisation?
Not at all! The organisations are completely voluntary, though obviously you can't take part in the challenges unless you sign up.

I am in more than one clan, is this a problem if they join different organisations?
The army and individual influence areas are separate from each other so you can be in more than one clan as the army is based on the clan not the people in it. It doesn't matter if your clans are in different camps, though it might be a difficult choice for you if they end up against each other!

We're not a combat clan, is this a problem?
The challenges will consist of a variety of tasks. They won't all be combat based and we'll try and have as much variety as possible

Our clan has changed our minds about our organisation. Can we defect?
You are welcome to leave or join any clan, so long as you mention this in the leaving thread. The army influence gained by your clans presence will be taken from the old organisation and added to the new but any challenge or special points you have earned will stay with the original.

Can I form a new clan based on one of the organisations?
Go for it! You are more than welcome to create clans based on the organisations (i.e. the Lords Lions, Reavers Raiders and so on) however you'll still need to sign the clan up to be associated!

My clan keeps changing size. Do we have to notify you of every change?
No, just tell us if it rises above your current size threshold (i.e 40-99, 100-149 etc). If you're still in the same threshold as you were before then you don't need to let us know.

- Mod Crow -
