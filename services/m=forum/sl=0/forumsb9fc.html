Hello everybody and welcome to Tree Find v.2 Thread.

~~~~~Clan Chat Rules...~~~~~

Tree Find is the least strict of the tree finding clan chats. There isnt many rules so please check em out


1. Follow all Jagex rules

2. Respect all clan members

3. No offensive language - including 'noob' and 'fail' and any abbreviations of swears which evade jagex censor

4. No trading items or lending items for money - you can only lend if it's for free and offered only once

5. Don't be in the cc on a f2p or pvp world unless you're a general (gold star)

6. No spamming. This includes --
- Continuous arguing or questioning directions given by ranks
- Quick Chat (unless you're in a quick chat world reporting a tree)
- Asking for everyone's levels (asking for an individual's level is okay to an extent)
- Continuously counting down xp to a level
- Excessive punctuation
- Using multiple lines of chat

7. Don't ask for evil trees - instead, report your world and wait for others to report (exceptions made if you are constantly hopping and afraid you may have missed a world)

8. No advertising clan or sharing/leeching reports with other tree clans

9. Do not advertise TF*C in other clan chats

10. Show respect for people reporting trees (if a person is reporting trees please take a break from your off topic talk, if possible join by reporting your world)

11. No asking to be ranked (tips on how to gain rank are futher down pg1)

12. Use correct tree format World/Type/Health/Location, only report trees if you can confirm.

~~~~~Evil Tree Basics~~~~~

Strange things are afoot in the woods around RuneScape. An unusual sapling has been seen sprouting among the trees, and it�s growing at an alarming rate. Aided by a familiar-looking farmer,(The helpful leprechaun) you could help encourage this burgeoning seedling - but keep an axe and tinderbox handy because it could become�an evil tree!

The leprechaun will give you a tinderbox and bronze axe to aid you in getting rid of this evil tree! He might even bolster your rewards with a special ability that will automatically bank any logs you don�t have the space to carry. This perk will last for between two and thirty minutes, depending on your performance.

There are 5 stages of the sapling before it becomes an evil tree. You need a farming level half of the woodcuting level needed to chop the tree to nurture the sapling into an evil tree.

This distraction and diversion offers a new way of training your Firemaking and Woodcutting skills; and there are other rewards up for grabs, too, including seeds, logs, birds� nests, and gold.

~~~~~How to find an Evil Tree!~~~~~

Evil trees can appear in one of 26 forest locations; just keep an eye out for leprechauns and thrashing roots (which might try to grab you). If you have completed the Tree Gnome Village quest, spirit trees will offer you directions, hints and teleports and tell you an estimated time till the next tree spawn after the previous evil tree has died. The 26 possible locations are listed further down the page in order of the hints given

Kindling is used to light the tree which gives you a greater chance of recieving 100% rewards and is got by cutting the roots around the evil tree.
You can trade kindlng for 25gp each after the tree dies but keeping it for another evil tree is recommended.

You can do this event twice a day. This day resets at 00.00GMT (or 01.00 GMT during daylight savings time). After this reset you can then chop another two trees!

Nurturing a sapling gets you farming xp but there are drawbacks to nurturing. If you nurture the tree it is counted as one of your two trees even if you don't have the required level to cut or fire it.

Note! Evil trees come up every 2 hours for each world.
They do NOT come up every 2 hours for all worlds.
Stars fall every 2 hours on all worlds, with these Evil trees they fall every 2 hours for that world. once a evil tree dies in a world it takes 2 hours for another to come in that world. Times for worlds vary, they arnt all at once.
Updates resets the trees so all worlds have a similar sprouting time but this spaces out after about a day.

~~~~~Suggested inventory:~~~~~

~~~~~Rewards:~~~~~

The rewards for each type of tree vary but mainly are
Logs (of the type of tree you are cutting)
Seeds
Nest
Clue Scroll (level 1)
Loop half key
Teeth half key
GP
Magic Lepricorn Banking (Lasts between 2-30mins)

The magic banking works when you have a full inventory any logs you cut (normal, oak, willow, maple, yew, magic) will be automatically banked for you!!

Elder rewards differ slightly as you get either yew, magic, teak or mahogany logs.

If you right click and inspect the tree you will see your earned % of rewards and also the tree's remaining health. The tree dies when a certain number of people gain 100% rewards unless there is less then 5ppl cutting. If this is the case the tree will be struck by lightning every 10mins reducing its health to 50%, 25%, 0% respectively.

~~~~~Frequently Asked Questions~~~~~



*Does inspecting the tree count as one of my two trees?*
No, you can inspect the tree without it counting as one of your trees. Only nurturing, firemaking, and cutting the tree (or roots) counts.



*Is there an ape atol tree?*
If you are a pure with level 1 defence Daero can now tele you to ape atol without you gaining any defence xp. Just speak to Daero but make sure you pick the right option!!



*Where is tree (when world is under 15mins or about to sprout)?*
The spirit trees only gives a hint to the trees location when the sap has sprouted. When the tree is fully grown you can teleport directly to the tree (as long as you have completed any required quests for that location)



*If I cut a tree and don't collect the rewards does it count as one of my two trees per day?*
If you cut the tree and don't claim the rewards the lepricorn won't let you cut another tree until you claim your rewards from the last tree. (NB. Your autobank time will be halfed if you collect later on)



*How do I get ranked in this cc?*
To gain a rank in this clan chat you need to:
1. obey the clan chat and runescape rules,
2. help explain how the evil tree d&d works to people new to this (maybe by asking them to read knowledge base and thread),
3. helping scout for worlds due and reporting in correct format,
4. checking the thread regularly and bumping,
5. helping advertise tree find at evil trees/spirit tree (without spamming chat obviously as this breaks rs rules)
6. Demonstrate you are mature and that you have a good temprement

Asking for a rank, or moaning that you have been helping and not gained rank will not help. If you have been contributing well done In the old cc it took several months for some people to gain ranks (for reasons beyond control) so please be patient keep helping out and being friendly and you will be noticed
