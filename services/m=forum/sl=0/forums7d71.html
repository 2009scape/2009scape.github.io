Hello, citizens of Runescape. Welcome to the Whitewater Police Department Headquarters. A police department in Runescape, you say? What could they possibly do with no jails, no handcuffs, and no police cars?

Well, friends, the WWPD is an organization of players dedicated to stopping the abuse of rule abiding people that is, unfortunately, seen every day. We are not Want To Be Mods, nor do we impersonate Jagex staff. We simply take some free time out of our day to find people who may not be informed on the rules, educate these people about said rules, and in the worst case scenarios, report people who still do not comply with the code of conduct Jagex expects us to adhere to.

In any society, there must be rules. Where there are rules, there will be people who break them. Doesn't it follow, then, that there should be some people within the society dedicated to stopping the rule breakers? We think so. From regular solicitation to real life threats to entire riots, WWPD will be there to help.

Of course, before you go thinking we're all about rules and guidelines, we love to have fun in our downtime. Parties, events, and friendship are all very much appreciated among our officers and dispatchers. All of us get along quite well because we share a common goal: safety and happiness for everyone in Runescape - and what could be better than that?

Patrol Officer Services:

1.) We patrol cities of Runescape. This is to help crackdown on rulebreakers. We strive to straighten up rule breakers and pass on the rules of Runescape.

`�.����������*����*��� Employee Descriptions ���*����*����������.��

Founder - Founded the Department, will always remain the Founder.

Police Commissioner - Is the leader of the Police Department. Maintains the Department and has all powers. They move and run the Department in a strong manner, and brings forth new ideas to improve, and maintain the Department. The Commissioner works with everyone below him to help maintain the Department.

Deputy Commissioner - This person helps to maintain and run the Police Department. They work with the Commissioner on all fronts to make the Department better, and organized. Has the ability to fire, train all recruits, and hire.

Development Director - Works with the Commissioner and Chief of Staff to make the Department better. Suggests and brings forward new ideas to help us improve.

Director of Police - Is the leader of all three areas of the Police Department including but not limited to: Dispatch and Patrols. May assign Chief's of Department with approval from the Commissioner. They may hire and fire, and can train all units.

~ Patrol Department ~

Chief of Patrol- This individual is like a governor, and is responsible for assigning the Patrol teams to different areas, but still close enough so that the squads may help each other. He decides whether or not a request will require backup. They can train Patrol recruits, assign Heads of Patrol, and can fire recruits.

Heads of Patrol- These people are like mayors, and are responsible for their squads in their assigned cities. They can choose their squad's roster, and assign people to specific parts of a city.

Patrol- These squads perform routine routes to stumble upon a rule breaker. These officers are trained to engage a target, attempt to talk them down, and take action.
