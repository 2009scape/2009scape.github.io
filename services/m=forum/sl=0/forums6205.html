July Jubilations



July Jubilations!




In the US of A it's a big holiday weekend from the 3rd - 4th of July and where better to spend it than in the lands of Gielinor? The event forums are rife with great community events for you to check out but if the choice is just too much, bookmark this thread and spend the weekend with us!

Some Events for you perusal...


Saturday 3rd July

Costume Competition

Fancy yourself a bit of an impressionist? Well now's your chance to prove it! Dress your in-game avatar up as your best lookalike and send a snapshot of yourself to 'competitions@jagex.com'. Competition ends 11.59pm Sunday!

Triumvirate Clan Wars Challenge

Help the Lords, Judges or Reavers fight for influence and control in Clan Wars battles on Saturday! Unfamiliar with the Triumvirate? Find more information or join in here!

Jagex Clan Cup Parade

Get your clan together and march into Falador square on world 141 at 8pm BST. Bring your best clan attire, banners and of course, your clan members!


Sunday 4th July

Summer Festival

Join us from 9pm BST all the way through to 3am BST as we celebrate the 4th July with a massive summer festival! There will be Scavenger hunts, Christmas in July and a chance to show of your Costume Competition entries with a parade!

Costume Competition



Costume Competition!




This weekend we'll be holding a look-a-like costume competition! The idea is simple; pick a RuneScape outfit that resembles that of a famous person or character, take a quick screenshot, and ping us the screenshot in an email! Easy.

To make things more interesting, we'll also be giving extra Kudos to those who come up with alternative RuneScape-related names for their look-a-likes, and for choice of location when taking the screenshot.

Rules

  • Entries must be sent to the competitions@jagex.com email address with the subject: 'Costume Comp'
  • Please only submit your own entries - don't take a screenshot of another player and enter that
  • Entries must be sent before the close of the competition (by midnight (BST) on Sunday 4th July 2010)
  • Entries must include your RuneScape username in the email
  • Please send entries in an easily viewable format (ie, JPEG, Gif, Bitmap)
  • Entries must abide by the RuneScape rules and terms & conditions

Prizes

1st Place - 6 months RuneScape membership
2nd Place - 3 months RuneScape membership
3rd Place - 1 month RuneScape membership

FAQ
  1. So what type of lookalike should I be?

    Absolutely anyone although if you do your gran, we won't be able to tell how good it is so best stick to someone famous. That could be a film character, a celebrity, an internet meme, a cartoon character the possibilities are endless!

  2. Are there any off limits characters?

    Well we'd prefer you not try and create a character that would be very offensive to others. You know who they are but basically, real life political figures might be best avoided.

  3. Whats the best way to take the screenshot?

    There are a couple of ways although for a nice shot, we recommend the Orb of Oculus (Available from Faruq in Al Kharid) to get you in full costumey glory. The other way leaves a little more to chance but is easier, just hit "Print Screen" (don't remember the mac version of that off the top of my head), paste it into Paint and away you go.

  4. Can I submit more than one entry?

    Yes you can!

Clan Wars Challenge



Triumvirate Clan Wars Challenge!


�War! that mad game the world so loves to play�
-Jonathan Swift

A weekend holiday is upon us and what better way to enjoy it than with some good old fashioned carnage! It's time to dust off that armour, string your bows and make sure you packed a fresh set of runes. It's clobbering time!



We'll be holding a series of Clan Wars battles between each faction across Saturday with plenty of time for you to earn some much needed influence for your faction!

The matches will be held on World 94 and at the following times:

Saturday 3rd July

Midnight BST - Judges vs Lords

11am BST - Lords vs Reavers

9pm BST - Reavers vs Judges


Unlike other matches these won't be one off rounds. You'll have 1 hour to get as many victories in as you possibly can. Speedy tactics and clever thinking will potentially earn you a lot more than digging in and waiting it out.

For every victory you will receive 100 influence. If you're good that could mean your faction walks away with a considerable amount at the end!

Don't forget to show off your faction colours! You should look at wearing Team Capes (available from the Wilderness sellers or on the Grand Exchange). We recommend the following:

Lords: Team Cape 21
Judges: Team Cape 31
Reavers: Team Cape 41

All three capes can be bought from 'William' in the Wilderness.

Map: Turrets
Victory: Knock Out
Stragglers: Ignore 5
Time Limit: No Limit
On Death: You keep your items
Combat rules: All F2P abilities

Clan Wars Challenge



Jagex Clan Cup Parade!




The Jagex Clan Cup is underway this weekend with the First Round matches of the Combat, Combined and Sub-Divisions taking place. While you can prove your combat prowess in the arena, show what your clan is worth to the world with our Jagex Clan Cup Parade!.

This Saturday at 8pm BST, we'll amass in Falador Square on World 141 for a parade across the lands. We'll head to Varrock and the Grand Exchange, showing off your clan's colours and pride along the way.

We'll then take a tour of the Wilderness to really see how tough your clan really is. Stopping by such landmarks as the Clan Wars Arena, Demonic Ruins, Red Dragon Isle and the Ruins. Ending in Edgeville for party in the bank!

Don't miss out! Show how proud you are to be a part of your clan in our Jagex Clan Cup Parade!

The Details:
Jagex Clan Cup Parade
World 141
8pm BST/Forum Time
Starting in Falador Square
Ending with a tour of the Wilderness
Bring clan attire, banners and your clan members.

Summer Festival



Gielinor�s Fourth of July Summer Party




It's that time of year! The weather's getting hotter which means that raw on the inside and burned on the outside meat based treats are back in fashion, nom nom nom. It's also the time of year for sitting in traffic for hours on end tin a bid to get to the seaside.

Great though that sounds, some people aren't too excited by the prospect of possible food poisoning and road rage. So, on Sunday evening, come to where there are no cars and however bad your cooking level, you'll eventually get a perfectly BBQ'd fish. You can then move on to a number of events that snowball fights aside, should be rage free! Yes, it's the 4th July Summer Party!

We'll kick off at 9pm and go on to 3am BST, on World 41! Remember, that's British Summer time! This may be an event to celebrate the holiday weekend in the states, but as ever, everyone is welcome. See you there!

9pm BST - MudSkipper Point Conga and BBQs!

No, this isn't snakes on beach but is actually a pleasant walk from Lumbridge to Mudskipper point! Gather up anyone you can find along the way and arrive to MudSkipper point just in time for a sardine or two. If you're running a tight schedule, feel free to meet us there.

At Mudskipper point the festivities will begin! You can show off your firemaking and cooking by hosting your own barbecues! You can even test your running skills in a mad race from Mudskipper and back again!

9.15pm BST - Scavenger Hunt!

We'll be running a scavenger hunt challenge at Mudskipper point. We'll challenge you to finding a number of specific items and it's your task to find them before everyone else! We'll announce the items at the time.

10pm BST - Christmas In July!

I don't know about you but sometimes I get a bit fed up with all the heat in summer. I'd sometimes just like to fast forward time to a colder season. That's why we're having a Christmas in July party!

Head on over to Ice Mountain to enjoy snowball fights, show off your Santa hats and we could even try a spot of caroling just to annoy the Icefiends! If you're feeling particularly festive you could also enjoy some present giving with your fellow players!

11pm BST - Firewalking race or Arms Race challenge!

Fancy an odd challenge? Head on over to Edgeville for a fire-walking challenge! Pick a team and select a runner to travel from Edgeville to Falador/Draynor/Lumbridge/Varrock and then back again. The twist though is that they can only move on fires! It's your teams task to place the path of flame so that your runner can win the race!

Not really fussed about a fire race? Fancy something with a bit more of an obvious goal? Well you'll be interested in the Arms Race challenge! The goal is simple, you need to choose to join either the Summer or Winter teams and then pick a group of you to represent your side in a Clan Wars battle! However, it's not that simple. You need to equip your soldiers with armour and weapons your team has crafted themselves! You've got just one hour to get your team equipped so be sure to work with your team mates to make sure you're as coordinated as possible!

Midnight - Summer vs Winter clan wars battle!

Time to see which team (Summer or Winter) has the best equipment and the best warriors! You'll have one hour in Clan Wars to win as many matches as possible in this war of seasons!

Midnight - Costume Parade!

Don't fancy the Clan Wars match but really got into the Costume competition? Well this parade is for you! Simply meet up in Edgeville in the gear you entered the competition with and we'll have a parade around Gielinor to show off your outfits!

1am - Victory Party

It's time for the winning team to party hard with Party Pete in the Falador Party Room! Join your fellow team mates in some victory dancing!

1.30am - Victory parade!

You've partied hard, but lets face it, what you really want now is to rub everyones face in your victory! So grab your mates and parade your winning team through the streets of Falador, Varrock, Lumbridge and Rimmington before finally making your way back to where it all began at Mudskipper point.

2am - Fiery finale!

It's time to bring the festival to a close so gather once more at Mudskipper point and set the place alight! if you've got a flagstaff you can even plant it in the ground for some extra fireworks!
