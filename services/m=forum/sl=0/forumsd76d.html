I have been recently thinking about ways that Jagex could implement to increase the trade limit cap whilst still keeping the RWT to a minimum. Here they are:

Suggestion 1

You could change it so that rather then have the trade limit based on quest points it could be based on your total skill level. And that for every 100 levels the trade limit increases by a rate(Say 2.5k) and that every 500 levels that rate increases(2.5k + 2.5k = 5k rate per 100 levels there after). For example:
� Total Level(200) = Trade Limit(10k)
� Total Level(300) = Trade Limit(12.5k)
� Total Level(400) = Trade Limit(15k)
� Total Level(500) = Trade Limit(20k)
� Total Level(600) = Trade Limit(25k)
� Total Level(700) = Trade Limit(30k)
� Total Level(800) = Trade Limit(40k)
� Total Level(900) = Trade Limit(50k)
� Total Level(1000) = Trade Limit(57.5k)
And the friends for X amount of months would also apply to further increase trade limit. This would only apply if it would be a higher trade limit then if it was based on Quest Points.

Suggestion 2

if it is possible, (and I as a programmer am pretty sure it is) why not simply add a variable to each account with the accounts total wealth. When that variable suddenly increases by say 100k in 5 min then the account becomes flagged so that Jagex can then investigate the account to see if it was RWT or just someone earning a lot of money. This way people can trade just as before the trade limit and still give money to new players and friends can give each other rune armour as presents and if they happen to give more then Jagex can look over the event and rule it RWT or a gift (Gift would be easily identifiable as the people would most likely be friends, and would probably had conversations which could probably be viewed).

Well thats all I had to say. Improvements to my suggestions are welcome and I will add your name as a reference if I use your suggestion to better one of my suggestions.
