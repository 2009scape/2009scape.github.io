-========================== Falador Castle agility course ==========================-============================================================================

Features:

- backpacked required for added weight
- increase of failing obstacles with more weight
- more weight in backpack gives more exp
- officer giving tasks, giving exp in other skills as well (tasks vary)
- introducing construction to create shortcuts on the course


Reasons:

- allows both high and low levels to use the course
- allows new abilities while reaching higher levels
- makes things less repetitive
- allows more entertainment while training

==========================================================================
================== Page 2 - Optional and more detailed Information ==================
==========================================================================


What Agility lacks in are as follows:
~ joining with other skills
~ new abilities when reaching higher levels (85+)
~ entertainment while training
~ increase or decrease in anything while training (such as a money bonus, or an entrance fee)


Some good things that Agility already has:
~ joining with quests
~ each level increases something (in this case, energy recovery)



More information about the agility course:
~ The course would be in a added basement in Falador Castle.
~ The agility course is cave/lava themed
~ The agility course would have a wooden door entrance in the cave, with a White Knight beside the door to take the entrance fees.
~ Once entered, pick up your weighed down backpack and talk to the White Knight officer to get your agility task.
~ The cave is filled with obstacles that must be taken to enter certain sections of the cave.
~ Could access a bank with the hard Falador achievement diary.
~ The cave would have to be at least 300 by 300 squares.
~ All tasks give extra exp in other skills or extra money
~ About 10 minutes each task
~ Lowest weight backpack gives 2k agility and a 500exp/1,000gp bonus depending on task (lvl 15)
~ Highest weight backpack gives 15k agility and a 3,750exp/7,500gp bonus, depending on task (lvl 90)
~ Constuction being introduced to allow quicker training (see next page)

Remeber, the cave is filled with obstacles that must be taken to enter certain sections of the cave!

============================================================================
===================== Page 3 - Optional and in depth Information =====================
============================================================================

~ With the highest weighed down backpack, it is impossible to never fail ANY obstacle, it is meant for level 90's and higher
~ This is a 2 level cave, so some obstacles may either take you up or down a level from completing or failing it
~ The fastest time being able to complete a task, therefore not failing any obstacles would take around 6 minutes. 4 minutes less then the ADVERAGE time to complete the course
~ Total of 35 tasks
~ Tasks are given at complete random
~ Because some tasks require many inventory slots, the obstacles give low damage so less food is needed

~ Construction will be used to allow quicker training. The roll of constuction is by either fixing or creating new obstacles which are used as shortcuts to get around the cave faster. This could add a very interesting element to the course.

~ In constucting, some obstacles may get harder then just tieing rope to a rock. On some higher level obstacles, you will have to make trips by going to both ends of the obstacle to finish constucting it. On even more complex obstacles, you will have to go to both ends of the obstacle multiple times for such obstacles like a small bridge.

~ To receive a task giving extra experience in a certain skill, that skill MUST be over level 5. If the skill is not over level 5, you cannot receive that skill's task. As an example: You're level 4 construction. Therefore, you can never receive the construction task untill level 5.
In addition, you will not receive full experience until level 40 in the specific task. The exp given are as follows:
Level 05 = full exp/15
Level 10 = full exp/12
Level 15 = full exp/9
Level 20 = full exp/7
Level 25 = full exp/5
Level 30 = full exp/3
Level 35 = full exp/2
Level 40 = full exp/1.5
