Love You and King Vexcia had been friends for three years on RuneScape prior to this, and had decided to make the Network as a last stand. They had the intention of bringing back the previous joys which they had previously experienced in Network, and planned to do it in style. The Empresa Network has one aim, to become a little haven of activity and entertainment. We plan to bring entertainment to all members of the Network, and through this plan to create businesses and ventures which bring entertainment to those outside the Network.
