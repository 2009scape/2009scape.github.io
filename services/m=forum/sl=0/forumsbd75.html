The Challenge Thread



The Challenge Thread



Official UK / Jagex / Forum Time is:


This thread is here for any J Mods, Champions, Battalions, Ministers or Elite Task Forces to create challenges in the ongoing struggle for influence.

All challenges will automatically be hidden until approved by a J Mod. J Mods will also confirm the amount of influence which will be awarded.

A challenge can quite literally be anything associated with RuneScape. Here are just some of the examples of challenges you could make:
  • A PvP Clan War
  • Join in the Off Topic Debate Thread
  • Have a machinima competition on the RuneScape YouTube Channel
  • A race around RuneScape
  • A stealing Creations match
  • A roleplay adventure
The options are nearly limitless!

Please Note: J Mods sadly cannot attend every single challenge (As much as we would really love to!). If you are proposing a challenge make sure you have a way of running it and recording the results without the need of J Mods.



Challenge Template


Challenge Title: What is the challenge called?
What is the challenge: A brief description of the challenge
When & Where: Date, Time & World
Challenger: Your Name, Rank & Organisation

Only those with a high enough rank should suggest challenges




Current Challenges


NEW! Triumvirate's Next Top Model!


We will all gather in the party room, the contestants on the southern squares, all 3 judges and the present head judge will be on the northern side. You must wear clothes which show what faction you're from. Once seated the Judges will choose 5 people from their faction that will head into the semi-finals. The Semi Finalists will all gather in the same clan, I will call forth names and they have 20 seconds to strut their stuff infront of our Judges. The Judges will then agree on 3 potential winners that will head into the finals. Once the finalists is chosen, it'll be up to the High Judge to choose the winner.

Time: 23rd July, 11:30pm Bst.
Challenger: Wand/Jonesdawiz
Reward: 2 Individual Influence for the Winner, 1 individual influence for the two runners up.

----------------

NEW! Individual: F2P Duel Arena Matchplay


Players register on a (yet to be created) forum thread for the tournament.

- Registered players given a ranking order according to F2P combat level. Ties split by fractional levels, then by alphabetic order of 3rd letter of player name.

- Players paired off to fight, with top ranked fighting lowest ranked, second top fighting second lowest etc. If there is an odd number, the middle ranked player gets a bye (which ensures no one gets >1 bye).

Each pair is given 24 hours to fight, with a neutral (not from either fighter's faction) observer required to confirm the result (especially to make sure both players made an effort to win, so no 126 lets a lvl 3 beat them to get the points, see below). Rules are set and non-negotiable, to save time organising fights (basically, everything allowed except forfeiting). Both fighters and observer report result on the thread. If volunteer observers cannot be found, fighters will be required to observe at least one other fight to continue in the competition.

When & Where: Sign up by 1am BST Friday July 3. Draw announced as soon as I can. 24 hours for each round, so time until finish depends on how many enter.

Challenger: Galahad_Sir

Rewards: 2 Individual Points

-----------------

NEW! Clan Challenge: Iron Defence


When & Where: Clan Wars. Date/time/world decided by the two warring clans.

What is the challenge: Each team has one person on their team that is more important than the others, dubbed the "iron man". This person MUST be wearing an iron full helm, iron platebody, and iron platelegs.

The goal of the fight is to kill the others teams "iron man" while keeping yours alive. The first team to lose their iron man loses.

Important details:

turrets map ONLY
Kill em' all
Safe(items kept on death)
Besides that, anything goes

Challenger: The Counterweights

Rewards: 2 Clan Influence

----------------

NEW! Clan Challenge: Battle of the Souls!


A clan based Soul Wars challenge!

Where & When: Sign ups begin 5th July - 11th July.

Challenger: The Agency (LTA)

Rewards: 1 Influence per victory, 3 Extra Influence for the Ultimate Winner.

-------------




SPECIAL! The Clan Cup 2010


Any triumvirate associated Clan that wins one of the three Clan Cups will earn 10 individual Influence (Instant Elite Task Force Status!) and an extra 200 Influence for their faction.

The Clan Cup is a massive Clan vs Clan competition which will show the clans who have what it takes to succeed over the others. You'll be up against every other clan in RuneScape so should you win it you deserve the massive amount of personal influence shown here!

In addition, you'll earn the title 'Clan Cup 2010 Winner!' For your faction in the Hall of Fame section of your home threads.
