1.4 *~* Starter�s Guide to Penguins *~*

* Speak to Larry or Chuck the polar bear (if you have completed Hunt for Red Raktuber quest) at the Ardougne zoo if you have never hunted before.

* They will give you a notebook that's only needed for checking current penguin points and count.
Also use this to check if the penguins have reset for a new week (it will say 0 penguins spotted).

* You can ask them for clues as for where the penguins will generally be. If you are missing one penguin, asking them for a clue will not necessarily tell you the missing one.

* You can hunt 10 penguins per week, for a total of 15 points.
In order to get all 15 points, you need to complete the Cold War quest.
Without the quest you'll only get 1 point per penguin, for a total of 10 points.
Harder to find penguins are worth 2 points and easier ones worth one.




*~* The Rewards *~*

* Return to Larry at the zoo to claim your reward or if you have completed Hunt for Red Raktuber see Chuck the polar bear in the southern cage of the zoo.

* You may choose between coins or experience in any skill.

* For coins - you will receive 6.5k gp for each point.

* For xp - you will receive 25 times the level of the skill you choose, per point. Level x 25 x points

* You're allowed to save up to a max of 50 points before turning them in.

* All points accumulated are exchanged at the same time so choose wisely.

1.5 *~* Guide to Polar Bears *~*

This addition to Penguin Hide and Seek allows us to spy on one polar bear per week as well as the penguins. The polar bears are here to help us stop the evil penguin plans.


*~* Requirements *~*

You must have completed the Cold War and Sea Slug quests as well have levels Thieving 38 Construction 45 and Hunter 45 to start the new addition to Hide and Seek.

You must speak to Larry at the Ardougne zoo to begin the quest, Hunt of Red Raktuber, and complete it in order to spy on polar bears.

After the quest and for ever after you will speak to Chuck, a polar bear who is in a cage in the southern part of the Ardougne zoo. He will now give you your rewards and any clues you need.

The polar bear will be in a well somewhere in RS and cannot leave it. Here are the possible locations:

* Rellekka
* Falador, at chain mail shop
* North Ardougne Farm
* Musa Point near the bar
* Rimmington
* Varrock east well, south of tea stall




*~* The Rewards *~*

* You will receive an additional point to use each week towards free xp or coin. This means there is now a max of 16 points available each week. The same conditions as for penguins apply.

* You have access to a new hunting ground where you can set deadfall and net traps and also track penguins.

* You get to tickle a polar bear.

1.8 *~* I Was Bumped/Kicked from Clan Chat! *~*

Here are the possible reasons why:


1. Random Bump:

When a ranked person enters any chat that is full (max capacity of clan chats is 100 people) someone will be randomly bumped to let them in.

If this was the reason, try to enter the chat again. People leave and join all the time.

Remember on busy days we also operate 3 additional chat channels that hunt together.

2. Accidental & List Shifts:

Sometimes mistakes do happen and a person may be accidentally kicked. This may occur when a person above or below your name on the cc list was being kicked but the list moved and your name is clicked instead. This is unfortunate and not intentional and we take every precaution to ensure this is rare.

3. Actual Kicks:

If you break any of the cc rules or Jagex rules then you can be kicked with no warning. Our rules are set with people's enjoyment of the game in mind. We reserve the right to remove anyone in order to maintain order in the cc.


The Bottom Line:

You were Random Bumped if you can re-enter chat within minutes.

You were kicked if you are blocked for an hour.

Note: Repeated entry of the chat name in quick succession may also block you from joining a cc for a short while.


We are not a Team who kicks for no reason. We are a Team that wants to maintain a fun environment for people to hunt penguins and meet other players. The ranks that have the power to kick have been chosen carefully and proven themselves.

1.10 *~* Clues & Resets *~*

A clue is not attached to a penguin but it is attached to an area that gets active when a penguin is there.

So a clue does not tell where a certain penguin is, it only tells that there is a penguin in that area.

The clue first below is for the respawn. The next are those of neighbouring areas that share a clue border. These are the roamers. Larry/Chuck may also give others based on where the penguin is at that time on any particular world.
