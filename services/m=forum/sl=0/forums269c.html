The rules:

Rule no. 1 - Respect all our employees and the work they make.
Rule no. 2 - Never get impatient waiting for a Logo. If there is a genuine issue with timing* of logo making, I will personally find out about this. (This is only in VERY extreme cases, and will probably never happen.)
Rule no. 3 - In your application, change the 'you' in "Did you read the rules?" to an I.
Rule no. 4 - No flaming, swearing or aggressive language.
Rule no. 5 - Do not criticise the thread. The thread will always win.
Rule no. 6 - Abide all jagex rules.
Rule no. 7 - Your clan will be judged whether it is worth it to make a logo for them. Chances are you will pass, however if not, do not get angry.
Rule no. 8 - To save thread space, you can choose up to 4 logos to put as examples. This will change in later threads.
Rule no. 9 - You must be the owner of the thread where your logo will be placed.
Rule no. 10 - In your application, in response to the question: "Did you read the rules?", type "Yes, I read them to the end."
