RANKING SYSTEM

=Recruits=
- Recruits are beginners in the clan, they are just beginning their great journey in this wonderful clan and are still learning the basics.
=Corporals=
- Corporals are members of the clan that have been in the clan for 3 days and have learned a bit about the clan.
=Sergeants=
- Sergeants are masters of the clan chat. They are always keeping a watchful eye out for new recruits entering the cc and keeping an eye out for rule breakers.
=Lieutenants=
- Lieutenants are either members of Senate or are in special positions in the clan. This rank is only given out for those 2 conditions
=Captains=
- Captains have become masters of the clan and are either high ranking officers or are the head of senate. This position is only given out for those 2 reasons.
=Generals=
- Generals are the leaders & co-leaders. This rank is only given out for special circumstances, if someone has the rank of General it is advised that you listen to them & do not argue with them.
