- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - Post 1.7 � Events - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
______________________________________________________________



�We have three event selection systems: predetermined, majority vote and impromptu



-Predetermined-

Every day (except Sunday) the first four events will be predetermined by the AC at the weekly AC Meeting. Look on the next post for these events. Events will start at the selected activities location (or it can start in the event leaders house).



-Majority Vote-

At Event 5 and Sunday events, the event leader will call for a few event suggestions in the Clan Chat (or it can start in the event leaders house). Then anyone who is going to the event will vote on them. The event that gets the most votes will be the one we do.



-Impromptu-

Anyone, at anytime (except scheduled event times), can start an event if 3 or more people go (including yourself).






-Event Guidelines-

Within reason, a certain event won't be more than once a day. Example- On Wednesday, we can't do Games Room more than once, but if there are not enough people at the later event to do the selected event, then you can do Games Room again.

The Leaders or Council Members have permission to kick anyone that argues or complains over the event choices or event that has been decided.

In order to get full credit, events must:
� Be at least 30 minutes long more is better.
� Have at least 3 people attending
� Anything less, within reason, will get half points

For all types of event systems, make sure that you or someone else posts an event report so those who came receive an Event Point.

We do all types of events.

Our home world is 99.




-Event Reporting Template-

Always use this to post who came to an event
List players full name and your full name
Also put a symbol next to your name so credit for posting can be given

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - Post 1.10 � Clan Chat Rules - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
______________________________________________________________

(These also apply to Events)

1. No spamming or drawings

2. Obey leaders
-If you have been kicked from the Clan Chat, when you come back do not complain or argue about it

3. Do not talk about reporting or accuse anyone of it

4. If you have any grievances with another player, private message them about it and sort it out
-That means don't whine about them in the Clan Chat

5. Do not try to intervene in arguments, it only makes things worse

6. If you try to bait someone into an argument, you will first be warned, if it happens again, you will be kicked

7. Policy on swearing for the Clan Chat and events: In order to keep chat beneficial, set a good influence for the greater community, and be a clan with high standards, we do not want anyone to swear. To not treat you childishly there are varying levels of punishment from only warnings to kicking. Regardless of our policy, swearing is filthy, immature, rude, and unnecessary.

Words that will only get a warning:
-Censored words
-Common uncensored words: damn, ass, hell, and etc.
-Telling people to shut up in a harsh or malicious way
-Abbreviations or acronyms with the F, S, B, or other extreme words in them


Words that will get a warning then a kicking:
-Anything from the first level maliciously directed at someone or used a lot after being given warnings
-Bypassing the censor


Anything that is really bad/offensive will receive an instant kicking
