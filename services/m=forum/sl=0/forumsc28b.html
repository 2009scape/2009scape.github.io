Clan Chat: A_H Chat

What could be better than a Clan Chat for the Account Help forum than a Clan Chat? Well, you thought it right! The same Account Help forum. But, lately, we have been thinking about it and it has been created - the first Account Help Clan Chat!

It all started with Kira Yamato's thread in Tech Support which was asking users to post if they were active enough in Tech Support forum. Then, I went ahead and asked Kira if I could be allowed to create a similar thread here to compile the names. Fortunately, it was a success.

Furthermore, they joined together and created a Tech Support Clan Chat. Again, deriving from their idea, I am creating an Account Help Clan Chat. I personally think we have enough people to keep it very active and to help those outside this forum as well.

In addition, when I created the thread after asking Kira, I was thinking of developing a Clan Chat. However, I thought that it would be better if we kept it here, given that J-Mods can read properly what we write and say if it's within the Rules.

Without further ado, let's get right into the deeper details of this Clan Chat!

____________________________________________________________________________

As always, rules are set to keep order and everyone safe. I am implementing these just to keep it as safe as possible and to make sure everyone included in the Clan Chat gets a good time and those needing help too. So, these are the things or actions that we don't wish to experience here!

� No unnecessary language. This includes flaming and offensive/inappropriate comments. Whenever you need help, we will make sure you receive your help as fast as possible.

� Spam is good but in can, not in our clan chat!

� Asking for ranks is not a wise question. It's earned by your actions.

� Respect is not given, it is earned. Let's keep it cool with everyone.

� This thread is for the Clan Chat, not for questions. If you have a question, feel free to create a new thread and someone will be there to assist you. If you don't find someone, you're free to poke us here and then we can ask you to create a thread so we can reply to it.

� Everyone is welcomed in the Clan Chat, not only active members, although only active members are and will be ranked to keep everything fair.

� When there aren't questions about Account Help, you are all free to discuss anything else. However, if someone arrives in need of help, we must do everything in our hands to assist them. Then, we can go back to our topics, if applicable.

____________________________________________________________________________

Ranked members will play a big role within this Clan Chat. Ranked members are and will be based on activity on both the Account Forum and the Clan Chat. The reason I am basing this on activity is to make sure we have lots of players available when others need us.

Obviously, ranks will be given on helpfulness as well, but the overall decision will be based upon how active you are on Account Help and the Clan Chat. This will also make sure you are known around. Activeness says all when it comes to helping. It also represents your personality as a helpfulness and active user within this community.

If you are known, helpful and active, you earn yourself a Captain Rank. Here's the breakdown:

� Gold - The most active and helpful players are given this rank.
� Silver - You are very active and an outstanding helper.
� Bronze - Your activity on the Account Help forum and Clan Chat is increasing dramatically and your helpfulness is awesome.
� Smiley - Everyone is welcomed. However, those that are added are because they frequent the thread and post. Also, these will be new users to both the Account Help forum and the Clan Chat.

As always, depending on your progress, you will be given a higher rank. Every ranked member but smiley will be able to kick. However, the guidelines on kicking will be provided further down since this is a big part of the Clan Chat.

____________________________________________________________________________

As I mentioned earlier, kicking plays a big role on this Clan Chat. Everyone knows that the kicking ability is there to make sure everyone within the Clan Chat has and enjoys an awesome environment and those ranked members will be in charged of this.

These guidelines should be followed in order to kick someone:

� Someone has an offensive/inappropriate/unacceptable username. This is an instant-kick.
� Spamming or another type of rule-breaking. Depending on the user, you're allowed to give a few warnings to the user. However, if they don't stop, you may kick them. Also, contact me so I can add the player to the Ignore List if they continue further.
� If someone is ruining the fun of others, kick.
� Basically, anyone who is breaking the RuneScape Rules.

It's nice to keep these short and simple but at the same time, it's cool to set some boundaries. I don't wish for anyone to get kicked, however, just because someone wishes to ruin the fun of others, it doesn't mean we have to allow you here.

Hopefully, nobody gets kicked and we all get to enjoy the fun of this Clan Chat!
