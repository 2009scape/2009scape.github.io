My thread is rather simple, all I would like is a larger friends list.

Im sure many of you reading this have 200 friends and in some cases more than that, like myself, have to keep deleteing people to talk to old friends private messageing or adding new friends.

Im not exactly sure how long I've been a member, I think its been around 4 years, but im sure friends lists have been limited to 200 people for much longer than that and I feel there needs to be a change.

Everyday now I have to go down my list, checking clans chats, to see who im going to keep added and who i need to delete to make space for a new friend im now finding my self deleteing really old good friends just to add someone whos on more often or that I need to add to do the job my clan requires of me. It really makes me feel bad but I find myself doing this everyday, but now something has changed... I just cant find anyone to delete my list is now full of people who are on almost everyday for as long as I am, and I always have around 50-80ppl on depending on the time of day.

I cant see the harm in letting paying members have a few more friend list spaces




Please, if you agree with me post it, even if its just "I Support"

Thank you,
