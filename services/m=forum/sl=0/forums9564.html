Hi!

As I'm sure most of you with an apple device have always wished they could play Runescape on their mobile device.

When you first bought it, you navigated to the Runescape website with excitement to play only to be met with a blue 'lego' block.

Well those times are over! Here I will teach you how to play Runescape on your device without ANY VNC program.

Sorry, but this only works for USA and Canada at the moment...


1) Go to the app store on either your iDevice or your computer.
2) Search for 'Cloud Browse' to find both a free and a paid app of that name.
3) If you read the discription, you will read that it is a firefox-like browser that allows both flash AND java!
4) Download and Install, and then open it.
5) Where you tap on the screen is where you click, you can move the mouse by tapping and holding.
6) Tap the URL bar and tap the keyboard icon at the bottom of the screen.
7) Slowly delete the whole default URL.
8) Navigate to the Runescape webpage.
9) Click 'play now' and Runescape will successfully load!
10) Login and enjoy playing RS on your iDevice! It isn't as laggy as you would think!


Well that draws to the conclusion of the guide, please post any ideas or even if it helped you!


I do not suggest you do anything dangerous (PvP and such) as there is a risk! Once again, I apologise that this is only in the USA and Canada app store, but I can't change that.

Thanks,
Epiqueness.


At the moment this is still in beta. After about 10-20 minutes, you will be sent back to the homepage. In the near future, you can expect this to NEVER happen and it will be released in more countries!
