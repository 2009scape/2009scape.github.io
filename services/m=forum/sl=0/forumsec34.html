�My "Rant"�

Although some people may consider this a minor annoyance, it is one of my biggest pet peeves. Can you guess it? Here, let me give you a ha... err, stub. That's right, if you haven't guessed it by now, one of my biggest pet peeves of RuneScape is the fact that we have all of these amazing graphics throughout the game but then when you look as close as your own character, you see stubs for hands.

The reason that this irks me so is because a while ago, although I'm not sure if you remember, everyone was getting upset with the tab interface changes because they looked more cartoon-like and made it look as if the game was aimed at children who were 10-years-old or under. Although we have received better-looking tab interface graphics, and we have been given AMAZING graphics (especially considering that this is a browser-based game), we still have the hands that you would see on an amateur doodle!

The solution is simple (or, at least, it's simple to know what I want the solution to be), give us normal hands!
