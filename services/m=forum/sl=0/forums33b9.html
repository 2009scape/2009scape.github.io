Current Ways to Find RSC

1. Search "Classic" or "RSC" on the homepage, then click link after link until you get there.

2. Bookmark or create a desktop icon for the world selection page.

3. Search "runescape classic" and then click the link.

4. Go to "game guide", then "manual", then "miscellaneous guides", then "runescape classic", then click the link at the top of the page.

5. Go to the announcement page and click the link located there.

6. Swiftkit has a link for Classic.
(NOTE: I do not in any anyway patronize the use of 3rd party software, nor do I use it. If your are compelled to employ it, please be careful.)

7. BRING BACK THE HOMEPAGE LINK

Do you have any other way that to access Classic? Please post it, along with your support if you wish, and I will add it to my list.

As you can see, most of these ways aren't really that convenient. So, sign this petition in order to get back our homepage link. We are yet again back to countless times of searching for classic deep in depths of RuneScape's website.
