Reavers - Individual Sign Up



The Reavers - Individual Sign Up

"Every act of creation is first of all an act of destruction." - Picasso




So, you've decided to join the Reavers? A wise decision, my young apprentice. As a member of the Reavers you've chosen to embrace chaos and mayhem, madness and devastation...and we also have cake...evil cake.

Just sign your name there, that's it...goooooood. Together we can crush the pitiful forces of the Lords and Judges and take our rightful place as rulers of the world!

Once you join you will add one point of influence to the Reavers and will be eligible to earn influence for the organisation in the future.

You really should start practicing your evil laugh now...You don't want to feel left out.

- Mod Crow -



What the Reavers represent


The Reavers represent chaos, change, intensity & action over temperance.

Their members consist of barbarians, mages, demagogues, rebels, tricksters & free spirits.



Things to think about once you join


Colour Scheme: If involved in a challenge or event where you wish to represent the Reavers we recommend choosing something in red and black. Spikes also help.

Posting: Let everyone know you're a Reaver by using the term 'Reaver' in your signature!

Attitude: By all means act as a Reaver but avoid doing so if it spoils someone elses enjoyment or thread.



This thread is for individuals to sign up to join the Reavers. If you want to sign your clan up then please use the Clan sign up thread
