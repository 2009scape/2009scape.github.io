� Clan Rules �
������������


Follow all rules

....General rules.

1. Follow all the Jagex Rules.
2. Do not use any language that could be considered offensive, racist, obscene or inappropriate. This includes swearing.
3. Do not advertise your clan channel in our clan channel.



....Star rules.

4. The hunters of a star decide if you can mine a star yes/no.
5. Stars that require clan help with one/more scoper and one/more spotters are closed for all people that did not hunt a star in that same wave.
6. The Finder of a solo hunt can decide wether he wants to open that star yes/no.



....Ranked members.

7. Do not ask for a rank. We will not give any ranks.
8. The person with the highest rank in the clan has the final disicion. Only a leader can get above that.
9. Ranked members should always talk with the person that did something wrong and explaim him/her what he/she did wrong.



....Other rules.

10. Do not discuss any off-topic talk when the stars are nearly down. Do not brake "wave chat".
11. If you break any of these rules, you will get warned or kicked. A rank will explain you what you did wrong.
12. Do not compare us with other clans. We are unique!
13. Free-to-play players have to follow these rules too, even if they cant mine stars.



....Application

14. When filling in the application form, change "Why do you want to join this clan?" to "What do you think this clan can offer to you?".


These rules are maintained by Costa Rider & Stizelswik.

A shooting star can have a total of 9 sizes. The number of sizes is totally random. Remember this: The lower the size, the more TOTAL xp you will gain. You will only gain 3k mining xp from the size 9 layer, while you can get 18k mining xp from the size 1 layer.

You will receive �star dust� when you are mining the shooting star, which can add up to 200. When the star is fully mined, a little alien will appear and he will reward you for releasing him. The amount of rewards depends on the amount of star dust.

With 200 star dust, you will get:
- 152 cosmic runes
- 52 astral runes
- 50002 coins

� Shooting star hunt �
�����������������
As you probably know, shooting stars fall every 2 hours.

To indicate how you can report a time to the clan, then use one of these forms:

a) W100, 19-28 mins, Kandarin
b) 19_28mns, W100 Kandarin


We start setting up the hunt 20 minutes before the actual star wave is down. Some worlds might be later, so we have a head world and side worlds.

When you like to have a spot, just say:

"I would like to take a spot on W100"
or
"I would like to take a spot on W100, but I cannot enter Burgh de Rott".


The Scoper then knows where to assign you.

...We activate wave chat, which means that most off-topic star chat should be calmed down. We normally don't activate star chat, only when we have more hunts and want rest
