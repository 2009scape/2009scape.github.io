Hi everyone and welcome to my thread

Have you ever run an event and found out at the last minute that it clashes with another, and that players either fail to turn up or simply disappear soon after the start of your event leaving you wondering what you did wrong? Well, chances are, you did everything right!

This thread is aimed at event organisers to use as a work in progress calendar/tool to view (not advertise) your events, and to ensure that your planning will be a lot more efficient, since so much time is spent behind the scenes (more than your average attendee realises!!)

You will hopefully be able to avoid clashes between your own event and that, let's say, of your friends' events.

I will be updating this thread regularly, I'll search through the forums to find dates and details, (The Events Workshop and submissions being my first port of call)in the hope that all organisers out there will be able to view and plan their events accordingly.

It would be very helpful, however, if organisers could post on this thread especially those who host much minor events so I can fill in the details on the calendar.

No matter how small your event may be, it will be beneficial to us all to find out when it is happening.

Thank you and keep up the great job you're all doing
