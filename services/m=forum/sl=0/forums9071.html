� Star Find�s Operations �

Star Find operates and runs in a very unique and intricate manner. To start out, before every wave of stars falls, Star Find organizes a list of every single members, non-Player v. Player world, along with the general area the star in those worlds is supposed to land in. This is done by a skilled team of one lister and several scopers.

After a List is compiled, it is then presented to the main chat channel for documentation purposes which will assist in further finding and keeping track of stars. Before stars initially fall, some players may want to scout the general area in which a star will fall in.

Once the stars fall, players will search for them, and then properly report them back to the chat channel. Afterwards, this process will then be repeated.
