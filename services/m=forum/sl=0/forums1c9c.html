------------------Slaying No Brainers--------------------------


I have been hearing a lot of people in cc complaining about being too poor to afford supplies to slay with. There are always some REALLY bad excuses behind them when you ask why though. Slaying is not rocket science guys!

You want money?

1. Do not use your bonus points to skip tasks! A very bad habit to get into. I put this down to simple laziness. 35 bonus points can be used to get you 250 deaths and 1000 mind runes. Every 35pts = 100k gp approximately.

2. Don't Slay beyond your bankroll!


a). Don't piety every task. Actually don't piety at all. Save your prayer pots for tasks you need to protect pray on. You should be getting enough ranarrs from drops to make your own comfortably. This goes for other herbs too. Make super sets instead of using piety.

b). Do not use a cannon unless you have some serious cash behind you first.

Yes these above methods speed up your tasks. But its no good going broke to get xp. You have to learn how to crawl before you can walk.

3. Much the same as above but I always laugh when people say they have no money only to find they wear Bandos or similar priced armour to slay in or have godswords. You don't NEED them! A whip, rune defender does the job and cheaper armour with better stats is available. Looking good does not make you slay better.

4. Spend "some" money to get some summoning levels so you can summon at least a bunyip. You don't need 99 summ/pray to slay.

5. Learn to DIY!!!


- My point is Slayer is a money making skill. There is no reason to come into cc and moan how poor you are. Yes, I understand times are tough, prices for some thing are outrageous. That means you have to be smart and do somethings the long way instead of buying the easy way out. Try doing other skills to help you slay. And most of all DO SOME QUESTS!!! It makes this game so much easier. You can either continue to make excuses or do something about it.

Happy Slaying

=-+-= Some Etiquette of slaying =-+-=

1) Crashing!

.a. Do not crash other people's slayer spots. Hopping worlds doesn't take that long and you can always check how much kills the person there has left by simply politely asking!

.b. If you get crashed do not respond to flaming. Be the mature person and ask this person to hop. If the person response is offensive, report her/him or add to the ignore list or hop yourself.

2) Cannons!

.a. Cannons are a great way to train slayer fast IF you have the cash to pay for for cannon balls. This is a fact.

.b. Have the respect to set up your cannon in a spot with an empty location preferably. Try not to disrupt other people's training spots.

3) Food!

.a. If you don't need food and you get some in a drop or if you are finished your task you can pass this on to fellow slayer who might need it.

.b. Runescape is not a charity organization. If you ask people for food and they do not wish to give you this respect their decision and do not flame them.

4) Dying!

.a. If you see someone die, repair or bless there grave. Put yourself in their shoes. If you died, wouldn't you want someone to do the same for you?

5) Knowledge !

.a. Last but not least. Share your wisdom with others. If you see a lower level player struggling, let them know nicely what he or she is doing wrong and what they could do to make it easier. Dont call them a noob and belittle them. Give good advice though, appropriate to a persons skill and combat level. Examples are the correct armour/weapons for certain creatures, slaying techniques, shortcuts etc etc. Be a good example and a upstanding citizen of Runescape.
