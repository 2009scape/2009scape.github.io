As many of you players with high Agility may know, the Agile Legs and Top are a great help...but, what if you had two more Agility worthy item's!

I have created an idea similar to how the Fire Ring and Gloves works with Firemaking!

!!!ATTENTION!!! (Looking for basic suggestions the players want to be an mini-activity integrated into my idea that the players propose.)

How would you go about obtaining such items and what are they you'd ask? ...This is where your Adventurous Agility Journey for experience begins!


To obtain an Agile Trinket that gives you an 2% Agility experience bonus...You would need to complete 250 perfect laps on the Advanced Ape Atoll Agility course. (Which will be described further on.)

Collect the bearers beloved Agile Trinket from a new Monkey NPC near Ape Atoll's Agility course. After you listen to their story about the trinket, how it helped them and what not...you'll receive the Agile Trinket. (Which is equipped in the shield slot.)

To obtain an Agile Cavalier hat that gives you an 3% Agility experience bonus...You will need to complete 150 perfect laps on both the Advanced Gnome and Advanced Barbarian Agility courses.

Speak to the Agile Legs giving gnome trainer to receive the Agile Cavalier hat. (You must have the Agile Trinket on you.) The gnome will notice your trinket, tell you all about that monkey and himself. He then gives you the Agile Cavalier.

(If you have already obtained the Agile Trinket, Top, AND Bottom, you may receive the Cavalier after only obtaining the Trinket.)

The Agile Trinket and Cavalier stack the experience bonuses the same as Fire Ring and Gloves do for Firemaking(Which is a fast skill in itself).

This will give you the total of an 5% Agility experience bonus.

The Trinket reduces your weight by -1 kg and the Cavalier by -2 kg. (There are plenty of "powerful" weight reducers as it is.)

(Speak to the Barbarian that gives you the Agile Top reward afterwards for a free 10k Agility experience.)

Now for the fun part about what this new Advanced Ape Atoll Agility course is...

The Advanced Ape Atoll Agility course is found just south of/after the skull hill climb on the regular course. Here is a short description...

You climb up vines leading to the Advanced Ape Atoll Agility area platform.

Climb a vine wall in a military monkey looking way, then jump off. Climb a skull wall using the skulls and jump off.

Perform an acrobatic leaping monkey front flip from the platform onto a bamboo branch, swing around once or twice and backflip onto another branch, then down to the ending vine crawl area.

(The Bamboo branch is supported by tangled vines wrapped around support beams on each side. With wound up vines connected to the platform.

70 Agility is required to enter this new Agility area. (or with Agility Potion/Summer Pie.)

You can fail the acrobatic obstacle occasionally.


Now to break down the XP values for you...

Vine Climb: 50 XP

Vine Wall Military Style Climb: 20 XP

Skull Wall Climb: 20 XP

Acrobatic Flips: 120 XP

For a total of: 210 XP

Normal Ape Atoll Agility is 580 XP per normal lap.

Without the Vine swing it is 480 XP per lap.

Total with complete new lap: 690 XP


I originally went through all of the options as to what the items would be and where you'd wear them. Gloves, no theres Penance gloves. Ring, maybe but people want to wear Duel ring. Amulet, people need to wear glory amulet.

Helm, just make it a hat. (So trinket in shield spot and a hat were the only options.) (If you can come up with other ideas where they'd go and what they'd be, please let me know.)


In addition to XP bonus, the new Agile items could also...

Agile Trinket: Slightly reduced failing of shortcuts/courses/obstacles?

Agile Cavalier: Reduce failing of shortcuts/courses/obstacles when combined with the Trinket?


(The new Agile items are not stackable with the horns from Barbarian Assault OR Brawler Gloves.)
