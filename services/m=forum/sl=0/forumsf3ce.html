Tech Posting Template

Hi everybody

We understand how frustrating it can be when you are unable to play your favourite game! In order to improve the level of support we can offer, we ask you to post as much of the following information as possible when alerting us of any technical problems you are having.

  • PC or Mac?
  • Processor?
  • System RAM?
  • Graphics card (make and model)?
  • Operating system (eg. Vista, XP, Linux, Leopard)?
  • Internet browser (Firefox, IE 7, Safari)?
  • Java version?
  • Description of problem?
  • When did the problem start?

Below is a list of some of the most popular graphics cards and how they compare to playing Runescape HD. If your card is able to run Runescape HD but it still does not work please ensure your drivers have been updated.

To find out what graphics card you have in your computer click the Windows/Start button and choose 'Run', type dxdiag into the box and press enter. At the top of the window that appears click display. Your graphics card make and model is written next to where it says name.
