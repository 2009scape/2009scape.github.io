� Welcome �

Now, first of, let's get the introductions sorted.
Sixth Realm is an all round community clan that welcomes both F2P and P2P alike. Our aim, being to encourage more F2P clan activity; we lookout for all sorts of individuals in our diverse community.

As a whole clan, we are keen on organising and taking part in events. In a way, you could say that we are also an events team - but all the same a clan as well.

The clan chat has been around since the dawn of time (well, at least since the release of the Clan Chat feature). As an official clan, we have been active for a good duration of time. We believe that the F2P clan community is just as good a place to start as any. We noticed that the non-member side of the clan community seems to be less active, and we would really like to boost this a little bit. Of course, that does not mean we are limited to F2P.

Anyway, feel free to join us on our events or take a look at our other threads. The clan chat is always open and feel free to give us a shout.

~^v~ The Events ~v^~

We like to think that we are more an all round type of clan rather than anything else. So, here's some of the events and activities we enjoy doing. These range from your general activities to some of our own player-invented games. Enjoy!

� Activities �

� Clan Wars - The all favourite battle of the clans.

� Fist of Guthix - Something that we enjoy doing every now and then to rack up some tokens or simply for a bit of pwning.

� The Duel Arena - Occasionally a duel couldn't do much harm. We hope to organise some kind of clan-themed tournament in the future if it's suitable.

... and a couple more that we are yet to try.

� Some of Our Own �

All rules will be explained in-game.

� Hide & Seek - An all round favourite. We tend to keep our games to safe all-accessible areas of the map, depending. You do non need any additional equipment (other than maybe some special issue ninja gear!).

� Scavenger Hunts - Usually with the aid of a plant marker and a handy set of clues. Try and tackle the devious treasure trail to find the plant marker waiting at the end and hopefully a prize too...

� Conga Lines - Conga time.

� Parties - Whenever there's an excuse to throw a party we will. We love to party.

� Fire Fests - You bring the logs (and preferably a tinderbox) and we bring the awesome element! Fancy burning down a city? Well, so do we.

~^v~ General Rules ~v^~

We don't want the events to be too chaotic, so just for everyone's enjoyment it would be great if you could bear these in mind when eventing with us.

� All RuneScape rules do apply.

� Please try not to be disruptive - spamming and advertising other threads/Clan Chats won't be the best of things.

� Try to be considerate of other players, and be aware that they are also trying to enjoy the event as well.

� Foul language is not needed.

� Remember, we are a clan, so it would be awesome if these rules were respected when on our clan areas!

� And most important of all - have fun.

~^v~ Time Conversion ~v^~

As RuneScape is such a massive game with all sorts of players from around the world, it might be useful to check all the time zone differences before organising an event.
In case you are wondering about a specific time zone, look below:

~ Key ~

GMT (Greenwich Mean Time) - Jagex/Forum official time during winter. Caters mainly western Europe (UK and Ireland etc.)

BST (British Summer Time) - Jagex/Forum official time during summer. British daylight saving time.

UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) - A standard international time often used that is equivalent to GMT, with minute differences.

EST (Eastern Standard Time) - Mainly used on the east coast of North America.

CST (Central Standard Time) - Often used in central parts of America.

MST (Mountain Standard Time) - Used in some areas of Canada, Mexico and the United States.

PST (Pacific Standard Time) - Used particularly in the states of California, Washington etc.

IST (Indian Standard Time) - Main time zone in India.
