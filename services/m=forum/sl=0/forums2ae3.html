More About Us

Team Aussie is an Australian based clan established by Admiral Neo (Neopet545) on August 10, 2008. The clan is currently the oldest Australian RSB clan and has no plans to cease. It is also known by its initials, TA.


Frequently Asked Questions


What types of events does Team Aussie organize?

We used to have a nice fancy list detailing what we did. However, it's simpler to say that we do everything a clan can do. From hunting Graador in the God Wars Dungeon to our very own Penguin Hunt Tour. Even if you have never heard of these events, we will encourage you to give it a chance.

There will always be a variety of events as the last day's event cannot repeat itself the next day.
-

When are the events hosted?

Generally when nothing important comes up (wars, meeting etc.), at least one event is held daily with the exception of Sunday. On Sundays, we encourage clan members to spend their time outside RuneScape or as most do, have some fun playing it. The time of the events are usually 6-8pm AEST (Sydney time).

We also have unofficial events held by our clan members throughout the day.
-

How are events picked?

Events are voted inside our clan chat right after every event. There are usually choices to pick from, the choice that gets the most votes is next day's event.
-

Do I have to attend the events?

No, they are totally optional (though encourage you to). Wars are an exception for this. You must attend wars if you are on Runescape at the time. We understand that some people might have real life commitments.
-

How can I find out about the website of the clan?

Please contact me in-game directly. Due to strict standards set by Jagex, only approved clan leaders can say the clan off-site forums.
-

Is Team Aussie's clan chat active?

Generally, it is. We have about 30 members on a typical peak hour. Maybe 5-15 at midnight. But most of the time, there's always someone to talk to.

The Official Clan Rules & Guidelines


General Clan Rules

1. Rules applied by Jagex on RuneScape are to be followed.

2. Join the clan chat whenever you can unless you absolutely need to join another. Unofficial clan events and trips are to be held in the clan chat.

3. Being in multiple clans at the same time is not allowed.

4. Do not request promotions, you will not get them.

5. Backstabbing clan members intentionally, or for fun is not allowed.

6. Always follow the leader's instructions and slowing/stalling the clan's effort is not allowed.

7. No flamming is allowed in any case. Includes people Team Aussie is warring against.

8. Assistance or any form of help (non-intentional included) to our enemies will result in a kick if procedures are not followed.


General Guidelines

1. Have some common sense and be mature, it's nice to have a clan chat without unnecessary questions/statements.

2. Respect and help clan members in need. They might be willing to help you too.

3. Bump at least 10 minutes after the last bump if possible to avoid spam.


Clan Warring Rules

1. You are to follow the leader/war specialist's instructions at all times.

2. You understand that if you do not follow instructions you may be temporarily kicked from the clan chat for the efficiency of the war effort.

Specific Event Rules

1. They will be mentioned at the start of the event.
