- many people post without reading, IT WOULD TAKE 28 SECONDS FOR THE RESOURCES TO BE DROPPED ONCE DROP ALL IS CLICKED! -

- An inventory can usually be dropped in less than 20 seconds, the reason it will take 28 seconds for an invenotry to be dropped via drop all shall be due to the convenience this option shall serve. -

- If a player wants they could use the normal drop option or the "drop all" option. - (Though if a player clicks on drop all and also starts dropping other resources manually via "drop" the drop all's effect shall be eliminated.


Many times when we are training our skills such as fishing and mining only for the experience, it gets really annoying to drop all the items by clicking drop again and again.

so why not have a command allowing us to drop all of a particular item.

(This would make the game a bit more realistic as well, e.g: When the truck that carries logs reaches its destination it throws all th logs on the ground right away rather than just drop them 1 by 1)
- Thank you Night Ivor, I got the above statement from your idea.

when we right click on an iron ore for example, we should get options:

use iron ore
drop iron ore
examine iron ore
drop "all" iron ore <- this should be added to the list!

This would prevent hand aches!

Some people think this would be unfair, so here's an idea to that problem.

The ores, logs, etc... would drop 1 by 1, a second per each. It would take a total of 28 seconds for all the items to be dropped. So it's not making it "faster to train", just preventing hand aches!


If you do not support and say "it'll make it too easy to train" I want you to give proof with your statement.

Also, bear in mind that RS works on system of ticks, 1 tick = 0.6 seconds, so it is impossible to have an inv dropped in 28 secs, but actually 28 secs is the average of the two possibilities JaGex could apply to this update. Drop an item/1 tick(16.8 secs for inv) or drop an item/2 ticks(33.6 secs for inv) ticks.
