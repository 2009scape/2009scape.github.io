Welcome!

This is the Official Runescape World Alliance thread.

RWA has been around multiple times over a 4 year period. We had two very successful editions and 2 that were not so good. But, that is what makes us great. We keep coming back stronger, better, and working harder to fix past mistakes. This time we have taken a new approach. Many successful ideas from our successful editions are being brought and used. We have also studied former and current unions to analyze which unions are lasting longer and why. Finally, we are adding new ideas to the table. This makes us a true hybrid union that brings things past and present together.

As a community union, we do not want to be the biggest or most powerful union, but rather be the most active. RWA is also the most united ever in RSB. If clans have a problem in RWA, we get together and solve it. We also have NO OFFICIAL TIMEZONE. That means events can be held at various times. It also means ANY clan anywhere in the WORLD can join, providing they can be active.

We will do our best to hold events at great times so everyone can go. We also want the majority of our events decided by the clans. To keep this communication intact, we do not allow more than 20 clans into RWA. Things may start off slow but we would like to have at least 1-2 events a week. This will allow clans to continue to have their own separate events. In the future we may update this to a 'Premiere Community Union' format by having a union event almost every day.

So come join the fun and close knit community. We not only offer the most, but give you the opportunity to get the most out of it.

- RWA Staff

--== Official RWA Requirements/Stages ==--

Even though we talk about accepting everyone because that is what unions are for, we still must have requirements to make sure not just any person(s) (inactive, traitors, etc) can easily get into our union. We do want everyone to be able to join but the TRUE members is what keeps our union running.

Clan/POC Requirements:
- Established at least 2 weeks prior to applying.
- Have at least 15 members prior to applying.
OR
- Clan having attended at least 2 RWA events (No establishment or member requirements)

Individual Requirements:
- Level 65+ Cmb
OR
- 85+ Range or Mage
OR
- 1000+ Total level
OR
- Individual having attended at least 2 RWA events.

RWA Amount Stages:
~ 1-10 clans (Low)
~ 11-14 clans (Medium)
~ 15-20 clans (Ideal)
:: No more than 20 clans will be allowed in RWA at any one time to minimize miscommunications and to be able to truly focus on the RWA clans. You can't do that with 30+ clans.

NOTE: Any staff members listed as RWA Staff may accept applications assuming they meet our union requirements, however, only leaders and co-leaders can deny applications. The Council can overrun the leaders decision with 3/4th vote.


~ Leaders: Responsible for the overall seeing and well being of the union. Their work is to help our in every task and to make sure all the staff members are doing their tasks on time and efficiently.

~ (Co) Leader: Has same powers as the leaders do except that the leader can override their decisions. They must also lead the union if the leader is absent over a 48 hour period.

~ Council Chairman: The Council Chairman is the highest representative of the clan body for RWA. The Chairman is voted into his position by RWA clans choosing and voting him/her there. The RWA leaders, vice leaders, and other staff not in any RWA clan will not take any part of deciding who is chosen for this position.

~ Council Members: Clan representatives.

~ Union Advisor: Experienced union vet who helps offer feedback or suggestions on actions taken or actions that need to be taken from a neutral perspective.

~ Union Ambassadors: These are the union's representatives to the clan. They will need to stay updated on their clans doings forum wise, activity wise, & the clans stability. This position will help us ensure there is the best communication clan to union and union to clan.

Union Secretary: Personal secretary to the leaders and Council Chairman of RWA. They help complete tasks that are asked of them. They also are to bring information/data for plans or ideas RWA is working on. They help keep the unions daily operations running on time.

���������������������������

1: Respect the union, its members, and its clans. Failure to abide, or if retaliation occurs, then the participants will be punished by the Leader(s) of RWA.

2. No backstabbing. Every organization has agreements or information that we do not wish to be leaked. If any member of RWA is found to be leaking ideas from a RWA meeting, then they will be removed from the union. Backstabbing can also be classified as any person(s) who go out in public to try and degrade RWA.

3. Be as active as you can be. This means both posting on the RWA forums and attending the union events/meetings. If you will be absent, please inform us. Also clans will be hurt by their own inactivity.

4. There will only be trials if the person is alleged to have done something not against an RWA rule. Otherwise, it is up to the RWA leaders what punishments are unless intervened by a 3/4th majority of the union clans.

5. Votes must be cast during the time given. Votes can�t be changed once a vote is cast. Any posts that appear as edited with a vote will be deemed void. People have within 5 minutes of correcting their edited post by REPOSTING the vote.

6. All staff members are to do the tasks specified in the staff descriptions unless told otherwise by RWA leaders.

7. We ALLOW multi-unioning as long as you remain active in both and respect both sides. Unions are here for the universal gathering of people�not limiting them to being only in one union.

8. Each clan must supply 1+ representative(s) to stay in contact with the union (meetings, forums). The union must supply staff members to stay in contact with every clan.

----------------------
Neutrality Policy
----------------------

The Neutrality Policy addresses the issues of being a union that allows multi-unioning and the issues of clans involvement in wars.

The policy protects the clan's, POC's, and individual's decision whether they wish to attend any wars involving the union. This policy prevents them from being able to be punished for not attending. However, this does not affect whether the union can punish them for other issues stemming from a war (i.e. instigating, flaming, etc).

We are a neutral union meaning we will try our best to only do fun wars. However, this is impossible to ensure so there always is the chance of a real war with another union. If a clan, POC, or individual is in RWA and the other union are warring, then they (the clan, POC, individual) may choose to be neutral and not pick a side or they can choose the side they wish to fight on. The Neutral Policy protects them if they wish to stay neutral and not get involved. But, the union is still allowed to punish (But it doesnt meant we will) those who pick to fight for the other side.
