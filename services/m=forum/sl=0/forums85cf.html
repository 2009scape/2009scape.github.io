Main Thread #11

Hey all and welcome to KDV's main forum This is the "hub." Read it for updates and tips from the leaders and members of the Knights of the Dark Void.

We are an 90+ Runescape clan with our roots in Pest Control. However, over time we have expanded our interests, and we enjoy all that Runescape has to offer.

We have four divisions:
1. Pest Control Division -- Knights who have mastered PC
2. Slayer Division -- Knights who have mastered killing dangerous monsters
3. Adventurers Division -- Knights who have mastered questing, skilling, or pvp

We are governed by a team of 8 Dark Voids, and we run a respectful clan chat.

To join, read the first two pages of this forum, then go to the top of page two and copy the application, hit reply, paste in the application, answer the questions, and submit it.

<:*<:*Clan Requirements to Join*:>*:> ~
_________________________________________________________________________

We are a Runescape clan that plays PC excellently, kills bosses efficiently, takes on other clans in fun wars, and has fun together as a clan with all that Runescape offers.

However, since our clan foundations are rooted in Pest Control, we seek players with notable experience in Pest Control and excellent teamwork abilities. Besides an in-game demonstration, the only thing that serves as proof of PC experience is void armor.


You are able to join KDV if:

** You are 90+ combat. Or with a combat stat 85+.
** Total level of 1200. Or with full void(helm gloves top and bottom).
** You are respectful to others.
** You are a team player.

If you meet the clan requirements, apply using the application at the top of page 2 of this forum. Take care to read and agree with the requirements and clan rules to be accepted as a Knight of KDV. You will also need to read the first two pages of the forum in order to answer all the questions correctly.

If you do not yet meet clan requirements but are working to join us, or if you would like to join us to see if we are a good fit for you, you may be able to attend some clan events. Ask one of the clan leaders if an event is scheduled that you would like to attend.
