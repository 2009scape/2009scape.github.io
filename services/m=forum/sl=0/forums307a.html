And then those you don't.

Your risked wealth.

It says you keep your ( for example) ..

- Bandos Godsword ^Good

*So what are the Cons?*


A. Commonly, less risk. Especially in terms of PVP Bh+1 worlds, in which Chaotic PK'ers and others would have a safer passage to victory. (Chaotic weapons have low alch value)

B. Player feedback. Jagex has made many terrible updates: Climbing boots, to be one of them. Sure this update might help most of RS, but it'll also cause lots more ruckus that ATM Runescape does not need.

C. Huge change in economy: Do you know why the Barrows helms cost so much more than the other pieces? Not because of it's rarity from the chests, but because it's the piece lost on death. Now, it'll be easy to keep it on death, causing huge economy changes.
-Also, pkers can effectively use Dharoks and only risk 1 mill in BH+1, meaning players quit and bad player feedback.


(Thanks to Gameboylight (Clan Leader) - Page 10, Post 2 for pointing out these problems/CONS to me )
