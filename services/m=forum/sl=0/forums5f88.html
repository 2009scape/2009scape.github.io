�-----------��� About me ���-----------�
Hello!
My name is, as I'm sure you guessed, Will Miss It.
I started in Runescape in 2004 when our beloved santa still was giving yo-yo's and no one knew what Soul Wars ever was.
My username back when was Terry39. I have no idea why I was calling me this. I guessed I liked the name...

Anyway, I'm from Norway and I love to play Runescape. I discovered it when I saw my brother play the game, and I wanted to try it.

About 26rd of April I created this user. After many tries and alot of failure finding the best name in Runescape history, I ended up with Will Miss It (Maybe not the best name ever?). I liked the name, and did the tutorial with it.
I started out getting every non-member skill to 30, since I had to wait until 1st of May until I could become a member. When I first became member, I started training my Agility, and some other few skills, just for the fun of it. Then, 27th of May the Castle Wars update came. So did the Halos of the Gods and the Faithful Shield. I started playing the game, to obtain 500 tickets and get Faithful Shield and a Saradomin Halo, who would match my 'future' untrimmed Agility cape, as I never got. Then the hidden update of the Faithful Shield came. It got a requirement of 40 Defence. Because of this, I just tried to get the halo.
Later, I heard that a fourth skiller had achieved 99 Slayer as combat 3. His name was Neon Slay. I watched a video of him getting 99 Slayer, and I suddenly fell in love with the Slayer cape. Of some reason, Agility just felt awfully boring. I wanted 99 Slayer.

I created a Skill Log on Salmoneus' forum and started my long-term goal on 99 Slayer as combat 3, getting the untrimmed cape.

And now we're here. as I am writing this, I got 997 zeals. 3 minutes before the match ends, and the counter will say '1000'. This is 63 Slayer for me.

17. July 2010 (13.07 GMT)
I got my Skull Banner yeasterday, as I've been working on the last two days.

First when I saw it, I had to get the skull. So I got some help from by friends and got it in the Abyss. Then I talked to Sir Renitee, who said I needed 16 Construction...
So after 16 Construction I went back to the Abyss, got skull, and back to Sir Renitee. I got my family crest. Now I had to make the banner.
So I used a friends Banner Easel, and that one said I needed 48 Crafting!
So I had to do Easy Varrock to use the furnance in Edgeville. One of the tasks were to have 50 Kudos. That required that I had to do the Dig Site quest.

After finishing Dig Site (and getting 31 Slayer in museum, ARGH!!!) I finally got 50 kudos and made almost 2K Gold Amulets in Edgeville.

I got level 48, made the banner and went back to Soul Wars...
