Information on thread and Assist System:

Reasons for enlisting as a volunteer:

- You can get free xp from using the Assist System

Assist System:

For an article on the Assist System and with Skill restrictions outlined, look at: Game Guide > Manual > Controls > Controls - The Assist System.

Skill restrictions:

The skills that you can be assisted in are Construction, Cooking, Crafting, Farming, Fletching, Herblore, Magic, Runecrafting and Smithing.

The skills you can�t be assisted in are Agility, Firemaking, Fishing, Hunter, Mining, Summoning, Thieving and Woodcutting.

Also skills that involve quest, achievement diaries and non tradable items can�t be assisted in.
Please note this isn�t a complete list of restrictions of items and features of skills that can't use the Assist System.

Boosts:

Effects of any temporary boost from the player giving the assistance, will be passed to the player needing assistance.

Teleportation:

We can help through using teleother spells in the Modern spellbook and telegroup spells in the Lunar spellbook. Also through the POH using portals in the portal room. In addition, exchanging uncharged for charged swaps for jewellery. Also exchanging the unenchanted for enchanted version of the jewellery if your on another spellbook and can't access the Modern spellbook.

Places the volunteer can go to may be limited because they haven�t done the relevant quests, achievement diaries or obtained items. We will use our discretion on teleportation, for teleports and exchanging jewellery.

Magic spells offered in thread:

- Volunteer uses their runes

For an article for magic spells offered and restrictions outlined, look at: Game Guide > Manual > Controls > Controls - The Assist System.

Morden spellbook:
Bones to Peaches if volunteer has unlocked spell from Mage Training Arena

Lunar spellbook:
We can offer tele group spells if required

Ancient spellbook:
We can't assist in, as none of the spells can be used.

Information on Ancient Effigies and Guides:

Ancient Effigies information:

The skills that you can be assisted in are Construction, Cooking, Crafting, Farming, Fletching, Herblore, Runecrafting and Smithing.

It uses the Assist System and you can�t be assisted in Magic using it.

- Starved ancient effigy - requires 91 in skill
- Nourished ancient effigy - requires 93 in skill
- Sated ancient effigy - requires 95 in skill
- Gorged ancient effigy - requires 97 in a skill

The skills you can�t be assisted in are Agility, Firemaking, Fishing, Hunter, Magic Mining, Summoning, Thieving and Woodcutting.

Ancient Effigies:

For an article on the D&D: Ancient Effigies, look at: Game Guide > Distractions and Diversions > Ancient Effigy.

Guidelines for requesting help:

- Respect the volunteer and be patient
- No payment required for volunteer
- After you�ve obtained your help, please edit your post and let us know you've obtained it

Guidelines for volunteers:

- Respect the player you�re helping and be patient
- No payment to be taken for help

Assisting guide:

- Meet volunteer as arranged
- Right click the volunteer and click on the option �Req Assistance�
- Then you can make the item or use the feature of the skill

Teleportation guide:

- Meet volunteer as arranged, and you may get to location through a teleportation spell or through their POH, by using the portals
- If it is a a teleother - Modern spellboook, or telegroup spell - Lunar spellbook, ensure you have accept aid on

Accept aid:

To turn accept aid on, go to your Control Panel, then Options, and also toggle Accept Aid to be on. So it says "Accept aid (currently on)" when you hover your mouse over the button or that the button has a red background when clicked.

Location guide:

- Meet volunteer as arranged
- Use teleportation methods to reach location

FAQ:

How does Assist and Teleport Volunteers work?

Volunteers offer advice first and we can assist you if required.

How does the Assist System work?

For an article on the Assist System and with Skill restrictions outlined, look at: Game Guide > Manual > Controls > Controls - The Assist System.

How does the Assist System for Farming work?

When the crop has grown, and you have used a volunteer to assist when harvesting the crop, you won't get any farming experience. But you can check the health of a tree without having the required farming level and still receive the experience for it.

What restrictions apply to Farming?

- Saplings can�t be assisted in, since the pots are untradeable
- Bushes and cactus can�t be assisted in.

How do we offer locations help?

We offer advice on getting to the location first and if needed we can help you and give teleports using teleother - Modern spellboook or tele group - Lunar spellbook if required.

What restrictions apply to Smithing?

You can�t be assisted in adding armour in your Skills Hall
