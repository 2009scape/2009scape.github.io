Possible Names: Crystalline Deity, Tower of Voices, Corridor of Light, Dark Lord Rising, Lleuad the Dark Lord, or Edrych Ar C**godol, Facing the Shadows

Brief Description: It has been quite a while since you've last heard from Thorgel or Arianwyn. But now it has come time for the Cadarn Clan, with the help from a few more, to seek entrance to the under-city and prevent the devious Iorwerth from resuming his hold within. And thanks to one foolish mistake, you end up in a battle fit for the Gods...

Requirements:
Must have figured out the secrets behind the 2nd Elemental Workshop
Have seen what's Within the Light
Have completed the Curse of Arrav
Have completed Garden of Tranquility
Have completed While Guthix Sleeps (optional: depends on if Jagex wants to have intense requirements)
Level 60 hunter
Level 77 Slayer
Level 65 Thieving
Level 50 Summoning
Level 70 Herblore
Level 70 crafting
Level 68 or greater Farming and Smithing
At least Level 100 in combat
Able to defeat four judgmental beasts consecutively
Having enough luck to defeat a Mahjarrat Lord
Must have 300 Quest Points at least. (or current maximum qp available if you want)

Extra Requirements (for the Letters to Seren):
Completed the Love Story quest


Difficulty: Grandmaster

See 241-242-729-61307810 for a thread on Prifddinas in general.

To Start: Talk to Arianwyn in Lletya.

Step 1: City of Danynt

I don't like introduction dialogue. But nonetheless, Arianwyn takes you to the entrance of a new place. What used to be known as the under-city of Prifddinas is really known as Danynt. At least it has a name. It's basically a large, rectangular room. Yea.

In the center it's kind of octagon shaped, but still the same room. Next to each vertex of the octagon is a statue made out of nothing but crystal. Two of the statues are of Lord Iorwerth and King Baxtorian, with the remaining six as other elves. You can probably guess that they are the 8 Elven Clan Leaders. In the center of the statues is a crystal spiral stairway. On the west and east faces of the Octagon are doors leading into other room. At the south end of the main room is another, larger door in the shape of an octagon. This larger door has 8 crystal seals around its inner vertexes and one in the center, all illuminated except for the top left one which has a crack through it.

You will find Arianwyn in the room center. You will talk to him. Arianwyn will tell you how Prifddinas is directly above you, but not to go just yet. You need a disguise. He will give you 3 chrisial seeds.

The focus is on the west and east face rooms. The room to the east is sealed behind a crystal door, regular sized. The room to the west has no door. Let's go that way.

Note: Make sure you have a charged Teleport Crystal for Lletya, teleports to various areas, and coins to charge the crystal again.

You head into the west room. In it are 3 Chrisial Patches, which each require a single chrisial seed and 68 farming (to be listed in the Special tab for farming). Teclyn will be nearby for you to exchange tools. The tree you plant will not be affected by disease. Although, when you attempt to water it you find yourself unable to. Ask Teclyn and he will say to find a grand farmer.

Step 4: Recruiting Army Part B

The last recruitment is King Awowogei, Monkey King. Even though he seems to only be joking about helping you, actually getting this for him makes him go crazy for the idea of helping you. This section is going to take much traversing across Runescape. When talking to Awowogei, he wants a basket of the most delicious fruits in the lands. You will first need a special fruit basket. In order to do this you need to start with special compost. Use a hammer on a Gold Bar to smash it, and use the remains with Pestle and Mortar to grind it. Fill a compost bin with weeds and use the Ground Gold with it before closing it. Use the resulting compost on a patch for Poison Ivy. While it grows, you will need a potion from the Apothecary to make the Poison Ivy harmless.

He will tell you the procedure, which is as follows. Make or buy a Super Anti Poison (3 or 4). Add this to a Super Restore Potion of the same dosage. Let the resultant mixture ferment for about a minute until you get a message telling you that it�s ready. The Apothecary will give you a final �secret ingredient� (for the small price of 5k) that will finish the potion: Ivy Cure (3). Use the Ivy Cure on your Poison Ivy when it�s finished growing. Step 5: Judgment

Once these are complete, use the God Grapes upon the basket to get the finished product. Take it to that monkey guy. He will join your cause, and you will (yet again) report to Arianwyn. Warning: The next time you go into Prifddinas you will be sent into fierce battle. You will see various forces within the Tower of Voices fighting Elf Warriors, while three warriors (lvl108) are guarding the stairs. KILL THEM!

Once the three Elf Warriors are slain, proceed up the stairs. This is where you must defeat the four beasts consecutively. Each room looks exactly the same. Once you enter one, the stairs disappear and the exit stairs appear once you defeat a beast. Each room has a picture of a flaming horse (think ghost Rider here, except better) on one wall. Each beast is a humanoid looking creature.

The first beast is named Bla. He is a master of Ranged attacks with a bow and arrow. He will always be 10 levels above the player. Immune to poison.

The second beast is named Rhyfela. He is a master of the Sword. He will always be 15-20 levels above the player. Immune to stat change and hold spells.

The third beast is named Newyn. He is the master of mixed combat and will use Ranged, Melee, and Magic (not much magic though). He will always be 20-25 levels above the player. Immune to stat change, hold spells, and poisons.

The last beast is named Addoed. He is a master of magic and will attack with a distribution of surge spells. He will always be 30 levels above the player and will use prayers for magic or melee, as he needs not ranged since ranged attacks won*t effect him much.

After Quest Notes
The names of the elves first seen in Prifddinas translate to Slave, except for Fforia whose name means Spy in Welsh.

The name of the priest, Choel, is translated from Welsh to mean Faith.

Like most names in this, Danynt is derived from the Welsh word for Beneath.

Llyfr Meistr is, of course, Welsh. It translates to "Book Master", which could work for a future Legends' Guild quest as well. And it Rhymes!

It was already believed that the Wise Old Man had an idea of how to get into Prifddinas. Looks like he tried the wrong door.

Even though this seems to be the last quest, more quests still can be found from this. For example, why is Fforia named Spy? What�s beyond the north gate?

After the quest is completely finished, Prifddinas will flourish with elves. The eight outer towers will have access, along with many stores and a furnace able to work with crystal. Maybe a new quest start?

One of the new elves in Prifddinas, Arfod, will sell the player Seren Armour after the quest (or maybe another subquest afterward) seen at *thread was eaten by page 51 monster*

The White Dragon Cave portal listed in the rewards is only open for use some of the time. It leads to the Distractions and Diversions of White Dragons. For moar detail, go to 241-242-736-61256431.

New! June 24th: The Judgemental Beasts can be fought once a day for their drops after the part in the quest where you slay them. They each have a hidden chamber within the Stronghold of Security.
Rhyfela can be found on the first floor.
Newyn can be found on the second floor.
Bla can be found on the third floor.
Addoed can be found on the fourth floor.

Chrisial Items:
Are available to be made after the quest. Each item needs the same amount of Chrisial Bars to make as the other versions of it.
