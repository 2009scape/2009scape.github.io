~Information~
><<|><><|>><

Elven Industries started out less than a week ago (on 7/12/2010) with the name of the Elven Company, anyway the cc "Elven Comp" for Elven Company was hacked and the higher ranks decided to create a new "company" by the name of Elven Industries.

The reason Elven Company and Elven Industries was created is for better customer service. Original most of its leaders were in a different company that just wasn't seeming that it was going to work. It was a good company that had a good foundation but no good workers to uphold the rules and be kind. A few of the members of that company got together and had the idea of interviewing workers and Elven Company was born.

The staff here at Elven Industries is finely selected through interviewing. Interviewing in game is a very good way to make sure that all of your workers are friendly to their customers. From our Woodcutting and Fletching branch all the way to our Mining and Smithing branch I can personally guarantee the best service an Elve can offer.

Elven Industries is a sort of 'non profit' company but that just really means that we don't collect gp for orders that a worker completes. I like to think of Elven Industries as a sort of "agency" that sets up its workers with customers and orders.

-J. R. R. Tolkien-

In J. R. R. Tolkien's legendarium, Elves are one of the races that inhabit the lands of Arda. They appear in The Hobbit and in The Lord of the Rings, but their complex history is described more fully in The Silmarillion, edited and published after Tolkien's death. More details about them are given in the author's other writings edited and published since then, such as Unfinished Tales and The History of Middle-earth. The History of Middle-earth also reveals their textual and conceptual history, as Tolkien had been writing about Elves long before The Hobbit was published.

-Hylian-

Link is described as a young Hylian boy from the land of Hyrule. His age varies depending on the game; incarnations usually vary from pre-teen to the late teens. Link often journeys through the realm of Hyrule defeating evil forces and Hyrule's nemesis, Ganon. To defeat Ganon, Link usually requires the mystical Master Sword and Light Arrows, or some other similar legendary weapon, obtained after many trials and battles gathering magical objects or using other items such as musical instruments and assorted weaponry.
