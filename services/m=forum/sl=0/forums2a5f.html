There is a lot of good looking items, which can look ugly with the right combination, and a lot of ugly items, which can look pretty cool with the right combination.

--------------------

UGLY (my opinion!!)

Infinity Boots on guys... well, the boots look really girly to me. But I guess it's alright as long as they wear an Ahrim's or Infinity Robeskirt with it.

Granite Platebody is mega ugly when it's worn with Rune or Dragon Plateskirt/Legs...

Dragon Medium Helmet's gotta be one of the ugliest helms in the game, although it looks alright with the Dragon Platebody in my opinion.

Dragon Platebody on girls is just... NO. Makes them look like a guy.

Dragon Boots look ugly, kinda like clogs with spikes, unless they are worn with Dragon Platelegs or Skirt.

Dragon Claws look plain weird and ugly to me.

Dragon Two-Handed Sword looks weird... the old version was way better.

Bandos Tassets look kinda ugly and weird on guys in my opinion, they only look alright with the Bandos Chestplate. They look good on girls, though.

Skillcapes... I dislike the shoulderpad thingies on 'em, unless they really nicely match the colours and style of the rest of the outfit.

--------------------

COOL (my opinion!!)

White Partyhat, just cause it is rare, unique and matches with a lot of stuff.^^

Spiked Helmet looks so awesome with Bandos armour.

Dwarven Helmet looks awesome with 3rd Age Ranged armour.

Seers Headband 1 looks great with a lot of things, like Barrows, Bandos and 3rd Age Ranged armour.

Dragonfire Shield... just looks badass.8) Unfortunately the colour of the shield doesn't really match a lot.

Marshal clothing, just looks so mighty and it's very rare.

Vesta's Longsword looks so shiny!!

Saradomin Sword looks even shinier, kinda like silver.^^

Armadyl Top and Skirt looks cute on girl characters.

Dragon Chainbody... old skool and it still rocks.

Verac's Flail loooks badass.8)

Saradomin Cloak... my favourite cape in the game.^^

Arcane Spirit Shield looks awesome!
