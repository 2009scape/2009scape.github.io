*A Nukes Thread*

Hello,

As I am a Construction Choob I think we should keep updating
this skill and I have a few suggestions to make updating
easier.

Firstly, I would like to say that some of these ideas are from other players, but most of them are from me, so if I mention your idea, hopefully it will become created.


Suggestions - New Rooms (Dungeon)


Surgery room - You can be healed 5x faster (90 ***situation,
someone will have the power to heal, right click > Heal player) In here there will be a box in the corner, with
bandages in, there will also be explosive potions to lower
Health.


Machinery - Catapults golore! A catapult, but you need
the parts, you can get them by killing other players, and will need a Crafting level of 30. In here they're will be cannonballs all over the floor for players to pick up.
(You will need a hammer and saw to make the catapult)

Spikes, Barricades, Bones, will be found in a box next to the north wall, inside a crate/box, 3 seperate boxes.


Barricades/Traps -

(All of these can be found in the Machinery part of the house)

Droppabe Spikes, (Stacked) damage of 20 LifePoints per one you run into.

Bones - Who needs to bury? When you can go for a trip! These make the player fall over and be frozen for 3 Seconds.

Barricades - Last for 10 seconds, and can be destroyed only by melee, LifePoints - 100.



Suggestions - New Rooms (Ground floor)


Forest -

Contains: (Trees)

Normal
Oak
Willow
Maple
Yew
Magic

They will give no experience but can be used in a Players fireplace or to liven the house up a bit, or for game purposes.


Hunting Area -


Graahks!
Kyats!
Chinchompas!

Players will hunt these for fun, no experience but players can use these for helping in the dungeon, fighting etc.

Summoning level for Graahk - 57
Summoning level for Kyats - 57
Hunting level for Chinchompas - 64

(Use a Kyat/Graahk fur on a players obelisk for the Summoning creature, only if they have one in their menagerie

Battle Arena - Battles? Gore? Blood? This is the place for you!

1vs1 intense battles! Just like the Rammernaut bosses arena
they're will be battles just 1 on 1, this prevents pile jumpers and other things that annoy players.

(You must invite another player, Right click - Invite to battle > wait)

You will have to wait your turn though!

Construction Levels -

Surgery - Level 45 Construction

Machinery - 55 Construction

Traps/Barricades - 55 Construction

Forrest - 45 Construction

Summoning Area - 67 Construction

Battle Arena - 70 Construction


All of these areas are for Dungeon/Ground floor and have been fairly put into the right level order for how good
they are.

These are my ideas so far and I will improve on them, I hope
you will all support this.
