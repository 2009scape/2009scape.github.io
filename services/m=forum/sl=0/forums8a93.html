Valjas sped across the rooftops, leaping over alleyways, and climbing over structures with uncanny speed, he smirked as the guards chasing him were halted by a giant gap in buildings he had just managed to leap. Valjas was the newest assasin to join the order, and he had just gotten his first kill, a witch doctor who was poisoning people he was supposed to save because an unidentified man was paying him more than the people who needed healing. Sometimes Valjas just wanted to kill every one of those fat royals who wouldn't share their food with anyone else while those people who follow him starve and scrabble about for gold and food in the streets. He wanted that to change. A new group of guards had begun pursuit, he turned around and whipped 6 throwing knives at the guards, one embedding itself in each of their throats, and just like that, his job was done. He got his knives back and took a leisurely pace back to the order for dinner and a night's rest.

You can play as a guard, politician, or an assasin. And the first politician, and the first guard to be accepted by me can be a person of power in the rpg and in the game itself, such as the pope etc. Any ideas for weapons in which a player makes up but wants for their character must be passed by me or a person of power.
