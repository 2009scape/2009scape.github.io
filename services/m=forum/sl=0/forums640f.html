======== UPGRADED SETUP TO OVERLOADS ======================


Hi guys! Going to be killing a lot of Tormented Demons this summer with my new CLS!

The goal of this experiment is to use 100% of my CLS charges at TDS and see how many claw drops I get!

@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@

The experiment is done! Although, I admit, I didn't use ALL of it at TDs, I'd have to say at least 93% of it was at TDs. The drops can be seen in the post(s) below. I may just start anew with another CLS though .

@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@

Method 1 (Steel titan):

GEAR:

Helmet: Neitiznot
Amulet: Glory/fury
Body: Black Dragonhide
Legs: Black Dragonhide
Boots: Dragon
Gloves: Barrrows
Cape: Soul Wars
Weapon: Chaotic longsword/rune crossbow/d claws
Shield: Elysian Spirit Shield/rune defender
Ring: Life (dying to a dc isn't in my plans)
Ammunition: 300 Broad bolts (don't like picking them up all the time).

I use glory with elysian or fury with defender since I really don't want to risk CLS...ever...lol. It should be pretty easy to see why.

ALMOST ALWAYS USE FURY/DEFENDER THOUGH! I only really take ely along if I'm lagging badly .

INVENTORY:

Darklight
Dragon Claws
Rune Crossbow
2 Overload (4)
Steel titan pouch
300 Steel of Legends scrolls
9 Prayer potions (4)
Sapphire Lantern
Teleport to House
2 Tireless run scrolls
8 Saradomin Brew

FAMILIAR (initial):

Spirit Terrorbird with 5 swordfish.

This may seem a bit odd, but the reasoning makes sense. I use the terrorbird to run nonstop to the demons and dismiss it once I have them lured. The luring process usually takes some food, which is why I store 5 swordfish in the bird. I then summon my Steel titan.


STATS:

99 attack
99 strength
75 defense
99 hitpoints
99 range
95 Prayer
99 mage
99 summoning
97 herblore
