Jagex Clan Cup 2010

Member List Management


Hey everyone,

Update: Please make sure that you are following the rules and advice of this thread. You must clearly state what clan you are updating the member list of and say what competitions that you're taking part in. Not to mention that each member list update must be done with a new post, edited posts will not be checked.

Welcome to the Jagex Cup Management thread! On this thread you can:
  • Edit your memberlist, add new members who aren't registered to another clan or remove members. If you are going to edit your memberlist, make a new post. Edited posts will not be checked.
  • As an individual, post if you want to be removed from a memberlist as you are no longer a part of that clan.
  • Any other issues that you have with your memberlists.

When you do post back to this thread, please ensure that you clearly state the clan whose member list you are editing, that the names are not censored and what competition(s) that you're taking part in.

Live Memberlists

It has taken a little while but the up to date member lists are now live. You can find them by clicking below:

Combat Cup
Sub-Divisions
Combined Cup

The Skilling Cup lists are coming but the Combat, Combined and Sub-Divisions are more important at this stage. The Skilling Cup member lists will be available the following week.

UPDATE: The member lists have only been updated to page 32 of the member list management thread, please check pages 33 onwards if you are looking for member list rule breakages. Going to be updated on Monday by 5pm BST/Forum Time.

Breaking any of the rules, including any rules agreed between the 2 clans at the arranging stage, will incur a heavy penalty. Every conflict and dispute that each clan has will be reviewed by a panel of Jagex Moderators and if it is decided that the rule was broken, the clan will be disqualified from the Cup. Any decision that Jagex make will be final and the decision can not come under dispute.
