An In-Game rp, just so the title doesn't throw you off.

Alright, let's get one thing straight: This rp is NOT new, it's the improved version of Lore of Gielinor (the rp).

I'm basically giving it a complete overhaul, with better and more thorough rules, a different bio format, etc.

Well, whilst we're on the 'rules' topic, how's about I list 'em?

1. All Jagex rules and regulations apply. Not only will you get banned from my roleplay for breaking any of these, but I will also report you for the violation of rules.

2. Use the bio format. (Not as serious, but if it's too common you're out.) Ofcourse, if you read this rule before I make the bio format, it won't make sense. I am the ONLY one who can enforce this upon Leaders.

3. Respect your higher-ups. If they rank higher than you, they have authority over you. Members of equal rank cannot enforce rules upon or bully each other, just to keep from... problems. Now, on rule four.

4. Be kind and respectful. If you wouldn't act like a donkey's behind in real life, you won't act like it here. Even if you WOULD, refrain from it.

5. No godmodding/overpowering/autokills. That will get you suspended if repeated more than once every day, and banned if you keep repeating it. Now, I'll remind you: Even I might do it from time to time, but I can't really suspend myself. If I do godmod, correct me. Ofcourse, a member of this RP might give you permission to autokill their character. If they give you permission, that's fine.

6. Keep it from PG to PG-13. No serious swearing, no bloody gory horrible mess, and... uhh... suggestive themes. This is made for fun, and a critical wound here or a @#$% there won't hurt anybody, as long as it's kept in moderation.

7. Don't advertise other rp's, like 'Randomrp (Ingame)' or 'Stuffandcrap (Ingame)'. Now, this doesn't apply to mods (Like Mark H), just to put it out there. It gets annoying, so don't.

The bio should be up next... now where's that colen shipment? Ah... here we go.

Name: (The most important.)

Age: (How old your character is.)

Race: (WHAT you are. Make stuff up, but no lava monsters. And nothing TOO weird.)

Affiliation: (What god you follow, good or evil, neutral, stuff like that.)

Optional - Affiliation Branch: (If you're in a designated group of your affiliation. For example: The Church of Saradomin.)

Hometown: (Where your character lives. For example, you may live in the lush forests surrounding Lletya, or be perched on the bone-chilling mountains towering above Taverley.)

Optional - Kingdom: (The kingdom your character recides in. This is optional, as you don't have to live in a kingdom. An example of living in a kingdom could be a house in Varrock. An example of not living in a kingdom could be an island off the coast of the Wilderness that nobody knows about.)

Clothing and weaponry: (What your character wears and weilds. For example, an elf may wear tight leather slacks and a brassard, using a thin dagger for defence.)

Optional - Motto: (What your character says often. In other words, a catch phrase.)

Short History: (A very brief story detailing your character's childhood, feats, and recent goings-on. If you can't think of anything, work on it during a session and leave 'Unknown' here.)

Other details: (Anything not covered by the other subjects.)

His kin were forced to move outside, since his family could no longer pay for goods such as food. They established a camp outside the town, surviving on wild parsnips, pawya, and the occasional grenwall as well as ponds of clear water and the dew their buckets managed to hold. He was forced to become a mercenary to pay for his family's wellbeing, going to elven royalty for jobs. Even now, his job doesn't pay enough to get them out of poverty.

Other details: Utterly hates what he's become: a Raider, of sorts.

Name: Sithaph
Race: Dragonkin
Alignment: Neutral
Age: Roughly 8,000,appears 25
Physical Appearance: At about 6' in his human form(see history below) Sithaph has a pale face and black hair. He wears armor forged of dragonite.His weapons vary, but because he is a dragonkin he doesn't really need one.
Gender: Male
Attitude: Arrogant, well versed in history of Gielinor(due to his long lifespan).
History: One of the few that remain of his race, Sithaph has temporarily assumed the form of a human to better view the recent events of Gielinor. His race plans to return to the plane of Gielinor from the stronghold that they are imprisoned in. Most of his former life is unknown, but of the Dragonkin as a whole it is known that they are extremely powerful, capable of forging the powerful dragon equipment coveted by humans, but unable to reproduce. Due to this, they fear death more than anything, and thus created a lesser race that was capable of breeding. These wyrms, as they were known, caused massive destruction in the 3rd age.However, their heavy involvement in combat left their race decimated, and the few remaining survivors evolved into the race now known as dragons.
Other Details: Dragonkin are nearly immortal, and have been known to die only in combat. He is very intelligent, and has seen or heard of most events in Gielinor that have now passed from stories to myth or legend.Oh yeah...and his abilities...well he is able to use dragonbreath(duh) although in his case it is fired from his hands and is long ranged(like a steel dragon's, go look in a house party with one if you don't know what that is). He can easily fight using all three corners of the combat triangle, although to maintain his human appearance he mainly uses melee when in his disguise. And...I think that's it...any more details can be obtained from me in-game or something I guess, or I can edit this post later on.
