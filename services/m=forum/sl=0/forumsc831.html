List of Races|


Humans- Dur.

Elves- A more advanced humanoid creature, they are slightly more intelligent than the human race, and are more fair and prefer the natural world than the industrialized world of humans. You know the drill, pointed ears,tall,woodland peoples and powerful magi.

Dwarves- A gruff and rare race in the lands of Arian. They live mostly in the mountains of the west, and some odd groups can be found in the cold peaks of the Wild Mountain Range, which is closer to the south than the west. They are very sturdy and host a natural resistance to most magics and poisons, making them a very hardy race.

Gualta: The Gualta are large hulky people with hunched backs and round, shapeless faces.The average Gualta has their sholders higher than the average man's head. Their large bodies and staggering strengh makes them popular with hard physical work, such as smithing, slavery and battle. Their natural strengh comes with a cost, however, very few Gualta can use magic, with only 42 magic uses within their recorded history, the Gualtas treat their magicians as prophets. The Gualta originate from large burrowed caves in the sides of the Wild mountain range.

Kahlin: Angel cat people. Not everyone in the race holds these traits. They can turn into felines but no matter what they have wings. They are seen as ill omens and are a very rare race. They survive off emotions, and blood. They can make their wings vanish if they must. Are rarely seen. They are able to foretell deaths.

List of Classes|


Warrior: People who specialize in the melee combats. Both armed and unarmed they can be potentially dangerous. Prefered by the Dwarves and Humans.

Magi: Someone who specializes in the magical arts. They prefer to cast from afar than get close and personal with their enemies. Prefered by the Elves and Humans.

Marksman: The class for those who like to shoot bows or crossbows from far away. They are trained well in the arts of both Marksmanship and Wilderness Survival. Prefered by Elves and Humans.

Silent Guardian: These are the agents of good. They are masters in stealth and marksmanship. They are excellent killers, but are not considered assassins. They are very philosophical on what is good and what is bad. Expert woodsmen and fighters, the only thing they lack is a good personality.

Druids: People who live by the natural order. They can bring either nature to fight for them or shapeshift into animals they have seen and battle in that form. They are trained evenly in both Magic and Melee. Prefered by Elves.

Battle-Mage: A person who uses magic to either power theirselves up or weaken their enemies, and then cut them down with a melee weapon of their choice. They are trained more in combat than magic, limiting their spells to buffing and debuffing magics. Prefered by Humans.

Monks: People who train their bodies to be the perfect weapon. They use a mixture of every known martial arts to perform deadly unarmed blows to their foes. Prefered by Humans and Dwarves.
