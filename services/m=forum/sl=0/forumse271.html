_Rules_

Workers Rules:

1. You must show activity at least once every 3 days this can be done by joining my clan chat and bumping this thread.

2. You MUST bump this thread at least once a day.

3. You MUST join my clan chat when you come online.

4. You must respect everyone, customers and workers alike.

5. You must not spam, or advertise any other busniesss or clan.

6. All Jagex rules apply

7. Do not ask for promotions as this decreases your chances of getting one.

8. If you take an order you must be able to complete it.

9. If you are abusive etc to anyone either here on the thread or in game you will be fired immeditaly.

10. If you are a smither you must get your own ores for an order of bars unless you wish to buy the ore off one of the workers here.

To show to me that you have read the rules you must post the following when asked did you read the rules on your application "yes of course I did, don't you believe me?"



Customers Rules:

1. When an order has been placed you must wait the full time (standered waiting time is 7 days at max but may be more depending on the size of the orders.)

2. You must not be abusive to any worker.

3. You must pay the full price for the goods in cash items are not accpeted.

4. Please join my clan chat to keep updated on the progress of your order.

5. You must have the money ready and waiting for us before you place the order, if you make us wait for the money your order may become invalid.

6. If you fail to show any signs of activity to us after 7 days of us completing your order, then your order will become invaild and you will may not be able to get after that unless you repost your order form.

This list is organised by Case*1234564.

Starting on 28-Jun-2010 (12:00am eastern time) Fortius Miners and Smithers will start to have the employee of the week, month and year.

You will receive points based on:
1- Clan chat activity;
2- Completed orders (not delivered, just completed;
3- Thread activity.

The new point scale for employee of the week month and year is as follows:

Ores:

1k of clay = 1 point
1k of tin = 1 point
1k of copper = 1 point
1k of iron = 1 point
1k of coal = 1 point
1k of mithril = 2 points
1k of addy = 3 points
1k of runite = 10 points

Bars:

1k bronze bars = 1 point
1k iron bars = 1 point
1k steel bars = 1 points
1k mithril bars = 2 points
1k addy bars = 3 points
1k runite bars = 10 points

Things made from bars:

Any thing made from bars will be calculated like this number of points you get for each bar times the number of bars you need for example if we got an order for rune 2hs you would get 30 points for every 1k rune 2hs you get

If it is an item that requires less then 1 bar to make like arrow tips you will divide the number of points you get from 1 bar by the number of items that bar makes for example 15k addy arrow tips would give the same amount of points as 1k addy bars

also anything on this list may change with time as I figure out what works and what dose not if it dose change tho it will be posted again
