�,��*~,�,��*Rules*��,�,~*��,�

1) DO NOT insult our logos, we spend time on them, they are not there for you to flame.

2) DO NOT spam this thread.

3) DO NOT use any logos on this thread unless they were made for you or that you have permission form the creator, even then you must keep the artists name on it.

4) IF any artist believes that for any reason you are spamming this thread (Posting your application multiple times) flaming, or just generally being a jerk, we will deny your logo.

5) CONTRARY to popular belief, Logo artists DO have lives. Please be patient, as if your logo is on the waiting list, it will eventually get made.

6) IF you don't like the logo make for you, explain why, do not flame.

7) USE the aplications! If you don't your post will be ignored.

8) CHANGE the "Did" in "Did You read the rules" to "Deed"

9) RESPECT me and the other workers here, they do not have to do what you want.

10) ALL Clans Must be at least a week old AND have 15+ members!!!! NON CLAN THREADS (G&A Threads, etc.) MUST BE AT LEAST A WEEK OLD!!!! IF YOU DO NOT MEET THIS REQUIREMENT, YOU WILL BE DECLINED!!!!
*** Many people ask us why this rule is here. It is beacuse it is very frustrating to spend upwards of an hour on a clan logo, only to see that the clan has disbanded.

11) EVEN IF you request a specific artist, you are not guaranteed a logo from that person.

12) CHANGE the "Deed" to "Does".

13) WE DO NOT make ASCII drawings as signatures, bumps, or logos with the word "bump" in it.

14) DO NOT lie on your application.

15) WE WILL ONLY make logos for the thread creator, just so we dont make logos that wont be used. If you are not the thread creator, do not apply for a logo for that thread.

**Looking for a logo.

Should you wish to have a logo made, I ask that you read through the whole thread first.
