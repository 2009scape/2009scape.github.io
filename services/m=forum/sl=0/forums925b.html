-~- Jobs & Ranks and What They Do -~-

=+=King & Queen=+= Rank: Gold star
The King and the Queen have absolute power over all other ranks. They are the leader of the clan and they give the final decision.

=+= Princes & Princesses =+= Rank: Gold star
Along with the King and the Queen, the Princes and the Princesses are the other members of the royal family. They help the leaders on their decision and help them rule the clan. New princes and princesses are chosen by the current Royal Family and YOU CAN�T APPLY TO BECOME ONE.

=+=Lords & Ladies=+= Rank: Bronze star
They come after the Royal Family. They can host events. New lords and ladies are chosen by the current Royal Family and YOU CAN�T APPLY TO BECOME ONE.

=+=Army General=+= Rank: Bronze star
He/She is the leader of our army. He hosts army training events to improve the army and leads the army with his/her tactics at a war. He/She can also kick from the clan chat. (For more information about our army check the 6th Post of the 2nd page.)

=+=Army Commanders=+= Rank: Bronze star
There are 3 army commanders, each for a division of the army. Mage commander, archer commander and the warrior (melee) commander.

=+=Subclan Leaders=+= Rank: Bronze star
Subclan leaders lead their subclan. (For more information about the subclans check post 4 of page 2.)

=+= Diplomats =+= Rank: 3 stripes
Diplomats work under the control of the Arch Chancellor. They work with the ally clan assigned to them to improve our bonds with them.

=+=The Grand Council Members=+= Rank: Silver star
There are 7 Grand Council Members. One is the Head of the Diplomats, one is the Head of the Army, one is the Head of the Law, one is the Head of Recruitment, one is the Head of the Subclans, one is the Head of the Skillers and one is the Head Councillor. (For more information about the Grand Council Members and what they do check the next post.)

=+=Royal Advisors and Lord Assistants=+= Rank: 3 stripes
They are chosen by the Lords and Royals to assist them. Each royal or lord can only have 1 assistant working with them.

=+=Royal Bodyguards=+= Rank: 3 stripes
They are chosen by a Royal to protect him/her in wars or events. It is suggested to those who wants to become a bodyguard to have a HIGH combat level to be able to protect their prince/princess.

=+=Army Members=+= Rank: 2 stripes
Army members work for the army. They fight for K.O.D in wars and they attend army trainings. You have to have at least 70 combat level to be a part of our army.
(For more information about our army check the 6th Post of the 2nd page.)

=+=Skillers=+= Rank: 1 stripe
Skillers are mostly the new members of our clan who is not in the army or has any other jobs. You don�t have to have any requirement to become a skiller. Skillers help our clan collect resources if we are in need of them.

=+=Jesters and Clowns=+= Rank: 1 stripe
Jesters and clowns are entertainers of our clan. If you don�t have any other jobs, you can be either a jester or a skiller. You don�t have to have any requirements to become a jester.
