Clan History

It was originally called God Skillers, which was founded by OozaruDragon (Lady Koriana). When it first started out Oozarudragon (Lady Koriana) recruited a few friends and some new people, some of those people are still in the clan. After a while Wize Old Me, an ex-friend of OozaruDragon (Lady Koriana) joined on and was made Co-Leader. Then the Leaders split up and formed two seperate clans, Rain of Fire and Bliss of Heaven. The two clans were bitter rivals due to strained relations between the Leaders. Eventually as time went on they became allies and B.O.H.'s leader, Wize Old Me, left Runescape and passed it down to another member Bluemagic176. He ran the clan for a while before real life took him away from the clan. He nominated Lil Spy 319 to run the clan from then on out. From there B.O.H. and R.O.F. eventually merged and become a whole clan again. In September, 2008 the clan was then known as Rain of Heaven's Fire. Later on there was a slight name change to Rain of Heavenly Fire. As time went on the clan became a medieval theme and took on the form of a Skilling Empire. We are still a fun loving group who enjoys the game for what it is and always looking for a new goal. If you are someone who enjoys skilling and finding new ways to play the game then join us today!

Clan History

The Caped Carousers are a group of mature Quest Cape owners of all ages. We get together irregularly in the spirit of the late lamented Alfred Grimhand, to appreciate fine ales and spirits by visiting the bars, inns, taverns and seedy dives of RuneScape, in a variety of fancy dress costumes - flaunting the outrageously over-the-top Quest Cape emote as we go. Clan History

Our clan is a great family oriented community with plenty of friendly members willing to help you throughout your RuneScape journey. In our clan we act mature, responsible, sensible, honest, loyal and respectful. We have a 4-0 war record and do an event every day. We offer an ENDURANCE program originally started by Lazerboyz from Immortal Retaliation. ENDURANCE motivates our members to work hard and achieve their combat related goals.
