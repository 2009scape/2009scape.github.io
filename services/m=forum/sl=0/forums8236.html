Our immediate goal:




To have a clan that can work as a team effectively and still have fun. This means that there will be no back stabbing, no non-participation in pking, and last but not least no leaking clan information or details.



-no back stabbing


-participating in pvp is mandatory


-no leaking clan information or details about clan chat




Very hateful or harsh remarks towards another player will be warned, repetitive insults will not be tolerated and temporary kicking will be used. Continued insulting or harsh language will not be tolerated in the future.


You will probably make several friends while being in this clan, the downside is you will at one point or another die and loose a large sum of money as well. Some do, and some don't but with my experience is that we all loose a lot at some point or another.


Just have fun and follow these rules and you will be fine.
