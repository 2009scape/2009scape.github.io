RANKING SYSTEM:


General = Owner/ co owner.



Captain = Officer



Lieutenant = respected member



Sergeant = further member


Corporal = Member



Recruit = Trail member



Smiley face = Friend of clan



How to gain ranks:


Firstly, you start off as a recruit for 1-2weeks to go up to a corporal.

To become a sergeant, it is bumped up to 1-2months.

To become a lieutenant, you need to apply to gain this rank, and show that you are a loyal&active member.This is only possible after sergeant rank.

To become a Captain, you need to come to most events, be a respectful clan member, must know our clan rules, help out in our clan anyway possible and most importantly not abuse your power.

We don't want any of are members to feel any less because of there rank,just be your self and have a good time.

All (Clan Of The White Tiger) members must follow these directions.

When given a command "Wt fi" or "fi" it means to Fall in line and be ready to move out.

When given a command "dd" it means to move on me and be ready for anything.

When given a command "rg" or "rg bh" it means to regroup/reform at the location commanded to regroup.

When if at anytime during a fight if one of the leaders/captains calls out a name,spams it you must turn around to pile that persons name that has been called.
Also a another way to tell who to pile is to pile who the leader is piling on.

(Clan Of The White Tiger) dos not bs and never will,all WT members have to keep this in mind.If you ever attack some one you will be asked to stop or off that's your first warning.But if attacking some one and continue hitting them
after being asked to stop you will be kicked from the event or worse kicked from clan so be careful,and listen.We also think its wise if this dos happen to you we do under stand you might be mad,or upset with what happened to you.But things like that get out of hand so that things don't get worse if some one bs's you,to tele,to run in to a safe zone,or ask them to stop.If you don't pick one of these choses and fight back you will be kicked from the event.

It is required that all WT members must be willing to risk more then two deaths per pk trip.This means that you have to be ready to re gear after death.

It is required to have at lest 3 magic return sets at all times,Mystic+.

It is required to have a combat level of 120+ at all times.If your F2P combat level is under 120 you must pouch to 120+ during any pk trip.

It is required to attend both bh+1 or pvp worlds during a clan event.

It is very important after any fight to fall in on the leader leading the event as fast as possible.

It is recommended to have tanking gear on you at all times.
