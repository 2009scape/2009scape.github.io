Before i say anything, I'm just going to say... WOAH! Runescape has changed so much since the last time i played, it took me about 5 minutes just to figure out how to post a new thread! Haha, (This is a compliment to Jagex, so i think it belongs here)

Anyway, this is just a thread of me telling you about my Runescape life, here in the world of RUNESCAPE! (I'm sure no one wants to hear me blab about myself, but i'm going to anyway )

I started back when i was in 7th grade, so about 4 years ago roughly, i started out in Lumbridge, like every other new player, not knowing where to go, or what to do. I met so many good people along the way, people in my own country (Australia) and had a pretty good web-social life. After playing for a few months, Runescape wasn't just a hobby, it was becoming more of an obsession, i played it for hours on end, doing the same thing every time having the time of my life. I loved RS too much, (this doesn't sound to much of a compliment But read on!), but as i said i met so many nice and awesome people, I could say that i had more web friends then real friends! By a lot! ANYWAY... bout few years on, i became a slightly high level, and did more advanced stuff, it became so much more fun! Then they brought in a few updates, that i can say on behalf of quite a few players, were quite bad, but I'm not criticizing in anyway i still loved it, the best by far was the G.E. tho But i still kept on playing.

The reason i quit playing Runescape for about a Year and half (probably even 2 years!) was because i was starting to dislike so much change in Runescape in such a small amount of time, i had trouble keeping up what was happening, but the thing that struck the core of the apple and made me quit, was one day i was doing a clue scroll, and i had a co-ordinate clue, and had to go into the Wilderness, and at that point, i forgot i had money, and all my Armour on. Once i got into wildy, a revengant pops up! Bam, half of my cash that i (Cont)
