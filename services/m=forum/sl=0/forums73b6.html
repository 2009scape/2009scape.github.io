\+/ Easy tasks \+/

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMPLETION OF: Cabin Fever, Ghost Ahoy, In Search of The Myreque, Wolf Whistle.

LEVELS NEEDED: 35 Cooking, 29 Hunter.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Cook a Tuna on port Phasmatys' Range.
You will need 35 cooking to do this, and you need to pay 2 ecto-tokens if you haven't completed Ghost Ahoy.


2. Enter Port Phasmatys without any payment.
Requires the Completion of Ghost ahoy.


3. Catch a Swamp Lizard in the Mort myre Swamps.
You will need 29 hunter.


4. Use the Myreque Shortcut to the hollows.
You need to complete In Search of The Myreque to enter this Shortcut.


5. Kill a Jungle horror barehanded.
Higher combat recommended. Also Cabin Fever Required.


6. Recharge your summoning points in Nature Grotto.
Requires the start of Nature Spirit, and the completion of Wolf Whistle.


7. Enter the Second Floor of the Slayer Tower.
Nosepeg recommended to prevent drain from Abberant Spectres.


8. Talk to Rufus in Canifis while having a Wolfbane Dagger equiped.
You need to complete Priest in Peril.


9. Buy a Moonlight mead at The Hair of the Dog Tavern.
5 gp needed


10. Buy and Drink a braindeath rum in "Harpoon Joe's House of Rum"
30 gp and the completion of Cabin Fever needed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

REWARD: "Skull Amulet", and 5,000xp on a level 30+ skill.

The Skull Amulet can (If charged) teleport you to Burgh de Rott, and has 1 charge. It can be recharged at the "Burgh de Rott Well" (Near start of Temple Trekking).


While worn:

� The Skull Amulet will now act like a ghostspeaking amulet.
� You can now get 4 extra prayer points at the paterdomus altar when worn.
� Gives you +4 Prayer stat bonus

\+/ Medium tasks \+/

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
REQUIRED: Cabin Fever, Ghost Ahoy, Darkness of Hallowvale, Nature Spirit,

LEVELS NEEDED: 55 Mining, 35 Fishing, 61 Agilty, 35 Firemaking, 44 Farming.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Mine Mithril Ore inside the Haunted mine.
You need 55 Mining, but you need to mine it INSIDE the mine!


2. Catch a swordfish at Burgh de Rott.
You need 50 fishing, and started In aid of the Myreque.


3. Refill your Ectofial at the Ectofuntus.
You need the Completion of Ghost Ahoy.


4. Charter a Ship to Mos Le'harmless, while using the Charm of Charos.
You need to complete Creature of Fenkenstein and Cabin Fever. Equip your ring of charos.


5. Guide an adventurer to Burgh de Rott.
Requires the Completion of Darkness of Hallowvale. It doesn't matter who you guide.


6. Take the Ground floor Agilty Shortcut, located in the Slayer Tower.
Requires 61 Agility and a nosepeg.


7. Enter the Barrows Minigame.
Only enter, by killing at least ONE barrows Brother and requires the start of Nature Spirit.


8. Make a Willow Pyre in Mort'ton, and burn a corpse.
This also requires you to obtain a corpse.


9. Kill a Vampire Juvinate with a Silver Weapon.
Requires a Silver Weapon. (They can be found east of Burgh de Rott)


10. Plant a kwuarm in the Port Phasmatys Allotment.
Requires 44 Farming.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

REWARD: Upgraded "Skull Amulet", and 10,000xp on a level 50+ skill.



While Worn:
(all the bonusses from the 1st amulet)
� You get now 2 charges when recharging at the paterdomus well.
� Your prayer will last longer while wearing this amulet.
� You can trade your ecto-tokens at the ghost discipline for random gems.
� Gives +8 prayer stat bonus

\+/ Hard Tasks \+/

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
QUESTS REQUIRED: Legacy of Seergaze, and all previous Quests.

LEVELS REQUIRED: 65 Agilty, 58 Firemaking, 75 Slayer, 77 Runecrafting, 50 Woodcutting, 75 Summoning and 66 magic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Use the Paterdomus shortcut to Morytania.
You need 65 Agilty to use the shortcut.


2. Burn a Asyn Shade in Mort'ton Funeral.
You need a minimum of 58 Firemaking. Also You will need an Asyn Corpse and a Eucalyptus Pyre log (or higher...)


3. Kill a mutated bloodveld in Meiyerditch dungeon.
You will need 50 slayer and the completion of Darkness of Hallowvale.


4. Kill a Gargoyle in Canafis Slayer Tower.
You need 75 slayer to kill it.


5. Spend Your Trouble brewing points on Red Rum.
You will need 2 Pieces of Eight to buy this. This means you need to play Trouble Brewing at least once.


6. Kill a Vyrewatch by using an Ivandis' Flail.
Just kill a vyrewatch near Burgh de Rott. You can only kill them with Ivandis Flail.


7. Cut a Mahogany tree on Moss Le'Harmless, while wearing full lumberjack armour.
You will need 50 Woodcutting, and full Lumberjack from Temple Trekking.


8. Craft a Blood Rune, while wearing a Blood Tiara.
You will need 77 Runecrafting and a Blood Tiara.


9. Make a praying mantis Pouch in the Nature Grotto.
You need 75 Summoning, and the Completion of Nature Spirit/Wolf Whistle.


10. Teleport to Canafis.
You need 66 magic to teleport via Ancient Magicks. You need to Complete Desert Treasure.



REWARD: "Skull Amulet", and 25,000 xp on a level 65+


When Worn:

� A higher possible loot from the Vyrewatch Burning minigame after "Lagecy of Seergaze".
� Ghasts will not attack you while wearing the Skull Amulet.
� You may now Change the "Skull Amulet" destination to Barrows (Temple Salve), Still 2 Charges.

\+/ Elite Tasks \+/

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(A higher dungeoneering level would be great)

REQUIREMENTS: 85 smithing, 85 farming, 75 firemaking
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


1. Talk to Brother Tranquillity, while wearing the thome of frost.
You need to pass several dungeoneering levels for this.


2. Smelt a runite bar in Burgh de Rott.
You will need 85 smithing.


3. Loot the barrows reward chest.
Loot, after you killed all barrows brothers, the reward chest.


4. Plant a Torstol in Port Phasmatys herb patch.
You will need 85 farming.


5. Burn the Corps of an Asyn Shade with a magic pyre log.
Requires 75 firemaking.



Reward:

2 times 40,000 experience on a level 70+ skill

� Unlimited teleports with your skull amulet.
� Every 24 hours 100 buckets of Slime from the Ghost Dicipline.
� 5% more slayer expierence for killing monsters in the Morytania/Moss Le'Harmless region.
