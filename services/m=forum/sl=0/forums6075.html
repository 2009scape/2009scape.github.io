Businesses & Services Guide

Welcome to the Businesses and Services forums.


What is the Businesses & Services Forum for?

Here you will find many player run companies and organisations powered as far as imagination can take you. Why not have a fine newspaper to read as you get up? How about booking a hotel for your daily meeting? And dinner afterward? This is all on display here through the newspaper, hotels, restaurants and other companies that are shown here. Need a taxi to get back to home after a hearty meal? That�s available as well!
And the magic of RuneScape allows for unique services not found in real life: why not use a teleport service instead of a taxi? If you need to loan some items to go to battle or simply to show off, some lending services are available here as well!
Fancy yourself a business tycoon? Why not run your own corporation? If you simply wish to run your own business or service, that�s ok too. Don�t be afraid to simply want to run a cosy caf�: no one will judge you but on the quality of your hospitality. The sky is the limit in terms of ideas:).


Guidelines

Do:

1. Make your Business concept unique. Make it stand out from the crowd.

2. Make your thread unique. Using your own layout, templates, designs and borders will make a real difference. Remember you�re trying to sell yourself.

3. Make your service clear and understandable. You might post the timezone you run in and specific task requirements.

4. When using another player�s service, remember that they are often doing this for fun rather than profit. As such, show respect and appreciation for their hard work.


Don�t:

1. Hijack or advertise your business on another company's thread. It is just common courtesy. Feel free to advertise your events in the B&S community chat thread.

2. Copy and plagiarise the hard work of others in your thread. You are welcome to take others� Business concepts as long as you have a different take on the matter.

3. Spam, Scam or Scram. No one will like you.


FAQ

  • What is Allowed in this forum?
    Any business or service you can think of as long as it is designed to be ongoing. Some examples include hotels, restaurants, newspapers, schools, corporations. Don�t be afraid to invent your own!

  • What Isn't Allowed here?
    - One time or short term services. Please use another forum that would be more suitable for the purpose of this service.
    - The Code of Conduct still need to be followed so no solicitation services (wedding, adoption, relational, counselling etc).

  • Is there a list of businesses?
    There sure is! Please check Jamandy52's thread HERE.

  • I feel that my business could sell better somewhere else. Is this ok?
    Of course! Feel free to post in a more relevant forum if you feel you would have a greater impact here (there is no obligation to do so). Some examples of this include: skills assistance, supplies, specific types of item lending.

  • We run a Gnomeball Football League, can we post here?
    No, the best place for competitive, user-generated content, is in the Tournaments Forums.

  • Can I post about individual Skill Assistance here? What about Open Altars? Effigy assistance?
    The best place to post these kind of requests is in the "Skills" forum.
    For open altars, go HERE.
    For effigy assistance, go HERE    .
