Jagex Clan Cup 2010

Jagex Cup Disputes


Due to the high number of member list changes, queries all of the other related issues with member lists - I've decided to split up member list changes and disputes into different threads. The Disputes thread will have priority over the member list changes and will be dealt with as soon as possible.

Welcome to the Jagex Cup Disputes thread! This is the place to raise any issues, breaking of the rules or disputes that you have about other clans or your own. On this thread you can:
  • Raise a dispute about another clan with regards to them breaking the rules, not responding before a war and any other dispute you have.
  • Any issues that you want to raise about you own clan like not being able to make matches or other various problems.
  • Any problems that don't necessarily fit the above criteria but you want to raise about the Jagex Cup.

When you do post back to this thread, please ensure that you clearly state the issue that you're having, the clan that you're having a dispute with and any proof that you have to back up your dispute. (Do not worry about posting URLs on this thread)

All of the rules for each cup are listed within the registration thread and these should be followed. Breaking any of the rules, including any rules agreed between the 2 clans at the arranging stage, will incur a heavy penalty. Every conflict and dispute that each clan has will be reviewed by a panel of Jagex Moderators and if it is decided that the rule was broken, the clan will be disqualified from the Cup. Any decision that Jagex make will be final and the decision can not come under dispute.
