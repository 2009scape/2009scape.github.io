For people who want to get combat up (and cash)!

Combat 3-10:
Train at chickens, collect feathers (stackable) and bones. Do not collect raw chicken because it is only worth 160 gp each. But the bones are worth 180 GP each, bank them each time you have a full set.

Combat 10-20:
This is the best level to train on Goblins in lumby. For cash, you can collect the bones.


Combat 20-30:
The cows in the cow field. They are good XP until 30, but now you should pick up the cowhides instead of the bones because cowhides are worth more.

Combat 30-40:
At combat 30-40, I would suggest on training on Barbarians, sometimes they drop chaos rune (3) which is stackable AND you can also pick up the bones

Combat 40-60:
Hill giants are fast experience at this level. For cash, you can collect the big bones and the limpwurt roots.

Combat 60-70:
Moss Giants, pick up big bones for cash :]

Combat 70-80:
Ice Giants, faster xp then mossies and hill giants and you can make cash by picking up big bones...again.

Combat 80+:
Cockroach soldiers drop runes and ores, and if you're lucky you may even get a rune scimmy and rune sq drop. Also they drop 100-400 coins. o__O

NOTE: This might not be a good guide for FAST combat. But it's at a pretty decent speed.
