Jagex Clan Cup 2010 - Sub-Divisions

Under 115 Combat


Due to the small amount of clans taking part, I've decided to carry out a set draw for the whole sub-division, here it is:

First Round
Match 1 - The Mad Bomberz vs Fateful Demise
Match 2 - K0 Unit vs Spartan Sausages
Match 3 - The Shadow Wolves vs flamestorm p
Match 4 - The Dreaded Warlards vs Element Kingdom
Match 5 - Hopelessly Entangled vs Longbow562

Higher Force get a bye to the Quarter Finals.

Quarter Finals
Match 6 - flamestorm p vs The Mad Bomberz or Fateful Demise
Match 7 - The Dreaded Warlards vs K0 Unit
Match 8 - Frozen Darkfire vs Higher Force

Semi Finals
Match 9 - Winner of Match 8 vs Winner of Match 6
Winner of Match 7 gets a bye to the Final.

Final
Winner of Match 9 vs Winner of Match 7

As the draw has been made for the entire sub-division, we are going to be bit more lenient on when you can arrange and play your matches. However, there will be a deadline on each round, here they are:

First Round Deadline - 23:59 BST Sunday 11th July
Quarter Finals Deadline - 23:59 BST Sunday 18th July
Semi Finals Deadline - 23:59 BST Sunday 25th July
Final Deadline - 23:59 BST Sunday 8th August.

Here are a few points to consider when arranging the matches:
  • Please arrange the time, date and rules, not explicitly stated within the main rules for Clan Wars, for your matches within the Jagex Cup forum. Create the thread with both clan's initials in the title, "MT v MK Com R1", as an example between the "Mod Timbo" and "Mod Kathy" clans.
  • All of the options within Clan Wars is up to the clans during the arranging phase except for the ones explicitly mentioned within the rules. If no agreement can be made, the J-Mods will decide the rules depending on how reasonable both clans have been during the negotiations. This will commonly be the default war rules, posted below, but may be different if one clan has made no effort to reach an agreement.
  • Be mindful of the other clans timezone if it varies with that of your own. If a decision cannot be reached before 24 hours before the round's deadline, a J-Mod will decide the time that suits both clans the best it can.


The Rules

  • Fights to be held in F2P or P2P 'safe' Clan Wars
  • There is no player cap
  • There is no returning
  • The match must be arranged, and played, before the round's deadline.
  • Arranging the games and the results must be posted in the Jagex Cup forum.
  • No one can enter the arena who is not on your memberlist or has 115 Combat, or above, in F2P.
  • Memberlists will be frozen exactly 24 hours before the start of a war and cannot be edited in any way either to add players or remove them.
  • Any disputes should be made in writing within 24 hours of the finish of the fight in the Jagex Cup Disputes thread.

Default War Rules

  • All styles
  • Clan Wars 'Classic' map (No portal hugging)
  • No Corrupt or Dungeoneering items
  • The team that spawns from the north attacks
