�Clan Meetings�

� Try to show up to as many meetings as possible.
� If your told to stop talking please do so.
� Doing as your told and attending the confrence and not looking around the house.


�How To Gain Ranks�

� Never asked to be ranked up.
� Do not get into an argument.
� Help other members of the clan.
� By recruiting people.
� Attending events.
� Bumping (one of the best ways).
� Bumping allies threads.
� Stay loyal to the clan.

�Explanation of Ranks�

Gold Star - These are the leaders, they are the deciding factor in clan disputes, and have the right to permanent ban.

Silver Star - These are the clan's most trusted members, they have all the rights of the leaders except they can't permanent ban. They're the eyes and ears of the leaders, and will tell the leaders anything that they need to know.

Bronze Star - These members have been in the clan long enough to see the basic principles of the clan. They can accept/decline applications into the clan.

3 Bananas - These are the people who bump and go to events as often as possible. They have been in the clan for a while, but not too long.

2 Bananas - These members haven't been in the clan all that long, but they go to events and bump often.

1 Banana - These are the foundation of the clan, they are awarded this rank just for being accepted into the clan, they don't have much authority, but with a little time, and a little hard work, they can be going up ranks in a jiffy.
