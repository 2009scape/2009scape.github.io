Hello and welcome to Eternal Empire recruitment thread!
We are a P2P clan running in the GMT (UK) time zone. The current clan requirements are 100+. Note that we are a friendly, NON-WARRING based clan.

The Eternal Empire was founded by TheJammed85 in 2006 and is also known as EE. Eternal Empire is a diverse clan, welcoming those with a certain level of maturity and capability of challenge. We, the clan community, encourage social interaction between each other as well as having fun at both in-clan activities and outside of clan activities.

The events of EE are mostly focused on monster-hunting and minigames. However, we do not do Bounty Hunter or Player Killing (PVP) a lot especially as an event. There will always be an event on Wednesdays, Saturdays & Sundays created by our Event Team. These set events allow for total clan involvement, developing team-work skills and, most importantly, for fun. For the rest of the week you are able to 'request any event you like!

If you would like to join the Eternal Empire, please read over our CLAN RULES here Recruitment Thread: 92-93-111-60299340 and fill in an application.
