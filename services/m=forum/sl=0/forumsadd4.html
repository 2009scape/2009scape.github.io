17-Jul-2010 16:04:58
Gold is really the best way to go for Mining and Smithing, especially if you've got Varrock armour from the diary and Goldsmith Gauntlets from the Family Crest quest. If you don't have both yet, I strongly suggest doing them. Varrock armour gives you a chance to mine 2 ores at once, saving you time and still giving you experience for both ores. And the gauntlets give you double the experience for smelting gold ore.

As for Prayer, I wouldn't bother with Soul Wars either, as has already been advised. I hate buying bones personally and what I did at your level was to just range Baby Blue Dragons. They're easy to kill in the Taverly Dungeon where there's three of them isolated alone and you can just bury their bones. May not seem like much at first but full inventories got me to level 70 quickly enough. After that I did adult Blues and used the Ectofuntus.

Raurg bones are very hard to come by. I mistakenly tried that route too and had to give up. Even though the baby d bones are little experience, you can kill them very quickly and sometimes even two or three at a time, if you're ranging them. It all adds up very fast. At lower levels, it's well worth the effort, and you can save your money for something else.

Um, you can also consider making cannonballs. The experience adds up there too pretty nicely. They always sell well and the only drawback is that it's a little slow.

You could mine the Coal and Iron for Mining experience, then sell both. That would get you about half the cost of the Steel bars. Then smelt them into cannonballs and sell them. Each bar will yield four cannonballs. You'd get a little more back than you paid for the Steel bars.

Experience wise, each cannonball made gives about half what a gold bar does with gauntlets but you make four from each bar so you're actually doubling the total experience made from each process.
