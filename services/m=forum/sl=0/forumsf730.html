Date of Friendly Team Xp Challenge: TBA.

The Friendly Team Xp Challenge (FTXC) is all about bringing Malicious Intent members together for an optional skilling challenge. The FTXC is held every 2 months and the Xp gaining occurs over a week.

The aim of the FTXC is to bring together skillers in Malicious Intent in a friendly skilling challenge (notice that in the name? ). For this to happen fairly, the competition judges get together and a specific skill (or skills) are chosen for the week. For this challenge to work, the only Xp that will be counted by the judges for the FTXC will be from the chosen skills.

Malicious Intent members will be working in teams, hopefully succeeding in bringing about the most Xp for a big congratulations and their name being displayed on the thread as the best in that skill. This challenge also intends to encourage new friendships to form and get the clan closer to each other, while also getting people back into skilling!

Judges are clan members that are not allowed to join in the competition themselves. They are also not allowed to favour any of the teams or members whatsoever.

Please fill out a Competitor's Application form to get listed as a competitor for this month's FTXC You will find the secret words in the rules.

~~How the teams are made~~

Members are to apply for the FTXC individually, and after enough names are collected, the members are then placed into equally+ numbered teams. Teams are chosen by the players' level of the skill that was chosen for the FTXC week, and these levels are balanced out as best as possible (say, a person with 99 in a skill would be paired with a person with 40).

Though FTXC is optional, please note that when you join the challenge you will be working in a team. Though your individual Xp will be counted, your team�s total Xp is what predominately matters in the end. Plus, the more Xp you get, the closer your team gets towards winning the challenge . Though team names are not necessary, they are welcomed and will be noted in the finale.

(+if there are odd numbers, compensation may be made to the team/s with the lowest number of players. Conditions apply. See Compensation Xp rules.)

~~How the Xp gains are to be calculated~~


At the starting time, members who are taking part in the challenge are to log out (so as to reset the highscores) and their Xp is taken by a judge. Once every member in each team is taken, let the Xp gaining begin! Players will have the week to gain as much Xp as they can. At the end of the week, players are again encouraged to log out, and their final Xps are taken by the judges.

If their level in the skill of the week is not ranked on the highscores, then the player (if p2p) will be asked to turn their Aventurer's log onto public so the judges can take their Xp from there.

(Note that the Xps are not taken during the week. This will hopefully keep the suspense for the finale )

~~Compensation Xp Rules~~

Compensation Xp will be given to teams whose members have not performed as well as they should. This could mean doubling or tripling the Xp.

Conditions:

1) There must not be more than 1-2 players (depending on team size) who have not contributed Xp to a great extent.
2) If a player has gained less than 30K Xp, it is doubled and no more Xp is counted thereafter.
3) If a player has gained less than 10K Xp, is is tripled and no more Xp is counted thereafter.
4) The compensation Xp is also given to team/s who have a smaller number of players in them than others. The compensation will be given to the player who achieved the least Xp in the team.



Penalty Xp losses will be calculated depending on the severity of the crime.

Conditions:

1) If the crime is slight, then the Xp loss can be up to � of their total Xp gain and/or percentage.

3) If the crime is confusing to players, judges will then deliberate and figure out Xp loss on a vote.
