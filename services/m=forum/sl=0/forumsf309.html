.:: Guild Rules ::.

1. All Jagex rules apply.
2. Do not discuss inappropriate content in our clanchat or on our thread.
3. Respect fellow members and any guests on the forums and in the clanchat, regardless of any differences between you.
4. Respect the ranks - They are there to help.
5. Be active! Attend events, post on the thread, and come into the clanchat. Help us build our community!
6. Do not ask for an Admin rank. If you meet the requirements for the 80 Farming rank, you may ask.
7. Do not spam the clanchat or thread.
8. Do not advertise for another guild/cause on the thread or in the clanchat.
9. After being given 2 warnings in the clanchat, you will be kicked on your 3rd offense. Further offenses may warrant temporary bans or removal from the guild.
10. Severely inactive members will be removed from the guild members list.

.:: Rank Explanation ::.

Ranks are based on activity, personality, helpfulness, respectfulness, and seniority. Farming level is only a requirement for the 80+ Farming rank, NOT the Admin ranks. There are currently 4 levels of rank: Master Admin, Veteran Admin, Apprentice Admin, and 80+ Farming. Master, Veteran, and Apprentice Admins have the right and ability to kick players from the clanchat.

.:: 80+ Farming ::.
Once guild members have been active (particularly in the clanchat) for 1 week, they may receive the Sergeant rank if they have 80+ Farming. Activity must be maintained to keep this rank.

.:: Apprentice Admin ::.
The Apprentice Admin rank is given to guild members who have been active in the guild for 1-2 months, and have shown the qualities of rank (see above) throughout that time period. The Apprentice Admins are given the Lieutenant rank.

.:: Veteran Admin ::.
This rank is given to members who have been fully active for 2-4 months, and may be an upgrade from the Apprentice Admin rank. These members are the heart of the guild and are fully supportive, active, and loyal. The Veteran Admins are given the Captain rank.

.:: Master Admin ::.
This rank is given to a select group of members. All founders and leaders maintain this rank. Also, any Veteran Admins who have shown longstanding loyalty and have proven themselves to show the qualities of a rank may receive this rank. The Master Admins are given the General rank.
