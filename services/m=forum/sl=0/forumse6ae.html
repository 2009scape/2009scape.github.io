What made you join Jagex?:

Two of my personal friends worked here and told me of an open position. I had just finished my college/6th form courses then and thought it would be a good life experience.


What is your occupation at Jagex?:

Well, over the last three years I have had various roles here at Jagex. I started out working in normal Player Support answering messages and reviewing account appeals etc. I then went on to train new members of Jagex staff for about 6 months before joining the Community Management Team.

I've been here in Community Management for about three and a half years now.


Which famous person/character do you look most like?:

I have been told I look like Ethan Hawk sometimes, sometimes I look like Daniel Radcliff (and sound like him too...) when I am having a bad hair day/shave off my beard.


Which famous person/character would you most like to look like?:

Anybody that *isn't* Daniel Radcliff.


What is your favourite cookie flavour?:

After four years, my fondness for cookies has all but diminished. If I have to have them I prefer oatmeal and raisin cookies.


What is your most interesting hobby?:

These days it's probably "Dungeons & Dragons" the tabletop RPG.

It's been a massive passion of mine for years and it's a fantastic hobby. It's social and helps expand creativity and various other skills.

It gets a lot of bad press, either people thinking it's something it isn't, that it's bad, or that it's really, really nerdy. When you get past all the stuff that surrounds it though, it's a truly brilliant, co-operative and fun game.


What is your favourite meal (starter, main course and dessert)?:

Starter = You can't beat some good prawn tempura or sweet potato(battered/deep fried)

Main = I love a *GOOD* home-made beef burger, with a tasty mayo/relish and good quality cheese. If not, then I tend to look at the steaks.

Desert = Either a nice hot fruit pie (Cherry preferably) with ice cream OR Sticky Toffee Pudding.


Most proud of?:

Traveling to various places, writing an article for a monthly magazine and having a lot of great memories with friends.


Regret the most?:

Missing my flight home from Japan probably wasn't my greatest achievement...


Pancakes or waffles?:

Pancakes.


Do you have any nicknames?:

Hahaha, hmmm... should I disclose it...
Due to some rather embarrassing photos of me playing a Princess from my Drama course days, I have earned the endearing nickname of "Princess".


What was your favourite subject in school?:

Drama!


What is your favourite colour?:

White or Blue.


If you could change your name, what would it be and why?:

I would change it to...

Trent Steel!

Why? Because it sounds like an awesome action hero name!


When was the last time you had pasta for dinner?:

Last week?


What is the most expensive thing that you've ever bought?:

Yeah, that emergency flight back from Japan wasn't cheap...


What is the most expensive thing that you've ever broken?:

I can't think of anything actually...


What is the funniest thing that you've ever seen?:

One of the Jagex Moderators being set upon by some "mature women" at a Karaoke Bar on holiday.


If you had to choose between keeping your first name or keeping your last name, which would you choose?:

Last name. It's my family name and I would feel guilty for "ending" my family name.
