����� Money Guide: Flipping �����

Flipping is the most profitable way to earn money from merchanting. If you understand the concept and can apply it successfully, you will have profit rates which exceed other methods of merchanting.

Flipping is a term which is defined as buying an item at a lower price to sell it for a higher price within the same update. Keep in mind that there is a 4 hour time period where you will be locked out of the market and kept from selling your items.

� Tips �
1. The initial amount you need to begin must be around 25-30M. Any less than that does not generate decent profits.

2. Use the Grand Exchange Database to check records of the markets of the items.

3. Check the marketplace forums to get an idea of its current price margins. If this does not help you much, visit World 2 to get an update of the item.

4. If you�re flipping a lesser known item, take the time to perform a 1x (or 100x test) depending on the limit on the item. Some people may buy 1 AGS to get a sense of the market on that day. Following the 1x test, you would be able to successfully gauge the offers in your GE slots to allow for maximum flipping.

� What to Flip �

Godswords (GS): They have a great market because there is consistent demand from players who are trying it out for the first time, need one for GWD, want to show it off to their friends, etc. The only exception is ZGS. ZGS doesn�t have great profit returns due to the fact that it is one of the least popular GS out of the whole. AGS and SGS have great profit margins, but do require that you have over 2B to perform 4 other flips at the same time.

� Dragon Claws (DClause): These are very popular due to PvPers buying and using them with intentions of owning other players in PvP worlds. They have great profit margins and when you are reverse flipping, you can be extremely generous with the profit margins. Most of the times, you can sell it for 60-150k below market which allows you to have substantial leverage in terms of profit margins.

� Bandos Tassets/Bandos Chest Plate(BT/BCP): This market is decent for flipping albeit the lower profit margins in comparison to dragon clause and godswords. Under normal conditions, you can expect returns ranging anywhere from 100-300k per item flipped depending on the market.
