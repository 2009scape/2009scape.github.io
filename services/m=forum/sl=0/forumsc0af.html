~ Welcome to Skyness 80+ ~

Skyness is a clan that is made by 01 May,Miauwtje2 and Het Schaap.
We used to be high-ranked players in a clan named thunderwolves, but because of some problems we deciced to make an own clan and have already made quite some success.

This clan is for meeting new people, helping others and ofcourse to have fun.
We do about 4 events per week. The events vary from castle wars to group fishing, and more. We are a clan that thinks that everyone's opinion counts. This means that the events and ranks are not only chosen by the owner, but also decided by the members of the clan. You can vote at the 'Poll' section.

Current member count: 32
Home world: World 115.

QFC: 87-88-659-61164350

in the chat who do not understand you. We ask you what language(s) you speak in the application section.

We hope you have a great time in Skyness!

~_Rules_~

We have some rules, you should have read these before you post an application.

1- Respect each other, rank doesn't matter.
2- Don't beg for a rank, this will decrease your chances, or maybe even de-rank you.
3- Be online at least once a week, note that you also should join the Skyness clan-chat.
4- Any offense inside the clan chat must be reported to one of the co-owners.
5- Please keep all arguments outside the clan-chat, any disagreement can be taken to the forums.
6- Keep all conversations inside the clan-chat as "mature" as possible, lots of swearing is not acceptable.
7- Try to help each other as much as possible.
8- Do not abuse your power.
9- Don't join any other clan unless:
You are given permission by the owner or co-owners
You are joining a Soul Wars clan chat
You are joining a Pest Control clan chat
You are joining a Penguin-Hunt clan chat
You are joining a Stealing Creation clan chat
You are joining a Shooting Star clan chat
You are joining an Evil Tree clan chat
10- Real life > Runescape
11- Speak English in the clan unless there is no-one that can't understand you. We all want to know your stories!
- Have fun in our clan chat!
