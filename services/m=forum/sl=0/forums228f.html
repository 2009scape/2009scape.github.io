I've played Runescape for many years now, I started off in Classic, quit in 04, returned in 05, and continued to play the game in RS2. In my time spent in RS2 and in the forums, I've seen many players ask or talk about what Runescape Classic was like. I'm one of the players that often spoke about it. In the past, I've reminisced with friends about it and have often wondered "What would the players nowadays think of classic?". Well, now that members have access to Classic once more, I can ask the question, and get some answers.

If you have never played Classic before recently, or haven't played classic for many years, I would like you to rate the game 1-10 with 1 being lowest and 10 being highest, and explain your rating. Please keep in mind, I'm not asking you to compare RSC with RS2, I'm simply asking what you think of RSC itself. If possible, simply imagine RS2 does not exist when rating.


Just for the fun of it, here's some descriptions I came up with for 1-10, keep in mind that your description doesn't have to match mine, I just did this out of random amusement

1- Good Lord this game is terrible, how did it ever survive for so long?

2- It sucked, bye bye!

3- I don't want to say it's horrible, but I certainly don't want to come back!

4- I didn't really like it, but I might check it out now and again when I'm bored.

5- I didn't like it, but I didn't hate it either.

6- It was fun, but I'll only stop by on occasion since there's RS2

7- I liked it quite a bit, I'll certainly spend quite a bit of time on it.

8- I liked it a lot, I think my time between this and RS2 will be 50-50

9- I enjoyed playing a lot, I might end up playing this more than the main game!

10- Wow! This game is better than RS2! Jagex should just make this the main game again!

Thanks for posting, and I hope you enjoy Classic!

I've had classic access for years now, but wanted to try the tutorial on another member account.

The server chose to place me into the most crowded world, so even talking to an NPC was nearly impossible. I could barely see them, but the Guide was busy each time I clicked on them.

I decided to go to a less crowded server, and it wasn't quite bad at all.

Mind you, my twin still has to finish tutorial island, and going through it, with the old reminders etc, is refreshing, like having an old fashioned tea party.

I do see many differences between Classic's tutorial and Runescape's current tutorial.

Oh, and to the person who says you can't run, you can! Use alt + click to run, as per an old post by Andrew from the olden days

I can much more appreciate how far the game has come since Classic, and while I may only play for relaxation now & then, it's nice to reminisce about the "good old days".

I may see if I can level my character a few levels before I toss it in.

I've seen newer brower games out there that are much more outdated than Classic, so Cheers to the creators, for a job well done, and for all of the countless updates you've given us over the years.
