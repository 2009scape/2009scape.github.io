This thread is dedicated to Karamja Achievement Diary hard task: become the Champion of the Fight Pits. After runing this service in more than year and due new diaries this group of dedicated players (am so proud of all them) suggested to help with Arcdougne diary hard tasks "Use a catapult in Castle Wars after gaining the knowledge from the Catapult Construction quest" and "Be on the winning side in a game of Castle Wars"

All tasks are run by volunteers, players whose are glad to help You finish this task for You without payment.

For those who needs to finish this task, please post here or contact available volunteers online, and be patient if we can`t help You just in time � we also players.
To find non busy volunteer sometimes can take some days- we are not 24/7 service we have own tasks to do, so those who seeks help with this task please be patient, and also dont forget to post status if completed task.
Remember to turn Private chat to On or add us and set chat to Friends in-game to be contacted!
As for all volunteers we do this not for reward, and reward not accepter.

~~ Volunteer guidelines ~~

1. Volunteers do not accept any charge. This is a free service, it has always been and it shall always be that way.

2. If you ask about being add to the volunteer list: I do not want to make big list of empty volunteers! A small list of active volunteers works much better than a big list of dead ones - so if YOU still want - You'll need to contact me in game and let me know your motives as to why you want to be one. I REALY don't want to see any "I want, but i will not" type people here. We have specific tasks to do.

3. Volunteers, if you do not want to be active any longer, please leave a message here so you can be removed from the list. If you are going on extended leave, please do like wise so I can make a note next to you name.

The way to contact a volunteer:

First, add a post in this thread; If you submit a post on this thread requesting help, once you've completed your task, please edit the post and let us know you've completed. That helps the volunteers who look at this thread to know who needs help and who don't.

Second, contact via private message any volunteer from the list; There is a list of volunteers in the first posting, private message them and see who's on.

Third, through clan chat (fpvolunteers) Sometimes this method will take a little while, so both posting and contacting volunteers are the preferred methods to complete this task. No one is forced to be in it, but was made as an alternative method to contact a volunteer. Also we can do some d&d or minigame actions, that's no problem. This suggestion made by Mj, we hope it will work and will be extenrded to something great in future. Anyways, all are welcome there.

~~ Fight pits completion guide ~~

The way it works:
-Come to the world where the volunteer is at
-Meet up with the volunteer at the fight pits. This task will not take more then 2 people to complete. So there is no need for extended combat.
-The person who volunteered to help you turns off their auto retaliate and removes all gear including weapon (in other words, the volunteer sacrifices them self so you may become the champion)
- this service is for helping not harming other players. -The volunteer will wait near the entrance to insure you became champion, sometimes, due to a glitch in the system, it does not registrar the first time.

~~ Castle wars completion guide ~~

The way it works:
-First need to find empty world.
1 volunteer can help 2 players at time - it makes 2x1. And if we take what it last for 25 mins (20 mins for game and 5 mins waiting) - the best way is to make 2x1 games, unless volunteers really do not mind to do 1x1 game.
-usually player who seeks this help not very familiar with this task - so saving his life to avoid reading some big guides, please show points of interest what need to be take on mind: table with stones, catapult, the way to banner, the way back to own banner stand and how to get points.
~IF there is other players who jumped to loosing site -please ask kindly to leave - otherwise if they tries to ruin game, take banner and do not allow to get point.
~after player who need help know how to get points - lets rock! ask him to make as much points as possible - thats in case, if you lose connection and you lose spot in castle wars, the game continues and player should have some advantage. (this is just in case).
~Enjoy it! there is always something to do while you helping with this task.
~Freeloaders - do not ask to this task for several times - we are NOT FREE TICKET service.
~addition everybody are welcome in cc "fpvolunteers" as its long task, every single free helping hand is welcome
