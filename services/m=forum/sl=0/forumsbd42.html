Ranks
.............


...Gold Star: Same as Silver, yet they are given control in just about every area. They would be seriously active and would always be trying to come up with new improvements for the community. Can accept allies.

...Silver Star: Same as Bronze, yet they are given more control in the clan and are considered to be highly respected. Only the few that have proven their loyalty will obtain this rank. A very honoured rank to recieve.

...Bronze Star: Very loyal and well trusted member of Remedy. They are a very regular thread bumper and also extremely active ingame. They help monitor the clan chat whenever ingame and often help out with the forums. Usually have access to Ventrilo. Can kick people behaving inappropriately from the chat. Can run events and often attend most wars. Try to improve Remedy. Can accept/decline new recruits.

...3 Lines: An experienced Remedy member who has been with us for a while. They are very active and help out where possible a lot of the time. A regular bumper and event attender.

...2 Lines: A regular Remedy member who knows the way of Remedy and are often able to run events. May bump regularly and start to become more active within Remedy.

...1 Line: A new recruit to Remedy and still learning Remedy's system. Hopefully soon to work their way up the ranks!

...Smiley: Have not applied to Remedy or are waiting approval. Sometimes it may be because i have not met you ingame, therefore i have not yet updated your rank
