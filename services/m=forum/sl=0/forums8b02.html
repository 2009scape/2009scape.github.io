Hello, Welcome to The RSB Bulletin's 31st Thread!
The RSB Bulletin is an Active RSB newspaper that strives to give the latest news on whats happening in the RSB!

The RSB Bulletin was born April 8, 2007 by Vipers02 & Saragirl555
On April 8th, 2010 Nj became the proud owner after 3 years.
Now the thread has been passed on to "RSB Bulletin" himself.


Here at the RSB Bulletin We have many different rankings:

We Rank Warring Clans, Non-warring Clans, and a little in-between .
We dont forget Juniors and Unions though.
We Rank Leaders and RSBans; both Top and Up and Coming.
We rank Logo Artists on quality of the logos.
We give detailed Interviews and reports to inform ALL people what is going on in the RSB.

Here at The Bulletin, We dont just give away ranks, to be awarded a rank here, you have to deserve it.
So take a seat, some reports and check out the rankings! Heck, maybe you will see your name up there soon!

Enjoy!
