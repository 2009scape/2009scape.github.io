Hey Jagex and fellow Runescapers. ....during my rune scape experience i have been wealthy at some points..and poor in others. During those times where im poor, i know i have a level 64 that i dont play much with 294m on him. I really wish i had use for that money, but unfortunatly, i gave up on the account and play my main now..but sometimes i really wish i could give the money on him to my main. But i cant because it is breaking a rule so my rant is that money should be able to be traded amongst accounts by the same owner.

So with this rant comes a suggestion....I think during account creation..we should be able to make an account name..that can host however many accounts Jagex decides(i think like 5-7 is probably enough).

This account can disperce money between accounts through some sort of inventory to inventory transfer or something. To avoid scammers, accounts that can give money to eachother have to be created under the username you create to host your in game accounts. Now i know this is not a solution to real world trading..so here is my proposition for that, in order to do this, you must upon requesting for money or item transfer, list all of your security information and internet provider upon time of account creation, this encourages people to save their information for later use such as account recovery and so forth.

No one will want to give this information to a site that may or may not be a scam. There is no fool proof 100% solution to real world trading, but this is the best i could come up with for now. Anyway this is my rant/fix for the rant. So please whoever reads this agree or disagree, thank you.


This is also a petition..so sign if you agree..and leave a comment or somethin
