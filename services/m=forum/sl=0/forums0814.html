The Lords - Individual Sign up

�I'm not interested in preserving the status quo; I want to overthrow it.� -Machiavelli



So you have chosen to become one of the Lords. A most noble and wise choice. As a Lord it will be your duty to keep order in the land.

We're here to keep things the way we feel they should be, to show others that we really do know best. Once you join us you'll add to our influence and help us to gain more in future.

Mod Calm



What the Lords represent


The Lords represent order, nobility, honour, duty and tradition.

They comprise their members of knights, guards, priests, nobles, tyrants & paladins.



Things to think about once you join


Colour Scheme:If you involved in a challenge or event where you wish to represent the Lords we recommend choosing something in blue or silver. The posher the better.

Posting: Let everyone know you are a Lord by using the term 'Lord' in your signature!

Attitude: By all means act as a Lord but avoid doing so if it spoils someone elses enjoyment or thread.



This thread is for individuals to sign up to join the Lords. If you want to sign your clan up then please use the Clan sign up thread
