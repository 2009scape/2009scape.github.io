~History~


(From blog: Achievement Diary: Elite Tasks, by mod Maz)
�Back in 2006, Mod Mark and I sat down and started to develop what we then called �area tasks�. This idea was concepted and developed into the very first Achievement Diary: Karamja. This was a completely new idea within the game - not a quest, yet not a minigame or skill. It was quite an adventure finding and pushing the boundaries of what was possible at the time, and yet staying within our core ideas for what was to become Achievement Diaries: "To highlight existing content that perhaps players hadn�t used in a long time or simply did*�t know about, and to show players around an area by using existing content in the area."�
This can really answer what are diaries, so history begins:

On 08-May-2007 there was released entirely new kind of content: the Achievement Diary (Quick find code: 13-14-342-43667080). This was something new at that time.
We can find it in description of �Behind the Scenes � May� (Quick find code: 13-14-329-42183135)
Back then it was small green button in quest interface. Experience we got from it, new gem mine was something awesome. And new first diary item - Karamja GLOVES with ability of teleportation, increased experience in Brimhaven Agility Arena and better prices in Karamjas shops.

Year 2008


Next diary was released on 14-Jul-2008 along with Lumbridge update. Three Cheers for Lumbridge! (Quick find code: 13-14-527-57185321) along with tutorials and moneymaking tips we received first and only free to play Achievement Diary Lumbridge and Draynor Village. We got 3 simple but very useful uses of one RING � restoring running energy (was really useful until energy update, but it`s still good) some free low alchemy spells and unique by then cabbage teleport. What else different between this reward and other diary rewards? It has unique name. While other diaries rewards goes with location name, like Varrock armor, Lumbridge diary reward is called �Explorer's ring�.
-
On 9th December 2008 we got double diary: Falador Achievement Diary (Quick find code: 13-14-589-58043922) and Fremennik Achievement Diary (Quick find code: 13-14-587-58043914). Those who were dare to face mithril dragon (so far most deadly unsafe monster in diaries history) were able to wear Fremennik SEA BOOTS. This is only diary what is required for starting quest (Blood Runs Deep, released on 15-Dec-2009). This diary reward also provided free teleport once a day, and was able to change teleport spot. Falador diary reward Falador SHIELD is unique by that what its only diary reward what has emotion, also after 13th January 2009 update on Varrock diary it left only diary what has nothing related with teleports.
