Plagiarism is not offensive?

Hello, I would like to talk about plagiarism. What is plagiarism? It has few meanings such as; a piece of writing that has been copied from someone else and is presented as being your own work or the unauthorized use or close imitation of the language and thoughts of another author and the representation of them as one's own original work. There have been many plagiarized/copied threads in the forums. The offending player may have done it to annoy or anger the original author, or may have done it to receive some fame. But the bottom line is that it is not original. Plagiarized threads usually lead to arguments and flaming; depending on what the players, the owner, and the original author say. I, myself, have seen many players creating plagiarized threads from other player�s threads and mostly my own. I have also seen players using signatures from other players, including my signature. I think that plagiarism is not taken seriously because it is not considered a forum emergency. If plagiarism is considered thread hi-jacking, it should be explained more and be taken seriously. Plagiarism should be enforced in the forums because plagiarism leads to many flame wars and it is also very offensive to the original players.

Hello there,

I must say that this is a well thought out (and constructed) thread and I would like to commend you for your efforts.

Just to clarify, plagiarism is something that we frown upon with as much ferocity as every other serious offence. If you are confident that another player has plagiarised your original material then you should not hesitate to report this via the Forum Emergencies thread. I for one really dislike the idea of someone taxing material from another thread, thus avoiding the effort of creating their own original material.

To conclude, if you feel that someone has plagiarised your thread, then please report them. If the accusations are justified, then we will simply ask for the plagiarised material to be removed.

It really is as simple as that.

1) To be honest I don't think this is that much of an issue. Plagiarism is a bad thing, certainly, but not as bad--or as widespread--as flaming, website advertising, etc. Unless the original content is something offensive (which I should hope it isn't), a copy isn't urgent. It's bad, but not an emergency.

2) I've seen something similar before. While it's an intriguing idea in other ways, I don't think it'll do much against actual plagiarism, because people who copy/paste others' threads are generally aware that they're doing so.

3) I've seen signatures suggested before as well, and there's some discussion of them on the Already Suggested sticky. However this particular version would take, in my opinion, more effort than it's worth. Jagex moderators can and should spend their time doing something more valuable to the forums. In addition, I don't think it's really fair to "lay claim" to a signature and demand that no one else gets the chance to use anything remotely close to it.
