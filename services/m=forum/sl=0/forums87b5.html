Ranks Guide.

Leader - (Gold Star):
The leaders make a majority of the events. And also monitors the Clan Chat aswell as the Thread, leaders are usually highly active in the Clan, this rank is not given out to any regular person. They will stand out from the crowd, and show leadership, and determination too see the clan succeed. (Can Kick)

High Council - (Silver Star):
As a member of the High Council, your job is to monitor the Clan Chat and Thread. You will help make and run events and be quite active. As a Council member you can decide who returns to the clan and who doesn't. For example: If a member quits the clan and wants to join some time after. You can decide if they join again or not, and wether or not they get there old rank back, or have to start the ranks all over again. Same role as Council, just more power over who rejoins the clan. Also has more kicking ability (Can Kick)

Council - (Bronze Star)
As a member of the Council, your job is to monitor the Clan Chat and Thread. You will help make and run events and be quite active. As a Council member you can decide who returns to the clan and who doesn't. For example: If a member quits the clan and wants to join some time after. You can decide if they join again or not, and wether or not they get their old rank back, or have to start the ranks all over again. Pretty much the same job as High Council but with less power over who rejoins, and less kicking ability (Can Kick)

Member - (Corporal and Sergeant)
As a member your job is too help bump the Thread and attend events. And show loyalty and respect to your fellow clan members. If you can do these things and keep it up, your chance of being promoted to a higher rank will increase.

Trial Member - (Recruit)
This is the rank given out to all new members. You will be on this rank for your first week in the clan, you can also increase your odds by helping to bump the thread, and attending events.
