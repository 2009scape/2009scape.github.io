Rules of Evil Reavers:
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Although we are so evil, we must play by the rules so we do not lost any points .


Rule 1: No Spamming, Flamming, Trolling in the Clan Chat or thread at anytime.

Rule 2: Respect other members of the Clan chat. Treat others the way you would want to be treated... except maybe Lords and Judges

Rule 3: If we have an event that affects the entire Reaver Community, please do your best to come to that event.

Rule 4: Follow all Jagex rules in the thread and Clan Chat.

Rule 5: Ask questions. If you don't understand something, ask a question. We are always happy to help (especially getting rid of those pesky Judges and Lords)

Rule 6: Have fun! But don't let the clan down. We need everyone in order to be contender!

Rule 7: Please join the clan chat when you can for communication purposes

Rule 8: Please bump the thread before and after you log on/off

Obtaining a Rank:
Ranks in Evil Reavers is based on experience and dedication to the clan.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
No rank: Available to anyone who joins the chat.

Friends: Anyone who joins the clan officialy
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
These first three ranks are based on your total level which show your ability to skill.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Recruit: Must have 1000+ Total level

Corporal: Must have 1500+ Total level

Sergeant: Must have 1800+ Total level
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
The star ranks will be awarded to those who show dedication and show responsibility to contributing to the clan

Lientandant: Will have the ability to accept applications

Captain: Will have the ability to kick.

General: Will have the responsibility of watching over the rest of the clan chat.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
These ranks can also be obtained by the approval of other star ranks of the clan chat and possibly other members of the clan.
