Kay ran through the village, avoiding long deadly sharp spears, halberds and swords as he did. He quickly looked back to see three armoured Dragon Troopers running after him holding halberds, which sent out a bright shine with the moon light. Kay's held his katana firmly, his hand stretched back behind him so that his arm and sword were like one long pole behind him.

<<I can't run forever.>> Kay thought as he ran down a dark alleyway towards the street parallel to the one he had just been running on. Unfortunately he was blocked off by to more Dragon troopers at the other end. Kay knew he couldn't stop now so he kept running and with quicky reactions and amazing reflexes he ran across the side of the wall, kicking off onto the other side then kicking off again towards the other side. As he did he stretched his sword out in front of him and drove it through the hay and wood that were the roof of the building.

"That was close." He said as he sheathed his sword and ran across the roof, jumping onto the next, and the next. He reached a small opening in one of the roofs further down the row of houses and jumped in, looking around. He walked out of the room he was in and procedded to descend the stairs. Once he had reached the bottom he walked towards a small trapdoor in the corner of the room and climbed down.

~Summary:

This RP is based in 1137 and is in a small city called Kerung which is situated near the middle of Ancient China. A group of warriors called the Dragon Troopers have taken control of most of China, including Kerung and are on a mission to exterminate all Ninjas.

Will you fight for Control and Order as a Dragon Trooper?
Or
Will you fight for Justice and Fairness as a Resistance Ninja?

~Village Layout/Description

Kerung is a small village in The Central East of China. It mainly consists of lots of houses where the villagers live but it also has a few small shops and one big market. Near the middle of the village is one bigger building which is where the Dragon Troopers have set up their main base with their barracks, armory and all their other stuff.

The houses are all made of stone which are all different sizes which make it easy to climb because of small gaps and some bits of stone stick out more than others. The roofs are boards of wood covered with thick hay and tied onto the wood then they are placed onto the top of the house. All the houses have two floors but only a few have basements.

The Village Market is right in the middle of the town and consists of a lot of stalls with food, jewels, some weapons and other stuff on them and is normally quite full with people. They aren�t aloud to sell weapons and there are always six Dragon Troopers guarding the Market to make sure no weapons are sold there.

The Ninja Resistance hideout is near the south of the village near the end of one of the rows of houses. Underground there is the main room which is where the people talk and socialise with each other.

There are five doors in that room, one on each side but two on the North side.

The one to the south leads to the ladder that goes upstairs.

The one on the North left side is where the Superior�s office is and is also the briefing room. It has two wooden chairs, a table and another chair on the other side. There is also a blackboard and chalk opposite the door.

The one on the North right side is where the beds are and the ninjas sleep here. There are 10 bunks altogether which means there�s 20 spaces.

The left door leads to the mess hall where there is always food being served and the door to the right is where the armoury is and all the weapons are kept here.

~Dragon Warrior Weapons and Armour

Their armour is normally a red leather long sleeve shirt which can have many designs on it and they wear red trousers which are quite loose at the top but are tighter at the bottom. They wear black boots and gloves along with metal shin pads and arm pads. Covering their chest and stomach is a metal platebody which looks like a tank top made of metal. Their helmet is like a medium helmet but it reaches the middle of the neck and the top stretches out and finishes in a spike.

~Their Weapons are:

*Halberd: A long wooden pole that looks like a long axe. The blade has a lot of wavy ends which make it extremely dangerous. Useful for Slicing opponents with ease.

**Dagger: You probably know what a dagger looks like but it is a small version of a sword and is useful for a secondary weapon. The hilt and hand guard of the Dragon Warrior�s daggers are either Dark Red or Black.

**Sword: Everyone knows what a sword is so it�s quite obvious. Dragon Trooper swords have a silver blade with a red dragon carved into it. The hilt is either dark red or black, you�re choice. The hand guard is also dark red or black and the tips of it bend downwards.

*: Can only be used as a Primary Weapon
**: Can only be used as a Secondary Weapon
