1.7 *~*A brief summary on how the event will be held*~*

1. First, we will meet at Draynor bank 30 minutes before the departure times listed above. This is to discuss what materials and quests are needed,and make sure everyone is ready. Join the appropriate clan chat to keep up with the tour. Destinations and locations will be said in the clan chats listed above. Please dont be late,the bus will leave on time!

2. Next, we will start the journey of finding all 10 penguins and the polar bear. Usually this takes around 1 hour to do, but with the amount of people expected for these tours it may take longer. There will be excessive lag due to the number of people participating in this tour(friends are welcome on the tour,as well as those getting 99). Try your best to make sure you are always"following" the tour guide to stay with the group(there will be group teleports). If you should get seperated, the location of the next penguin to be spied will be told in clan chat.

3. After we have completed spying everything, we will all meet in Ardougne zoo near Larry and Chuck. Once we have all arrived, I will make a small speech, so please try to keep the chatter to a minimum(we are planning on making a video). After the speech I will start counting, for people to start getting 99. Once you declare you are "ready" you will be put at the top of the list above. If you have "ready" by your name that will be your number for the event. For example, when I count to (1) "guppy999999" will turn in his penguin points to get 99 cooking. When I count to (2) "Arnoud X122" will turn in his points for 99 cooking. All the way to the end of the list. The list is ever changing to put those that are "ready" at the top. So know your number the day of the tour.

4. Once everyone has set off fireworks and gotten 99, we will all split up to go get our capes(99k gp needed). Once everyone has their capes, we will meet at my house in Yanille for an emote/house party!
