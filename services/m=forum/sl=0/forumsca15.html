Rules of this thread

  • No flaming
    Even if you think someone's question is really daft, just answer it nicely or leave it be. There is no need to attack people on the forums.

  • No Spam
    People who spam think they are funny. Believe me you're not. Unless it's in the form of an appropriate question, any spam will be removed and action may be taken. Just enjoy the thread, don't ruin it.

  • Keep it appropriate
    The forums are accessed and viewed by people of all ages. Please remember that not every question you may have would be suitable. Please avoid questions related to:
    • Adult content
    • Real World Law breaking
    • Relationships
    • Serious medical issues
  • No Politics
    You don't need to debate someones political idealogies here.

  • No Religion
    You don't need to discuss or challenge someone's beliefs here.
