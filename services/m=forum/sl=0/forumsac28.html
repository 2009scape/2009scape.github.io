UPDATE: It's been more than a year. I think it's the least Jagex can do to at least acknowledge there is a problem. Or, y'know, our existence.

The Problem:

I recently spent 900k to get my first Crystal Bow, and I was really excited. Hoping to earn some of that cash back from a clue, I headed to the Hellhounds in the gold mine east of Ardougne. My first hits: 0, 0, 1, 0, 7�

I never hit above a 15. According to sources, at my level, the Bow�s max hit should be 18. That�s decent. Yet, after an hour of use, I never hit anywhere close.

A Rune Crossbow with Broad Bolts hits 16s consistently. My max is 23, and I hit 21s fairly often.

~*~

At its cheapest price, a Crystal Bow costs 72gp per shot. 360 per shot when you first get it!

Broad Bolts cost 60gp, and with Ava's Accumulater I can get much more than half of them back � putting the price, for comparison reasons, at about 25gp per shot. Probably much less.

And when you factor in the tactic of swapping out Crystal Bows as they degrade and weaken, you could be paying more than twice what I have listed. That could be 150 or even 200 coins a shot - that's the price of an adamant bolt, friends, which has plus THIRTHY on the Crystal Bow's maxed ranged strength.

~*~

So a Rune Crossbow gives 2 less attack bonus that a New Crystal Bow, actually surpasses it after 500 shots, does not degrade, and is more cost effective even at the Crystal Bow�s lowest price.

The Crystal Bow requires 70 ranged, 50 agility, two rather challenging quests, and 2.5mil to get it to its lowest price.

A Rune C�bow with Broad Bolts requires 61 ranged, 55 Slayer, and 40k.

My Solutions:

Originally a price reduction was a part of these ideas, but I would *much* rather increase the worth of the bow than decrease its price and leave everything else the same.

1. Raise the base Range Attack Bonus to +110, and have it reduce by no more than -3 every degrade level. Putting it at a +83 Bonus at 1/10 � still low, but much better than +64.

2. Increase the number of shots. Currently, we get 2500, but anyone whose used the C Bow in the Fight Caves knows that 2500 just doesn't last very long, especially with the Bow degrading the way it does. Coupled with a power increase, this would overpower the bow, but I wanted to throw it out there.

3. Raise the max hit (the range strength bonus). Currently, at 10/10, it is 25% of your ranged level, giving it a level 99 max of 25. At 1/10 the max hit is 20% of your level. I suggest raising this to 30% at 10/10 and 25% at 1/10 (essentially raising the hit by 5) - or maybe the max hit could even stay the same?

4. Add our lovely bow-recharging elf lady to the Lunar NPC Contact spell, so that we can recharge all our crystal equipment from anywhere - perhaps for an increased price.

_____


After running this thread for a while, several people have posted against making any change. One arguement was that the Crystal Bow is a PvP weapon, and is fine the way it is. In this sense, the Crystal Bow was the first Ancient Warrior's piece (Morrigan's, etc.) - it lasts for a very short time. But it hardly compares to the PvP timed sets in terms of stats. That's bad. It has a similar drawback, without the bonus of being uber powerful
