Combat Cup - Round 3 Results


Welcome to the Combat Cup Round 3 results thread! When your match is completed in the Cup, post your results here! When you are posting your results, please ensure that:
  • You mention what clan you represent
  • Both clan names in full, not just the initials
  • Actual results of the match
  • The quick-find code, found at the bottom of each forum thread, of the results and/or declaration topic.

If you have a dispute over the results of the match, any broken rules or a clan has not shown up to a match/not been in contact to arrange a game - let us know on Jagex Cup Disputes.
