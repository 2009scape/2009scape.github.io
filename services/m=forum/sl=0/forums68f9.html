01. Who are we?

The Superior Corporation currently Owns and Operates 4 Companies on the RuneScape Forums. These are Superior Mining Company, Superior Herblore Xpress, Superior FishMongers, and Superior Fletching Emporium. We are always looking for new places that are in need of such companies.


Why a Discussion thread?

To help make the company trade threads last longer, all discussions will take place here. It will give non trade related chatter a home of it's own. This also allows for people to communicate in ways other than a simple "hey" or "cya" in game.

02. Clan Chat Guidelines
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Clan Chat Channel- "Urc Co"

Rules of the Chat:

01. No unacceptable language
02. Do NOT Spam the channel
03. No Marketing spam (spamming while selling or buying an item)
04. No advertising for or selling of items not listed by any of the Company Divisions
05. If you are kicked, do not re-enter the chat on another account, simply wait for the hour to be over or you may be kicked for a longer period of time.
06. Treat all employees and/or customers with respect - DO NOT name, flame, or blame. DO NOT harass.
07. No breaking Jagex rules

Ranks of the Chat:

Gold Crown - Jagex Mod
Gold Star - TSC Owner
Silver Star - TSC Managers
Bronze Star - Company Managers & Chat Managers
3 Stripes - Employees who have gone the extra mile
2 Stripes - V.I.P. customers & Employees
1 stripe - new recruits

~~04. Discussion Rules~~

Keep it pleasant. Do not play the "name and shame" game. Naming another player on the thread for the purpose of blaming or shaming her/him is not allowed by the forum Code OF Conduct. If you do it, you will be politley asked to remove the name. If you do not, within a short period of time, you will be reported to forum emergencies.

This thread is for duscussions related to The Superior Corporation group of companies and any affiliates. This is not the place to conduct a discussion on the merits of barrows armour versus dragon. There are threads for that, so please use them.

~^~ 09. History ~^*

Starting around the end of September (not long after the first launching of the predecessor known as "United Runecrafters and Company *URC&C for short}), I was asked to maintain the orders list (because the previous person was unreliable) for that thread and was given the position of head thread manager, whereby I accepted applications and orders and kept the thread bumped and active. The thread died several times because it was poorly and sporadically maintained by the "founders". I was then made co-owner and also became the thread host for that first company. Two more threads were started and from inception I was "Council" for one and "Co-owner" for the other (let me repeat that: co-owner from the first day of the first posting). A "corporation" was also started, where ipso facto I was again "Co-Owner".

Again the new threads died for lack of supervision and maintainance on the part of the "founder". And once again, I revived them and kept them going. I enlisted the aid of several very capable and dedicated players who helped turn them into thriving entities. By the end of October my purported "partner" (having got himself in hot water for other actions that he felt would impact on the companies) had decided that his participation in these threads needed to be curtailed and so he bowed out of the picture and left the following on the old corporate thread to make the successorship clear to all:

~^~ 10. History ~^~

At the beginning of December, during a corporate meeting of all members it was democratically decided that the threads needed more unification to make them easily identifiable and thus "The Superior Corporation" was born out of the ashes of the old. It was also decided that all the various divisions' naming would come under this "Superior" umbrella. So slowly as old threads reached their end and new ones were created to take their place, the names of some of the divisions underwnet a transformation to what you see listed above. And through it all, some amazing people have signed up to be part of this enterprise and help carry it forward to deal with all the obstacles thrown at it. It will continue to survive and thrive because of all these dedicated and conscientious players. I tip my hat to each and every one of them and say thank you for every minute you expended on making this corporation what it is today.
