ASSIST CLAN

Assist clan. This is what you will type in to enter the clan chat channel.
Apologies if that was a little condescending but you would be surprised at how many people do not know how to enter a clan chat.

IF YOU HAVE ANY OF THE SKILLS REQUIRED TO ASSIST SOMEONE THEN PLEASE LOG INTO "ASSIST CLAN" AND HELP AS THERE IS A GREAT DEMAND FOR ALL SKILLS BUT ESPECIALLY FOR RUNECRAFT, CONSTRUCTION, FARMING, SMITHING AND HERBLORE.

PLZ GIVE YOUR ASSIST FREELY AS WE THINK THE EXP YOU GAIN IS MORE THAN ENOUGH PAYMENT.

IF YOU WANT TO CHARGE FOR YOUR ASSIST THEN THIS ISN'T THE CLAN FOR YOU. SO PLEASE DON'T WASTE YOUR TIME BY ASKING FOR PAYMENT AS BEING KICKED FROM CLAN CHAT OFTEN OFFENDS.

We are hoping that this will become a permanent clan where players can get assistance free of charge.

This is a clan for assistance on the new Ancient Effigy Distraction and Diversion.

For example:-

Player 2 needs 95 herb assist though doesn't have the lvls to offer any assist for this D&D though might be happy to stick around in cc to assist others for his lower skills.

As you can see everyone gets what they came for.

And if that wasn't plain enough then hop into "assist clan" cc and we will be happy to explain it there.

Open to anyone who needs assist or who can offer assist.

Hop in to "Assist clan" cc and let us know what you need or what you can offer

SKILLS THAT ANCIENT EFFIGY REQUIRE:

Agility or Crafting
Construction or Thieving
Cooking or Firemaking
Fishing or Farming
Fletching or Woodcutting
Herblore or Hunter
Mining or Smithing
Runecrafting or Summoning

Some skills you cannot get assit for so here are the skills which we can assist on,
Crafting,
Construction,
Cooking,
Farming,
Fletching,
Herblore,
Smithing,
Runecrafting.

As you can see the Ancient Effigies always have an option for a player to get assist if they are unable to "feed the knowledge" themselves.

Starved ancient effigies require a skill level of at least 91 to investigate. Awards 15,000XP.

Nourished ancient effigies require a skill level of at least 93 to investigate. Awards 20,000Xp.

Sated ancient effigies require a skill level of at least 95 to investigate. Awards 25,000XP.

Gorged ancient effigies require a skill level of at least 97 to investigate. Awards 30,000XP.

After feeding the Gorged effigy it will turn to dust and you will be left with an exp lamp to use on any skill you choose. The formula for the Dragonkin lamp exp is lvl^3/20.2 or in English lvl to the power of 3 divided by 20.2

I can now confirm that you can assist beyond your 30k daily limit without gaining the extra exp.

What I did was, I assisted for a lvl 95 smith and then for a lvl 97 craft, though I had only 5k left on my limit I was still able to assist the craft but only received 5k exp.
So it will be beneficial if clan members with 97 Herb, Con or RC can assist a lower lvl first such as lvl 91-95 and then assist lvl 97 and still get the 30k limit.

The reason I single out these skills is I have found that they seem to be the ones that players are finding it hard to find at a higher lvl.

As it stands you can only gain 30K exp from the assist system in a day. You can find out how long you have remaining by asking someone to req assist from you and then it will say at the bottom of the screen the time left till you can

SKILL BOOSTS

Credit to Sundeep1 for looking most of this info up for us ;^)

For those who don't have stats but are close the following boosters can be used(stews can also be used to give +1-+6 but are rather consuming to make and luck dependant):


1)Crafting-Crafting potion +3

2)Construction-teas in house depending on level of shelves, cup +1, Porcelain cup +2 and Gilded Porcelain cup +3

3)Cooking-Chefs delight +4/5 (depending on your level), Mature Chefs delight +5/6 (depending on your level)

4)Farming-Stranger plant +1 (+4% of your level), Garden Pie +3

5)Fletching-Fletching potion +3

6)Smithing- Dwarven stout +1, Mature Dwarven stout +2

7)Herblore- Greenmans Ale +1, Mature Greenmans Ale +2

Stews are:

Orange for Construction, Cooking, Crafting, Firemaking, Fletching, Runecrafting, and Smithing.

Yellow for Agility, Prayer, Slayer, Thieving, and Hunter.

Brown for Farming, Fishing, Herblore, Mining, and Woodcutting.
