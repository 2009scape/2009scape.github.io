Report system and respective staff,

I think there is a huge problem with the way the current report system works, but before I give my honest opinion (Please note this is by no standards a rant, an attack, or anything of the kind that negativity would claim to,) on the report system, I wanted to commend the work done by the staff and their moderators. I truly understand that the staff must receive thousands of reports daily, and already have in place a system to help sift out the good reports from those not. From what I�ve heard the report system (as I have zero concrete knowledge of its workings I have no true base on which this is formed from) relies on numerous reports of the same event or abuse to trip the system and enable the staff to take another look at the said abuse or the report of a player moderator. While I do not wish to undermine or deconstruct the work done by the staff in making this system work very well, I do wish to pin point one direct area of the report system, which I believe receives a heavy amount of the reports, and place a suggestion on how it could be made better, and work to boost the moral and reinstitute some of the lost community support for the report system.


The Problem- Inappropriate User Name Section

What I am finding personally, and as I talk to people I interact with is that the report system for inappropriate user names relies on the �quantity over quality� idea in determining which reports should be looked at. While a majority of the reports may be unjustified in their reports there are a good portion of them that go unchecked because they are single reports that get hidden within the flood of thousands, most likely hundreds of thousands of reports daily. This is truly tarnishing the prestige of a report system that has held its own, and has done a dang good job for the past 6 years I�ve personally played and had experiences with.

My Suggested Solution- Moderators

Because I do not know the inner workings of the report system this is more of a skeleton idea rather than a concrete body, but the idea is the same; change needs to come. What I do assume is that the reports get filtered into different areas based on which abuse was said to be committed. Now instead of reading maybe half of them, or reading them all within a two month period, why not give a quota to some desired moderators to scan them over and report back which ones seem justified to the staff for further scrutiny? I know personally speaking I do a bit of 99 questing and I am performing skills that you click and watch with tons of time that could be used to do more productive things. I�m not saying that the staff needs to go out and hire more moderators just for this purpose, but if you were to ask moderators if they would like to perform this while they are doing their off skills or commonly known �afk� skills I am positive the results would be jaw dropping.


Test Case- (All numbers are fictional)

Moderator A decides to pick up the cause and glance at reports for the staff.
Moderator A is given 200 reports of supposed inappropriate usernames and is asked to determine which are justified and which need to be looked at again by staff.
Moderator A determines that 124 of the reports are in fact justified in the bounds of the rules and regulations set up by Jagex and informs the staff that they believe these 124 reports should be looked at again.
Moderator A spent a total of 2 hours of fishing doing this for the staff and asks for another quota for the next day.

Conclusion- Why Bother?

In a short period of time a moderator can go through a report and check to see if a name is inappropriate with regards to the rules and regulations. Although the moderator has no power to change the name, instead of 200 reports going to the staff (and if we expand the numbers to make it more realistic) 5,000 user name reports weekly, we can acquire a more manageable number such as 3,000. While 3,000 user names which can be determined by the rules and regulations (those of the moderator) that are inappropriate the final decision on whether or not the change is made will still be up to the Jagex staff themselves.
What I am trying to prove here is that players respect the game and choose to report players as they are inclined to do so by the rules and regulations to keep the game fun for all. When people are allowed to name their characters �IlIilf, Igotta bonur, and Fuc4 U PK ME� and reports have been filed close to a month ago with zero change in the name either my idea of what an inappropriate name is incorrect and names like that are �cool� now, or the report system needs a boost from players willing to use their time and make the game better.
