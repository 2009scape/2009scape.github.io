NOTE: This thread is regarding an existing piece of in-game content and the website and, thus, can be posted here. Don't move it please.

JMod Fnord post on p. 6.

OK, I think that everyone who has looked in R.U. in the past 2-3 weeks has seen the massive furball regarding the Flagstaff. I'm going to outline the problem and post the possible solutions here. I am completely willing to add new ones, so if you have a good problem/solution, sharing is caring!

Problem 1: The Flagstaff is in violation of the RWT Rule's prohibition on exchanging in-game items for non-in-game items/services.

Problem 2: Any player that redeems a Flagstaff technically can be banned without warning at any point. It is unclear if this potential still exists if the Rules are updated in an ex post facto manner.

Solution 1: Modify the Rules to make an exception for items distributed by JaGEx.

Solution 2: Modify the distribution system so that the same item is available to all players for free.

NOTE 2: The discussion of whether it fits the JaGEx definition of RWT is being tabled on the assumption that any solution that fixes Problem 1 will fix that issue as well.

NOTE 3: The RWT Rule is as follows:

"Game items must only be exchanged for other items/services within the game. Exchanging game items for items or other benefits in other online games, real-life money or other real-life benefits is not allowed"

If you disagree with my conclusion, please stick to the Rule, as that is what this thread is about.

Counter-arguments.

The Flagstaff is free/a gift. You arent really paying for it, you just get it along with the ticket!

Response: Receiving a Flagstaff is contingent upon another purchase, and thus is not free. Changing the meaning of the words "free" and "gift" to allow them to be contingent upon the purchase of something or performance of a task would completely rob them of meaning.

The Flagstaff doesnt give any advantages, so its not RWT!

Response: There is no requirement that the item give your account and advantage, only that it advance your charactor. Since the RWT Rule FAQ DEFINES the acquisition of ANY item as an advance, that argument is completely erroneous.

Following your logic, buying membership would be RWT, so you must be wrong!

The RWT Rule does not forbid the purchase of in-game services or access to items, which membership is, so my logic would do no such thing.

JaGEx owns everything, so ownership of the actual item never changed!

The Rule obviously is using "exchange" as shorthand for "exchange control of". If not, it would be impossible to exchange anything and violating the RWT Rule would be impossible.
