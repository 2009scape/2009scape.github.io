Hosted by [Physid], this thread is credited to Lucid(Gop World)

***Please be in Gop World cc to be paired up***

::: | - Rules - | :::

- You may only enter on one account.
- At least one tournament judge must attend your match.
- Cheating of any kind will not be tolerated.
- There will be no defending in this tournament.
- Matches must contain a colour swap.
- If a disconnection occurs, the match resumes where it left.


The tournament will be a knock-out - Beat your competitors, and you're in to the next round!

Simple and easy.

Competitors will be matched based on their agreement to world preference, once there is a world chosen, THERE WILL BE NO SWITCHING.

Games will consist of two matches, a match being 6 altars, THERE WILL ALSO BE NO MEMBER WORLD. A colour swap MUST occur, and the game must be attended by a judge. No other watchers are allowed, unless agreed to by both competitors.

The tournament will work in terms of 'rounds'. If you beat your opponents in Round 1, you can only verse other competitors who are in Round 2.



Results will be posted by judges.

Information For Judges
������������������

To ensure your activity remains high enough for the game to continue, please use barriers in a discrete manner.

You MAY touch to gain tokens, however, only do so if it will not affect the result of the altar. If you need more information on 'touching', please ask me in the clan chat.

Please keep a record of the game results, as well as the scores of each altar.

Application will start below, and game will start when brackets are full, when it's full there will be no more signing
