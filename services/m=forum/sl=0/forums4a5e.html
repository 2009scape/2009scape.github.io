��,�,��Notes and Guidelines on the clan ��,�,��
����������������������������������
The clan was first founded back on November the 15th 2008, since then it has changed names and leadership, it was first called the Imperial Triad and carries on some of the same fundamentals of the original clan.

Upon Joining the clan you will have been expected to have read the rules and understood how denarii work (the clans currency). Your first week in the clan is symbolised by a friend rank, showing members that you may not understand some of the rules/ procedures. The friend rank is used as a trial rank to check if you are suited to the clan and vise versa.

If there is a problem, dont hesitate to post up a notice if you want to leave, we understand that different clans suit different people. thanks.


��,�,�� The Rules ��,�,��
����������������
1. Follow Runescapes and Forums Rules
2. Give EVERYONE respect
3. No multi clanning, ask in regards to exceptions.
4. log into our clan chat (til chat) as much as possible.
5. Keep up to date with the forums, which includes bumping, excessive bumping isnt required or wanted. 1-5 bumps a week will suffice.
6. Read all the rules and guidelines of the clan.
7. There are exceptions to every rule, especially this one.

��,�,��Denarii��,�,��
�������������

Denarii is a currency, to gain ranks within the clan. Denarii can be used in the clan, as an insentive to to participate in useful activities to benefit the clan.

How Denarii are gained:-

Hosting an event + 3
(Must have 5+ attendancies and an event report posted up)
Attending an event + 2
Posting regularly + 1
(maximum of 2 Denarii, must be spaced atleast a day apart)
Being generally helpful (varies)
Posting information of change in combat member's list. +1 denarii.
informing clan of name change +1
Recruiting someone +1 (denarii will be added after trial period and the new applicant must state your name in application)

-


How Denarii are lost:-
Breaking any of the clan rules - (depends on how serious)
Not reporting a kick - 7+ denarii
