Tech Community Feedback








Hi all,

Thanks for visiting the Technical Support Forums.

We really do hope that you have an enjoyable and trouble free experience when playing the game.

We realise that on some occasions players can experience unexpected issues and therefore call on the expertise and support of the technical support community, and as Jagex, and the RuneScape Technical Team, we wish to provide a good presence in what truly feel is a fantastic community to ensure you can get back into game or find a quick solution to your problem.

I have created this thread as we are looking for your constructive feedback on the level of technical support you receive and anything further you think we could do or that that you would like to see within Tech Support.

Please do not post technical queries on this thread!

Feel free to post!

Thank you,

~Mod Ac
