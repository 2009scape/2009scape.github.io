.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.||How to Play: Technical Rules||:.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.:.

(Don't worry about these until you know the basics!)

- You may steal ONE BASE PER AT BAT

-You may only steal when the pitcher does not have the ball or is not on the mound. The mound is 5 spaces away from the strike zone and the 2 middle spaces.

-If the ball is caught when the batter is on the same square as the catcher, it's a foul ball.

-The batter has to be on the square in front of the catcher to get a hit. It doesn't matter if he touches the ball.

-It's only an out if the fielder has the ball equipped.

-The pitcher has 35 seconds to throw the ball until auto ball.

-The catcher cannot leave his row. If there is a misclick he has 5 seconds to get back in.

- 4 innings, 3 outs, 3 strikes, 4 balls

- When running home, it counts as a run as long as you're not on whatever square the catcher is (the catcher has to tag you, nomatter what.)

-During games, you are encouraged to politely and respectfully challenge calls made by umps. However, excessive arguing is UNACCEPTABLE. You get two strikes before ejection.
