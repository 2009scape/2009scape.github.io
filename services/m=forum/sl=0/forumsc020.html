13-Jul-2010 23:07:56
Awesome idea, but just beware of taking data from anyone, and the bias that could be included in that. I've run a lot of threads logging kills and trying to approximate drop rates. I've done it for: TT Rewards, Draconic Visages, and Unique Slayer Drops. They've all been hugely successful in the Item Discussion Forum, part of that was the structure in the threads.

Keep who you take data from right now just to people that you trust. Otherwise you'll only get the anomaly reports from people you don't know. You'll see a lot of people posting ridiculous streaks with no drops, and lots of people posting with say, 3 effigies in one task. If you'd like to enlarge this thread in the future, and make it a more established project, then I can direct you to some of my threads that you can use for a template if you'd like.

I'll definitely be paying attention to this thread in the future
