Now melee obviously has the advantage in defense stats.

Weapons, Ranged needs to fletch and cut arrows, whilst melee can bind a weapon and go on. (Yes you can bind 125 arrows, but that isn't even enough for a small dungeon)
Also the best arrows give stats even weaker than rune arrows, and you can't fletch these. The only thing ranged is good for is Lexicus runewirhgt and skeletal horde somewhat.

Also since ancients isn't used in the spellbook magic is severely underpowered, magic can hit 300 SOMETIMES on forgotton warriors, but melee easily does the same. Mages need half an inventory of food, but due to having the lowest defense stats they also need the most food, which doesn't fit in their inventory along side all the other items.

Melee also has ALOT more weapon types, even stronger than weapons outside of deamonheim. Ranged gets weaker weapons, arrows and armour, magic basically gets the same.

Oh and resources, there's EASILY 3 ore rocks in one room, and almost always at least 1 ore rock over tier 7+ in a room.
Mastyx are somewhat uncommen, and usually spinomastyx species, and well magic plants are pretty rare to find good ones of.
