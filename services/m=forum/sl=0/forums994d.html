I recently was runecrafting astrals and I began to wonder how come those with higher than the required level to runecraft double astral and nature runes don't have the potential to get triple runes even though one can't achieve the level in game for triple runes.

Jagex has essentially already done this to deaths, laws, and bloods giving a chance of double runes after their required runecrafting level despite their being no achievable level where one could runecraft double laws, deaths, and bloods. What I ask is why this same thing isn't applied to astrals and nature runes; if one has above the level required to craft double runes they should be able to have a chance a triple runes. It would also give higher runecrafting levels a greater purpose for training for and crafting these particular runes.

Jagex, allow runecrafters a chance at triple runes for astral and nature runes after the required level to craft double of the given rune just like they have done for double laws, deaths, and bloods.

- Jemmygrove (page 1) has suggested that cosmic runes have the same problem also.
-I own u 4ev (page 1) also suggested make this an universal update meaning have it applied to all runes such as waters, airs, earths, and all other runes with level caps.
