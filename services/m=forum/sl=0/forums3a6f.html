Monday, 28 September 2009 - Hey everyone. Matt here. I've been extremely busy with school lately and would like to apologize for not keeping up with this thread as much as I should have been. First, I want all 80+ stats and 70 Slayer which should come soon and to afford 99 Prayer. It's nothing new I suppose, since everyone always seems to ask me "How much money do u got?" or the most common of them all, "Pray level???" With the future username/screenname update to come, I might consider changing my username to something more subtle and mysterious rather. Tell me what you think.

Q * Prayer level?
A * SEE HIGHSCORES PLEASE IF YOU SEE ME INGAME! Lol, I cannot stress this enough. I'm honestly asked this *several* times during each play session on a normal day. Also, my Prayer level is listed in my Goal section of this thread if you haven't noticed. xD

Q * Can you add me to your Friend's List please?
A * Sorry, but I do not add anybody to my Friend's List unless I truly get to know them first. I add people for *who* I am, not what I *have*. Items are just pixels, skill levels are just numbers, and gold pieces are just another item. However, friends can last a lifetime. There's a real person behind each and every account; think about it.

~ Frequently Asked Questions #2 ~
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Q * OMG lolololololol NOOB u only got one 99 hahahahahhahahahah.
A * I don't play RuneScape solely for grinding to 99 in skills. RuneScape is about much more than achieving 99 in skills tbh, which is why I have so many other goals listed on this thread. Sure, it'd be nice to max out all of my skills one day. Then again, that's every skiller's dream. I just want 99 in skills I enjoy for now. I like to keep my skills balanced instead of having five 99s and all my other skills low. Maybe one day I'll get more 99s; let's see what the future brings?

Q * What combat level will you be when you get 99 Prayer?
A * Level 15 Combat.

Q * What method are you going to be using to train Prayer to 99 when you can afford it?
A * I'll be using the classic Gilded Altar offer method in POHs, which gives 350% the experience you'd get if you had buried the bone normally if both incense burners are lit with marrentill herbs. This is 252 XP per Drag Bone compared to 72 XP. You can find "Open" Gilded Altars that other players will let you use in the Events Forum.

Q * How do you build one of these fancy altars?
A * If you want to build your own altar, you'll need a Chapel Room first of all, as well as build two incense burners along with the altar to get the full 350% bonus. If you don't light the incense burners you'll only get 250% experience per bone. You'll also need 75 Construction (or 72 with Teak Shelves 2 Gilded Tea's +3 temporary bonus, or 69 with Crystal Saw and teak shelves 2 Tea's +3 temporary bonus). To build an altar you'll need the following materials: 4 Gold Leaves, 2 Marble Blocks, and 2 Bolts of Cloth. Don't forget your hammer and saw either, which you probably won't do if you have the high level to build one of these anyway.
