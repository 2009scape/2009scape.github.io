You can get gout tubers from two main ways.

First, you can play the minigame. The gout tuber event happens in the same way that you would find a gem rock under the brush, so get chopping! Make sure you bring a spade along with you as this is needed to pick the gout tuber should you get the event. This method can take some time to do as the gout tuber is a rather uncommon event, so be prepared to spend a decent amount of time (an hour or two maybe).

Second, you could guy this item in the GE. Last I checked, it was around 35k in there (this was some time ago!), so it isn't too expensive. Provided you didn't just skip to this paragraph, you should know by now that the gout tuber is kinda hard to get, however, so it might not buy immediately. Leave the offer in for a few days and there is a very high chance of getting one. Also, you should note that these are often sold on the forums at a much higher price due to junk trades. Last I checked, you'll see offers in the 200k-500k range for this.

Finally, just as two warnings, you do not want goutweed for this! That is actually the herb that corresponds to the gout tuber, which is actually a seed. Also note that, despite it being a seed, the left click option is "eat." You do NOT want to spend 500k on one and then eat it.
