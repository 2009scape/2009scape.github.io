Hello, all!

I've been working recently on the first development stages of a new quest, and this one has particular meaning to me.

As such, I've taken the liberty of writing a devblog post on what it's all about, which you can read here.

Feel free to comment or to ask questions in this thread, and I'll check back later on...

cheers,
Moltare Erda


Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

Anything about the development timescale, required levels or quest content.

At this stage it would be unwise to commit to too much detail. I mostly just wanted to share the gist of my idea with you all.

Will there be more information about the Void Knights/the pest threat/Guthix?

That was a major facet of my wanting to do this series, so I should jolly-well hope so.

Is this a new storyline, or a continuation of others?

This is a complete new storyline, although the seeds for it have already been planted and you will have met many of the major players.

Who is the old enemy? Is it a Nomad sequel?

You'll have seen him before, too. It's not Nomad, though. In fact, I bet you'll never guess who it is...

Will the series be mostly plot or mostly action?

I don't see a conflict here let's have plenty of both over the course of the series.
