Ultimately my goal is to have 137 combat with an untrimmed HP cape. Alas, this goal has almost slipped out of my grasp. With the release of the dungeoneering skill, I've used up nearly 1m total combat xp training it. This xp was originally allotted to getting myself charms.

Now my only options are: A) Spend 120+ hours at soul wars healing people to obtain charms. or B) Wait until the next Bonus XP Weekend and use all the charms I have banked to get 96 Summon.

So since option A is completely unappealing, I've chosen option B. And while I wait until the next Bonus XP Weekend I've been training a few of my 'skiller' skills.



Another goal of mine was leveling 'a bunch' of skills to 99 all within minutes of each other. Since I never defined how many 'a bunch' is, I keep adding more skills to the list that I want to level then. Currently I have 8 level 98 skills, with a few getting close to there.

The skills I currently have planned to get 99 at the same time are: Attack, Strength, Defense, Range, Magic, Prayer, Cook, Fletch, Smith, Craft. And potentially: Farming, Thieving, Construction.



I'm also quite hooked on the Shattered Heart minigame. I started collecting rocks as soon as it came out, and usually am able to complete a statue within 2-3 days of completing the previous. My goal is to have the complete statue in my POH when I get all of my level 99s.


My last goal is to obtain my own set of Third Age Mage robes. The set I'm wearing in my adventure log picture I had bought for an hour from my friend solely for the picture. I'll be getting the money for this by catching Kingly Implings. (Don't post asking if I'm looking for scouts or ask where I hunt, since that is not allowed in this section.)
