1. Story

Gather now all of you folk interested in hearing one of Gielnor's many stories......

Long ago, the elven nation lived all across Runescape, as numorous in population as humans were today. They were considered family at this time, but dark time came upon all as it was rumored that a new rune alter had been found. This alter seemed to have great power surging from it, and both the elves and humans took greed at this. Many claimed that the alter held the power of Zamorak, and those who could get to it would be the strongest people across Gielnor. The humans believed the elves may take this power as their own, so they fought the elves in battle, eventually driving them to the deadly forest of Isafadar. As time came and past, things about the humans and elves changed. The elves never left their forest, and the humans soon forgot about the elves, and everything seemed at peace. But the elves wanted revenge on the humans for forcing them away, at whatever cost.
********************************
King Lathas stared at the moonlit sky. His brother had been traveling near the forest of Isafadar, and he felt jealous of his brother. Lathas had never had as much control over Ardougne as his brother, and he knew that with him away he could control Ardougne by himself. He had heard stories of the elven nation wanting revenge on the humans, and if they still existed, he wanted their help in gaining power over Adrougne. Maybe he could give them access to something in return....
*******************************
The elves may have never forgiven the humans, therefore they kept trying to seek access to the death alter that the humans had long forgotten. This alter stood below West Ardougne, and only two humans knew of it: Lathas and Tyras. Lathas knew that the power of the elven nation was unmatchable to others, so as he met the leader of the elves, they made a deal: they would kill King Lathas's brother, but Lathas must give them West Ardougne in return. 2. Rules. I ask that you obey these throughout the sessions for others' entertainment as well as your own.

1. Obey all Jagex rules.
2. no godmodding. you cannot control what someone does in real life, so i dont see how it is different here. you must have permission to godmod.
3. no auto-hitting. if you are fighting an npc, a gamemaster will control how the npc fights.
4. No spamming the forum.
5. No powerplaying.
6. Keep Language clean please
7. Keep romance pg-13
8. Have fun

3. Alignments

Good- someone who works for what they believe is right for all and will strive to achieve their bliefs

neutral good- someone who does not follow a side of evil but must be pushed at first into working for their beliefs

neutral- one who may either follow a good or evil path at times, but generally doesn't become involved in worldly affairs, such as war.

neutral evil- one who does not follow a good side, but commits acts of evil for self benifit.

Chaotic evil- one who may enjoy the suffering of others as well as commit acts of evil for another's downfall

4. Race options.

1. Human- people live all over Gielnor and can use all 3 forms of conbat, but could be weak to all 3 forms as well.

2. elf- creatures that are like humans but have not lived across Gielnor for sometime, they generally use range and mage.

3. half elf- creatures that are both human and elven, generallly looked down upon by the elves for not being pure elf.
