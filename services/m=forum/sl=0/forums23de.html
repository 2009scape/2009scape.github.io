Sorry if this is rant-like, but I'd like it to stay in suggestions. If it has to be moved, at least move it to Constructive Criticism.

Can you not just make PvP in general like it used to be? I'm not talking about the drop system (although it's horrible), I'm talking about the way pking used to be. It was simply more fun.

I'm tired of having a target, or only being able to pk in 7 worlds. I'm tired of not being able to protect my item in worlds where I don't want a target. I'm tired of getting worse drops because I teleport when I see 20-man teams. It's just beyond unnecessary. I want PvP to be like it used to be (for the most part): no targets, protecting items, and the wilderness in every world.

***TL;DR***: So basically, I'm suggesting the wild like it used to be before 2008, but with the same failed EP system we have now.

Below, I have arguments as to why it was better in the olden days, and I also give solutions on how to make them like the old wild.

Reasons with solutions:

1. This new style was just unnecessary

What made you implement the idea of targets? Are they really supposed to stop RWT? Cause they don't. All they do is make me mad when they won't fight, or they want a dds dm, or they stay in multi. I shouldn't be assigned to kill someone, I should kill who I please and not have to worry about a target. And also, why on earth do you only limit BH worlds to the G/E, Edgeville, and the wild? It is beyond annoying. If I die with barrows gloves or my prayer book I have to log out to buy them back. If I'm multying and I get a target, I have to log out to go get on lunar. Why not let players go wherever on these worlds, while keeping only the wilderness dangerous?

SOLUTION: Remove targets. They're unnecessary and are just a distraction. Let people go anywhere.

2. Not enough worlds

Public clan chats can now rule PvP just because there are so few worlds. If I try to multi with 2 of my friends in world 18, we get rushed by 50 failures of jaja, so we hop to 65. But oh wait, then there's another huge legit team in this world, so we can't pk here either. So then we bank our good stuff and head to a non +1 world, and yet again there's another huge team so we teleport. And since we teleported from a 3 vs 20 battle we deserve to lose 20% of our EP. And we weren't even being attacked, it's just the teleport animation lasts too long and we got hit 2 seconds after we really teleported.

SOLUTION: Make the wilderness dangerous in every world (make pking in the wilderness available in every world). But hey, I know the wilderness wasn't originally made for killing other players (pking hasn't taken place in the wild from the beginning, right?), so for those complainers there could be several worlds where the wild is safe so you can do clues, use the abyss, etc. This gets rid of the over-population of the wild, especially in multi. Small teams can finally pk and have fun. World 18 would still be where everyone edge pk's though, like it used to be.

3. Kinda like #1, these features don't prevent RWT (reason wild was removed), they're just annoying

-Teleporting while in combat = loss of EP. I still honestly don't know why you put this in here, but I think it had to do with ep'ers? Well it hurts pkers a lot more than it hurts ep'ers. Also, teletabs don't work. So if I teleport to Camelot, I get sent to Edgeville.... but if I click a hometab to my house it wont work...?

-Auto-pro item/no pro item. Once again, this was trying to get rid of ep'ers yet it affects the pker's more.

-Auto-skull. Yet again, it was put in due to ep'ers, and what do ya know? It affects the pkers more.

SOLUTION: We shouldn't lose EP when we teleport and we should be able to use protect item. Also no auto-skull, keep it like it used to be.

4. Size of the wild

First off please remove the crater if you are to bring back the old wild's size. This somewhat ties in with #2 in that it's harder to find other people, so it's actually a thrill when you find another team.

SOLUTION: Re-size the wild. It doesn't prevent RWT... I remember Jagex saying that they did this so RWT'ers couldn't go off in some remote place and do their business. But now with the EP system this doesn't make a difference.

5. Now, if all of the above SOLUTIONS were to be brought in, the wild would be the exact same as it was before 2008, all except the EP system. Unfortunately, I believe the EP system will have to stay with my new suggestion, because otherwise, RWT would be possible since people could just get unlimited kills, and it would be even easier with every world having the wilderness available. Sorry, but for now I cannot think of a better drop system than the EP system.


Conclusion

I believe that all of the above would make a much more enjoyable PvP experience. The only thing "bad" about the true wild was the drop system, nothing else. I don't see why Jagex had to change it so drastically beyond that. None of the new tweaks to PvP prevent RWT except the EP system; therefore, I'm keeping it in my suggestion. Although it's not EXACTLY like the old wild, it would please some of the old players for the most part. We could still keep BH worlds and PvP worlds for those that like them better than my idea. Don't want to be unfair to others.

Thanks for reading. Please post your feedback.
