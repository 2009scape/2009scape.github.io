Looking For A Corp Beast Team!


Want some buddies to help take down the toughest monster in Gielinor? Then this is the thread for you!

You can use this sticky to find a group/member for a "Corp Beast" Team. If you want to fight on of the other bosses around RuneScape, use the appropriate thread.

If you are looking for a team we ask that you don't post more than once every ten minutes, this is to avoid spamming the thread unnecessarily.

Remember that it's important not to advertise another player's Clan Chat for privacy reasons. If you need a Clan Chat in your post, why not use your own?

To make it really clear exactly what you're looking for we advice that you post using one of the following templates:

Looking for a Team Member:
Date and Time:
Location and World:
Preferred Combat Type:
Additional Requirements/Information:

Or

Looking for a �Corp Beast� Team:
Date and Time:
Location and World:
My level is:
My main combat type is:
Additional Requirements/Information:

It is best to check the last few pages first as those are the newest request posts and you may find that the player or team you are looking for is already waiting for you!

To make it easier for other players to find the person or team that they are looking for we ask that you edit your post to "Team/Member Found" if you are no longer looking to team up.

We hope that these threads will make it easier for you to find a boss team, and to claim your deserved rewards!

Good luck brave adventurers!

Note: there seem to be a few nasty players who take pleasure in spamming this thread. If you do this, you're likely to lose your posting privileges for a few days. You have been warned! Don't ruin this thread for everyone else.
