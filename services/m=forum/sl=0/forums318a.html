-Advanced Races. Page 184, Valkians-

Unlike most items, a Valkian's plating and weapons were made of Ruvirium, a metal that most races were unaware of. Dragon armour could simply shatter under a lance, and even Godswords were hard pushed to get through it. Ruvirium could be broken with standard weaponry, providing that the surface area of the weapon was small, and alot of force was put into the blow. However, Ruvirium is incredibly deadly when mixed with the natural abilities of Valkians.

Valkians were, and mostly still are, the creations of the first Creator. Although they are still mortal, they have an incredible ability of self-regeneration, even if something as small as a flake of skin is left. They can also regenerate what they were wearing at their time of death.
