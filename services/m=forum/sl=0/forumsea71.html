Hello, All, and, Welcome to Roso's Tribe of Mis'fits, We are a Community of Gamers, that have Joined Together and made Our Own Little World Inside the Game of Runescape. We are a Community of Friends, and, Family, Who, In-Game Work Together for the Greater Good of Our World. In Our World, You can buy, sell or barter goods, Made by Players. We like to Train Our Skills, and Work on Quests, to Improve Our Character and make It a More Useful Part of the Community, to Help the Tribe Grow into a Strong Organization. Because...

In the World of Runescape, Where The Kings and Queens, Rule... The Days Of Knighthood are Still Alive... Truth, Honour, Loyalty, Justice, and Courage, are the Main Stays of The Game... We Affectionately Call RuneScape.

Roso's TRIBE of Mis'Fits, Offers A Little Something For Everyone, and For ALL Ages. Becomming A Part Of The TRIBE, means That You Become a Part of a Growing Family that Likes to do Many Things, and Even Have Fun in a Friendly Environment. Our Tribesmen & Women, Often Enjoy a Good Conversation as well as Participating in Functions, Or Exploring the Realm of RuneScape.

Teamwork, Is Just One of Our Goals, With the Introduction of Our Form of Military, The WARRIORS OF ROSO'S Tribe, Is Born. A Selective Force with Duties and Functions With-in The World of Roso's Tribe... Only Members Of Our Community Are Allowed Access To-" The WARRIORS." Elected By WARRIORS, and Given The HONOURED Status Bestowed Upon A WARRIOR. These Respected Tribesmen & Women, Stand United
