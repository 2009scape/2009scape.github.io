Desert StrykeWorm
Amount: 164
BestDrop: High amounts of Pure essence, Snap dragon seeds.
Clue Scroll: yup!

- Desert stryke worms were a great task. Easy to kill,and they barley do any damage... as long as you pay attention and watch out for the special which hits 300. Other then that.. fun task, high amount of gp gained from drops. PS you should invest in an Enchanted water tiara for this even though u sacrifice the slayer helmet, it makes the task go buy much quicker and easier.
