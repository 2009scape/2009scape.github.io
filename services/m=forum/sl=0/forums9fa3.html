Hello, and welcome to the forums of Seekingglory. We are a clan looking for great people who are mature and enjoy the company of others while on their RuneScape journey. As you may, or may not know, we are a clan based on respect, and maturity of the clan members. We try to provide a friendly, helpful atmosphere where you can hang out while you play; A place where you can offer or receive ideas and tips and share your goals and dreams. We are a close knit community of friends and we invite you to join us. We have several planned events a week while there are several unplanned events from time to time. Rules
������
1) First and foremost is RESPECT!
2) Join the clan chat as often as possible
3) Attend clan events as often as possible.
4) Bump the thread(s) with something positive
5) Do not ask for rank you must earn it.
6) No multi-claning. (unless a soulwars clan)
7) No pointless arguments.
8) No politics what so ever.
9) If you don't have anything nice to say, just don't say it.
10) When asked to stop something, stop immediately no more than 2 warnings will be granted before being booted.

Home World 22
Our clans home world is 22. Although it is not required of you to play in this world, it is where the majority of our clan members play and where most of the events will take place. Please enjoy each others company. Have fun, support each other and help lift each other up in their achievements


Rules for Membership-
Keep the cc clean- please limit you explicit language.
It is unneccesary.No sx or political or racial discussions or comments.
Join cc every time you log in. Bump this forum at least 2 times per week.
Attend all events that you are able to or comfortable with .
Do not ask for rank up. To earn a rank increase you must be an outstanding member who deserves recognition. Example- A person who does not start arguments or belittle others, who is friendly and helpful to others , who attends events without complaint on a regular basis.
If you have a friend who you want to reccomend for clan membership have them log in and leave an application
