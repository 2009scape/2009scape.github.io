~ Weekly RP Night ~
"There is no better way to drink a beer than with good company!" - Unknown Dwarf

Finding your fellow Roleplayers is not always easy. A lot of roleplayers form smaller groups and like to run self-contained stories without expanding into the wider community. Whilst this is awesome, we'd love to see more people getting involved in Roleplaying, whether completely new or those who just never knew it happened in RuneScape.

The Weekly RP Night is a chance for *all* roleplayers in the Community to come together and RP, make new friends and possible find new groups to join.

Whilst we fully support RP Night, and a Jagex Moderator may pop along to RP themselves,this is more of an opportunity for the community to get together. It's not strictly about J-Mod interaction.

RP Night takes places as follows;


World 42
10pm - 2am GMT
Every Friday



In terms of where we want to give you a number of suggestions but also suggest you feel free to wander and explore the world to meet different people and roleplayers.

Our suggested locations are;


The Jolly Boar Inn
North East of Varrock center
--
The Green Arrow Pub
South East of Ardounge



RP Night is a really great opportunity to meet other roleplayers and get involved in Roleplaying if it's something you have always been interested in.

Please feel welcome to come along if you can, please encourage your roleplaying friends to come along too.
