The Time Conversion Thread

Welcome to the "Time Conversion Thread", kindly brought to you by Laurencio2. Here you will find all the information you need to help you get to the right place at the right time, no matter where you are in the world!

Please remember that Jagex is based in the United Kingdom and, as such, we use Greenwich Mean Time or British Summer Time (depending on what time of the year it is!).

5. Introduction
�����������
.� � Hi all and welcome to The Time Conversion Thread! This thread has been created to assist everyone in converting times from any place, to any place, with as little confusion as possible over what timezone someone is in, and whether that person's timezone has changed. Previously, the only conversions available to us was a simple chart showing GMT offsets of a few cities around the world. This, didn't show differences in different countries, and also did not take into account Daylight Savings. This caused the vast majority of RuneScape players to convert times up to approximately two hours out from the time it should have been.

.� � I have been converting times for people for approximately two years now, so have a lot of experience in knowing where to find the right information, so the information found here *should* be correct. I update this thread whenever I get a chance, and try to around time-zone change time. I've tried to compile the information in an easier format to find what you need, and have high-use information on the first page. Currently, a lot of the thread is under re-construction, and you can find the old posts on pages 3-5.

.� � One of the most common mistakes people make when converting timezones, is assuming the meaning of GMT. GMT, or Greenwich Mean Time, is a time which does NOT change throughout the year to accomodate for any daylight savings changes. Greenwich is a place in London, where all timezones are measured from, as well as lines of Longitude. During the UK Summer (and parts of Spring and Autumn), the UK use BST or British Summer Time, which is GMT+1. Often on the Runescape forums, I've noticed people refer to BST as GMT, causing many conversions to be wrong. This thread will use the *real* GMT as a base for all conversions, as it is easier. Please make sure that you remember that UK Time and GMT are different for half of the year

Please note that this event runs over a timezone change (daylight savings begins in North America)



North American Conversions:

Atlantic: Begins 8AM AST Friday, Ends 9AM ADT Monday
Eastern: Begins 7AM EST Friday, Ends 8AM EDT Monday
Central: Begins 6AM CST Friday, Ends 7AM CDT Monday
Mountain: Begins 5AM MST Friday, Ends 6AM MDT Monday
Pacific: Begins 4AM PST Friday, Ends 5AM PDT Monday
Alaskan: Begins 3AM AKST Friday, Ends 4AM AKDT Monday


European Conversions:

Eastern: Begins 2PM EEST Friday, Ends 2PM EEST Monday
Central (majority): Begins 1PM CEST Friday, Ends 1PM CEST Monday
Western (UK): Begins 12PM GMT Friday, Ends 12PM GMT Monday


Pacific Conversions:

New Zealand: Begins 1AM NZDT Saturday, Ends 1AM NZDT Tuesday
# Eastern: Begins 11PM AEDT Friday, Ends 11PM AEDT Monday

QLD: Begins 10PM AEST Friday, Ends 10PM AEST Monday
NT: Begins 9:30PM ACST Friday, Ends 9:30PM ACST Monday
Japan, Korea: Begins 9PM JST Friday, Ends 9PM JST Monday
