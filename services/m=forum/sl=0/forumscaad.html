But that's not all. I hope you're seeing what I'm trying to say. You know, no matter what you'll do to the prices in the Grand Exchange, these manipulators have -NO- reason to sell any of their stock -EVER-. Even if they want to, they can't because the worth of their stock easily goes multiple times over the maximum cash limit. They are pretty much -FORCED- to keep on their rare items.

Now this not only applies to the discontinued market, but as soon as all the rares are bought out and too hard to get, guess what happens then...?
