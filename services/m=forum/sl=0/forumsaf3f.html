Well, here it is. Officially the shortest full story on the story forums, that isn't something to do with losing a whip in the wilderness and wanting compensation. It may be a bit confusing to you, but hey, thats the idea. Constructive critisizm isn't just welcomed, it's encouraged. Thanks.
NOTE: This story is fictional! FICTIONAL!
---------------------------------------------------------

Banned. The one word that fills every players heart with fear.
Yet here it was, before my very eyes.
How had it happened? I was a good player... I never did anything wrong. I played by all the rules. What had gone wrong...
I patiently trained my skills... I never quarrelled with other players, even if they really deserved a good kick.
I was so happy when I got to become a member. More skills to train, more things to do. And that armour... how great that barrows armour looked, how the players wearing it were surrounded by others looking on in awe.
I had to have that armour. I got 70 defence, the long tiresome way. It was a long hard journey, but I was so happy at the end...
Then came the money. I tried merchanting, I really tried. But I just couldn't seem to make money. Skilling- I'd had enough getting my defence to 70.
There wasn't any other way. I'm telling you! I couldn't have gotten it any other way! Could I?
Well, here I am... Bus Of Misery, first stop: tutorial island. I'm not a bad player... am I?
