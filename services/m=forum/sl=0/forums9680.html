Hi, I posted this a bit more politely in Suggestions but it got little attention but entire support from all who posted.

I'm posting again because the problem seems to be worse the more I look at it. When I die, I've recently figured out, (some the hard way too ffs):
Barrows gloves are kept in priority before many items, including most weapons (whip, and friend says d scim too)
Helm of Neitzinot (spelling I think) same as barrows gloves, is often first item you keep.

But now, worst of all, a 50k granite shield is kept in priority over 3.2m Armadyl Helm, amulet of ranging and bandos boots? All I can say is W T F???



Items we keep when we die should be done automatically according to their value on grand exchange. (this will fix most problems) and we should also be able to MANUALLY decide what we keep on death if we want to.




I'm a person who dies a lot - I do risky things with expensive-ish items for the job, and my internet connection is known to lag out a lot randomly. I have lost over 5m (like half my money) because of keeping the WRONG items on death, those items being about 50 times lower in value! (armadyl helm lost for granite shield, for example)


This needs to change, I'm sure nobody will disagree with this



This is purposely placed in Constructive criticism and not Rants because I don't want it to be like that, but really, I think this HAS to change.

The current systems decides what is kept in the higher of highest high alch value to lowest. Therefore, since the high alch price of barrows gloves is higher than that of many weapons, it is kept over those weapons.

I agree that we should be allowed to manually decide what is kept, but I also feel that a certain "risk" must be involved with your death. Not being able to choose what is kept requires that you plan accordingly and keep that sense of "risk" into dying.

I'd say this isn't too terrible an idea. But then again, it would take a lot of risk out of dying.
