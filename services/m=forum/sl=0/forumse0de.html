Hello Everyone; this thread is more for me just to keep track of how my 99 is going but feel free to read as much as you want. As the title says, I am currently going for 99 Defence - I have level 85 at the moment (which may seem a million miles away, but I've worked out that 150k xp a day will see me get 99 in just over two months). On my path to 99 I'm going to be using the Slayer skill as well as Abyssal demon camping. This thread will also count as a personal Runescape blog where I post my views on certain updates too.

Step One:

In the next few days/weeks I'm going to be going for 85 Slayer. I'm about 150k xp away from 84 at the moment, so I'd say I'm about two to three weeks away (during this time I should have got 90 Defence).

Step Two:

From then on in, I will be camping abyssal demons. I'm expecting the whip drop rate to be about 1/500 - 1/750. So I should be getting at least 17 whips (51m).

-----------------------

Why 99 Defence?

I've recently achieved 99 Range - So I though 99 Defence would go hand in hand with 99 range for Armadyl GWD trips. The money I earn from Merchanting (500k-1m per day) added on to the money I should be making from whip drops (~1m Per Day) should all add up to pay for armour I can use at GWD, whilst leaving about 50m over to continue merchanting at the same efficiency.

-----------------------

Thanks for reading - Obviously my xp per day calculations are quite rough, as until I actually go and slay the demons for my self - I'm not sure what sort of Xp rates I can expect.

-----------------------

I should be adding a post per day on how things are going.
