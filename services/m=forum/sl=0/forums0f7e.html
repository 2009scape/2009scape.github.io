Where there is an 'or' in the draw, that means those results either haven't been posted, the match hasn't been played at the time of the draw or some other circumstances. Once these matches have been resolved, the draw will be updated.

Once your war has finished and the results are in, please post these on the Combat Cup Round 3 Results thread.

Now that the draw has been made here is the most important point.

The deadline for all matches in Round 3 to have been arranged and fought is 23:59 BST Sunday 18th July. This means that all matches must be arranged before Sunday 18th July (or a J-Mod will pick a time on Sunday 18th July) and then played by the end of Sunday 18th July.

Here are a few points to consider when arranging the matches:
  • Please arrange the time, date and rules, not explicitly stated within the main rules for Clan Wars, for your matches within the Jagex Cup forum. Create the thread with both clan's initials in the title, "MT v MK Com R3", as an example between the "Mod Timbo" and "Mod Kathy" clans.
  • All of the options within Clan Wars is up to the clans during the arranging phase except for the ones explicitly mentioned within the rules. If no agreement can be made, the J-Mods will decide the rules depending on how reasonable both clans have been during the negotiations. This will commonly be the default war rules, posted below, but may be different if one clan has made no effort to reach an agreement.
  • Be mindful of the other clans timezone if it varies with that of your own. If a decision cannot be reached before 24 hours before the round's deadline, a J-Mod will decide the time that suits both clans the best it can.


The Rules

  • Fights to be held in F2P 'safe' Clan Wars
  • There is no player cap
  • There is no returning
  • The match must be arranged, and played, before the round's deadline. (Current deadline for Round 3: 23:59 BST Sunday 18th July)
  • Arranging the games and the results must be posted in the Jagex Cup forum.
  • No one can enter the arena who is not on your memberlist.
  • Member lists cannot be edited in any way, either removing or adding, 24 hours before a war. If a clan does attempt to edit them within the 24 hours, the players cannot fight still and will not be recognised as part of the member list.
  • Any disputes should be made in writing within 24 hours of the finish of the fight in the Jagex Cup Disputes thread.

Default War Rules

  • All styles
  • Clan Wars 'Classic' map (No portal hugging)
  • No Corrupt or Dungeoneering items
  • The team that spawns from the north attacks
