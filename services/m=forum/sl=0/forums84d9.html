The Phoenix 110+ Named Best Clan (100+) The Runescape
Awards 2010



The Phoenix was a legendary bird which according to one account lived 500 years, burned itself to ashes on a pyre, and rose alive from the ashes to live again! As the mythical phoenix died and was reborn so have we emerged from the ashes, and have been reborn.

We are a fun clan and this clan was born in the ashes of others and will soar the same as the phoenix, We are an 110+ Combat clan looking to have fun with our friends, and make some new ones, our aim is to have fun. We are not a Warring clan, We are more of a Skilling clan, but we have all kinds of events to fit all members and 2 other threads, our Goals and Achievements; where members post all their Goals in Runescape and we have the Suggestions and Polls thread, where members can post a suggestion and other members within the clan Vote on it to be passed. Welcome to our Welcome to our Clan's Goals Thread Have fun!
