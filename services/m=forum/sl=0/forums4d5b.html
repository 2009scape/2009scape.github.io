Hello! Welcome to the Mod Tytn Appreciation Thread V2! This thread is now a joint-operation between Cerff and myself. All of the info on Mod Tytn's contributions to the game can be found in Cerff's posts below. Now onto the topic.

----------------------

Mod Tytn! One of the unsung "Hero's" of RuneScape, and Master of Quest Development.

----------------------

A little background information:

Mod Tytn is one of the Lead Content Developers for RuneScape.

Mod Tytn is most famous for his work on Quests, ranging from the Legends' Quest to While Guthix Sleeps. The general idea, Mod Tytn has a mind of which none of us could even begin to comprehend. He works hard to make excellent content for us, and it's time for some appreciation.

In short, Mod Tytn has done a lot for the RuneScape community but gets very little attention. He has developed numerous quests and mini-games that we all enjoy and it's about time we showed him our appreciation. So! If you support Mod Tytn and appreciate all that he's done feel free to post your favorite quest/mini-game or just leave him a kind message. I will keep a count of supporters but not a list.

So Thank you Mod Tytn for all you've done, and we hope to see more from you in the future!

Mod Tytn's Current (and future) Projects:


The next quest we know Mod Tytn is working on is Part 5 of the Myreque Questline, however on the old thread he said this when asked about its development and it coming out this year:


"Hey and sorry for the disappointment but this seems pretty unlikely.

But we're all working on other great projects which I'm sure you'll like just as much!



Mod_Tytn"

So, other great projects instead! If he says we'll like them I do believe he's probably right though. For more info & discussion on Myreque 5, visit the QFC mentioned in post 2.

------

Also, Tytn said on the old thread he is currently working on Hunter and Prayer updates. It's safe to assume these are the projects mentioned in the above post

Here's the quote from the old thread:

"Hey all,

Many thanks for the incredible feedback, really lovely of you all! I am hoping to move onto Myreque part 5 soon but there are a number of other smaller projects which need my attention in the interim. First of these is a Hunter related update, I may write a blog on this. Second are some prayer related investigations!

However, as you well know, there are many stirrings in Morytania that do need some investigating and I hope to pour my attention onto these shortly."

[He went on to mention how amazing One Small Favour is ]


So, there you have it! The next three things we can expect from Mod Tytn! Keep up the good work!
