Welcome! We are the Akrasian Empire, an Imperial Republic POC (Player Owned City). We provide opportunity for virtually anyone, we provide a friendly clan chat and thread for those who are mature enough to talk in it, and we provide friends to keep your back and help you out.

Besides that, we are versatile in many ways: If you...

are a leader, you can lead as a senator, army officer, or religious leader.

are a hard worker, you can work up the ranks using our token system, and attend events.

are just looking for a fun clan, then you can come to our events at your leisure.

If you are confused about our description let me explain. We are imperialistic because we wish to expand ourselves further and further to the edges of Gielnor. We are a republic because we have a senate elected by the people through which all things are done. We are a POC because we conquer land and do all sorts of things with it.


As for levels, we are open to all levels, yet we divide the army into different units based on friends and combat levels, so that everyone is in their right place. As of yet, Free players are not allowed to join but we do plan to expand there soon.

We also offer jobs as soldiers, cooks, laymen, etc. and our departments provide every sort of job that there is in this game (within the game rules of course).

Post 3 - More Information

We also accept orders for many kinds of products, just post and we will pay our members with both cash and tokens to complete it.

Post 5 - Rules

Although rules may seem boring, if anything is to be organized, it must have rules. These are simple and clear, so just take a minute to read them if oyu want to join.

1: Follow all Rules of RuneScape.

2: Be respectful and do not harass anyone.

3: Bump the thread at least once a week to be kept from being removed for inactivity.

4: Do not be a part of or recruit for other clans unless permitted by the senate.

5: Use the clan chat, you may leave occasionally, but spend most of your time in it.

6: Do not spam i.e. repeat useless messages just to take up space in chat or forums.

7: Listen to authority, do not disobey unless if it is too demanding.

If you break one of these, then you will receive a strike. Once you get a third strike, you are banned from the empire.

8: Post on the thread weekly, I will not remind you of this, so remember.

If you break rule eight then you will be put on the inactive list where you will lose two tokens a day until you post.

Post 7 - Akrasian Tokens and Ranks

In order to get anywhere in this clan, you will need some money. Not gold pieces like in-game but Akrasian tokens. These tokens do not exist in-game but they do on the forums to keep track of how well you are doing. Tokens can be earned in the following ways:

Daily Post on Thread (every 12 hours): 4 Tokens
Answering Weekly Poll: 10 Tokens
Attending Event: 10 Tokens
Attending Clan Meeting: 15 Tokens
Finishing Tournament or Task: (varied amount, see posts 30-31 for more information)
Doing a Weekly Puzzle: (varied amount, see post 9 for more details)
Recruiting someone: 10 Tokens
Taking Part in Public Industry: (Varies)

If you are a casual member then you only earn half the amount shown except for recruiting.

This is what you can spend your tokens on:

###Ranks###
-----------
For information about priveleges in clan chat, please see post 36.

Plebian: A new recruit who has a whole career ahead of them
Cost: Free
Priveleges: Participate in all public things and can bump and do poll. Has .5 vote power in elections

Citizen: One who has earned the right to more freedoms
Cost: 70
Priveleges: Same as Plebian and 1 vote power in elections

Gentleman: One accustomed to the empire and has more power within it
Cost: 150
Priveleges: Same as Citizen but can now participate in federal tournaments.

Noble: A veteran who is seeking to help lead the clan
Cost: 300
Priveleges: Same as Gentleman and may lead events as well as 1.5 voting power

Lord: One of the rulers of the land with a direct influence on the government
Cost: 500
Priveleges: Same as Noble plus a power at certain point to veto senator

N.B. You must apply to become a lord and it will be voted on
######

Besides ranks, you can buy other things:

Land: Please Post 13 for more Details

Remove a Strike: 10 Tokens
