Graphics Team Q&A Questions



Greetings all!

Recently we ran a poll asking you which areas of the company you'd like to hear from for our next video on the RuneScape YouTube Channel. You voted for the Content team first and gave us a huge list of questions to throw at them. Well we've filmed their response and it'll be available to view on our channel very soon!

We're not just sticking to the one video though! We're going to be filming a few of the teams you really wanted to hear from, and next up will be The Graphics Team!

Yes, you wanted to hear from the masterminds behind all the 3D models, concept designs and artwork available for RuneScape. We'll be bringing your questions to them at the end of the week so now is your chance to ask anything you have ever wanted to ask about the world of graphics!

Before you ask about new updates and space hamsters...


To help you help us, there are a couple of points we think are worth considering before posing your question:

  • Shorter questions have a better chance of being asked so if you can avoid a 500 word preamble before your question, that would be great.

  • The video won't be an interrogation so loaded questions that are pretty much rants are less likely to be answered. Afterall, we'd like to be invited back.

  • Remember your questions are being brought to the Graphics Team, so they won't be able to answer any questions about game mechanics or future updates (Unless it's what type of brush did you use to paint them!).


Cheers all!

- Mod Crow -
