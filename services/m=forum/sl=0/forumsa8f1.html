~( Rules )~

If you've read the information about our events, and can make our show times, congratulations ! You are just one step away from submitting your application to WWA management. Please read the following rules thoroughly! And remember, always follow Jagex rules.

ALL RULES APPLY UNLESS STATED OTHERWISE

~ Match Rules ~
-No food, drinks, potions, or any edible/drinkable item that boosts or effects your stats in any way
-No Magic or Ranged attacks
-No prayers that are visible (example: Protect from Melee)
-No using items that were loaned to you
-No Phoenix Necklaces
-No outside interference
-If a competitor in the match leaves the ring, they, as well as any other competitor that may have already been out of the ring, have a count of 10 to get back in.
-You may only leave the ring 5 times during a match.

~ Tag Team Match Rules ~
-A team wins when both competitors of the other team has been eliminated.
-A tag is made when you leave the ring via your corner and say "Tag!"
-A team can win if only member of the opposing team has been eliminated for special matches which will be named.

~ Championship Rules ~
-A title cannot change hands via disqualification or count out unless stated otherwise.
-If a champion uses the rule above then there shall be a immediate rematch and if he gets counted out or disqualified he will lose the championship.
-If a champion loses their title, they are not guaranteed a rematch. Management makes decisions regarding rematches.
~If a challenger misses his title match and he does not inform a member of WWA Management prior to the title match. they lose their championship match.
