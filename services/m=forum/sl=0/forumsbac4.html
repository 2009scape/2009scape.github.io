The year is 2014 five years after the infection ended. No one knows how the infection started. Was it from a secret viral experiment, or was it, as many religious people would say, the wrath of the gods. No one knows. The war turned in the battle of England, Great Britain. There we must of taken out at least ten million infected. This gave survivors the courage to fight back against the infected.

Sadly even though we won the war there are still traces of the infection around. It could be dried infected blood, animals carrying the infection or just infected that never got killed. Of the world billions of people only a couple hand fulls survived.

Now the infection has resurfaced, starting in the town of San Jose located in California. The military camps in San Jose had been slowely over run by infected. The US army had pulled out months before and left the state guard to defend. Now most of the state guard is dead or infected. It is up to you to decide who will live...

Our story begins with a young man named Falco, about the age of 15, he felt the impact of the war around the age of 9 and 10. Most of his family is dead he assumes and he has lived on his own since. Falco always kept is dad's guns in case of something like another outbreak happening again. He set up numerous places to run and hide in case of another war.

Zombie Types:

Normal (commonly seen)- just your regular walking dead target practice zombie.

Dogs (commonly seen)- quick moving and hard to hit, really difficult to deal with

Gang Members (commonly seen)- just your normal zombie with enough smarts to weild a gun and be able to bash you over the head with it before it eats you. Some of them know how to actually use it but don't really know how to aim.

Fatso's (commonly seen)- like a normal zombie, only before they died they were extremely fat or overweight (this is not supposed to make fun at people just add some more variety)

Soldiers (uncommonly seen)- many soldiers from the California area had already evacuated, but some remained because that was their home. But now they belong to the zombie race. But they have full body armour, head gear, and are sophisticated enough to use their weapon.

Mutated Zombies (very rare)- a zombie that was just a normal zombie, before it wandered into a military/radiation waste deposit landfill, it is now smart enough to coordinate and communicate to other zombies, and is very powerful and hard to take down.
