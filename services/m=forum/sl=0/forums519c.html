The answer is: Yes, they do.

Today was similiar to every other day, I was happily Farming when a male player straight-out accused me of being a man.

When I joined RuneScape a few years ago, I was of course targeted by several players accusing me of being a male. I did my best to tolerate and ignore all of these people, but then the insults had appeared everywhere, from everyone, on a day-to-day basis.
These opinions were often followed by conspiricies such as "all female avatars in RuneScape are just males interested in younger boys."

These hypothesis's that any lady playing an online game should be a male pretending is rude, cruel, and unfair. I completely agree some males take advantage of the gender change in-game but it's unjustified to assume every female avatar is a relevant situation.
Females should not be harassed, attacked, and unfairly accused of actions we are not responsible for, especially without completely looking into the situation.

I realise almost nothing can be done the cause, I just ask of some consideration and fair judgement before irresponsible accusation.
