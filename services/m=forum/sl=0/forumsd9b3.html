Quality, not quantity is the answer here. Take a lot of time on your next appeal. You might be frustrated that they are all getting denied, relax, make sure you re-read every answer you are providing to check it's correct. Gather as much information as possible, and from the earliest time you can remember - the earlier the better.

You've said you've answered recoveries.
Try and provide your payment details, they won't want your whole credit card number if you pay by that, but the last 4 digits of it will help.
If your 'email adress is linked to your account then use the verification code that you received when registering it.
If you've ever moved house include when and where.
Give the approximate guess of when you made your account if you don't know.
Try and provide the first ever pass you used and 2 more earlier ones.
Write down your membership agreement numbers aswell if you have any of those saved, from when you made your account members.

Yes Jagex do look at your account appeal, if you are the real account owner and it's getting denied, it simply means there isn't enough information detailed in your appeal. Don't give up and good luck.
