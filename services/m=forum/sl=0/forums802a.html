1.1- Game Improvements to anti-macro / Beautify
~~Why not keep all the numbers in a same colour?


~~Change the colour at a specific interval. (Lets say, every 30 minutes)

Or, numbers switch colours?
Example: 1 switches to green, 2 switches to yellow.
(however, this may confuse players)
OR, Complete Random Colours!


Reason: I have to agree, the spinning digits is a really great idea! However, they can still spin a little faster, in my opinion. I suppose, only human beings can read that while its spinning, dishonest macros shouldn't.

It beautifies the Vinesweeper field as it gets unearthed as well!

Colour of the numbers in Vinesweeper game don't really matter, even if a player is suffering from total colour blindness, he/she will still be able to identify the number.

I feel that these suggestions will not worsen, may not enhance the vinesweeper experience as well. It will be a good way to anti-macro however.


2.1 - Current Issues
����������������

2.11 - Bugs & Glitches
Vinesweeper fields get glitched at times, especially when theres plenty of players, players have to manually reset them in this case, by digging all surrounding patches near the glitch, till the gnome farmer comes.

If a "patch debugging mechanism" could be added to this game, it will prevent such problems.



2.12 - Flags disappearing during resets
This may happen several times if there are farmers collecting dead potatoes/potatoes around nearby patches. If this problem is solved, it will help a lot especially vinesweeping in crowded areas.


2.13 - Rabbit issues
Rabbits "eating" potatoes & flags even if they are 3-5 squares away from the flag itself! This problem should be fixed, as it isn't logical that rabbits could eat things out of their reach.


2.14 - Slow and unresponsive farmers
At times, farmers do not collect flags. Instead, they idle around and scratch their head. Resulting in a higher chance of having a flag to be eaten by a rabbit.

4.1 - Additional feedback to MMH
��������������������������

Also not to forget, MMH, field areas in the North, East and West seem to have slower flag collection, and takes over a minute at times. I've also noticed that each farmer is allocated with a specific roaming area.

I suggest that Jagex widen these roaming areas, with farmers from different areas overlapping to a certain extent, as some areas are considered "hard to reach" for farmers.









~To elaborate on high difficulty fields...~

Hard fields should be given a slightly different point computation system. Novices may find that probability of flagging a potato is very high. Therefore, they may do constant random flagging.

You may add in some bonus score at the end of every (lets say) consecutively correctly flagged spot, to prevent abuse of the high difficulty field.

Also on current Vinesweeper limitations.
At times, I constantly receive low points(20-50) for a correctly flagged spot, although my farming level is 98(99 now). There should be increased consistency in awarding points, depending on farming levels.

Many people are not interested or do not bother, simply because they don't really learn the basics well.








Another thing that can be improved:
An improved and interesting tutorial may make it appealing to players. Instead of going straight into the game.

There could be a new feature, to allow players to play minesweeper(9x9 grid) in Runescape, without losing cash or points. This will help players understand the mechanics better.








Players really look forward on getting better harvests and also lower disease rates as levels grow.

Looking forward to a better farming skill

| 5.2 - Bot suspect test |
�������������������


If you suspect someone of botting, try this.

- When his/her flag runs out, flood the south stile by clicking it non stop(after he/she exits).

- If he/she's first reaction is to go to the west stile, continue to observe.

-After entering VS from west, observe whether he/she digs or not, or exit from the South stile, before re-entering.

-Always wait for last flag to be collected before restock.

-If you continue to flood stile after the exit, the bot will continue to run for a few times.
*If you ever botted vinesweeper, don't be suprised to see your name here.

**Please Send A Report Abuse If the test result is positive!**

All I have to say is, it takes too long for the system to ban them, they could have bagged a high amount of experience by the time effect is taken.

As far as possible, I guess Jagex needs to start on this revamp. Thank you for your time everyone.:)
