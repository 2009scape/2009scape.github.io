Hi there,

As to mirror the above comments, there is no "magical hidden" level 4 clue scroll.

ALL clue scrolls of the same level are the same, regardless of what drops them. For example, all normal dragon bones are the same, regardless which dragon drops them.

So yes, you're just as likely to get 3rd Age killing Jellies for clue scrolls, as to if you were killing Black Dragons for clue scrolls.
