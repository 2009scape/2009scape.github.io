-+- Recruitment Thread -+-

Hello and welcome to Eternal Empire recruitment thread!
We are a P2P clan running in the GMT (UK) time zone. The current clan requirements are 100+ combat. Note that we are a friendly, NON-WARRING based clan.

The Eternal Empire was founded by TheJammed85 in 2006 and is also known as EE. Eternal Empire is a diverse clan, welcoming those with a certain level of maturity and capability of challenge. We, the clan community, encourage social interaction between each other as well as having fun at both in-clan activities and outside of clan activities.

The events of EE are mostly focused on monster-hunting and minigames. However, we do not do Bounty Hunter or Player Killing (PVP) a lot especially as an event. There will always be an event on Wednesdays, Saturdays & Sundays created by our Event Team. These set events allow for total clan involvement, developing team-work skills and, most importantly, for fun. For the rest of the week you are able to 'request any event you like!

If you would like to join the Eternal Empire, please read over our CLAN RULES below and fill in an application.

-+- Clan Rules -+-

1. Follow 'ALL' Jagex rules.
Failure to do so can result in being kicked from the clan chat, banned from clan or even at risk of someone reporting you.

2. Follow 'ALL' Eternal Empire rules.
Failure to do so can result in being kicked from the clan chat, banned from clan or even at risk of someone reporting you.

3: You must attend 2 events every month to remain active in the clan (exile events and 99's do not count).
You will be made inactive and need to re-apply if you fail to follow this rule.

4. No Multi Claning - Though you are allowed in minigame and PvP clans.
Banned from clan if found multi Claning.

5. If you seek help report to a Leader/Commander.
We can't help you if we don't know what is wrong.

6. Bump the Recruitment Thread daily
This goes well for your promotion and it helps us get more members. Many of you have been shown this clan by initially looking at the RT because it was bumped, please do the same for other people.

-+- Clan Chat Rules -+-

7. Clan Chat is for registered members (who meet the Requirements) of the clan only.
No pure/noob accounts are allowed in unless they have applied. Kicked without warning.

8. No religious debates allowed in clan chat.
Given a warning at first, if not stopped - kicked from clan chat.

9. No Spamming in Clan Chat.
Given a warning at first, if not stopped - kicked from clan chat.

10. No excessive cursing or usage of harsh or discouraging language\words.
Given a warning at first, if not stopped - kicked from clan chat.

11. Only English to be used for full conversations.
Given a warning at first. If not stopped - kicked from clan chat.

12a. No asking of clan ranks in the clan chat.
A rank is a privilege - given to you to show your loyalty to the clan. You can't ask for loyalty but to be it. Given a warning at first. If not stopped * kicked from clan chat.

12b. No discussion of clan ranks in the clan chat.
Given a warning at first. If not stopped * kicked from clan chat.

13. Ranks may be taken away in clan chat.
This will only happen if we reach 100 full members as the friends list can only hold 100 players so it allows more people into clan chat. You will still have your rank and will be-able to view it on the discussion thread. Check the �Clan Chat Ranking Policy� for more info.

14. Be nice to people who come into clan chat.
