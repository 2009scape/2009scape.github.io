Hello, and welcome to the thread that will play host to the biggest annual community event in this Triumvirate!

Now, some people might be asking, what is the Triumvirate Gathering? Well it will be a community event in which all the Triumvirates gather and support their faction!

We all know how Jagex has sponsored Challenge Events, and Individual Events, but I bring to the table a new idea of a form of event, a Gathering Event. This gathering event consists of all 3 of the factions in the Triumvirate system. It is not one for Reavers to cause utter chaos (although some will), it is not one for Lords to try and control everything, and it is not one for the Judges to try and make it so the amount of chaos and control are equal, it will be just a friendly group of people, showing a kind of advertisment into the Triumvirate system.

For the rest of the thread I will be showing how this event will play out and work, so bare with me while I make it more clear to those of you who are still wondering, "What?".

~Details

The event will be held on July 24th at Varrock, World 50 at 6PM BST.

Before 6PM, each faction will gather at their own seperate spot just outside of Varrock. It is suggested to bring your most representative colors for your faction, and your best outfits!

- The Lords will gather at the Eastern Gate of Varrock

- The Judges will gather at the Western Enterance of Varrock

- The Reavers will gather at the Northern Gate of Varrock (Just southeast of the Jolly Boar Inn)

(There will be a need of event coordinators to help run this, which I will talk about later in the thread)

When I tell the event coordinators of each faction, the factions will charge into Varrock Square meeting each other.

After that, the factions will seperate and start trains, trying to get the longest train possible and gathering people even outside of the faction into their train. After about 10 minutes time is up, the factions will see who has the longest train, and thereby can conduct the best arrangement and amount of people of all the factions. (Judging volunteers will be listed later on in the thread).

When we have everyone neat and organized into a train, the factions will head back into Varrock Square for a fire lighting ceremony, and make Varrock Square bright filling every square with fire.

After everyone has use their logs and Varrock is alight, there will be a contest to see who has the best outfit for their faction (Contestants may sign up with details later in the thread, and more Judges are needed)!

After that, the after party starts at clan wars! Factions can challenge each other, different clans, and do generally whatever they want during this time period .

Have a suggestion for something else we could do? Just post it and I'll look over it!

~The Longest Train Contest

Here is where I will review the specifics of the Longest Train Contest.

Each faction will have exactly 5 minutes to gather up the longest train they can, and they are limited to gathering people in Varrock (Includes the Grand Exchange).

An Event Coordinator picked by me will lead each faction train. I will PM them when the 5 minutes are up, and they will bring the trains to the eastern gate of Varrock, then walk all the way across to the western enterance to Varrock. The Judging Volunteers will then walk to the end of each line to see who has the longest line. That faction will be named the current leader in The Longest Train contest!

~ The Costume Contest

The Costume Contest will be one of the main attractions of the Gathering, to see who has the most spirit - in the form of clothing and armor!

The winner of this will look good and have unique combinations of costumes. It has to be unique, because if you are wearing the same outfit as someone, you both will be disqualified! The person who has a different costume then the rest and still looks stunning will win the contest.

The judging will occur in the Varrock Castle Garden, so remember to report there when it is time to judge (event coordinators will tell everyone when it is time).

Everyone will need to listen to the Event Coordinators and Judges to tell where to go. 5 Judging Volunteers will grade out of 10 on the outfit (They will be grading their own faction), and then I will average the scores. After that, the top 5 averages will come and be evaluated side-by-side, and then the winner will be picked.

Each faction will have one person who wins the Costume Contest, so good luck to you all!
