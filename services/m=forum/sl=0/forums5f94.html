This short guide will show you how to find the exact time of the event in your time (where you live) all you need to know is the name of the time zone you live in. The time of the event will be posted. You will have to add the number that is posed next to your time zone to figure out the event time. I will be posting in 24 hour settings as the forum time uses this.

If you go over 24 (hours) you will need to add 1 day, and then take the difference (-) of the number you got above 24 with 24 and that will be your time. (example: the time went to 30, so I take 30 - 24 = 6, The event will then be the date after it says and at 6am.)

If you go under 0 (hours) you will need to subtract one day, then you will take that negative number and add it to 24 to find the time of the event. (example: The time went to -3, so the event will be one day before the posted date, at... 24 - 3 = 21 (9pm))
