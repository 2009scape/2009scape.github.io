Mac 10.6.4. Issues







Dear Players,

Recently is has come to our attention that many of our players with Apple Mac computers and the Mac OSX Snow Leopard operating system has experienced a number of issues since the Apple software update.

From what we can tell this is affecting other games as well as RuneScape.

The issues appear to be mainly affecting nVIDIA graphics based systems (the majority in this case) which causes game crashes and freezing.

For now, you can play RuneScape in safe mode or alternatively downgrade to the previous update for Mac OSX (10.6.3)

We will continue to evaluate all issues reported to us on this and help out where possible. We really do hope that Apple release a fix so players can continue to experience the game as normal

The thread has been left open for you to leave any comments and to give any further details on your specific experiences with this problem.

Best regards,

~Mod Ac
