High Level Content

Quick find code: 188-189-39-60953920

Idea - Players can smith items of higher quality than Rune

Summary - By requiring players to have multiple skills at 80+ Jagex can introduce high level player made armor and weapons that won't unbalance the game.

Assume that Mithril is level 20, Adamant is level 30, Rune is level 40, Granite is level 50, Obsidian/Dragon is level 60, and Barrows/Crystal is level 70. Players with Smithing 80 to 99 can make Level 40 items. With Smithing and one other skill (like Crafting) at 80 to 99 skill level, then a player can make Level 50 items. With four skills 80 to 99 she can make level 60 items. With eight skill she can make level 70.

At this point, Jagex can open up the smithing skill to be maxed at 120. With smithing 100 to 119 players can make Level 80 items. With two skills 100 to 119 players can make Level 90 items. Etc, etc.

Jagex can further limit the impact on game economy by introducing factors to raises tedium or cost. Example would be having to mine or fish or burn items that have a 1 in 100 (or higher) chance of producing a usable item to make higher level armor. In addition, these intermediate materials can be made untradeable to ensure only one player does it.

Combo-skill concept is made to be applied to smithing and works well into Runescape concept. Make it happen Jagex.

Quick find code: 188-189-10-60961657

**** Skill Boosting Prayers ****

Summary: New prayer book that offers boosts to various luck based skills such as woodcutting, agility and hunting.

Why high level: At 70+ prayer, players have access to prayers that boost certain skills 30%. This offers enormous benefits to praying while high level skilling as less failures equals more experience.

Balance issues: Greater boosting prayers mean more prayer points used. When on a "Skill Prayer Book" the player's prayer is not used for their combat level (encourages Pures and Skillers to use this update). Brings prayer in line with summoning for offering Combat and Non-combat skilling benefits.

Quick find code: 188-189-757-61039397

Spirit Bow and Crystal Axe

A player with 90's in a number of skills can utilize his Spirit tree to create a Spirit Bow (70 range to wield).

Requires 90 Woodcutting, Farming, Crafting, Fletching plus a number of quests to get the ability to make this bow as all steps in the process are untradeable items. Further, those with 90 Prayer can also create Spirit arrows to create spirit arrows.

This thread also introduces the Crystal Axe that is used to cut down the spirit tree. It requires 70 Atk/50 Agility to wield in addition to 70 WC to use on trees. It degrades over time as it misses attacks or fails to get a log (sort of missing an attack on a tree).

These are very balanced with existing game mechanics and the economy. Making a spirit bow requires the destruction of a spirit tree so can only be made once every 5 days. In addition, logs to make spirit arrows can only be obtained once every hour. The crystal axe follows the mechanics of other crystal items and fits well even in combat.
