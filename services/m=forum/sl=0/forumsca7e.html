GRAND EXCHANGE - PRICE FEEDBACK



Please use this thread if you would like to offer feedback on the price of items in the Grand Exchange.

We are specifically interested in the items which you think you can't buy or sell because the price is either far too high or too low, rather than those where the price has been changing due to other players buying and selling.


When submitting your feedback, please remember that there are lots of factors which contribute to the market price of items. One common factor is how much players might be prepared to pay for potential experience.

Your feedback will be used alongside other data to help us monitor whether there are any problems with the prices. By using the following template, it allows you to present your feedback clearly and for us to use it efficiently.

Item:
Current Price:
Expected Price:
Reason for expected price:

Please note, if an item has already been reported to us, there is no need to report it to us again.

In addition, we would like to thank you all for the feedback that you have posted so far.

Some things that have been changed as a result of your feedback are:

● Flatpacks which had too high a price and were difficult to trade.
● Godswords which did not move their prices enough in response to the market.
● Daggers with odd prices when poisoned.

We have also collected some great suggestions from the last thread which have been noted and are on our list to improve when we have time in between other projects.


Also, we would like to assure you that we are aware that some items which have a very high value have been causing some frustration.


Many thanks, and keep your feedback coming!
