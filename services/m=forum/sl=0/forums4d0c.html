To make these monsters drop level 4 clue scrolls you must speak to the Puzzle Master in Lumbridge. The Puzzle Master is a novice when it comes to killing monsters. He will allow these monsters to drop 1 level 4 clue if you give him a level 3 clue obtained from any monster. (Example: You kill hell hounds for a level 3 clue. You give the clue scroll to the Puzzle Master allowing all of the monsters listed above to add level 4 clue scrolls to their list of potential drops and removing level 3 clue scrolls from their potential drops (if they have them as a drop potential). After your first level 4 clue scroll their drops go back to normal.)

Ruin Keys: When you obtain a ruin key you have now gained access to one of the third age guardians. The third age guardians are a boss you can do weekly in the hopes of great rewards. You can collect all of the ruin keys but you can only access them once a week.

Third Age bosses are like players with 99 attack, strength, defence, magic, and range but with 1000 constitution. Since they are not mortal they are also immune to poisons dragon fires and many magical effects. But what makes these unique is the fact you cannot damage one unless all of the Guardians are being attacked at the same time in that world. (Forcing you to split up your party and improve team work in your clan).
