~ 3. How the Running Process Works Getting Your Fast Free Double Natures ~

GUIDELINES FOR RUNNING
- You must supply your own pure essence
- Request assistance from our crafters wearing gold robes
- Do not wear gold robes yourself as this will cause confusion
- You must come inside the altar to be assisted. This means you need a nature talisman/tiara (can be bought from the Grand Exchange and does *not* require any level to use), or use the abyss
- Please do not bring any animals, with the exception of abyssal creatures, Spirit Graahks, Geyser Titans and Terrorbirds (the only animals beneficial to runecrafting)
- You must stay in line - those who cut lines won't be assisted
- We do NOT allow V.I.P's
- Once you are finished with the assist, please say "done"

WHERE TO GO
- Fast Free Double Natures is always located on world 36; however, check the last page of this thread or join our chat channel (FFDN) to see if the backup world (world 39) is in use if world 36 is having technical issues.
- The nature altar is located in the southern part of Karamja island, directly outside the northeast corner of Shilo Village. For a helpful map, see: Game Guide > Manual > Skills > Runecrafting > Runecrafting - Locating the Altars

RECEIVING ASSISTANCE
- Form a line behind a crafter, and move up in the line until it is your turn. When it is your turn, assist the crafter, and you will be able to craft your own double natures. Examples:
*If you craft 28 pure essence while being assisted, you will gain 56 nature runes.
*If you craft 27 pure essence while being assisted, you will gain 54 nature runes.
*If you craft 26 pure essence while being assisted, you will gain 52 nature runes.
*And so on...

~ 4. Banking Methods ~

After receiving your fast free double natures, there are many ways in which you can bank to get more unnoted pure essence and come back for more natures. The methods are ranked in order of their speed. Note: You can also bring along an abyssal familiar to hold 7 pure essences for you.

1. Spirit Graahk
-Use a ring of dueling or glory to teleport to a bank. Then, use the spirit graahk familiar to teleport just west of the nature altar.
---Requirements: Level 57 Summoning to summon the Spirit Graahk.

2. Abyss
-Use a glory to teleport to Edgeville bank, and use the Abyss to travel back to the nature altar. Be sure to watch out for revenants.

3. Fairy Rings
-Use a glory to teleport to Edgeville bank. Walk northeast from the bank until you come to a bridge across the river. Cross the bridge, then head south to the fairy ring. Use the combination CKR to teleport to Karamja, just a bit west of the nature altar.
---Requirements: Must have completed the beginning of the Fairytale 2 Cure a Queen quest to gain access to fairy rings.

4. Gnome Gliders
-Use a glory or ring of dueling to teleport to Al Kharid, and use the bank. Fly the gnome glider sitting north of the bank to Karamja. Walk north along the river until you come to a log. After crossing the log, the nature altar is to your southwest.
---Requirements: Must have completed the Grand Tree quest.

5. Brimhaven House
-Set your house location to Brimhaven. Use a house teleport, then have your butler unnote your pure essence. Walk out of your house and head southeast to the nature altar.
---Requirements: Level 40 Construction to change your house location to Brimhaven and some cash to pay your butler.

6. General Store
-Run to the general store north-west of the nature altar, and use your noted pure essence with the store's NPC to exchange for unnoted essence.
---Requirements: A good amount of cash to exchange your essence.

~ 5. Becoming a Company Crafter and Crafting Guidelines ~

To become a crafter, you will need to sign up using this form and post it on this thread:

Runescape Name:
Runecraft level:
Do you agree to follow our guidelines?:
Have you read the entire first page?: (Hint: We are looking for a specific phrase)
Do you agree to not allowing V.I.P's?:
Will you support company guidelines, help update altar status, and answer calls for assistance?:

PLEASE NOTE: You do not need to re-post this if you have applied before or changed your name, we keep all users and update names automatically (or within one week)

CRAFTING GUIDELINES
- You MUST have 91+ runecraft
- Assist the runners as they move up in the line and wait for them to finish
- Allow at least 2 crafts per runner
- Wear gold robes (Canifis Clothing Shop) when crafting
- You must form a line when crafting and not stand on other crafters
- Acknowledge that you may occasionally lose your bronze crafter star in the channel, and all that is required to regain it is to simply ask an Operator politely (see Post 1 on Page 3 for more info).
- Respect all runners
- Absolutely no V.I.P's
- While not required, feel free to bring along some teleother runes in your inventory to help runners in case they forget to bring their own teleport


***Finally, for new crafters filling out our application:
In response to the question "Have you read the entire first page?", you should answer "I took the hint" to show you actually did read the first page and for your application to be accepted.

~ 6. Frequently Asked Questions ~

Q: Do I need to ask someone if I can run?
A: No, you do not need to ask if you can run, just come.

Q: Are you selling natures or buying pure essence?
A: No, please do not advertise selling/buying on this thread or in our chat channel.

Q: Are you currently crafting natures?
A: Yes, there are always crafters at the nature altar - but see the last page of the thread for the current number of crafters present.

Q: Can you switch worlds?
A: No, we will always be on world 36 unless otherwise indicated on the first post.

Q: Why did a crafter let someone cut the line?
A: A crafter's assist screen is constantly up and we may easily miss a cutter. Also if a runner is poisoned, crafters use their own judgement to help the person get his nats before he dies. Dont worry, we will not let anyone take advantage of this.

Q: I have pouches, how many times may I craft while being assisted?
A: Company crafters are required to allow at least 2 crafts per runner; however, most crafters will allow as many crafts as you need.

Q: Can you come outside the altar?
A: No, you must come inside the altar to be assisted.

Q: Do you supply the pure essence?
A: No, you must supply your own. However, search the Runes Forum for "Free Ess" for companies that can provide you with some for free.

Q: Who do I assist for natures?
A: Locate a Company Crafter wearing gold robes, and line up behind their line.

Q: I am under level 91 runecraft, can I join the company?
A: Sorry, no. Because you need level 91 runecraft to assist double natures, we do not allow anyone below level 91 to join.

Q: I'm wanting to join the company in the future, so how can I raise my Runecraft level?
A: Refer to this great Runecraft guide: 98-99-696-58530389
