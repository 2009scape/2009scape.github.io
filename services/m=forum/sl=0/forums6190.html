Our clanï¿½s system of government is very simple. We have three leaders who share equal power within the clan. They each maintain the clan chat account, thread, and rule over the other members. Under them we have a group of silver stars who aid the leaders in their tasks. Under the silver stars we have a few bronze stars who also help maintain order, the rest of the clan is filled with just regular members who MAY be promoted to either silver or bronze star. We have a few special positions such as Warlord and Event Coordinator that either are ranked as silver stars or are gold stars (main leaders)
