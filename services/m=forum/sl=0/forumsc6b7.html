Thread of the Week!�



Greetings Forum users!

This thread is for bringing to your attention the Thread of the Week!�

Each week, we will scour the forums for a thread which truly shines! The chosen thread will have the honour of being turned into a sticky for a week. After the week is up, the thread will be in the 'Hall of Fame' until Chewy has eaten your thread.

This thread will give you the title and the quick find code to the thread. I will give a brief explanation in the thread as to why it was chosen.

So, what type of thread could be worthy of this prestigious honour? Simply put, a thread which is informative, funny, interesting or helpful in nature.

Of course, all chosen threads need to adhere to the Code of Conduct.

See the post below for further details!
