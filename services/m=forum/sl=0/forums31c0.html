What should you include in your posts?
> Is this method F2P or P2P?
> Any skills/quests required
> Approximate profit/income (per hour)
> Explanation (such as a "how to" description)
> Any dangers that players should consider

It is strongly recommended that, if you have multiple moneymaking tips, you post them in separate posts. This is not a requirement, though.

Please make sure that you include a detailed explanation in your posts. Also, please mention any risks that players should consider before trying the moneymaking method (if applicable).

Note that all concepts listed above *must* be covered in your posts to have your posts listed on the "Tip of the Week" and "Highly Recommended" posts, Index, etc., although players won't be penalized for leaving out one of the above concepts.

Tip of the Week

For the week of: 10 July 2010

Page 9, post 6
By: F3AR3DLEGEND

----------------------------------

Mort Myre Fungi!!!

Requirements:
>Nature Spirit
>Desert Treasure
>50+ Prayer Level
>House With Chapel+Kharyll Portal

Equip your silver sickle (b), and some weight reducing gear (spottier cape, boots of lightness).

Go into Mort Myre Swamp.

Go between two rotten logs (there can be anywhere), but stay between two for most profit.

Cast "bloom" with the silver sickle (b). Collect the Mort Myre Fungus...

Repeat until prayer is out or inventory is full. Teleport to house (tele tab) and recharge pray/go back to Canifis.

Finally, you can sell your Fungi. (or collect more then sell)

It averages 600K-800K/hour if Prayer is atleast 50. You get more at higher levels, but over 60 is same profit as level 60 prayer.

Mort Myre fungi sell at 1.2K/~1.3K ea on ge! Awesome profit!
