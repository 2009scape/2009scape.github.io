GOP Terminology
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The Runecrafting Guild lobby is home to a very diverse community of players. There are a large amount of terms that are not used anywhere other than here, so here�s a handy list of some commonly used terms so you don�t get confused.


1vs1: private game whereby two players solo
2vs2: private game whereby all four players play
50/50: a horrible way in which players essentially admit that they�re not willing to learn how to play
A/D: private game whereby one person on each team defends and the other attacks
Afterlag: altar based trick. See *Advanced section � afterlag� for more information
Angle: generalised term for any way that an orb can be manipulated
Average solo: the solo score you usually get
Blue wizzy: using the blue wizards to join so that you are placed in a random team
Co ord: term used to describe the popular duo strategy
Defending: stopping opposite orbs from being scored
Glitcher/ glitching: breaking your wand at the exact time that the portals opens so the game will start but you won�t get a penalty
Green/G: the green team
Holder/ holding: in the official world, this is when somebody doesn�t let go of an orb. Also means when you don�t pull out of an attract/ repel
Lagshield: what happens when an orb goes out of sight in a hold. See *Advanced section � Lagshield� for more information
Max solo: the most orbs you can score by yourself
Piler/ piling: when two or more people try to manipulate the same orb at the same time
Pro: vague term used to describe �good� players
Random: any player that isn�t very good
Sabb: when a player deliberately prevents their team from winning
Sitter/ sitting: joining a game and doing nothing so that the other two players can solo
Solo: scoring all orbs by one person
Stacker/ stacker: when lots of people try to join the same team at once
Tap: when you interrupt yourself manipulating an orb
Trick: another term for an angle
Yellow/Y: the yellow team

Attractor Wand: The default wand you will be holding. This wand pulls orbs towards you. You should spend as much time on this wand as possible.
Repeller Wand: This wand pushes orbs away from you. It is a lot harder to use than the attractor wand. Don�t attempt using this wand until you�ve got a decent hang of the attractor wand.
Barrier Generator: The third wand you are given. This wand can create a barrier which no orbs can pass through, and will need to be destroyed before they can. Barriers of the corresponding colour to your team can be destroyed with one attempt. Barriers of the opposite team may take many more tries...

Once you join the game, there will be a short wait before you enter the portal in the north of the room. This will take you to the air altar, where the battle will begin!

The goal of gop is to get as many of the different coloured orbs into the squares next to the altar. If you are on the green team, you want to score green orbs, and vice versa.

The game consists of 6 altars, (8 if you are members) each of which has its unique specialities and difficulties. These are:

Air altar: One of the two smallest altars there are. Use your attractor wand at all times. Difficult spawns to get involve any surrounding the portal. A score of 40+ is decent here.

Mind altar: A very larger altar, with lots of obstacles. Often it will be quicker to use your repeller wand to score. Possibly the most fun altar there is; as it is the altar where most tricks (better known as �angles�) can be found. Most spawns can be tricky to get to, but if you know where to stand for each of them it should be easy enough. A score of 15+ is decent here.

Water altar: A medium sized altar. Most orbs should be in range for you to score, however, a few spawn very vary away on the north east, east, south east sides, and well as near the peninsula. Use your attractor wand at all times again. A score of 25+ is decent here.

Earth Altar: Another very large altar. Almost no orbs can be scored straight away, most need to be put to a new position before you can. Again, the repeller wand comes in handy here. A score of 12+ is decent.

Fire Altar: A small altar. Use your attractor wand at all times. Difficult spawns to get involve any that involve the trees. A score of 35+ is decent here.

Body Altar: A medium sized altar. Use your attractor wand at all times. Difficult spawns to get involve any near the veins. A score of 30+ is decent here.

Chaos Altar (members): A very large altar. You can only stand on some spots along the walkways. Sometimes your repeller can come in handy here, but I wouldn�t recommend. Difficulty spawns invole those near the ladders or near the portals. Free player turning to members often find this altar very difficult. A score of 15+ is decent here.

Nature Altar: The other very small altar. Use your attractor wand at all times. Difficult spawns to get involve any surrounding the portal. A score of 40+ is decent here.
