.������.������.������. Clan Rules .������.������.������.

-=-Basic Clan Rules -=-
- All Jagex rules apply
- Do not Multi-Clan
- Listen and obey leaders
- Stay active in our Clan Chat.
- Respect all members no matter who they are.
- If your going to be inactive for more than two weeks, please fill out an absent form, otherwise you may be swept. This can be found further on in the thread.

-=- Clan Chat Rules -=-
- Racism or race jokes will not be tolerated.
- Spamming will not be tolerated unless, for example someone levels up, a quick session for congratulations will be allowed.
- Arguments in the clan chat will not be tolerated.
- Religion can be talked about in the chat unless argument starts.
- Do not make fun of other members.
- Respect all clan members and visitors.
- If you are asked to told to stop argument you must stop immediately.

-=- Event Rules -=-
- Events will ALWAYS take place, even if a leader is not present.
- If a leader is not present, the next highest rank in the clan chat will lead the event.
- Always be respectful to clan friends and new recruits attending the events; failure to do so will result in a kick from the clan chat and further punishment, if a leader decides.
- If you attend an event, you must stay for over half the time in order to be on the Event Attendance List.
- Attending an event and not participating, example: letting clan mates kill Graardor while you sit in a corner, will result in being taken off of the Attendance List and punishment, decided by leaders.
- Event Attendance will be filled out by Event Managers for every event.

-=- House Meeting Rules -=-
- NO pulling trapdoors, if asked twice it will result in a kick from cc.
- Clan Leaders sit in the throne chairs.
- Sit on the benches in the throne room or dining room. If there is no more space, sit on the floor.
- If you have a question, please say, *question* OR *raises hand*

.������.������.������. Rankings and Expectations .������.������.������.

-=- Ranking System -=-
The members who have been selected as primary positions of Requiem may receive rankings, depending on their position in the clan. Combat level and friendship with leaders will not help you obtain rankings, you must earn them. Ranks are given out in accordance to how much of a positive impact a member has made on the clan. If you want to rank up in Requiem, you can attend events, be an active member in our clan chat, bumping constantly, and overall helpfulness to the clan.

Leaders will decide on rank ups if one feels that a member is worth of such promotion. If a leader feels that a member deserves a rank up, there will be a vote in the leading party deciding whether or not the prospective member truly deserves one or not.

-=- Clan Expectations -=-
- Be Active. The more active you are, the more our clan will grow.
- Be loyal. If you are loyal to Requiem, you will gain respect from leaders and fellow clan mates.
- Respect everyone, in clan or not.
- Listen to clan leaders.
- If you are online at war time, you must be at the war, unless you are not on the posted Members list.
- Bump the thread at least 5 times a week.
- We hope that everyone will attend events, yet they are not mandatory.

�.��`'��.� Activity Expectations �.��`'��.�
We are a USA and GMT based clan that will hold two events a day. Events are optional, but we hope all will attend on a weekly basis to maintain activity in Requiem.

There are also many other ways to stay active in Requiem other than attending events. We also count activeness for bumping the thread, being in the clan chat, taking part in our weekly skilling competitions, and our Warrior of the Month tournament.

Though Restrain wished to keep close relations with Age of Glory, part of their leading team at the time decided otherwise. Requiem was accused by AoG for "Recruiting members." In the end, no members were in fact "Recruited" from AoG, every member came at their own will. On March 13th, the first Co Leaders and Council members were chosen.

.������.������.������. Rank Information .������.������.������.

Ranking-up in Requiem requires dedication, activeness, and over-all benefit of clan improvement. Ranking does not come with Combat Level�s or Political issues. Even if you are a leaders best friend, you will receive no special treatment and will be evaluated equally as to a member with no ties.

Clan Leader:
- The Clan Leader is the final voice of all decisions. The over-all well being of the clan is in their hands. It is also their duty to make sure the clan is running smoothly, without error, every day.

CoLeaders:
- The Co-Leaders are appointed by the Clan Leader. Their job is to help run and maintain the clan. They discuss important issues with the Clan Leader.

Forum Manager:
- They should be the most active member of our forums. It is their job to keep the forums clean, call a Mod if needed, and encourage members to bump the forums.

Event Manager:
- The Event Manager's will be responsible for making Requiem's weekly Event List, taking attendance events, encourage members to come to events and also countdown time until an event in the CC.

Disciplinary Justice:
- It is up to these member to ensure the safety and security of our clan community. If punishment need be, they will discuss the issue with Clan Leaders.

Military Overlord:
- The Military Overlord will be in charge of our clans War Leading. Also, they will be in charge of our Mini-Warring Unit.

Diplomatic Ambassador:
- The Diplomatic Ambassador will be in charge of relations with other clans. They are responsible for checking in with Allies, bumping their thread, and basically keeping peace within the Clan Community.
