~*~ MINOR LEAGUE FOOTBALL ~*~ [T2]
===================================

Hey everyone, this is Turtlecity97. I recently started playing RuneScape after a long break where I was too busy with exams and tests. I have played RuneScape for five years and RuneScape football for about 4 years. You may have seen this thread in the past, and now it is back! The only reason that league ended is because one newbie player (who I forgot the name of) got mad at the rules because all I said was that they were the same as URFL and NRFL. Then, it just like, fell apart.

Well, now I am back and I hope that this will be my most successful thread yet, along with TSN. If you still have no clue what this thread is about, I should explain now.

This thread is intended for the free agents who did not make it to any teams in either of the "big leagues" or players who just are really bad at RS football. This does not mean that you cannot join if you are on a team already. ALL THE GAMES WILL BE RECORDED AND PUT ON YOUTUBE.


Enough with my long, boring speech . If you want to play, just sign up on Post 3. The whole point of this is to have fun playing RS football.



~*~ Stuff you need to know ~*~
------------------------------------------------------
* This is American football, not soccer.

* I am in the Central Time Zone.

* Games will always take place around 7:30 CST. I'll add a time conversion chart later.

* Games will not take place during URFL and NRFL games.

* American football rules

* You need to know what 2+2 is.

* Jagex rules.
