||| Thread of the Month |||
===================
The Thread of the Month is a title given to one exceptional thread each month. To be considered, the author must have put their thread onto the index. It will be selected within the first week of each month and will change the 1st week of the next month...

March's thread of the month is ??? (TBD).

------------------------------------------------------------

Welcome! After polling people whether or not my time is better spent on this thread, versus the character booklet, the thread has been made a sticky due to the overwhelming number of yeses. This was originally supposed to replace the Character Booklet, but in a stroke of genius, I have turned the CB into the CB *AND* the Beginner's Guide.

All threads will be listed for 1 month. At the end of the month, the threadlistings will be deleted and you will need to reapply to be on the thread.

To be listed, fill out the below -

Genre:
Thread Name:
QF:
By: (this is your name(s)(if multiple authors))
Summary: (please summarize the thread in 3 lines or less)

---

Since this is going up at the end of the month, I will allow entries to be up until the end of March.
