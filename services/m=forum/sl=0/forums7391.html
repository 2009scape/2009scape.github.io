Graphics Card Comparison

Hi everyone,

Below is a list of some of the most popular graphics cards and how they compare to playing Runescape HD.

Please remember this is only a guide and there are many other factors which determine whether your computer can run Runescape HD.

The minimum requirements are:
1.5 GHz Processor.
256MB of RAM.
64MB 3D Graphics card.

If you do not know what graphics card you have in your computer then follow these simple steps to find out:

Vista/Windows 7:
1. Click the �Start� button
2. Right click �computer�
3. Click �Properties�
4. Click �Performance�
5. View and print details.

All other versions of Windows:
1. Click Start
2. Click Run
3. Type "DxDiag" (without the quotations) and hit enter
4. Click the display tab
5. Your graphics card name and model is next to name.

Please use the following links to jump to the relevant page:

NVIDIA
ATI
Other
