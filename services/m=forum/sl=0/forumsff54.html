Hello everyone.

Don't you hate being alone in the middle of nowhere doing slayer with no one to talk to? I think that it would be terrific if we could do slayer tasks with friends! This would make 99 slayer alot more bearable.

Here is my suggestion:

Allow group slayer tasks. Everyone in the group would get the same task. Group sizes can vary from 2-5 people (any group larger than that would clog up the area too much).

Also instead of everyone getting individual tasks of that monster, they would get a large number of monsters they would all have to contribute towards killing (100-1000 monsters depending on the number of people and the master). This ensures that everyone in the group finishes at the same time as they all have the same pool (100-1000 communal pool) of monsters that they have to kill. That would be better because it would allow everyone to get a new task at the same time rather than having to wait around for the slower members of their group.

This would *not* involve shared experience in any way or shared slayer points. Experience comes entirely from individual people killing their own monsters. Each member would get a slayer point for every 10 monsters killed.

Continued...

For example, lets say that we have 3 people doing a group slayer task. They have been assigned 600 monsters total. Rather than assigning them 200 each, they are assigned 600 and they each contribute towards killing those 600 monsters. If one of those 3 people logs out, the other two can continue to kill those monsters until the other person logs back in. Since that person who logged out is not killing any monsters, he/she will not recieve any experience. Once all 600 monsters have been killed, the task will be done for all three people (even if only one or two out of those 3 people actually did the task). This ensures that everyone in the group finishes the task at the same time rather than having everyone finish at different times. This means that they can continue on and get a new group task at the same time.

Also, lets say that one of those three people killed 350 monsters while another one killed 100 and the other one killed 150. Those three people would have different experience amounts based on the number of monsters they individually killed. Their is no shared experience at all. They would also recieve a different amount of slayer points. The first person would recieve 35 slayer points while the second person woudl recieve 10 and the third person would recieve 15.

This could also allow for new slayer challenges similar to the current kalphite queen and tzok-ket-jad tasks. Groups could be assigned to kill all 4 gwd bosses (you get experience for the kill counts too) or all 3 dagganoth kings or maybe even a dungeoneering boss (you would also get experience for the monsters you kill in the dungeon). This particular idea would be a new addition to slayer and I'm not sure if it would be good or not. You guys decide.
