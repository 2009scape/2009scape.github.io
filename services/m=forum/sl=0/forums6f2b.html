Ahoy! I, Redwall421 of Pirate Inn, a crew led by Captain Olof, have opened a store in World 42 (our homeworld) in Port Sarim. At this store located south of Grum's Jewerly store, I stock in three categories: Clothing, Weapons, and Edibles.
