��*`����*��� The Solution ���*�����*��


To safely logout from your account without worrying with anything, we should have a PIN CODE to LOGIN into the game. Yeah, that's it: a simple PIN code, just like we use in the in-game bank, Grand Exchange, Drop Party room, Player-Owned House, etc.

That PIN code should be able to be activated by the player on the 'Account Management' section and should have different numbers, to be even harder to find it out.
IMPORTANT: THE PIN MUST BE JUST LIKE THE BANK PIN - CLICKABLE! (not just like pass words, which you type). The reason if this, is because keyloggers will be able to find it out, if you type it.

The person MAY or MAY NOT have an Account PIN, just like Recovery Questions, bank PINs, etc. If he wants to activate it, he must do it by himself, but it is STRONGLY RECOMMENDED doing it.

Statix, on page 11, said he heard of some specific type of keylogger that takes screenshots everytime you click your mouse. These are sometimes used to find an account's bank pin. So, how to solve this problem with the Account PIN? We'd need to have the numbers on every button disappear when you rollover one button. That way even if a screenshot was taken when a player clicked, no numbers would be visible on any button, and the hacker wouldn't be able to find out the number the player clicked, by looking which one is missing.

Lucky Froob, on page 26, suggested that the PIN should have as many digits as you wish. The current bank PIN have 4 digits, but on the Account PIN the player could choose more numbers, if he wanted (to be harder to guess and to protect the account even more).

x Sammi, on page 35, suggested that all RS PINs should only be able to cancel if you confirmed it thru an * mail confirmation (some kind of code, or send a new PIN for your * mail). This would only work for those who have their * mail registred, of course.

Take time to read the F.A.Q. because, if you have any doubt about this system, your answer might be there!

Q1: What would this avoid?
A1: That an easy question. This Security measure would avoid hi-jackers, pass word scammers, hackers and mainly keyloger viruses. It would also avoid hackers wasting people's rewards such as Dungeoneering, Stealling Creation, Mobilising Armies, Soul Wars, Pest Control, Penguin Hide'n'Seek (mainly levelling undesired skills for pures, or maybe collecting the points in cash, and then droping it), among many others...

Q2: Wouldn't it be boring have to type your PIN everytime you login into the game?
A2: Well, as I said above, you MAY or MAY NOT activate it. If you choose not activating it, you must agree that your account isn't as secure as those which do have their Security PINs activated. Also, if you have a bank PIN, you don't care much having to type it everytime you login and want to use the bank, do you?

Q3: What if the hijacker or keylogger find out my PIN?
A3: Hijackers won't be able to find out your PIN, unless it is too easy, because, after all, the system will be exactly the same as the Bank PIN system. They won't be able to try many PINs or they'll be blocked trying to, just like the bank PIN, that people have to wait seconds and then minutes, to try the PIN once again.

Q4: On what else would this service help us?
A4: If successful, this service may be able to protect even your forums, the hability to change your display name, to change your personal details like pass words, recovery questions and so on. But this might only happen if the first step (Account PIN when logging in) be successful and people like it.

My idea would be to improve these PINs all around the RuneScape Website Features (mainly the securiy ones, like account management), but focusing the opinion of the player: if he WANTS or do NOT want to activate each of the PINs, around the website.

Q5: Why don't you simply don't bank your items before logging out? It's much easier!
A5: Simply because sometimes you are far away from the bank, or just don't want to bank all your items. Sometimes you don't want to teleport from where you are, just to bank your items and keep them secure. People won't want to bank mainly if they are at God Wars Dungeon; at Avianses; PvP/Bounty Hunter Worlds; killing Abyssal Demons, Granite Lobsters or even Tormented Demons; training a skill that is away from the bank; and just want to take a break, have a lunch or something like that. And finally, if the person have his bank full, he won't be able to bank his items quickly.

Q6: What if I forget my Account PIN? What will I do? I won�t be able to play RuneScape!
A6: If you forget the Account PIN, you must recover it. To do so, we�d use a mix of the 2 existing ways to recover a pass word and a bank PIN. The person could EITHER cancel/change it without sending a request to JaGeX (and wait 3, 7 or 15 days, depending on the settings he chose � pretty much like how to cancel your bank PIN nowadays), OR change/cancel it by sending an Account Recovering Request (just like the pass word recover request nowadays), fill the form, and wait until JaGeX analyze your form and cancel/change your PIN to the wanted one.
� Note that if the person actually knows his PIN and want to cancel or change it, there will be no need to wait a certain time to cancel or change it.

Q7: Why don't you just make a long and complicated pass word? No one would guess it and it would be much easier than make all this engine work...
A7: Simply because this security measure isn't just to avoid hijackers, but keyloggers too. So even if I had a long and complicated pass word, it could still be keylogged.
