RECENT IMPORTANT NEWS:

- 99 Slayer!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
- I got a Draconic Visage from a Steel Dragon! (Not from Slayer)
===============================================================

My current Slayer level: 99/99

I have been training Slayer for quite some time and think it's an awesome skill. I have had a Slayer Log before, unfortunately it died. So... I have now created this one!

I achieved 99 Slayer on June 26th, 2010. I use Kuradal, the Slayer Master in the Ancient Caverns. I continue to keep Slaying and I appreciate all the support everyone has given me and has continued to give me.

If you leave a message on this thread, I WILL respond to it. (Under the circumstances that your message is not spam.) I love to get messages from supporters, it makes Slayer so much more worth while.

If anyone else is training Slayer and has a log, tell me and I would be happy to check it out. I'm always happy to support a fellow slayer. However, please don't post your thread's quick find code on this thread with the sole intention of advertising.

99% of the time the messages posted on this thread will be accurate, but I'm only human and we humans make mistakes, so sometimes they may be inaccurate.

As I said before, good luck to all fellow Slayers and I hope you take the time to support this thread.

Thank you.
