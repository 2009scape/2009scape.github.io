I've stuck with you all these years Jagex. I used to log on and play every day with my friends. Slowly and slowly they quit, but I still found enjoyment in RS... even with the removal of PKing, easily the worst decision Jagex has ever made. I don't play as much as I used to, but this game was still fun to me. After everything you've done- removal of trade limit, removal of wilderness, GE, and updates that have been slowly declining in quality for more than a year now- many of your veteran players have stayed by your side, defended you even. You've lost millions of dollars, perhaps even millions of PLAYERS. The removal of the trade limit and PKing marked the end of an era for Runescape- not necessarily bad, at first.

Now, most of you should know that the removal of the trade limit, staking, and the wilderness was a tough blow to Runescape- but you may be wondering why I included the GE in there as one of the worst updates to date. This is because of the MASSIVE item fluctuation it has been causing. Remember when Pure Essence was 100gp, and furies were over 10m less? For those of you that do, you remember the good old days. Inflation (much caused by the GE system and subsequent updates) has ruined the RS economy.

If you're thinking in your head right now, "Well the removal of the wilderness, free trade, (etc.) stopped luring, scamming, and RWT.", then yes, you are correct. But being lured, or scammed, was a part of the learning experience. (If it's never happened to you, you probably won't understand what I'm saying.) But to that, did the wilderness ditch and the blinking "Item Removed" notification not VASTLY decrease the amount of scams and lures? RWT was a problem that had to be fixed, there is no doubt of that. BUT RWT WAS JAGEX's PROBLEM, NOT OURS. They should have updated their software, put other measures in place- doing what they did may not have been necessary.

And now, Jagex spits in our face after all we've been through, with yesterday's C-Boots update.

So my question to you, and to Jagex, is why? After all this time, Runescape was getting better- rising from the ashes. Things were starting to look up... but to everyone's surprise, you once again tried to fix something that wasn't broken. That's right, CLIMBING BOOTS. To be honest, I couldn't care less about the people who have become rich from this. This isn't about jealousy- it's about the massive inflation this will cause. You can expect to see many items rising and rising if Jagex doesn't do something about this.

On a closing note- I feel that many people are quitting or extremely close to quitting. Jagex should be warned that one more terrible update will be the final straw for thousands of players.

TL;DR: Inflation is turning Runescape into Ruinedscape- a long string of bad updates has made Runescape less of a quality game, and with the latest Climbing Boots update Jagex is on thin ice.


If you want Jagex to do something about inflation, leave a support message.

***EDIT: Just to clear up, I didn't HATE the G.E. (to the contrary, it was not only one of the worst updates, but one of the best). The accessibility it created was extremely necessary, but some of the adverse affects it created were not to my liking (merch clans, inflation, massive price changes).
