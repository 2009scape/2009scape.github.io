StormyMorgan is given credit for this idea.


~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Say you come along and do not know how to farm but you would like to learn. Most people with level 1 or low farming have no idea where to start, and get discouraged early on. But wait, don't fret! You can submit an application to FAM to be a "student" or "apprentice". One of us (the volunteers that chose to join this project) would see your application, accept it, and take you under our wing. That person would be your farming mentor. Yep, that's right. You get one of us all to yourself! All of our volunteers are trusted and are considered to have mastered the basics of farming, so you don't have to worry over being taught incorrect information. This is completely free.

You would work out when is best between the two of you until you feel you understand the basics: allotments, herbs, wood trees, fruit trees, and making money with farming. We also have extra lessons involving prices, quests with farming, etc. if you are interested. For each lesson, you and your mentor will be in a cc, and your farming mentor will show you (not tell you) how to farm different things. We would offer as many of the lessons you want, and once you feel you know the basics, we would announce you formal farmers and you "graduate" from Farmer's Apprentice Movement- in other words, FAM.

If you are interested in becoming an official member of FAM, post an application under our forum saying so, and we'll find you a good farm mentor as soon as possible. If you wait more than an day, please private message "HisJoliCoeur" or "Cyncerity", and we will set you up. Our privates are always on, and anyone can message us for farming questions. We'll be more than glad to answer them, and if we can't right off the top of our head, we will find it out.

By the way, you don't have to join in order to ask a question.

Thanks!

-----FARM QUEST GUIDE UNDER CONSTRUCTION-----

One of the many ways to obtain farming XP is through quests. By completing farm related quests, you can gain farming XP without having to tend after patches and worrying about your crops dying. It's a great way to gain XP, especially for the lower leveled farmer.

Some quests also offer invaluable rewards that will aid you immensely. After getting rewards such as the Ectophial, the magic secateurs, or the disease free herb patch; you'll wonder how you ever farmed without them.


~~~Quests that reward farming experience~~~

`NOVICE QUESTS`

Recipe for Disaster: Saving the Goblin Generals - Requires level 1 farming - Rewards 1,000 farming XP

Perils of Ice Mountain - Requires level 10 farming - Rewards 500 farming XP


``INTERMEDIATE QUESTS``

Forgettable Tale of the Drunken Dwarf - Requires level 17 farming - Rewards 5,000 farming XP

Garden of Tranquility - Requires level 25 farming - Rewards 5,000 farming XP

Spirit of Summer - Requires level 26 farming - Rewards 2,000 farming XP (plus a bonus 10,000)

My Arm's big adventure - Requires level 29 farming - Rewards 5,000 farming XP

Enlightened Journey - Requires level 30 farming - Rewards 3,000 farming XP


***EXPERIANCED QUESTS```


A Fairy Tale Part 1 - Requires level 1 farming - Rewards 3,500 farming XP

Rum Deal - Requires level 40 farming - Rewards 7,000 farming XP


`````MASTER QUESTS````

Grim Tales - Requires level 45 farming - Rewards 4,000 farming XP

Back to my Roots - Requires level 53 farming - Rewards 24,000 farming XP
