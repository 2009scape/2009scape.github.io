This weeks Player of the Week is...

Jamandy52! Congratulations!

I'd like to personally thank you for all your contributions you've done not only to the Businesses & Services section, but for the overall forums. You've had a positive attitude each time I see you and that really has a great impact to those around. You're one to help others before yourself, which shows your a great friend and community member.

I asked Jamandy52 a few questions. You can read them below.

- What made you join Businesses & Services?

"I wanted something new and a bit unique from my old neighborhood.

I had previously worked in a supply company and loved it."

- Where do you see yourself in one year in B&S?

"In one year, I want to see my current company with the branches we dream of not only available and running, but also fully translated and operating in Portuguese and Dutch.

I also want to be able to enjoy participating with the Community as a whole as I have been dong and maybe a bit more. "

- Lastly, what goals do you currently have in-game?

"I am going to be the first player to have 5zillion experience. The honest way (all 99's maxed at 200m exp).

On a smaller level - 200m Experience! "

Jamandy ended our session with the below quote:

"As long as we keep dreaming, we can keep achieving. Regardless of whether we actually accomplish what we set out to achieve."
