Regular In-Game RP Groups


Greetings all!

Do you have a in-game RP Group that meets quite regularly? Or maybe an RP Guild that hosts various In-Character Events?

If so, please feel free to post here and let me know so I can advertise your group to the whole Roleplaying Community.

It can be very difficult for a Roleplayer to find some friendly in-character faces in World 42 and hopefully with this thread and a lot of In-Game RPers getting involved, we can make things a lot easier for such poor souls.

When submitting your group to be posted here; please fill out the following template;
