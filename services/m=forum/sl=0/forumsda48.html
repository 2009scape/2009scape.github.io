Hi there.

I am a lvl 107 Ancient with 99 Magic and I would like to discuss on Farcast techniques.

Remember this thread is not about Ranting Magic being underpowered: We have to coupe with what we have.

Please do not spam or flame on this thread and respect all users please.

As we all know Ancients were the first to be killed once discovered by meleers, and they often die very quickly, if they aren't strong enough. This makes everyone doubts the power of the Ancients.

However Magic is a difficult skill to master. Unlike Melee and Ranged, Magic has ALOT of clicking to do, unless you're using auto-cast which isn't all effective, most of the time Hybriding is needed.

This discussion would mainly benefit other less experienced Ancients who often find it very hard to defeat Meleers (which class we are all entitled to defeat, at least most of the time). Ancients who are more experienced may share ideas, currently methods in farcasting and suggest ways to improve these techniques.

We may also discuss how to use Ancient magic to its fullest extent in different combat situations:

Duel Arena (And different opponents wearing different armour)
Castle wars
Clan wars
Fighting pits
Bounty Hunter Etc.

If you have a suggestion of a new technique or discovery, feel free to share it on here.

I'm looking forward to some interesting ideas but please remember this discussion is solely about How To Kill With Ancient Magic.

Below are some of my ideas, and Highlights of some great ideas posted by other players.

You may now post.

Englishkid62: When Hybriding in a 1 v 1 situation, upon freezing your opponent, you can stand in his spot and change gear unnoticed, which gives you an advantage of suprising your foe.

Englishkid62: It is probably best to lure some enemies away if you are being piled by a great number of meleers in a clan wars situation, if you are about to die. This stretches the opposing clan and the further you run, the further you split up their defences and gives your clan a better chance to win.

Englishkid62: When surrounded by 3 meleers (say 1 in the square before you, 1 on your right and 1 on your left), You can barrage the person in front of you, this allows you to do damage to all 3 meleers much more efficiently. And Run

B 0 0 M 3 D: A tip for BH, if your using Msb, i've found in my expirence, if your far away from your oppenent and can only just see them click spec on for your bow. Then blitz them then really fast click attack. it will, if done correctly, hit the blitz and spec at the same time. Also if your 1 v 1 in someone and your ranging with msb just when your about to fire a arrow cast a barrage. The arrow will hit at about the same time as your barrage this can sometimes KO if you get good hits

Englishkid62: The best way to fight multiple Meleers is to LET THEM PILE YOU, and hit them all with one burst/barrage. Then run, and farcast them. Repeat when they unfreeze, let them pile you in a position that they can all be hit at once (If not, run to another place ). If you have an Ancinet friend who's also fighting off multiple enemies, why not double the damage by barraging their own little group of meleers as well?

Age Of Str1: If, your using a Dbow or R cbow with dragon bolts, the element of surprise is very important. For example, you step out of the safe zone, stand on top of any person, but not the guy your attacking, Blitz, hit them with a couple of dragon bolts, then finish off with a Dbow. If you do this correctly, ur opponent won't know where you are.
