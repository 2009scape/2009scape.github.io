Strategy To 99 Runecrafting

1-14 Air runes

14-50 Fire runes

(I used a ring of duelling to teleport to the fire rune altar in Al-Kharid and the bank at Castle Wars)

50-75 Nature runes

(I used a graahk to teleport me near the nature rune altar near Shilo Village and ring of duellings to bank at Castle Wars)

75-82 Zmi altar

(I teleported to Ourania prayed at the altar and banked spending fire runes and ran to the altar)

82-91 Double Astrals <-----My current method

(I teleport to Moonclan Island banking at the bank and ran to the altar)

91-99 Double Nature runes

( I will use the same method I used at level 50 except I will use a medium, large and giant pouch when runecrafting and will craft double the nature runes)
