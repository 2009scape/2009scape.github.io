I thought of this idea while training agility on the wilderness course. A Revenant Knight was stalking me from outside of the course, and I noticed his cape.

I just now realized he had a cape because all the other times I was running from them So I got to thinking...
What if Revenants dropped "Revenant Clothing"?

Makes sense right? They were supposed to act as "PKers" when the old school wildy was removed (RIP Wildy). Since they represent these "PKers" shouldn't they drop decent items? Don't get me wrong, I have heard they drop some... rewarding items, but nothing really worth getting excited over.

Revenant Clothing would give some protection against the actual Revenants. It would also have a cool glowing/transparent look (similar to the "Ghostly Robes" from after the Desert Treasure quest). You could only obtain these robes/clothes by killing a Revenant. They would obviously be non-tradeable. They would give a slight magic/range/melee bonus since Revenants use all three. They could be members or non-members (its up to jagex really).

Revenant Weapons could also inflict slightly more damage against these foul creatures. Having some special attacks and a cool ghostly look, they would be dropped by all different kinds of revenants. Although not many Revenants wield these weapons, I can think of two that do. The Revenant Knight and the Revenant Imp (if I remember right). There could also be a Ranged weapon, like the one Revenant Orks/Knights use to throw and freeze its target. It would inflict a decent amount of damage and freeze its target every once in a while. (Thanks for reminding me RainofSkulls)

Let me know what you guys think
