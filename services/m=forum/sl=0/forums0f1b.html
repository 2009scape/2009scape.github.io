Events Community Home


Official UK / Jagex / Forum Time is:



Hello and welcome to the Events Community Home thread - your first port-of-call for all things relating to our burgeoning Events Community!

Whether you're a creative player looking to put on an event for others, or you're a canny party-goer who wants to know where the latest and greatest events are, this thread is for you! So why not stick around, have a read of the resources available and then get stuck into the important business of meeting some like-minded friendly and creative people?


Key Event Support Threads

  • Events Community Home (<-- click for 14th Jan news!)
    The thread you're on right now. Here you'll find links to important events-related resources (below), as well as news and discussion regarding the Events Community in general. If you've got a question or some feedback, you can post it on the end of this thread! Click on the title above for the latest news post!

  • Event Teams Directory
    The Events Forum is full of creative people who develop and run events. Some of them have banded together to form dedicated groups who aim to make their own brand of events on a regular basis. Check out this list for the low-down on who's who and then get involved with the teams you like the sound of!

  • Featured Events & Live Alerts
    Want to know what's on right now or during the coming week? This is the place to find out. Here we list a range of interesting headline events for the coming week (sourced from our own forums and from supported fansites), as well as allowing forum users to post live alerts for their own events that are about to start within the next hour.

  • Events Workshop & Submissions
    This thread has two important aspects. Firstly, it contains an essential guide to making really solid events, including guidelines on how to list event details properly for promotion. The second part of the thread allows you to post finalised details of your events so that we can review them. Solid events posted here are in with a chance of being attended by J-Mods & posted on our RuneScape Events Twitter feed!

  • The Time Conversion Thread
    All the information you could need in order to get you to our events at the right time! If you need any help working out what time it is in one place when you're in another - this is the thread to visit!


~ Community Management ~

Event Teams Directory [ v2.7 ]



Want to get involved in events but don't know where to start? Looking for friendship, fun or even a little extra responsibility? Look no further! Check out our list of dedicated Event Teams and get involved with them!


This list is ordered according to each team's original start date.
= Teams focused on bringing players & volunteer mods together!

  • BAM - Battle A Mod
    Looking for a bit of friendly combat or minigame-related fun? Why not join this group who are dedicated to bringing volunteer mods and players together in glorious competition?

  • Town Square Q&A
    If you need to solve the mysteries of RuneScape, this is the group for you. Here volunteer mods and players unite to answer any burning questions you have about the game, the rules or mods themselves!

  • WFE - Weekly Forumer Events
    One of our oldest & most established Event Teams, they're dedicated to organising all kinds of fun events and activities. Get involved!

  • RS Outreach: Player & Mod Events
    If you like skilling, minigames or just chilling out with nice folks in general, our volunteer mods running this initiative have something for you. Pop in & say hi to the crew!

  • TEP - The Eclipse Project
    A group for all you dedicated skillers who want to break up the hard training regime with something fun. All players welcome!

  • CORE - Community of Runescape Events
    A group dedicated to producing fun events for all the community - including French, German, Brazilian, Dutch & Spanish players!

  • CGF - Creative Games & Foruming
    Providing the community with fun events both in-game and on the forums, this lively bunch are always on the lookout for new faces!

  • Equinox Events
    Equinoxclan have been offering weekly events since Oct 2009. Why not come along to this fresh offering all about events with a story?

  • Skilling Guild Events
    Working hard at life and RuneScape can be a tiring business! Why not unwind with this group of friendly chillaxing eventeers?

  • Wize Waffle's "Mod Wars!"
    An Event Team dedicated to organising informal battles between J-Mods & players over different games. Unique points system, too!

  • CnE - Chat N Event
    This group is all about hanging out & having fun with no formal membership structure. Want a quick & easy way to join in? Try them!

  • TGNP - The Global New� Project
    A team dedicated to assisting new players through advice, help with skilling and charitable donations! They also run events too!

Do you already run a group dedicated to putting on open events for all kinds of players? Does your group have a focus that isn't presently covered in the teams above? If so, please drop us a line with your team thread QFC on the Events Community Home thread so that we can check you out. We just ask that you've been active and reliable for at least a month before we list you!
