Tech Community Talks










Hi all,

The Tech Support forums are the number one resort for information and great community support for players experiencing technical issues.

However, it is also the premier location for the tech enthusiasts of RuneScape.

With that, we have created somewhere for you to discuss technology, be it your favourite operating systems, new advancements in technology, talks about set ups and system specifications, even tech specific events happening within the community.

Please note, this thread isn't strictly for tech issue reports, it is advisable to create a separate thread where tailored support may be given.

Enjoy!


~Mod Ac
