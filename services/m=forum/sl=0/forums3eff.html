QUEST ITEMS LOST AND FOUND!
Quick find code 20-21-160-58630650
=============================

Hello and welcome!

Some very common questions asked in the quest forum is how to get a quest item back after it's been lost. This is NOT a list on how to get items during a quest. This is a list of how to get quest items AFTER the quest has been completed since the method of getting them back is usually different. If you need help on how to find an item DURING a quest please create your own thread here in the Quest forum.

The list is in Alphabetical Order to make it easier to find what you are seeking. All items are listed by their proper names. For example: Pendant of Lucien, not Lucien Pendant. (Special Thanks to Wildcat 661 for sorting it out and for testing many items as well, you've been a great help!)

Some items have "Drop and talk Method" listed. To quickly explain, this means, drop the item you have on the ground, talk to the NPC to get the item again, then pick back up the one on the ground allowing you to have two of the item you normally would not be allowed to.

===========================
Quest items - Clearing your bank.
===========================
Many people keep quest items in their bank just in case they are needed later, these items can quickly use up a lot of bank space. Use this guide to learn where to get back quest items and if you don't use them then you can safely remove them from your bank. In some cases you will need to buy back your quest items if you ever need them once again. Some items are not replacable!

========================
Quest Related Books (page 7)
========================
Just to note here that many quest related books (and even non-quest related ones) can be found by searching any book case in your Player owned House (POH) with the exception of very few books. Books are only automatically added to your bookcase after you have found them. For complete list of books refer to Page 7. (Special thanks to Winterhook for this great tip!)

=================================
Achievement Diaries Reward List (page 7)
=================================
On page 7 is a list of all the rewards from the achievement diaries and where to go for replacements if you lose them. I decided to include this because many tasks require quests or are quest related. Many have asked for this, so here it is.

10th Squad Sigil: Waymottin next to Zooknock in Ape Atoll Tunnel system. Note: You can have more then one by "drop and talk" method.

Akthanko's Camulet: Lazim inside Enakhra's Temple southeast of the Desert Bandit Camp.

Ancestral Key: Telegrab from table in Nardah's Museum. Note: You can add this key to your Steel Key Ring.

Ancient Hymnal: Speak to Azzanadra in temple beneath Digsite.

Ancient Mace: Buy from Tegdak in his room east of the Dorgesh-Kaan bank for 1,000 gp. You can also buy from other players or The Grand Exchange.

Ancient Staff: After the quest you can buy from Eblis for 80,000gp. After that you can try killing the mummies for the very rare drop in Azzanadra's Pyramid. You can also buy from Grand Exchange or other players.

Anti-Dragon Shield: Duke Horacio in Lumbridge Castle will give you another. This can also be bought from other players or the Grand Exchange. Note: You can have more the one by "drop and talk" method.

Arcenia root: Digsite cave. Operate the winch to enter.

Ardrigal: In Tai Bwo Wannai, Head north of Trufitus' hut until you reach a cliff, and then head east to a peninsula with several Palm Trees on it. Search one to find another unidentified herb, which will be Ardrigal when cleaned.

Armadyl Communique: Not replacable.

Armadyl Pendant: Talk to or kill some Armadyl Guardians in the Temple of Ikov behind the Ranging Guild to get another one. Note: You can have more the one if you drop the one you have before getting another. If joined Lucien side, keep killing them until you get the drop, it's not always on the first kill.

Asgoldian Ale: Buy an Asgarnian ale for 3gp in Falador pub and use a coin on it.

Ava's Attractor: Speak to Ava in Draynor Manor and pay 999gp.

Ava's Accumulator: Speak to Ava in Draynor Manor to get Ava's Attractor for 999gp. Pay an extra 75 steel arrows to upgrade it to Ava's Accumulator. You can have more then one.

Balmung: Destroy option says to see Chieftain Brundt or Queen Sigrid but only the Queen will actually replace it free of charge for the first time only. You can find her next to King Vargas in his castle on Miscellania after Blood Runs Deep Quest.

Barcrawl Card: Not replaceable.

Barrelchest Anchor: Speak to Brother Tranquility on Mos Le'Harmless to get another one. Then speak to Smith with the Book O'Piracy in inventory on the docks just south of him to repair it for 230,000gp. After Rocking out, Book O'piracy is no longer needed to speak to pirates.

Base Key: Search Tapestry in southern room of Zemourgeal's Fort.

Base Plans: Search Tapestry in southern room of Zemourgeal's Fort.

Battered Key: Search the bookcase in the house SouthWest of Seer's Bank. You get a message that says "You find a book titled 'The Elemental Shield'". Click to Continue and then you'll get the message "You also find a key" at which time automatically shows up in your backpack.

Beacon Ring: You can get another from Zaff in Varrock Market Square free of charge.

Beads of the Dead: Speak to Yanni Salika in Shilo Village and ask him "Do you have anything interesting for sale?" and he will sell them to you for 1200gp.

Bearhead: From Mountain Daughter Quest, speak to Hamal, the Chieftain at Mountain Camp. Note: You can have more then one by "drop and talk" method.

Bedsheet: While wearing your Ghost Speak Amulet, talk to the inn keeper in Port Phasmatys. He will give you another bedsheet but it will be brown(wearing it will show green though). To make it bright green, you use bedsheet on the pool of slime down the trapdoor at the Ectofuntus. Note: You can have more then one of each kind.

Birdfeed: Not replaceable.

Black Mushrooms and Ink: You can find black mushrooms in the goblin cave directly south east of the fishing guild in a couple locations. You can also find them by the stairs at the Ruins of Uzer. With emtpy vials in your inventory, click on your pestle and mortar then on the black mushroom and you'll get a vial of Black Mushroom Ink. You can make as many as you like. The ink can be used to dye things like desert robes and origami balloons.

Blamish oil: Use a harralander in a vial of water. Buy a sample bottle for 10 gp from the Canifis general store. Kill any blamish snail in Mort Myre swamp and use a mortar and pestle on the snail meat. Use the vial containing harralander with the Blamish snail slime.

Blessed Hatchet: Speak to Slayer Master Turael in Burthrope. After While Guthix Sleeps, speak to Spria in the same location. You'll need to give them a mithril axe and holy symbol of Saradomin to get a Blessed Hatchet. Note: You can only have one. Fun tip: This was called Blessed Axe before Jagex changed all axes to hatchets in April of 2009.

Blessed Golden Bowl: Make a golden bowl using 2 gold bars on any anvil and use a bull roarer in the Kharazi Jungle and Gujuo will bless it for you. Fun tip: This was irreplacable up until February of 2009!

Blurite Sword: You were able to make extras for yourself but if you lose them after the quest it's not replacable.

Body Body: With two body bars, hammer and Ragged Book from Elemental Workshop III quest in inventory, smith this on any anvil.

Body Helmet: With one body bar, hammer and Beaten book from Elemental Workshop II Quest in inventory, smith this on any anvil.

Body Shield: With one body bar, hammer and Battered/Slashed Book from Elemental Workhop I Quest, smith this on any anvil.

Bomber Cap and Jacket: Auguste in Entrana.

Bone Key: You can get replacement by speaking to Yanni in Shilo Village. Fun fact: This was irreplacable up until Feb 2010.

Bonesack: Speak to Odd Old Man from Rag and Bone Man quest for replacement. Note: You can exchange the bonesack for a ram skull helm by speaking to him as well.

Bonesack(e): Destroy option reads: The bonesack looks like it will fall apart if you drop it. You would have to go to the Odd Old Man for a replacement. Note: You can exchange the bonesack(e) for a ram skull helm(e) by speaking to him as well.

Bone Seeds: Talk to Malignius Mortifer with a Pot and a Lid or an Airtight Pot. Note: Can only have one at a time.

Book of Balance: If you don't have this book yet speak to Jossik in the lighthouse to buy a damaged book of Guthix for 5000gp. If you already have this book completed and lose it, Jossik will replace for free and you will not have to re-add any pages. Note: You can only have one at any given time, drop and talk does not work.

Book of Binding: Search a bookcase in your home.

Book O'Piracy: Bill Teach on one of the boats in Port Phasmatys or search your POH bookcase. After Rocking Out Quest, you no longer need this to speak to pirates.

Boots Of Lightness: You find these in the Temple of Ikov behind the Ranging Guild. (Don't forget your knife and lightsource). Note: You can have more then one.

Broav: With a knife in inventory, use some logs on the same pitfall trap from While Guthix Sleeps just west of the Hunting Expert located northwest of Oo'glog and then place a mort myre fungus for bait on top. Dismantle the trap after the Broav is caught. Bring it to the hunting expert for re-training and you now have your own pet Broav once more. Broav's eat mort myre mushrooms for food.

Broomstick: Maggie West of Legend's Guild.

Builder's Costume: This costume consist of a hard hat, builder's trousers, builder's boots and builder's shirt. To get Builder's Trousers search the 'plants' around the Tower of Life. For Builder's Boots, Speak to No Fingers first, then pickpocket him which can fail, so try a few times. For the Builder's Shirt, talk to The Guns and give him a Beer from Yannile in exchange for the shirt. For the Hard Hat, talk to Black Eye and answer three questions correctly which are (3 nails, torn curtains and 10 clay pieces) and he'll hand one over. Note: Can only have one Builder's Costume and this can be stored in your POH. While wearing entire Builder's Costume and you activate 'beckon' emote, your character will whistle.

Bull-Roarer: Give a cooked oomlie wrap to a Jungle Forester south of Shilo Village. You can have more than one. (Oomlie wrap - Shake a leafy palm tree near the water pool in the Kharazi Jungle and use the palm leaf with raw oomlie and cook it). Fun tip: This was irreplacable up until February of 2009!

Cairnellian Armour: During the Quest you were able to get a second set of this if you killed him twice for the drop but if you lose the second set, it becomes irreplaceable.

Caitlin's Staff: You can get another from Xenia in Lumbridge graveyard.

Camel Mould(p): You can make another positive mould by using soft clay on the pedestal in Enakhra's temple.

Camel Mask: Use soft clay on pedestal in Enakhra's Temple. Note: You can make more then one.

Canopic Jars: Not replacable from both Missing My Mummy and Icthlarin's Little Helper quests.

Catspeak Amulet: Speak to the Sphinx in Sophanem. You'll need a cat of any growth stage with you. Note: to have more then one, drop the one you have, speak to the sphinx, then cast telegrab on the one you dropped. You cannot get this amulet if you have catspeak amulet(e) without destroying it first.

Catspeak Amulet(e): Speak to Hild in Burthrope for her to enchant it again for 5 death runes. You'll need a catspeak amulet from the Sphinx in Sophanem first. Because Catspeak Amulet(e) has a destroy option, you can only have one.

Cattleprod: In the fenced field south of the Fishing Guild. You need to wear protective clothing to get it but you cannot get this clothing back after the Sheep Herder quest.

Cave Directions: Destroy option after the quest is completed will say to speak to Cheiftain Brundt of Rellekka, Mawnis Burowgar of Neitiznot or King Vargas of Miscellania for a new copy. But after the quest is completed King Vargas is no longer in Miscellania.

Cave nightshade: Get a Skavid Map and a light source and enter the caves north of Gu'tanoth. This can also be grown using the farming skill by planting Belladonna seed at farm patch in Draynor Manor.

Chain Link Mould: Search the crate in the room to the west in the Myreque Hideout in Meiyerditch. Note: You can have more than one by dropping and searching the crate again.

Cinnamon: Enter dungeon on Entrana. Kill lvl 25 Zombie until they drop a bronze hatchet. Head south and cut branch from Dramen Tree. Use pestle and mortar on Dramen branch to get cinnamon. Note: You can have more then one.

Cleaning cloth: Buy from the G.E. or other players. Buy for 60gp in Tamayu's spear shop in Tai Bwo Wannai village. You can also make it by using Karamjan rum on a piece of silk.

Climbing Boots: Tenzig for 12gp located southwest of Troll Stronghold.

Colorwheel: While wearing full set of Mourner clothing only(no jewelry or weapons weilded), head to Mourner's HQ in West Ardougne. Go down trap door, head to Head Mourner's office. Search the desk and take A New Key. Use key to open the western door and head far west until you reach the gate of the Temple of Light. Search the mourner guard leaning on the north wall to get both a colorwheel and Edern's journal.

Code Key: Picklock crate in southern room of Metal Door in Zemourgeal's Base under the Chaos Temple.

Commorb v2: As a security precaution, you're Commorb will automatically self-destruct when dropped but Sir Tiffy Cashien will give you another one free of charge in Falador Park.

Composite Ogre Bow: With wolf bones in your inventory, cut down an achey tree and use your knife to fletch the logs into an unstrung bow. The wolf bones will be used in the Fletching process. Then use a bowstring with the unstrung bow to make a composite ogre bow.

Conch Shell: Not replacable after The hunt for Red Ruktuber Quest.

Conductor Mould: Not Replacable.

Crystal Bow and Shield: Buy from Islwyn who travels with Eluned in Lletya. Crystal bow costs 900,000gp and the shield is 750,000gp for new ones.

Crystal Chimes: Use your crystal seed or crystal saw on the singing bowl in the Tree Gnome Village Dungeon (will need to repeat part of The path of Glouphrie Quest). If a crystal saw, select "revert-crystal" singing bowl, then select "sing-glass" singing bowl to get crystal chimes.

Crystal Mine Key: Take the innocent looking key from the crate deep in the haunted mines and this becomes the crystal mine key. You do not need to fight Treus Dayth again to get it. Note: This can be added to Steel Key Ring.
