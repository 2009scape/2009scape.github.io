Post 3: Clan Chat Basic Rules.
> ALL Jagex Rules apply.

> Must listen and respect all Generals & Leaders at ALL times, failure to do so may result in a kick.

> No Flamming or Spamming on Thread or in Clan Chat.

> No Chat of 'The Fifth Age' Clan on thread or in Clan Chat.

> Don't provoke Clan Members - Don't start pointless arguments.

> Freedom of Speech is welcome, as long as it is NOT affecting other Clan Members and/or Countries.

> No Racist, Religious or Political Comments/Chat.

> Please do not advertise other Clans on forums, on Thread, or in Clan Chat.

> If there is any conflict with another member, let a General or Leader know ASAP. Causing or making arguments may result you in a Warning against you or a Permanent Ban, depending on he level of subject.

> Please don't say any offensive comments to other members of the Clan Chat.

> Please don't say any inappropriate comments.

> Respect ALL Ranks, regardless.

> No random comments or words, that may be offensive to others in such way.

> No Multi-Clanning please. This includes other Skilling Clans, Combat Clans and Combined Clans that you can't be in. But, you can be in MiniGame Clans such as CW, SC, SW, etc. (Please state in your Registration Form if your in another Clan Chat for anything!)

Post 5: Clan Chat Ranking System & Black Mark System.

-----POINTS SYSTEM (RANKING SYSTEM).-----
Ranks will depend on how you work with the Clan Chat.

Points aren't that hard to achieve too.

Friend (Smiley Face): 0 Points
Recruit (1 Banana Rank): Total of 30 Points Earned.

Corporal (2 Bananas Rank): Total of 75 Points Earned.

Sergent (3 Bananas Rank): Total of 150 Points Earned.

Lieutenant (Bronze Star Rank): Total of 225 Points Earned.

Captain (Silver Star Rank): Total of 400 Points Earned.

General (Gold Star Rank): Total of 1000 Points Earned.

Other members can also nominate points to other members too.

Points For:
> 5 Bumps - 2 Points
> Recruiting a Friend/Member - 3 Points
> Quite Often Active - 3 Points
> Being on Home World (44/108) - 1 Point
> Attending a Clan Chat Event: 3 Points
> Hosting a Clan Event: 4 Points
> Being in a Competition Round - 3 Points
> Change Display Name to AOS (CAPITALS) at the beginning of your Name - 10 Points. (Example: AOS XDZ)

TO BE UPDATED PROGRESSIVELY!

-----BLACK MARK SYSTEM.-----
SYSTEM:
1. 1st Warning.

2. 2nd Warning.

3. 3rd Warning - WILL be a kick too, regardless (Temporarily)

4. 4th Warning - 1 day Ban.

5. 5th Chance - Permanent Ban.

EXPLANATION.
Bans, from 1-3 will be cleared at the 1st day of Each Month.

Ban 4 will be cleared, if AOS JPod2010 and 1 Co Leader agree.
