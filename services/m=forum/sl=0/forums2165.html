� Grandmaster
� The Grandmaster is the one in charge of the Order, This person runs the Order of Varrock and decides all aspects of the Order. This includes promotions and demotions. This person plans meetings, runs events, maintains the thread and makes sure the Order runs smoothly

� Marshal
* The Marshal is the second in command in the Order and the one in line to become the next Grandmaster. The Marshal acts as an Advisor to the Grandmaster and helps any way possible. This person can accept applications, and host events and meetings. In the event of the Grandmasters absence, the Marshal will act as a regent.

� Under-Marshal
* The Under-Marshall can host open meetings and non mandatory events. They can suggest New knights to me and will be next marshal when the other marshal is promoted or steps down.


* Knight-Errant
� The Knight-Errant is the heart and sole of the Order of Varrock, they make up the body. Their goals are to help the Kingdom reach its goals, to create projects, be the voice of the people, help others, become a role model.
