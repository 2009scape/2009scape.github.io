Hello, I am a zerker pker in the making, and value my time at 2.5m an hour. I am maxing out, and need 600m for the whole process. It should take about two months to make the money, but as I do not intend on continuous moneymaking, I need to choose, of my most expensive commitments, the order in which to get turmoil, overload potions, 99 summoning, magic, and ranged. I have the advantages of each below.

Turmoil:

-Has greater melee damage bonus than extreme strength and overload (39.6% as opposed to 39%, I think)
-95 prayer costs less than 96 herblore
-More helpful for slayer
-Makes melee training about 40% faster (taking into account only the strength bonus)
-The only cost after 95 prayer is from prayer potions

Overload:

-Applies to all combat stats

99 summoning:

-The Steel Titan is useful for slayer tasks such as dagannoths and kalphites
-Beast of Burden familiars allow faster training in collection skills, such as mining and prayer
-Level-boosting familiars speed up powermining and hunting
-Unicorn Stallions and Bunyips help with impling hunting at Mos Le'Harmless, armored zombies
-The drag. claws and steel titan combo is one of the most reliable ways to rush

99 magic:

-Vengeance, Ice Barrage, Spellbook Swap, Miasmic Barrage
-I can get enough money to barrage rock lobsters for 99 magic and summoning in a few weeks

99 ranged:

-A much easier time in the Fight Caves, especially with Void, soul split, turmoil, overloads, and vengeance
-The ability to hit 600s with the enchanted drag. bolts special

Note: I will edit this post later today, but feel free to post your opinions now.
