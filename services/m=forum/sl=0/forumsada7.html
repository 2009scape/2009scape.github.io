Ranks will not be handed out. The rank you have reflects how much you help the clan and its members. The member who follows the clan rules, attends events and bumps the thread will be promoted. Recruiting new members into the clan is also a great way to impreove your rank.


� Recruit - Casual member, logs in cc when in game and attends at least one event per week.

� Corporal - Active member, shows initiative and helps others. Attends one to two events per week.

� Sergeant - Very active member, bumps the thread constantly and attends two to three events per week.

� Lieutenant - Leads events and has the power to accept applications in our thread if the applicant meets the requirements.

� Captain - Member of the High Council. They may be called upon when big decisions concerning the clan arise. They moderate the cc, keeping the peace. May kick a member if required.

� General - Founder will have this rank as well as leaders. They may accept alliance requests and war requests, aswell as declaring wars.
