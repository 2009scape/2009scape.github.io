-�- Rules -�-
===========

1. Please respect all Members and please respect Ranks. Banter between players is allowed but please remember if they ask you to stop, please stop - failure to do so will result in further action.

2. All Jagex Rules Apply. We do not encourage rule breakers.

3. Please Bump the Clan's Thread as regularly as possible, however, please be sensible when bumping - bumping too much is considered spam.

4. Please Attend as many Events as possible - we appreciate that players have a life outside of Runescape but we expect players to attend at least one event a week.

5. Please do not join multiple Clans.

6. No Flaming - either on the thread or on the Clan Chat, this is considered as spam. This includes rival Clan chats and threads.

7. We ask that if a Clan Member dies to Bless their Grave if at all possible.

8. Be in Clan Chat whenever you can - Failure to do so and you will be deemed as inactive, this will most likely result in being expelled from the clan.

9. Please respect other people's opinions, you may not necessarily agree with them but please be courteous. Please act politely at all times.

10. You may invite friends into the Clan Chat but please inform us of it. Please also respect that this is a Clan Chat and not a party chat. We encourage you to get as many of your friends involved with the clan as possible.
__________________________________

We will generally follow a '3-strike' system with rule breakers. This means you will have 2 warnings from high ranked members before further action will be taken. This does not mean you may break rules twice, we see rule breaking as a serious offence.

Please also note that if you find you're being kicked by a high ranked member for nothing or what appears to be in the rules, or you're being abused, to contact another member of the clan such as myself. We will deal with everything accordingly - hopefully this will never happen.

How to gain Ranks:

Bump!
- Bump our fine looking thread a couple of times a day and you will be rewarded. We dont look the other way when people work for the clan.

Attend events
- Events are a big part of the fun of a clan. We ask that recruits attend at least one event per week. We appreciate that some people can't play Runescape that often. However, theres no cap to the amount of events you can do a week. The more events you attend, the more noticed you will become.

Manners
- Manners in general are the keystone to any good clan. Being excessivly rude will not be tolerated. Being polite at all times makes a nice friendly atmosphere, and will also give us a good reputation so we can become even stronger.

Stay in the Clan Chat when at all Possible
- This makes the clan more enjoyable for everyone. It helps us to get to know each other, and create a more friendly close community. You don't have to be in the clanchat all the time, but if you're not, it would be nice if you let us know you won't be and why.
