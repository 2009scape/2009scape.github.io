~99 FISHING GUIDE~

In this Fantastic fishing guide I will tell you the best and absolute loctaion on where to fish the fastest, and make pretty good money, (I made 120m+ with this guide, but the prices have changed a lot)

1) 1-20 Shrimp/anchovie(at 15 for anchovie)
Requirments: N/A
Location: Best Location is Draynor
Equipment: Small Net

2) 20-68 Fly fishing trout/salmon(at 30 for salmon)
Requirments: Shilo Village quest (if fishing there)
Location: Barbarian village, bank is edgeville/soul wars portal if you wana bank instead of power fish. BEST fly fishing is done at shilo village (shilo village quest must be completed)
Equipment: Fly fishing rod, and Feathers

3) 68-70 Lobster
Requirments: 68 Fishing for guild faster there
Location: Catherby/Fishing Guild
Equipment: Lobster pot

4) 70-85 Monkfish
Requirments: Swan Song
Location: The Piscatoris Fishing Colony (must have completed Swan Song)
Equipment: Small Net

5) 85-90 Cavefish
Requirments: N/A
Location: Living Rock Caverns
Equipment: Fishing Rod,

6) 90-99 Rocktail
Requirments: N/A
Location: Living Rock Caverns
Equipment: Fishing Rod

NOTE: (living rock cavern, it is north from fally and east from edgeville, it is in the drawven mines, once then inside go southwest, and to too far there should be another dungeon icon, then go down there, you should now see a bank. if seen a bank your in the living rock caverns. now just follow the crowd to the fishing spots. which are cavefish or rocktail.

IMPORTANT(only for if you are fishing cavefish/rocktail): go to WORLD 84!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! if you don't then you will be attacked, in w84 there is a less chance of you getting attacked, in all means juts wear your best armour, and always keep an eye on your character, just incase you get attacked. If you do happen to get attacked then just either kill (if high combat level)(90+) or leave them and head a little bit somewhere in another direction, then come back to that fishing spot.
