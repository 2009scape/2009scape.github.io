Hello RSB, and welcome to my 1st thread here in a LONG time, but this is important...
Now, amidst all the squabbles and grief here, let me bring up a VERY important point that MANY of you have CLEARLY overlooked...
What is Runescape?
Now, in reading that question, some of you said "just a game", some said "MMORPG" and some said "Role-Playing".
Perhaps there are other responses, but I'm sure the 3 listed are the dominant...
Now, if you think it's "just a game", I think you should "just" play elsewhere, where you are not affecting other people.
That and perhaps it would be easier to grasp the concept of "just" games than it is to grasp Runescape.
If you said "MMORPG" then you are technically correct, but failed to realize the core in that acronym...
What is Runescape?
Well, cut out all the rest, and you have "Role-Playing".
That is to say that you can be and do whatever you want, as long as it fits the role you choose.
Since these are midievel times, your list of cuss words should be severely limited, you should fear being hanged for treason, and the word "noob" doesn't exist...
So, my advice is: KNOW YOUR ROLE!
Let there be rivalries, alliances, empires,
clans, conflict, etc, but remember, these are NOT personal matters!
