Useful Hunter items:

These are items you should stock up on when following this guide.

Bird Snare: Bird snares can be purchased from the Hunter shops, located in Nardah and Yanille.

Rope: Rope can be obtained in exchange for 4 balls of wool or 15 coins with Ned in Draynor Village. It can also be made by spinning Yak hair on a spinning wheel.

Small Fishing Net: The Fishing Tutor in Lumbridge will give you a small net if you do not already have one. Small nets can be purchased from the Catherby Fishing store for 5 coins each, or from the Piscatoris Fishing Colony & Port Sarim Fishing stores for 40 coins each.

Box Trap: Box traps can be purchased from the Hunter shops, located in Nardah and Yanille.
