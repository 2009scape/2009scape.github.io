Prestige

I will try to explain this the best way I can in my own words.

The exp you get on each floor is based on 3 things

The first thing is the floor exp.
-You get a certain floor exp everytime you complete a dungeon floor.

The second thing is Prestige.
-Everytime you do a floor that is not checkmarked (ticked), it gives you 1 Prestige. As long as you don't redo floors (Grinding), you will gain a prestige bonus for each floor, and one prestige.

Then it averages your prestige bonus and your base exp together and that is how much exp you gain per level.

Each floor you do will give you 1 Prestige.

What happens when you've done all the floors you can do at your level, and you cannot do anymore without repeating ones you have already done?

RESET BUTTON- *Warning this is dangerous*
When you cannot do anymore floors at your level, you need to reset your floor progress. What this does is *locks* your prestige in place, and you still get prestige bonus, but it takes away the checkmarks (ticks) by your floors, and you can redo them. You keep doing this each time going up to the floors you can do, when you can't do anymore, or can't unlock any at your level, that's when you reset and repeat. Pretty soon you'll be 35 prestige, and you'll just reset after you do 1-35!

Other Exp/ Bonus/ Penalties.


Complexity
- You can have a complexity of 1-6. When you start at 1 complexity you will be given the combat triangle armor (Mage, Range, Melee) And weapons, and food.
Each level complexity unlocks more skills you will use during your dungeoneering adventures. If you stay at low complexity you will be given a penalty which will be -% on your exp at the end of the floor. I suggest staying at complexity 6.


Penaltys for dying.
- When you die you get a -12% Penalty. It adds -12% everytime you die. Make sure you take plenty of food so you don't have to deal with this!


Bonus Rooms - (Thanks to Sumurai8 for Submitting this correct definition)
- Every room in a dungeon that doesn't contain a key that is needed to reach a boss and that isn't the route to such a key or the boss is considered a bonus room. Each door on that route could be opened by at least one of the party members. Each of the other doors are bonus doors and the level that is needed to open these doors are completely random, but not necessarily a skill door.


Level Mod
- This is determined on how many monsters you leave in the dungeon. The more monsters you leave, the less exp you get. I suggest killing all monsters possible.


Guide Mode
- This will give you a penalty of -% exp. If you have this checkmarked before you do a floor, it will give you an easy map of the easiest and safest route to the boss in order to complete the dungeon. I don't suggest doing this unless you are absoloutely confused about everything and just starting off.

FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions


-What's the hardest boss to solo?
*I'd say the nightgazer, because you have to keep a torch lit. Also the pummelor, because you have to lure him to the green splats, and that decreases amount of time you spend killing.


-Best weapon to use?
*Spear, or battleaxe. Both give good str bonus, The spear is slower than battleaxe, but hits harder. The battleaxe enables you to use shields, and is handy for dragons.



-What does complexity do?
*It has 6 levels. Each level introduces more and more skills/resources into the floors. The higher complexity, the more bonus exp you get. At level 6 Complexity every skill is unlocked.


-What's the xp per hour?
*If you are soloing it is about 15k an hour. You can probably do small floors and take about 10-20 minutes for each of them. This includes all bonus rooms, and all monsters for more bonus %. This does not include depleting all resources.

*If you are duoing it is about 20-30k an hour with a good partner and high skills. You do this on 2 People, Small Room. If you have enough food just go to boss if not fish a bit and then go. You will not start recieving 20k+ an hour until after about 25-30 Prestige.
*The best exp would be doing 5:5 Large Teams on 32-35, then rushing 1-31 on 1-4 complexity on small teams.


-What are the stats of the weildable rewards?
See page 3, post 7-10


-How do I get past the doors that require a higher level then 99?
*You must make herblore potions that boost skill. There is a post on page 1 of this thread that explains potions and what they boost.


-Do I have to fish every fish, hunt everything, mine every ore, wc every branch and so on so on to get bonus exp?
*NO. This gives you no bonus exp. The only exp it gives is the little exp you get in that skill. EX: Cutting branch = wc exp.

-IF I kill a dino, will I lose exp?
*NO, This only gives you hunter exp if you hunt + 2-5 extra hides.
*If you kill, you recieve NO penalty. Just no hunt xp /1-2 hides.
