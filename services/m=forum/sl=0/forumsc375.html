How do you find a Dungeoneering party?


Hey all,

We've set this feedback thread up as we are very interested to know:

How do you find a Dungeoneering party?

Do you stand in the middle of the castle yelling out your request or do you make use of the auto-grouping rooms? Conversely, do you form a group in the Adventuring Parties forum and then meet up in-game?


Auto-grouped parties - Information

Auto-grouping rooms are for players who want to quickly raid a multiplayer dungeon with minimal fuss and organisation. This is done by entering rooms that 'auto-group' you into parties according to your total skill level.

There are two types of auto-grouping rooms in Daemonheim:

'Themed' Rooms - These have Dungeoneering floor requirements (for more information on what a 'floor' is, click here). Themed rooms can be found in the castle courtyard, to the left and right of each staircase.

Free-for-all Room - This has no Dungeoneering floor requirements at all, can be found between the two staircases. It is worth entering the free-for-all room if you want a raid that is likely to test your abilities to their upmost.

We look forward to reading your answers!
