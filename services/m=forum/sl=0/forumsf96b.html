Welcome to Free Fall,
We are a P2P clan running in the GMT (UK) time zone. The current clan requirements are 110+ combat P2P.

Free Fall was founded by JD in January 2008 but has now moved into a different clan on 27th May 2010 with all of our members. Free Fall is a diverse clan, welcoming those with a certain level of maturity and capability of challenge. We, the clan community, encourage social interaction between each other as well as having fun at both in-clan activities and outside of clan activities.

The events of Free Fall are mostly focused on monster-hunting and minigames. These events allow for total clan involvement, developing team-work skills and, most importantly, for fun. We do all clan events and we let you chose what u want to do.
With some great members we like to go to many events including Stealing Creations, Pest Control and much, much more.

We also like to grab our Swords, Bows and Staffs and battle against large monsters, the likes of King Black Dragon, Godwars Boss Monsters, Kalphite Queen, Chaos Element and much more including:

-+- Clan Rules -+-

1. Follow 'ALL' Jagex rules.
Failure to do so can result in being kicked from the clan chat, banned from clan or even at risk of someone reporting you.

2. Follow 'ALL' Free Fall rules.
Failure to do so can result in being kicked from the clan chat, banned from clan

3. Be Active.
Clan sweeps will be carried out at random. If you are marked as inactive you must reapply unless you give a good reason as to why you were inactive. Attend events, go into the clan chat or bump the RT to show your activity. ATTEND ALL WARS.

4. No Multi Claning - Though you are allowed in minigame and PvP clans.
Banned from clan if found multi Claning.

5. If you seek help report to a Leader/Commander.
We can't help you if we don't know what is wrong.

6. Bump the Recruitment Thread daily
This goes well for your promotion and it helps us get more members. Many of you have been shown this clan by initially looking at the RT because it was bumped; please do the same for other people.

If any of these rules are broken this could result in a temporary or permanent ban from the clan chat.

============================================================================


-+- Clan Chat Rules -+-

. Clan Chat is for registered members (who meet the Requirements) of the clan only.

. No Spamming in Clan Chat.
Given a warning at first, if not stopped - kicked from clan chat.

. No excessive cursing or usage of harsh or discouraging language\words.
Given a warning at first, if not stopped - kicked from clan chat.

. Be nice to people who come into clan chat.
Point them to the forums if they wish to join the clan.

If any of these rules are broken this could result in a temporary or permanent ban from the clan chat.

-+- Members Rules -+-

. No racism in anyway directed towards any Free Fall member.
Immediate ban from clan.

. No racist remarks
This will result in a minimum of 2 week's suspension from the clan.

. In your application put a smiley face followed by some enters to prove you have read all of the rules.

. Respect all clan members and allies.
Not everyone has the same ethnic background or religion etc, and so they may not have the same point of view as you. Please respect that.

If any of these rules are broken this could result in a temporary or permanent ban from the clan chat.

============================================================================


-+- Event Rules -+-

All regular rules apply to the events as you should be respectful, kind and helpful at the
events. Other than Wars, events are not manditory but please try to come to all the events you
can. We will be taking attendance and keeping a list as to how active everyone is but that will
not get you kicked from the clan. It may very well help you get ranked higher though.

We do not war often but when we do and you are online, you must attend.

This should not be a problem because I know everyone wants to support our clan and help
us win all wars. If you can't be online during a war, that is understandable. But if you are
online when we have a war and you do not go and help, you will be kicked from the clan.

Leaders of events please take attendance at the events and post the list on our thread. Anyone can post attendance but the one leading the event should do it.

-+- Clan Chat Ranking Policy -+-

Please read this policy carefully. Your accepting it in full is a condition of your use of the ClanChat. If you do not agree with this policy, or any part, please do not use the ClanChat. Your using of our ClanChat is treated as an acceptance of this policy in effect at the time of your use.

Clan rank is available to any 110+.and depends on time joined and how much activity they have in the clan.

Clan ranks are only increased every SATURDAY, no other days and no exceptions.


-+- Trial Period -+-


Firstly to gain your rank, please apply on this forum using the clan application template. Afterwards your clan activity shall be monitored for the following one to two weeks.

Some events and ALL wars must be attended for you to pass this trial period.

If you fail to do this you shall be deleted from clan. After this period you must still carry on joining events and attending ALL wars, then you will progress up the ranks ladder.
