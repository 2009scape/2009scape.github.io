~~~Ranks~~~

Leader: Gold Star in the CC. Empire of Ice's Leaders, they do everything to hosting events, recruiting, accepting members and promoting ranks.


Council: Silver Star in the CC. Also know as our Co leaders, they can also accept members into the clan.


Warlord: Bronze Star in the CC, leads our wars with pride.


Elites: 3 Stripes in the CC, due to memebers being very active, attending a lot events and bumping our thread.


Members: 2 Stripes in the CC. To stay active need to try to attend 2-3 events a week, to keep from being sweeped.


Trials: 1 Stripe in the CC. Need to attend 3 events in one week to become a member.
