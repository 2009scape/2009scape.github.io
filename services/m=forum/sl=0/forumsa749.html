Have you ever had a one-itemer come up to you and start annoying you in a bounty +1 world? Well I have a solution! If Jagex made it so that -if- you DON'T risk 75k or more of wealth, you lose everything on you! That would make it so that one-itemers would not be able to bring weapons that they can really damage you with, like a Godsword or Dragon Claws. One-itemers that would want to keep their good items would have to carry 75k+ worth of other risked wealth on them, and that would make killing one-itemers not so bad anymore!

Of course we would see no-itemers, but it's a lot better than getting hit by a Godsword or Dragon claws.

Jagex would have to make HUGE warning about this when you log onto a Bounty+1 so that people know they cannot protect items unless they are risking 75k+.

Bottome Line: If you do not risk 75k+ worth of wealth in a bounty +1 world, then you lose everything you are carrying. (To prevent one-itemers)!

Do you agree? Not agree? Please post if you support or have a question/comment/concern!
