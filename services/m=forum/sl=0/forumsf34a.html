~ Rules ~
=========

--------------------------------------------------------
�Note: Rules may be added over time
--------------------------------------------------------

- 1. As always follow Jagex rules for yours and players safety.

- 2a. When you enter the clan chat, please say your request so people know you need something and not end up skipping you.
~2b. On a side note, if a smither is free - be sure that you are ready and not making a smither wait on you. (Ex: You make a smither wait 10 mins due to you finishing a dungeon.)
~2c. To this rule - make sure you have the level to wield or use what ever it may be. (We don't pay attention to your stats). Unless you plan to train for the weapon later on and bind it early.

- 3. Make sure not to spam for someone to help you, but you may ask nicely a few times on the progress.

- 4. Be patient and wait your turn, sometimes they can take long and sometimes short.

- 5. Make sure to follow the instructions of the smither and help out the best you can.

- 6. Don�t flame other members. (Any issues should be taken to a pm).

- 7a. If someone with rank and kick access has abused their powers. Please post here letting us know and no false reports please.
~7b. For anyone that has rank and has accidentally kicked someone, please send them a pm apologizing.

- 8. Forum rules do apply and "Trust Trades" are not allowed here. (trading in-game items/gold for services)

�If you no longer want rank in the cc, please let me know so I can remove you.
�Any ranked person that has changed their name to something else, please post here your previous name with your new. (Needed to keep roster updated)

Cheers,

~ How to work with the party ~
=======================

�Choosing the world
- It would be best to go to the homeworld of the smither so they don't have to keep hopping for different players.

�Forming a party
-Working with a party will vary based on the preference of the smither. Some may like doing duo party or a trio. 4+ is not recommended as the maps become to huge and more challenging to gather the ores. The floor resources is based on the teams levels, thus it may lower the chances of the higher tier ores.

�Gathering ores
~Gathering ores should be smooth as possible. There are a couple of ways to go about this.
- Person already has the mining level to get ores easy.
- The person may have the mining level, but also like to make backup cash with alchs. The players should leave all the decent stuff such as cash, charms, and weapons on the floor at base (home tele), for the alcher to pick up.
- Have a player run around gathering up the resources such as tree, plant leaves, fish, and other things.
- Doing the airs method (Look below in the tips on how to do so)

~ Tips ~
===========

� Alch your previous binded item and bind your new items ASAP. (lagging out hurts). Double check if you binded before ending a dungeon.
� If you have some binded arrows, you may alch them since they will appear next round back with what you had. (T11 arrows recommended for this as they alch 3k each)
� Players can buy essence from the smuggler and craft into airs, then selling to the smuggler for profit. This process will need to be repeated until the cash is made. (Tip: place your airs on the bottom row and set your setting to one button click)

Cheers,

~ Helpful Potion Assistance ~

Increasing your Mining levels will give you the upper hand of being able to mine a ore with a boost.

~Weak gatherer's potion (Herb level: 24)
Supplies: Sagewort + Red moss
Effect: Boosts Woodcutting, Mining and Fishing by 3 + 2% of levels

~Gatherer's potion (Herb level: 57)
Supplies: Wormwood + Red moss
Effect: Boosts Woodcutting, Mining and Fishing by 4 + 4% of levels

~Strong gatherer's potion (Herb level: 90)
Supplies: Winter's grip + Red moss
Effect: Boosts Woodcutting, Mining and Fishing by 6 + 6% of levels

---------------

Ever needed a small bit of levels to make something? Well it's possible to make something with either of these potions.

~Weak artisan's potion (Herb level: 27)
Supplies: Valerian + Red moss
Effect: Boosts Smithing, Crafting, Fletching, Construction and Firemaking by 3 + 2% of levels

~Artisan's potion (Herb level: 60)
Supplies: Magebane + Red moss
Effect: Boosts Smithing, Crafting, Fletching, Construction and Firemaking by 4 + 4% of levels

~Strong artisan's potion (Herb level: 93)
Supplies: Lycopus + Red moss
Effect: Boosts Smithing, Crafting, Fletching, Construction and Firemaking by 6 + 6% of levels

---------------

Poison is a very popular thing and people may ask of it for their spears or arrows. A p++ requires the herblore level of 87. Planting Winter's Grip requires 73 farm for the poison. Seeds are buyable so you are able to farm the needed herbs easier.

~Weak naturalist's potion (Herb level: 30)
Supplies: Sagewort + Misshapen claw
Effect: Boosts Cooking, Farming, Herblore and Runecrafting by 3 + 2% of levels

~Naturalist's potion (Herb level: 63)
Supplies: Wormwood leaf + Misshapen claw
Effect: Boosts Cooking, Farming, Herblore and Runecrafting by 4 + 4% of levels

~Strong naturalist's potion (Herb level: 96)
Supplies: Winter's Grip + Misshapen claw
