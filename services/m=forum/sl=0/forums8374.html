Our clans system of government is similar to that of the United States, We have three Major offices that balance each other out. So no Person in the clan has to much power. Here are the offices:

- Royal Office:
The Royal office is like the Executive branch. It holds the power to manage the military in time of war and can declare war with 50% of the Senates vote. This is also the office where the Supreme Commander (Leader) resides. There are three positions in the royal office here they are,

~The Supreme Commander: S u p e r 71
Leader of the clan can Call clan meetings Declare war (with 20% Senate vote). He can also veto any senate vote unless it is 75% or higher. One of the big Three

~The Vice-Commander: SIL Poseidon
Second in Command, He can declare clan meetings and propose votes for the senate.

~The Commander: SIL Owns ALL
Third in Command


- The Senate:
The Senate is like the congress it proposes rules and bills for the clan.The Senate has Six positions they are,

~The Head of the Senate: SIL Owns ALL(Subject to change)
Rules over the senate can propose votes, Call senate meetings and is part of the Big Three.

~ The High Priest of Saradomin: sil PrEdAtOR
Keeps order in the clan, stops arguments, Helps newcomers settle into the clan, He also keeps moral up and encourages clan mates.

~ The High Ambassador: Lord Angelus

~ The Head Tacition: SIL Cinnder

2. Have Fun!
3. Do not fight or argue with clan members, also do not flame ours or anyone's thread
4. If you leave the clan and come back you do not get your old rank back (Unless you rejoin the clan within three days and if you haven't joined another clan you get your rank back)

6. Bump on the Forums.
7. Come to Events, Wars, and meetings if your on
8. Be in the clan chat at all times unless you've asked permission from an official.
