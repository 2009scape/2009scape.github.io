Places of Interest

=-=-=-=-=-=-Death Island Jail-=-=-=-=-=-=
Where all of the inmates are held.

=-=-=-=-=-=-Leech Stream-=-=-=-=-=-=
This stream runs all the way around the island acting as a natural barrier. It is the only way to the docks where the prisoner transport boats are held. There is only one bridge across and it is heavily guarded. It is about 15 wide and impossible to jump, it is also 7 feet deep. Falling in here means certain death as you are guaranteed to be eaten alive by leeches.

Different NPC's:

Guards:
Your average prison guard with a shotgun or pistol, they aren't very strong and are only around for bringing prisoners to death island and guarding the cells.

Nurse/Doctor:
These doctors are usually only here for the money, they will treat most minor injuries but if it is to severe they will dump you outside and let the wolves get you.

Wolves:
(yes they are NPC's) Wolves are usually out late at night looking for scraps to eat, but if you manage to escape the facility watch out for the wolves because they will attack you on site and they carry different diseases.

Rattle Snake:
Extremely poisonous snake that will rattle its tail when you get near it. They are all over the forested part of the island and seem to have developed a taste for human flesh. They wont wander more that 5 or so feet away from the forest but are extremely deadly. STAY AWAY!

Janitor:
They come in late at night (around 10:00) and clean the floors. They are more widely know for selling stuff to inmates for a cheaper price than inmate dealers. But they only come once every 4 days.
