"Just a small side note. I know quests are intended for everyone, but still not everyone considers themselves a quester. For the people who do, quests are more important than for those who don't. What I'm trying to say that 'epic' boss fights may sound appealing and attract more people to doing quests, but don't forget the group 'true' questers, which favorite activity is doing quests, who can't wait for a new quest to come out, who speculate about the history of Gielinor and what there is yet to come, and who know the story of Runescape by heart.

You can look at it from two ways;
- A quest without a story isn't a quest, a quest without an 'epic' boss fight still is a quest.
- A quest needs a story, but an 'epic' boss fight does not need a quest.

Xaror" (Page 6)
