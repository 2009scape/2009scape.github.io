=======================STUN=======================

i'm going to show you some facts and info about stun and i will also compare it with other ways of training magic.

NOTE: this post is about stun WITH sc armour (you can do it without the armour but that will cost much more and it's even a little slower)!

required level: 80
xp for each cast with sc armour: 180xp!
stun casts 80-99: 61380 casts
cost of one cast(current medium price; 15-may-2010): 160 gp
cost 80-99: 9820800 gp or 9.8m
1 sc armour piece lasts for 272 casts, that's a good 48960 xp!
sc armour needed 80-99: 261
The cast time for Stun is 3 seconds, as it is technically a combat spell.


now, this may seem a lot; getting 261 hats �nd doing 61k casts. well, it's not. But this will keep you busy for a month that's for sure, but i'd rather do a way with variation, less "clicks" and one that's actually much cheaper too!

now you can gather 3-4 sc hats if you're playing in a non-combat sc clan. so let's say that you're busy for 65-75h and you're done getting the sc hats/points. always keep your sc armour, don't drop it, but *recharge* it!

i.e. 1 hat is 24 points, bottom is 26 points and top is 28 points, recharging for *any* armour piece is only 20 points! so i think you understand wat i'm trying to tell you.

now, i also want to prove how fast stun is:
take 2:30h for getting/recharging the sc armour + another 2:30 for the stun casts; this will give you 500k xp in 5 hours or just 100k xp/h.

WAT I DID: i always played 10 games a day, that's good for 230-240 points and i trained magic till every hat was out of charges. that gives you a good 500-600k xp a day.

FOR STARTERS: i highly recommend getting 1 sc top, 1-2 sc bottoms and 8-10 sc hats FIRST! once you've done this, you're good to go. sell your magic armour if it's something like dragon'hai robes or mystic! why; sc armour has the same stats as the armours mentioned and you get some money for the runes too.

=================== STUN LOCATIONS ===================

there are only a few places where you can train stun in runescape because stun is supposed to be a spell you can only use on a NPC/player once if the stun hits them whithout "splatting". those places are:

zombies north of the edgeville border.

dummies in lumbridge (only for lower levels so i doubt it works for most of you).

zombies in the magic guild's basement.

balance elemental in your house. i used this one because none can steal your target and because you can always tele to it (at least 37 construction required).

a great place to stun is in fight caves. Just get to wave 3 use a safe spot. This will guarantee that no one would mess with you and will have no randoms to distract you! But could get a bit lonely at time... so join a cc. (credit to Lebron To NY)

there are more places but i just gave you the most important ones.



=================== HANDY TIPS ===================

always get your spellbook menu as close to the target as possbile, show only tele spells and combat spells (stun is a combat spell).

turn your "accept aid" off, some people want your target so they will teleother you and that slows you down too.

always have a games necklace in your inventory to teleport to the wilderness volcano to go to sc.
