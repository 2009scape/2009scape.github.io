Plot: You are captured by a mysterious scientist and his cronies. Naturally, you attempt to escape, but he sticks a sharp needle into one of the major veins of your neck, paralysing you. Satisfied, he then gets his cronies to throw you into a cell, where you hit the wall next to a tall 19-year old boy. That's when everything goes black.
When you wake up at last, the first thing you see is the boy's concerned face. Sitting bolt upright, you look around in a panicked manner, until he places his hand on your arm and calmly says "It's no use to try escaping. Trust me, I've been here over 2 years, and I know a lot about this place. But I still haven't discovered a way out".
Curious, you start talking to the boy, and discover that his name is Mark and that he was, as he claimed, captured 2 and a half years ago, on the date of his 17th birthday. You ask why he was captured and he replies he doesn't know, though he was certainly injected with something the same day.
He then adds that his sister, who was captured the same day, might know what's going on. You ask where you can find her and he turns, pointing to a girl the same age as him, who is staring out of the bars of the cell, a morose expression on her face. You talk to her, and she reluctantly tells you that 1) The scientist and his cronies kidnap teenagers for experiements, 2) The needle gave you the DNA of a particular animal, and 3) Her name is Danielle, and she, along with Mark, was one of the original test subjects for the experiment. Mark then walks over and suggests you work together with him and his sister to find a way out. You agree readily, and that's where the fun begins.
----------------------------------------------------
Whew! I'm really sorry for the long prologue, I just wanted to make the basic story clear.
----------------------------------------------------
Anyway, here's a couple of rules.
Normal rules apply (we all know what this means, right?)
Maximum of two characters each.
And do have fun!

I'll use my two as an example, firstly Danielle, then Mark.

Name: Danielle
Gender: Female
Age: 19
Alternate form: Dragon.
Human appearance: This argumentative female dragonling has deep-set, slit-pupiled, jade-colored eyes. Her silky, wavy, blue hair is worn in a style that reminds you of a peacock's tail. She has a lean build. Her skin is pale. She has sharp reptilian teeth and a small but concealable set of wings covered in brown scales. Her wardrobe is tight, and is mostly green.
Alternate appearance: This dragon has a serpentine body. Its scales are the color of fine purple wine. This dragon has thick limbs with six splayed digits on each foot that end in long claws. It has frill-like wings that stretch between its forelimbs and the end of its tail. This dragon's head is wedge-shaped. It has round nostrils located on either side of its snout. This dragon has wide eyes that are the color of burnished iron. It has no visible ears.. Winglike bony plates project from the back of its head. It's lips are drawn back, making it look like it's smiling.
Personality: Her human form is welcoming and friendly, though does tend to storm off in a huff if too many people talk to her (not counting Mark) within the space of an hour. In her dragon form, the dragon's calm and natural arrogance tends to assert itself, for which she usually suffers the consequences when she morphs back.
Simple weapon: Small knife.
History: She grew up a normal child in Florida with her parents and brother, Mark, though was captured along with him on their 17th birthday. They don't know the reason why yet.
