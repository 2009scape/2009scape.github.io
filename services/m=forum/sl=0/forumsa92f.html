Clan Rules
____________________________________________________________________________
1. Obey all Runescape Rules

2. NO scamming, spamming, hacking.

3. Disrespect, vulgar behavior etc. towards fellow clan members or the clan name is unacceptable.

4. Spying on us for another clan will not be tolerated.

5. All events and event suggestions must go through a leader/administrator. If not it is to be organized through swiftkit or in game.

6. No spamming or flaming of any sort on Clan Chat.

7. If you have power in Clan chat do not abuse, your access will be lowered and not given back unless told so.

8. Follow your leader's orders.

9. Do not advertise another clan

10. Do not beg position or power. We will raise your position if I consider you trustworthy and loyal, begging doesn't help and in fact makes you less of a candidate for promotion.

11. You must be active on clan Chat and in Runescape to maintain your position.

12. And of course, have fun, that's what this clan was made for, meeting new people, having fun, and become rich.

Ranks Guide
____________________________________________________________________________
Leader (founder): Leader Ranks will only be given out to members who have proven Leadership and Determination to the clan. Leaders will lead most of the events. Leaders will be the only ones aloud to kick in the Clan Chat.

Co-Leader: Co-Leaders will be given to members that show improvement, outstanding leadership and great activity. Co-leaders will lead many events and organize them as well. if you are looking for a co-leader spot please look at the application above.

Event-Coordinator: Main person after leader to organize events. will give events suggestion and such. event- coordinators will be given too. to apply for this go to the application section above.

General: Generals are higher members that have been noticed outside the crowd and have showed a good leadership role.

Members: you will start out as a member as soon as you make an application and is accepted, hopefully, you will strive to be better and get higher ranks.
