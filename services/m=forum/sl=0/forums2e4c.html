* Anagram Clue Scrolls *
===================

~ Definition ~
Anagram Clue Scrolls will provide you with a series of letters and your job is to find the hidden word. This hidden word is a name of an NPC (Non-Player Character) that is located somewhere in RuneScape and that�s the person you must speak with to obtain your reward or get your next Clue Scroll.

~ How to Answer ~
The main objective of this type of Clue Scroll is for players to find the NPC�s name by using the letters given to them and given them that answer is no fun for them at all. What you must try to do is give hints and clues as to who this person might be. Perhaps by telling the player the profession of this NPC or what he/she wears. You could also give hints to the whereabouts of this NPC. But remember not to give the name away.

~ Example ~
� Clue Scroll: Ty Teb
� Answer: This is Betty who works in the Magic Shop located in Port Sarim.

~ Appropriate Answers ~
� You can find this person in a place with loads of Sailors and Boats, working in a shop.
� It's a person who works in a shop that Mages may find useful at a Port that's connected with Karamja.

~ Direct Answers (Not Allowed) ~
� You�re looking for Betty who can be found in Port Sarim.
� You can find this person where there are lots of Boats. Her name is Betty.

* Challenge Clue Scrolls *
===================

~ Definition ~
Challenge Scrolls are more mathematical than the other Clue Scrolls, and requires you to work out a mathematical equation or count a certain number of things at a particular location. Once the answer has been found, you must return to the person who gave you the Clue Scroll to give them your answer. Please note that if you choose the wrong answer, you can just continue answering until you get it correct. You will not loose your Clue Scroll or get a reward that is worth any less.

~ How to Answer ~
The main objective of this type of Clue Scroll is for players to find the answers to either the mathematical question given to them, or to find out how many of a certain something is at a certain area in RuneScape. So you do not want to give away the correct answer. What you want to do is find a way to help them along the way to finding the answer to their Clue Scroll. And you can do this in a couple of ways. Firstly, you can advise them to use a Calculator to work out any equation, or give them a little mathematical equation of your own. Secondly, if the Clue Scroll is asking, �How many� are there at�?� then advise them to go to that location and count. If they are still stuck after that then help by giving them a mathematical sum to work out, or something similar.

~ Examples ~
� Clue Scroll #1: How many General Stores can be found in Varrock?
� Answer: One

� Clue Scroll #2: If "A" is 10 and �B� is 5, what is 2A + 2B?
� Answer: 30 (2xA[10] = 20 and 2xB[5] = 10. 20+10=30)

~ Appropriate Answers ~
� Clue #1: ~ I suggest you go to Varrock and look around and see how many you can find.
� Clue #2: ~ Its Algebra. Think about what A and B equals and you need to work out what A is times 2, and what B is times 2, and then add that together.

~ Direct Answers (Not Allowed) ~
� Clue #1: ~ There is only one General Store in Varrock.
� Clue #2: ~ I know! It�s 30!

* Co-ordinate Clue Scrolls *
====================

~ Definition ~
Co-ordinate Clue Scrolls give you Co-ordinates, which lead to a specific location somewhere in RuneScape. The Co-ordinates may look something like below:

10 Degrees 01 Minutes North
01 Degrees 06 Minutes West

The Numbers you can see are subject to change and so are the directions. So �North� could be 'South' and 'West' could be �East�. And all Co-ordinates start at the Observatory as you can further away from it, the Degrees (60 Minutes) increase and so to the Minutes (1 or 2 walking spaces). With the help from your Sextant, Watch and Chart (Required Items) you can find this location and then dig on that spot.

~ How to Answer ~
Answering Co-ordinate Clue Scrolls can be a little more complicated than the other Clue Scrolls as you can be a little more direct. You can give the player a rough location of where they can start to search and dig. What you cannot do is tell them the exact spot or roughly the exact spot.

Encouraging Players to use their Sextant is a very good method to use as they learn how to navigate themselves. And a Sextant, indeed, is there to be used. So the more players understanding the use of a Sextant, the more people will understand Navigation. Please remember that there is another Sticky in this Forum that gives all the information you need based on Navigational Tools and Navigation. (Paragraph Credited to Slip Jay)

~ Example ~
� Clue Scroll:
10 Degrees 01 Minutes North
01 Degrees 06 Minutes West

Answer: Southeast corner of the Gnome Ball Field.

~ Appropriate Answers ~
� Have a look around the Gnome Ball Field.
� If you go to the Gnome Ball Field, use your Sextant to find the specific spot.

~ Direct Answers (Not Allowed) ~
� Dig 2 spaces East of the Tree in the Southeast corner of the Gnome Ball Field.
� Find the tree in the Southeast corner of the Gnome Ball Field and then dig right next to it.

* Emote Clue Scrolls *
================

~ Definition ~
Emote Clue Scrolls give a simple instruction, which will tell you to wear a particular costume or nothing at all, and perform an Emote at a certain location. The most difficult aspect about these Clue Scrolls is the items required.

~ How to Answer ~
These Clue Scrolls give you very little room to be able to answer without it breaking the Rules. You could say that the Clue Scroll itself is giving the player a direct answer so all you need to do is advise them on how to obtain the Items needed (If they are unsure on that) and if they are not sure where to look. Just give them the general area. You cannot give them the specific spot that they must perform the Emote on.

~ Example ~
� Clue Scroll: Cry in the centre of Varrock Bank. Equip a Rune Platebody and an Amulet of Magic.
� Answer: Self-Explanatory

~ Appropriate Answer ~
� Get the Items stated in your Clue Scroll, then you need to go to the location stated in the Clue performing the Emote stated in the Clue.

~ Direct Answer (Not Allowed) ~
� Get a Rune Platebody and an Amulet of Magic and then go to Varrock bank and cry 2 spaces west of the Plant.

* Map Clue Scrolls *
===============

~ Definition ~
Map Clue Scrolls can be quite easy and can also be quite challenging. What these give you is a Map of a small area in RuneScape and your job is to find this area, and dig on the X displayed in the Map, or if they display crates, then you must search them.


~ How to Answer ~
When answering queries to Map Clue Scrolls, never give away the exact spot or the location as it spoils the hunt for the player. In order to help them, point them in the right direction and advise them to use the World Map.

~ Example ~
� Clue Scroll: (The map is of Seers Bank and the �X� is next to the front door).
� Answer: N/A

~ Appropriate Answers ~
� Northeast of the Fishing Guild in a small village is where you need to look.
� Look in a place of high fortune in a small village where the locals have perfect vision.

~ Direct Answers (Not Allowed) ~
� Go to Seers and you need to dig 3 spaces East of the door.
� The map is of Seers Bank and you need to dig around the entrance.

* Puzzle Boxes *
============

~ Definition ~
When doing a Level 3 Treasure Trail you may often come across that you get given a Puzzle Box. These Puzzle Boxes make a certain picture and you must arrange the squares in the correct order. Once you have completed the Puzzle, return to the person who gave it to you.

~ How to Answer ~
Providing players with answers to Puzzle Boxes is probably the most complicated. There isnt much help you can give them, without your answer being direct so you have to be careful in what you are saying. Please remember that walkthroughs are not allowed on the Forums. The best thing to do would be to advise them to read the Treasure Trails section of the Knowledge Base where there are Images of the Puzzles. These Images can be used as a guide for them.

~ Example ~

� Answer: N/A

* Riddle Clue Scrolls *
================

~ Definition ~
Riddle Clue Scrolls will give you a small riddle, which you must work out in order to obtain your next Clue. Some are easier than others.

~ How to Answer ~
Be careful when answering these, as you will need to use your judgement. As some riddle Clue Scrolls are more complicated than others, obviously the easier the riddle is, the harder you�re hint should be. Never give away the answer because what fun is that? None.

~ Example ~
� Clue Scroll: In a place where the air smells of fish, speak with someone who specialises in weapons that take flight.
� Answer: The owner of the Ranger Shop in Catherby.

~ Appropriate Answer ~
� Head to the Town next to the Beach, popularly known for its Fishing Area and speak with someone that is good making sharp objects that fly through the air.

~ Direct Answer (Not Allowed) ~
� Speak with the owner of the Ranger Shop in Catherby.

* Search Clue Scrolls *
================

~ Definition ~
Search Clue Scrolls require to search in the area stated, and search for the object stated like a Chest or Crates etc. Searching the correct object will get you your next Clue Scroll or reward.

~ How to Answer ~
Be careful when answering these, as you will need to use your judgement. Depending on where the Clue Scroll takes someone, that area could be a large area or a small area. Obviously if it�s a small area, then it isnt going to be hard to find what you�re looking for, in which case, you cant really say anything other than what�s already in the Clue Scroll. If the area is quite large and requires a sufficient amount of searching, then perhaps you wish to point them in the right direction.

~ Examples ~
� Clue Scroll #1: Crate, Draynor Village.
� Answer: The crate found in the Bank at Draynor Village.

� Clue Scroll #2: Drawers in the Southeast corner of Varrock.
� Answer: The drawers in the Southeast building of Varrock.

~ Appropriate Answer ~
� Clue #1: ~ Take a look around Draynor Village, where you may find a lot of people conducting trades.
� Clue #2: ~ Just take a look around where the Clue Scroll tells you.

~ Direct Answers (Not Allowed) ~
� Clue #1: ~ Look in the Bank at Draynor Village.
� Clue #2: ~ Check the Southeast building.
