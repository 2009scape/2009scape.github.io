Welcome to the Ancient Effigies Thread.

This thread is going to be dedicated to helping players with the D&D Ancient Effigies. This thread isn't like most threads out there. It was written by 4 players with the same goal- to help other players get the help they need with this D&D, whether that be thru the assist system, or answering questions, or even compiling data for research purposes.

Feel free to post any information on effigies that you may come across and join in the assistance of fellow players in need.

All content on this thread is written and compiled by the respective owners of each part, but we all worked together as a team to write this. Our group was formed by the 1st 3 effigy assist chat creators after the update on June 15th 2010. Rather than war and fight for thread space we joined forces to provide you with the biggest and the best effigy assist chat we can.

Special thanks goes to

and the D&D community for their great efforts to work together to help other players.

Petal 7 brought this to my attention and I believe it is worth mentioning:

You can only assist up to 30k exp a day.
One assist can wipe you out for 24 hrs: a 97+.

If you first assist a lower request: 91, 93 or 95, then you can also assist the 97 and help more than 1 player. Either way you get 30k exp, but this way you can provide assistance to more than 1 person, thus cutting down the needs for assistance slightly.

What is an Ancient Effigy?
-Ancient Effigies are rare drops from almost every monster. At when first found they are thirsty for knowledge, and your job is to seek out to satisfy their craving for knowledge.
__________________________________________________________________________

How do I get an Ancient Effigy?
-Ancient Effigies ONLY drop from monsters, they cannot be obtained by any other means, as of yet. They are a very rare drop, but the higher the level of the monster, the greater the chance of the drop.
__________________________________________________________________________

How do I satisfy the Ancient Effigy?
-To satisfy your Ancient Effigy you require one of two skills presented to you by the Effigy.
Also, Jagex state:
* Starved ancient effigies require a skill level of at least 91 to investigate, and become nourished.
* Nourished ancient effigies require a skill level of at least 93 to investigate, and become sated.
* Sated ancient effigies require a skill level of at least 95 to investigate, and become gorged.
* Gorged ancient effigies require a skill level of at least 97 to investigate, and will crumble to dust, their mysterious purpose fulfilled.

Each level of investigation will require one of two skills, but these skills are always paired like so:
* Agility or Crafting
* Construction or Thieving
* Cooking or Firemaking
* Fishing or Farming
* Fletching or Woodcutting
* Herblore or Hunter
* Mining or Smithing
* Runecrafting or Summoning
And one of these skills can always be assisted.

What are the Rewards?
-For satisfying a starved Effigy: 15,000 xp is awarded.
-For satisfying a nourished Effigy: 20,000 xp is awarded.
-For satisfying a sated Effigy: 25,000 xp is awarded.
-For satisfying a gorged Effigy: 30,000 xp is awarded.

After the gorged Effigy is investigated it will crumble to dust and you will be rewarded with a dragonkin lamp.
__________________________________________________________________________

NOTE: If assisted with the skills, you will not recieve the experience for satisfying the Effigy, only the dragonkin lamp.
__________________________________________________________________________

NOTE: If ever you cannot find someone to assist you in investigation your Effigy then historian Minas will trade you an antique lamp for it, which you should avoid at all costs as there will more than likely always be someone here that can assist you, and the experience reward is significantly lower than that of the Dragonkin lamp.

Q.How rare are they?

A.It depends on the combat level of the creature. The higher the level of the creature, the greater chance they have to drop one. However, even on the toughest creatures it will still be rare to get an effigy.
__________________________________________________________________________

Q.Do all monsters drop them?

A.No, not all creatures drop them, however the vast majority of creatures do.
__________________________________________________________________________

Q.Does it show up on your Adventurer's log?

A.Yes, it will show up when you get one in your Adventurer's Log.
__________________________________________________________________________

Q.Can you get more than one of these?

A.Yes, there is no restriction on how many you can obtain. Having found (or not found) an effigy before does not affect your chance to find another one. Bear in mind, however, that they are rare drops so you shouldn't see them dropping too often.
__________________________________________________________________________

Q.Can you have more than one at the same time?

A.Yes, you can have more than one at the same time.
__________________________________________________________________________

Q.I was just wondering if you could tell me if the ring of wealth affects the chance of receiving an ancient Effigy?

A.No, it does not have an affect on the chance to get one. Sorry!
__________________________________________________________________________

Q.Can i boost my stats to meet the requirements?

A.You can boost your stats to meet the level requirement.

Q. Can I boost to the required level and then assist someone?

A. Yes,it has been proven that the assister can boost to meet the requirement, and then help others.
__________________________________________________________________________

Q. Can I assist someone if I have reached my limit of 30k exp?

A. Unfortunately no. However if you have not reached your limit, i.e you have received 29k exp, you can still assist. However, you will only get the xp up to 30k xp.

Example: An assister has 28k from the assist system. If then the assister assist's a player with a Starved Ancient Effigy, they will only receive 2k exp in the relevant skill, and not the 15k exp.
__________________________________________________________________________

Q. When can I assist someone again if I have maxed out in the assist system?

A. You will be able to assist again 24 hours from your 1st assist
