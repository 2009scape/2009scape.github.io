Hot Topic

~ Players' Gallery ~
Hot Topic!



As you may have seen in our recent newspost, we're planning on giving the gallery a bit of an overhaul in order to make it bigger and better than ever. To do that we'll need your ideas and feedback, after all, it's a product of the community!

To get the ball rolling and the discussion started, here are some of our initial ideas below. See what you think and give us your thoughts!

● Rename the Players' Gallery to the more all-encompassing title of "The Gallery". This way, we can show you not only the best in player-created offerings, but also works from guest contributors and even Jagex staff!

● In order to give each gallery a unique flavour, we'd like to start promoting monthly themes to help inspire would-be contributors. But what themes would you be interested in? Simple titles are the order of the day here - stuff like "Deadliest Enemies", "Women of RuneScape" or "Peaceful Scenes" would all make good starting points for a gallery theme. See what ones you can come up with!

● We'd like to start running some high-end competitions with quality prizes for budding artists. If you're an artist, we want to know what kind of high-value prizes you'd like to see. Perhaps there's some good art-related software out there. Maybe some quality art materials? Maybe a Graphics Tablet would suit you. Let us know!

● Along with the monthly subscription prize for each months' "featured artist", what other small prizes would you like to see? Give us some suggestions!

This Month



This month, we'd like to invite the talented designers amongst you to send in your alternative outfit designs to the Players' Gallery.
