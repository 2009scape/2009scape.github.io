-_WORKERS RULES_-

1.All Jagex rules apply.

2.You must bump thread at least twice a day.

3.You must be in clan chat onetimeskill ( will be OMEGACORP very soon).

4.You must be polite to all workers and customers.

5.You must be active.

6.You must complete all tasks given to you within the time limit given.

7.You must attend all meetings and events (as much as you can)

8.Do not ask for promotions, it will be given to you if I see you are deserving of it.



-_CUSTOMERS RULES_-

1.You must wait the full 7 days for your order. (if needed)

2.You must not be rude to workers or staff.

3.You must have the money ready before you order.

4.You must not waste or time.(if you do you will be refused instantly if you order again)
