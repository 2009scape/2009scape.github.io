Rules:

1. Follow all rules of Jagex, that infinite squid of wisdom!

2. No God Modding! (Deciding somebody elses fate for them)
Example 1: -you miss me-
correction: -I step away, so you miss me-

Example 2: -you notice his pointy ears-
There is no correction, you can't decide what somebody else sees, knows, feells etc.

3. Do not autohit! (Make offensive actions without a chance to react)

example 1: -slashes you-
correction 1: -Tries to slash you-
correction 2: -slashes at you-

There must be indication of try in your offensive actions, or then you must do an action that doesn't affect your opponent or his equipment directly, but the environment instead.

4. do not Metagame! (know things you shouldn't know)

5. do not power game! (Be insanely overpowered)

Example: -Powerful force revives me from death-
This is wrong, it is just too powerful.

6. You mustn't role play without am accepted character biography.

7. Do not be naughty.

8. Don't start fights in cc.

9. No rank begging!

10. Major kingdom changes (leader swaps, city concuering, etc.) must be taken to Elwood, and only stars may control citis/kingdoms.

11. Do not seek catnip stash.
