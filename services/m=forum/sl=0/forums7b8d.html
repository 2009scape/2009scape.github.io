===== Dungeoneering Overdose=====

Join Chat: "Dung O D"





This cc has been created for people who want to play the new skill Dungeoneering. It is here for people who wish to team up with other people in a dungeoneering cc, and with people that know what they are doing or wishing to learn how to play effectively with others in the new skill. Also hopefully for all who dungeoneer can create teams faster.

With more members joining everyday people are able to play with teammates they know, or at least comfortably know that new teammates know how to play or are willing to learn.

We are also a help clan for people who don�t know how to do Dungeoneering. Anyone can come into the cc and join our Dungeoneering family. Please apply to support us so we can grow and stay active in the runescape forum community =]


*Note* If you are not doing Dungeoneering you can still stay in the clan chat, this is also a cool place just to hang out =]

Rules===

~~ CODES OF CONDUCT ~~

By becoming a member and upon entering our chat channel you agree to the following rules and must realize that they are there for a reason. All Runescape rules apply by default. In addition:

1. All members must help keep Dungeoneering Overdose alive by staying in CC as much as they can, even when NOT dungeoneering.

2. NO asking for ranks

3. Respect everyone who enters our CC. Remember, you have to have respect towards others to be respected back. RUDENESS is unacceptable behavior.

4. All game related topic are allowed in our CC, however, Dungeoneering topics will have priority over all other discussions, specially when a question is being asked.

5. Intentionally and/or maliciously giving out false information to any CC user will be considered as a grave offense.

6. RANKs are the moderators of our CC. They will determine what is or is not appropriate in-channel for the good of the majority, and their decisions must be respected.

7. Offensive languages will not be tolerated in our CC & therefore must be avoided.

8. Fighting is not allowed in our CC. If you really have to, take them to PMs.

9. No SPAMMING.

10. No MERCHANTING.

11. No advertising and/or recruiting for other clans or CCs.

Non compliance of these CODES OF CONDUCT can result in the following consequences which includes: warnings, temporary kicks, permanent ban, revocation of membership, and offender/s may be Reported for Abuse.

````` Suggestions for additional CODES OF CONDUCTS will be highly appreciated. You can post them on this thread for consideration.
