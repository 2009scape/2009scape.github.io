Yes, Guthix is more powerful than Zamorak or Saradomin. The only god that can match his power is Zaros, apparently. Guthix is god of balance and nature, not peace. However, it is unlikely he would use a godsword. More likely he simply intimidated the other gods with his sheer power, since none of the other gods at the time had the strength to match him.

Possibly what few remnants of Zaros' followers after his departure could've forged a godsword which could give scope for a Zarosian godsword, however, knowing that humans are incapable of forging a godsword, and since many of Zaros' followers became Zamorak's followers, it is unlikely.

Seren doesn't participate actively in the God Wars, seeking only to prevent incursions into Elven territory. However, Seren did make the Crystal bow and Crystal shield, so a crystal hilt and Crystal Godsword aren't too far off in terms of concepts. That being said, Seren is rather peaceful, so she wouldn't seek to gain dominion over the other gods.
