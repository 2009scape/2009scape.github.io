Hi,

I'm sure most of you know that next week is supposed to be the launch of the Dungeoneering improvements, which include the highly anticapated release of the Resource Dungeons.

These resource dungeons include some sort of resource inside (Well duh ), whether it be a new site for a specific monster, resources for skills, etc, they may be here!

However these dungeons are going to be scattered around RS. To make things easier I was thinking we could gather a few people and station people around RS before the update is launched, and when it is they can search the area for any hidden dungeons.

The plan is to gather about 10-15 people (or as many as I can) and have you guys positioned on a location. When the update is launched, you will look around your area for any dungeons, and when found, you can post here. This information is also going to a fansite which I help edit.

So if you'd like to be a volunteer, just let me know! You need to be able to get on before the update time (which is next week, most likely Monday I guess), and know what the resource dungeon looks like (An image is available in the blog!)
