Like all clans we have rules. These are mandatory simple rules that will need to be followed by all clan members to maximize the fun in the clan

~ Respect all members in the clan. ~

~ Follow all Jagex rules. ~

~ No Multi-Clanning (This doesn't count for soul wars, penguins etc.) ~

~ Be active in cc while on Runescape. ~

~ A minimum of 3 Bumps a day please. Note: BE SURE TO ALLOW AT LEAST 10 MINUTES TO PASS BEFORE POSTING ANOTHER BUMP. (A bump is a message from you to the clan members)~

~ Be mature and don't get into conflicts with any other clan members. ~

~ Attend at least 1 event per week. ~

~ Inform Lieutenants, Captians or Leaders if you will not be able to attend event, be active each week ~

~ Most importantly.....HAVE FUN! ~
