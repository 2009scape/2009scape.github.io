Like all clans we have rules. These are mandatory simple rules that will need to be followed by all clan members to maximize the fun in the clan

~ Respect all members in the clan. ~

~ Follow all Jagex rules. ~

~ No Multi-Clanning (This doesn't count for soul wars, penguins etc.) ~

~ Be active in cc while on Runescape. ~
