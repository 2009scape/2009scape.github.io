The Best Diary Rewards is more than a year and a half old

Hello everyone! I would like to know which of our many Achievement Diaries has the best rewards.

You can define 'best' however you like. Generally, it would be the reward you use the most. But it could also be the reward that has most impacted the way in which you play the game - which is slightly different. Or, for the less experienced, it could be the reward that you want to get the most. One person suggested you go by which reward you would most hate to lose. Anything works . Just try to be fair.


The current rankings:

1. Varrock, with 158 votes

2. Lumbridge/Draynor, with 76 votes

3. Karamja, with 74 votes

4. Seers Village, with 44 votes

5. Ardougne, with 23 votes

6. Falador, with 19 votes

7. Fremennik, with 9 votes

Total Votes: 393

>> Only vote once! And make sure you specify which reward you're voting for if you discuss them all. I can't tally fractions of votes.

>> Due to the staggered release of Diaries, my poll will always be significantly biased towards those Diaries which were released earlier. It's unavoidable . If I'm still around, I would like to remake the Poll once all of the Diaries are released...someday...


To help you decide, I have listed below the rewards for all the Achievement Diaries, as listed in the Game Guide.

Please Beware: Spoilers Below!

$) Lumbridge/Draynor ($

Breakdown:

Travel
Money
Runecrafting
Free Teleport
Combat


Beginner:

The Explorer's Ring
>Replenishes run energy once per day.

'Explorer' emote

Experience Lamp - 500 exp in a Skill of your choice.

Easy:

Explorer's Ring Upgrade
>30 free Low Alchemy castings per day (requires Magic level 21).

>10% chance of crafting an extra air, earth, fire or water rune for each essence you use.

>Replenishes run energy twice per day.

Experience Lamp - 1,000 exp in a Skill of your choice.

Medium:

Explorer's Ring Upgrade
>Access to the Cabbage Dualway/Cabbageport - free teleport to the cabbage patch near Port Sarim with unlimited uses.

>Replenishes run energy three times per day.

Experience Lamp - 1,500 exp in a Skill of your choice.

$) Varrock ($


Breakdown:

Mining
Smithing
Crafting
Money
Cooking


Easy:
Varrock Smithing Armor Level 1
>Chance of smelting two bars at once when smelting ores up to (and including) steel in Edgeville.

>Chance of Mining two ores at once when Mining ores up to (and including) coal.

>Greater chance of receiving skull sceptre parts in the Stronghold of Security, and the completed Sceptre will last for 10 uses instead of 5.

>Gets you 16 battlestaves in 24 hours intervals.

Experience lamp - 1,000 experience in the skill of your choice.


Medium:

Upgraded Varrock Smithing Armor
>Chance of smelting two bars at once when smelting ores up to (and including) mithril in Edgeville.

>Chance of Mining two ores at once when Mining ores up to (and including) mithril.

>Chance of Smithing at a slightly faster rate.

>Gets you 32 battlestaves every 24 hours period.

Experience lamp - 5,000 experience in the skill of your choice


Hard:

Varrock Smithing Armor Upgrade
>Chance of smelting two bars at once when smelting ores up to (and including) adamantite in Edgeville.

>Chance of Mining two ores at once when Mining ores up to (and including) adamantite.

>Armour can be used to access the Cooks' Guild in place of chef's hat

>Gets you 64 battlestaves every 24 hour period.

Access to bank areas in the Cook's Guild.

Experience lamp - 10,000 experience in the skill of your choice.

$) Falador ($


Breakdown:

Armour
Prayer
Farming


Easy:

>Falador Shield 1
Restores 25% of your prayer daily

Grants a prayer boost of +3 when worn.

New emote can be preformed by wielding the shield and selecting 'operate'

1,000 Experience lamp to use on skill of choice for levels over 30.


Medium:

>Falador Shield 2
Restores 50% of your prayer daily

Grants a prayer boost of +5 when worn.

Increases Farming experience when using the allotment, flower and herb patches north of Port Sarim by 10%.

>5,000 experience lamp to use on skill of choice.


Hard:

>Falador Shield 3
Can restore 100% of your prayer daily and grants +7 prayer bonus

Access to new White Lily seeds from Wyson, the Falador Gardener. They provide protection for all allotment plants when grown in a flower patch.

More experience when gardening in plot north of Port Sarim.

10,000 Experience lamp

$) Fremennik Province ($


Breakdown:

Banking
Transportation
Kingdom Management


Easy:

>Fremennik Sea Boots 1
Can deposit things to your bank through Peer the Seer.

Allow you to contact the Fossegrimen from and make a sacrifice to enchant your lyre.

Fossegrimen will give you extra enchantments if you make the sacrifice in person.

5,000 experience lamp.


Medium:

>Fremennik Sea Boots 2
Allows you to jump between the piers of Miscellenia and Etceteria.

Increases your approval rating faster than normal.

Gives you one free lyre teleport a day, if you have an enchanted lyre in your inventory.

10,000 experience lamp.


Hard:

>Fremennik Sea Boots 3
Option to change the lyre teleport destination to Waterbirth Island.

Sell unnoted flatpack furniture to Advisor Ghrim, and he will automatically put the money into the coffers used to gather resources.

A skill lamp giving 15,000 experience.

$) Seers Village ($

Breakdown:

Money
Fletching
Woodcutting
Crafting
Mining
Healing Weapon


Easy:

>Seers' headband 1
Headband counts as a dim light source

Talk to Geoffery in the flax field to claim 30 free flax per day

Coal trucks will hold 140 coal

Extra log given when cutting normal trees anywhere

Experience lamp - 1,000 experience in the skill of your choice


Medium:

>Seers Headband 2
Headband light increases

Coal trucks will hold 168 coal

Bonus 10 experience when cutting maple trees anywhere

Talk to Geoffery in the flax field to claim 60 free flax per day

Experience lamp - 5,000 experience in the skill of your choice


Hard:

>Seers Headband 3
Headband light is brighter still

Coal trucks will hold 196 coal

Significant increase in speed when using the spinning wheel in Seers' Village

Lady of the Lake will offer to enhance Excalibur, boosting its stats and the effect of its special attack

Talk to Geoffery in the flax field to claim 120 free flax per day

Teleport to Camelot spell will teleport you to the centre of Seers' Village

Slight Prayer boost from Seers' Village altar

Cheaper enchant for mystic staves from Thormac

Experience lamp - 10,000 experience in the skill of your choice

$) Ardougne ($

Easy:

>Ardougne Cloak 1
Unlimited teleports to the monastery south of Ardougne

More of the drops from Tower of Life creatures are noted

Reduced chance of being caught when stealing from the stalls in Ardougne market

Receive 200 death runes when trading a cat to the citizens of West Ardougne

10% Prayer boost when entering Castle Wars

1k experience lamp


Medium:

>Ardougne Cloak 2
A daily teleport to the Farming patch north of Ardougne

Wizard Cromperty will give you 100 noted essence per day

Ability to toggle the ring of life to Ardougne

If you use the lever in Ardougne to teleport to the -Wilderness, you'll receive 10 seconds of immunity

Even more of the drops from Tower of Life creatures are noted

Reduced chance of being caught when picking pockets in Ardougne

Chance of receiving more runes from Ourania Altar

Experience lamp - 7,500 experience in the skill of your choice


Hard:

>Ardougne Cloak 3
Wizard Cromperty will give you 150 noted essence per day

Ability to toggle the Watchtower Teleport to inside Yanille

After thieving from the chests in Ardougne Castle or under the chaos druids' tower, you will be teleported to East Ardougne's northernmost bank instead of to a random location

Yet more of the drops from Tower of Life creatures are noted

Reduced chance of being caught when picking pockets or stealing from stalls anywhere in RuneScape

28k Experience lamp


Elite:

Unlimited teleports to the Farming patch north of Ardougne

4x 50k experience lamps for any skill over 85
