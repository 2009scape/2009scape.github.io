The Questing Clan of Runescape (QC) aka "Clan Quest" was organized as a group of friends attempting to get the Quest Cape in may of 2009. As we spent time in the cc together, and became closer friends, we realized that our small group of friends had the potential to become a real clan. On the 9th of November, 2009, Clan Quest was formally organized, and the first recruitment thread was opened.

As a clan that organizes all serious Questers, Our aim is to create a community of players who have completed, or strive to complete, every quests and diaries in the game.
The QC as a group serves as a resource to any member. We are here to help you solve quests, complete diaries, and provide assistance to those who have not obtained their Quest Point Cape.

Everyone of our members aspires to complete all Quests and Diaries. Many of our members are avid followers of RuneScape Lores. We are not your skillers, your pvp-ers, or your merchers. We are a more elite band of runescape players. We are the adveturers. We are the people who write the guides others read.

Our Clan does not have any interest in inter-clan conflict, holds no combat requirements, and does not specifically require in game participation of its members other than presence in the Clan Chat Channel. We are a mature group, focusing on bettering ourselves in runescape, and in the real world.

~+~OBTAINING CLAN RANKS:~+~



~OFFICERS~

Creators: Players with rank of general who assisted in clan formation.

Generals: With exception of the clan creators, the general rank shall be granted to any captain, whom, has demonstrated exemplary loyalty to the clan, and the clan interests. This Captain acted as an example to all fellow clansmen, and has maintained active participation in the clan for a period exceeding three (3) months at a minimum. Coupled with these requirements, the Captain must then obtain a two-thirds (2/3)(67%) active suggestion of all generals, coupled with a two-thirds (2/3)(67%) approval of all generals in presence of the the General Clan. At no time may there be more than 1 active general per 10 clan members.

Captain: A player may obtain the rank of Captain in a few ways: (1) hold the rank of Lieutenant for more than six [6] months, or (2) Holds the rank of Lieutenant and receives active suggestion from any four [4] captains, or any two [2] Generals.





~MEMBERS~

Lieutenant: Obtained by completing all quests or all Diaries

Sergeant: Obtained by possessing 200 or more quest points.

Corporal: Obtained by possessing 100 or more quest points.

Recruit: Members with less than 100 quest points maintain rank until 100 quest points are obtained.

~!~THE TRUTH ABOUT CLAN EVENTS~!~

The Creators of this clan researched other clan policies (even joined a couple clans) before creating this one. A primary characteristic of clans was their need to hold clan events, and punish members for not attending. The purpose of these events, to a careful observer is not to actually hold the event, but to serve as a means of attendance for the clans.

Noticing the necessity of events to maintain an accurate roster of a clan, we do not state Clan events will never occur. However, while formal events may occur they will be an incredible rarity. If a formal event does occur, it will most likely take place on the clan world (66).

Informal events will occur as suggested and organized by members. These events are not mandatory and are simply for the entertainment of all present. Any person in the cc may suggest an event, and any who attend may do so at their own discretion. (Example event idea� Spamming the GE with QP Cape Emotes >>HEHE<< )

Whenever a new quest is released, it is considered a Formal Event, but attendance in the cc is not mandatory as we realize real world �Stuff� happens. Join us when possible.

If a formal event is planned it will be posted on the thread, and mentioned in the cc for weeks in advance.
