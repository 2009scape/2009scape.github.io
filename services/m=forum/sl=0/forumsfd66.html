Clan Logos Sticky
Welcome, this sticky should be used by members of the clan community to post, request or advertise any clan related art.

Any non-clan related art belongs in the 'Bored? Draw #ere' sticky - 55-56-552-58043340.

You are still welcome to create threads for logo companies or competitions. To ensure that these threads are used for discussion we ask that the actual art is posted in this thread.


Requesting A Logo
If you would like to request a logo then we recommend that you seek out a logo creator's thread or post here using the following template:

Name of clan/thread:
Quick find code:
Basis/specification for logo:
Size/dimension limits:
Other notes:

Using the template will make it easier for logo artists to find your request. They'll be able to review your thread and find out what you want to see in the logo (basis/specification).

If you post a logo for someone then please make it as clear as possible who the logo is for. It is usually worth posting on the clan's thread to let them know that their logo is ready.


Rules
There are a couple of rules:

1) Asking for or offering in-game benefits for a logo is not allowed as it is considered to be a breach of the rules.

2) All work must be original. Any logo that appears to be a copy of another will be hidden.
