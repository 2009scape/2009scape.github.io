Hello Events community!

Well, I've been around for a few weeks now and made a few changes to try and help bring the Events Community together and help your events to be as successful as possible.

Now it's time to step this up a notch.

This thread is here for you to put your heads together and come up with some suggestions on how Jagex can support you in the Events community.

Jmod attendance is one thing, for instance. Having a Jmod at an event can be a bonus. However, it can sometimes be the case that a Jmod presence tends to overshadow what has been going on, meaning that the event becomes almost secondary.

So what else could we do for you? Assistance with forum threads (� la Events Workshop & Submissions)? Places to advertise your services to Events groups (i.e. Catering service providing food for 99 parties)?

Let me know: I can't promise that all ideas will be implemented, but they will at least be a starting point.

So, what are you waiting for?

-French-
