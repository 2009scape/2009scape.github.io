"Okay, but Summer, what is the title of the post all about??

Great Question! So, we have been talking back and forth with a bunch of players from 2009scape and we have made a decision based off of this. For awhile now plenty of people have been concerned about their items being wiped so mass sales have been going on with a few people leaving. Which is no biggie, I mean we will miss them but it is impossible to please everyone.

World 1 players

Hi friends! I am one of you! I am crazy enough to play the game as it was with out the need for any QoL that the other world brings. The legacy core had a melee system that was so garbage that you technically weren't even playing x1?? I mean you may scream at me asking what the hell am I talking about. Let me explain. Apparently, with the old combat system you were able to land a hit 3x the amount you should have been able to. So training combat was almost as if you were x3. Another set back was the whole clean economy. With our Collision of the Worlds update we gave players a chance to leave items with world 1 characters to make it what we deemed fair back then but detrimental to our economy. So a clean wipe is what we had decided was best for the pure world.

World 2 players

Hi friends! I am not one of you but that is okay. I still, however, am a member of the staff that does still care about all our players. There has been some concern rising about losing the items that had worked for and while I do understand I can not say we are on the same page and this is why I am a world 1 player. So, the idea was to remove all member items since when 2.009 was released all content will first be f2p and then member content will come in a chronological order. The amount of slayer items and just quest locked items like dragon scimitar would have made it a tad unfair to newer players. After all the complaints of the wipe we had decided to allow world 2 players to keep all of their items and let you deal with the economy created from exploits. Quest data will still be deleted but you will keep both your stats and your items on world 2.

Update on 2.009

We are currently on v0.9 of the Alpha testing, which means we are getting closer and closer to getting to where we need to be to make a release. We do have some exciting news that I will not be sharing at this time but it will for sure make the wait a lot more worth it when it is finally here. I am super happy that the majority of you have decided to stay even if that means waiting things out. Until next time we will discuss the beta!
