Musical Musings (17-Jun-2009)

On the 9th of June 2009 we launched the 'rest' feature, bringing you new ways to recharge your run energy. The update introduced a new feature to the game - the musicians. RuneScape is now populated with talented (and occasionally not-so-talented) musicians who are more than happy for you to while away some time listening to them play, while also getting your run energy back.

We would like to know if the musicians have had any impact on your day-to-day RuneScape life since they were introduced.
