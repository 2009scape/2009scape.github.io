Guaranteed Content - Christmas 2008 (07-Jul-2008)

The 'Festive Suggestions' thread has been running on the Suggestions forum for nearly two weeks now, and you've come up with sledgeloads of wintery ideas.

We have looked at all of these ideas and added them to a few of our own, to create our sixth Guaranteed Content poll.

We need you to decide what the 2008 Christmas event should be. In addition to choosing your favourite theme, activity and reward, we would also like you to select the format that you would like the event to take.

Please pick carefully, because the winning options will be made into this year's Christmas event.

View results for: All | Member | Free
