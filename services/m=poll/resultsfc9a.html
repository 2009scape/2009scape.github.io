Achievement Diaries (19-Feb-2009)

The poll orks would like to know what you think about Achievement Diaries. This poll is all about the diaries' tasks, and the difficulty levels that are associated with them. We would like you to think about what makes a task 'Beginner', 'Easy', Medium', or 'Hard'. Please note that we are allowing free players to vote in this poll, as they may have been members in the past and have their own opinions about the Achievement Diary tasks.

If you were about to begin an Achievement Diary that featured the following list of tasks, what difficulty level would you imagine each task to be?
