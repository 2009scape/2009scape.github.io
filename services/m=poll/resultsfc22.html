We're going deeper underground... (01-Jun-2010)

In the dark depths of Daemonheim, two brave adventurers have battled fearsome foes and outwitted fiendish traps to ask us questions that could shake the very fabric of reality.

Well, not quite. This week's poll is inspired by questions submitted to the poll email address from Valandil1588 and Ibun - nice one guys!

So you devious dungeon delvers, we want to know what races through your mind as you stare down Rammernaut or Astea Frostweb. Who do you explore the depths of Daemonheim with? Are you a true Leeroy Jenkins and rush ahead to face dangerous monsters, or do you prefer to plan ahead and gather resources? Or are you as yellow-bellied as the famous Heim Crab and haven't taken your first steps into Dungeoneering yet?
