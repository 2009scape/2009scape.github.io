Skill-based Minigames (05-Jun-2008)

We have already asked about your preferences for PvP combat minigames; now we want to discuss skill-based minigames. What kind of skills would you like a new minigame to be based on? What rewards should it give? And should it be more on the action-packed side?

Total Number of Votes: 74561

Question 1 - 
Do you prefer playing in combat minigames (Bounty Hunter, Duel Arena), skill-based minigames (Impetuous Impulses, Pyramid Plunder), or minigames that mix different skill and combat elements (Barbarian Assault, Burgh De Rott Ramble)?
Combat minigames 40% (29789 votes)
Minigames that mix the two 33% (24892 votes)
Skill-based minigames 22% (16349 votes)
I do not like any minigames 5% (3531 votes)

If you were playing a skill-based minigame, would you prefer to play it in a team or on your own?
