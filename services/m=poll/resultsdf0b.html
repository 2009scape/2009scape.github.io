What would you do? #2 (16-Jul-2007)

This week sees the return of the 'What would you do?' polls, in which you must think carefully about certain situations and decide what you would do when faced with them.

Keep voting to really make a difference in RuneScape.

Total Number of Votes: 100101

Question 1 - 
You approach a non-player character (NPC) that you need to talk to for a quest. Which of these most applies to you?
I skim-read the chat, stopping when anything seems interesting or relevant 48% (48059 votes)
I read everything that is said 33% (32887 votes)
I try to skip through as much chat as possible 19% (19155 votes)
Question 2 - 
Think about your favourite non-player character (NPC). What makes them so special? (Choose all options that apply)
They drop great loot, or give me great things 67% (66937 votes)
They do cool things, or have good storylines 42% (41725 votes)
They look good 30% (30077 votes)
They would be great to meet 16% (15688 votes)
It is fun to make up stories about them or pretend to be them 15% (14791 votes)

Note: This is a multiple-choice question. Each value reflects the percentage of voters who chose that option. Since players can pick multiple options, this will not add to 100%.
Question 3 - 
Your favourite non-player character (NPC) dies in a quest, never to return. How do you feel?
I don't care, it's only a game 61% (60771 votes)
I would feel sad as I had grown attached to the character 39% (39330 votes)
Question 4 - 
You have received an invitation to a street party in real-life, which states that fancy dress is optional. Would you...
Not wear a costume 42% (42507 votes)
Wear a costume 34% (34380 votes)
Not go to the party 23% (23214 votes)
