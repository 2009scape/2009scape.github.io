Postie Pete asks... (08-Oct-2007)

A few of RuneScape's web-based characters have got together and scribbled some questions on the back of Postie Pete's skull. After all, why should the in-game characters get to create all of the polls?

Postie Pete and the poll orcs would be very grateful if you could give your opinions on a number of website functions and updates, as it will help their work immensely.

Total Number of Votes: 55629

Question 1 - 
Please check all those website updates that you read or view when they come out.
Behind the Scenes newsposts (teasers of future content) 72% (40288 votes)
Players' Galleries 54% (29841 votes)
Development Diaries 46% (25467 votes)
Postbags from the Hedge 37% (20410 votes)
Lores and Histories 33% (18446 votes)
None of the above 15% (8490 votes)
