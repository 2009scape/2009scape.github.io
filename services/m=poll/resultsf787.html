What would you do #4 (18-Sep-2007)

This week sees the fourth, and last, of the 'What would you do?' polls. This poll focuses on some different activities to the previous ones, and asks you to think about your reaction to several interesting situations.

Total Number of Votes: 69642

Question 1 - 
A new update to your favourite skill has just been released. In this update, two new ways of raising that skill have been introduced. One is highly efficient, raising your stats at a quick rate. The other is slow, has a large variety of rewards that go with it, and is enjoyable. Which are you more likely to play?
The slow but enjoyable way of raising the skill 51% (35338 votes)
The highly efficient way of raising the skill 47% (32587 votes)
I would not play either update 2% (1717 votes)
Question 2 - 
You go to a fortune teller, who informs you that in a year�s time you will, through your own hard work, become the richest and most powerful person in the world. She congratulates you and then reveals a magic box. She says that if she opens it, you will skip forward to that time � but you will miss the year leading up to it. What do you do?
Tell her to leave the box closed. You want to experience the year leading up to it. 71% (49643 votes)
Tell her to open the box. You want to be rich and powerful now! 29% (19999 votes)
Question 3 - 
Imagine that you are fighting a monster to gain Slayer XP. Once dead, it drops some tanned snakeskin. Although you have no need for snakeskin, and have never made any snakeskin armour before, you have the Crafting levels to do so. Do you...?
Pick it up and bank it for later 75% (52316 votes)
Leave it 25% (17326 votes)
Question 4 - 
Which of these statements most applies to you?
I get there eventually but there is no rush. I like to experience everything, if only to complete everything that the content has to offer. 52% (36105 votes)
I read everything I can about the update so that I know as much about it as possible. If it does not offer me a faster or more efficient means of raising my stats, giving me cash or giving me more items then I will not bother with it. 37% (25764 votes)
When new content comes out, I go in feet first and get there as soon as I can. I read nothing about the update (except possibly how dangerous it is) so that I can be surprised. 11% (7773 votes)
