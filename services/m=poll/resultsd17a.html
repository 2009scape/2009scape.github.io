Guaranteed Content - Wallpapers (03-Sep-2007)

This week's poll is all about wallpapers. Firstly, we'd like you to choose which player-submitted idea (chosen from a previous Suggestions Forum thread) should be made into our next wallpaper, available on our site.

The winning idea, announced on the 10th September, will be made into a wallpaper by our professional graphic artists, and will be available on our website soon!

After the Guaranteed Content question are a few other questions, designed to find out what you think about our wallpapers in general; your answers will allow us to produce even better ones in the future!
