RuneScape Wet and Wildy! (01-Apr-2010)

We would recommend that you read the RuneScape Wet and Wildy newspost and blog before you complete this poll. RuneScape Wet and Wildy is an adventure theme park that is currently being constructed in England for RuneScape players to visit. Take a ride on the minecarts, eat at the emmental workshop and meet some of your favourite RuneScape celebrities.

As the opening date of RuneScape Wet and Wildy approaches, we hope to gather your opinions on a few details. Your help is invaluable in making the park as perfect as possible.
