The FunOrb poll! (08-Apr-2008)

FunOrb is just over a month old now, so we would like to know whether you have had a chance to give it a go.

For those of you who have not had the chance to visit, FunOrb is our new website, housing a large selection of browser-based games with new content being added every two weeks. The Poll Orks are a dab hand at Arcanists, don't you know.
