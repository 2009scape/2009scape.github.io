Social Media Awareness (26-Apr-2010)

Greetings pollsters! At Jagex, we are always looking at new ways to communicate and interact with our fantastic player community. One way we have done this recently is by using a variety of social networking sites such as Facebook, Twitter and YouTube for our games. It is great to see so many of you watching our videos and interacting via these sites, but we want to know what *you* want from our social networking sites to see if we need to do more.

View results for: All | Member | Free

Total Number of Votes: 9370
