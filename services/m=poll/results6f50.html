The 2009 RuneScape Awards! (11-Jan-2010)

As we part with 2009 and wish it all the best, it seems like a great time to reminisce and consider all the great times we have had. We said goodbye to Zanik and hello again to RuneScape Classic, while we tried to keep the Mahjarrat from killing just about everyone. But what RuneScape event of 2009 really captured your imagination? Vote for your favourite releases of the year, and see what demanded the attention of other RuneScape players.
