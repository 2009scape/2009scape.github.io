The 2008 RuneScape Awards! (18-Dec-2008)

The Poll Orks are feeling all nostalgic, and they want to share their mood with you. 2008 has been a very busy year for RuneScape, packed full of updates, from quests, to minigames, to graphical improvements.

We would like you to vote for the updates that you enjoyed the most, the updates that made a real difference to your gaming experience.

The most popular options will give us a very good idea of the sort of content you really like, so that we can make sure that 2009 is even better!
