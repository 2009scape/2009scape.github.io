Tough Calls (06-Jul-2010)

This week's poll is a "Player-Inspired Poll" courtesy of Mr Computer2. They wanted to know what the good and great players of RuneScape would most likely do if faced with impossibly tough in-game decisions. We liked this idea so much, we've decided to use their question to kick us off. Be warned, though, all the others come from our own fiendish minds. Enter if you dare!

View results for: All | Member | Free

Total Number of Votes: 10911

Question 1 - 
Your local Bank of RuneScape is closing. You're faced with moving the money yourself or paying an extortionate moving fee. Would you rather:
Pay the moving fee of 25% of your Gold Coins immediately, with no other penalties 78% (8464 votes)
Trek through a PvP world for 5 hours carrying all of your Gold Coins around with no armour or weapons 22% (2447 votes)
Question 2 - 
An evil Makeover Mage appears and casts a tranformative spell on you. You've only got time to counter one of the effects before you change forever. Would you rather:
Keep your name - otherwise, how will I know if people are talking to me?! 71% (7794 votes)
Keep your appearance - I like my hair, clothes and build just the way it is! 29% (3117 votes)
Question 3 - 
Thurgo offers you a new experimental weapon. You excitedly accept, but later realise it only has two settings. Would you rather the weapon:
Instantly halve your opponent's Lifepoints, but at a cost of half your XP earned that day 67% (7272 votes)
Instantly kill your opponent and yourself. And your familiar. And your butler 33% (3639 votes)
Question 4 - 
The XP Genie is having a super bad day and decides to mess up your stats, offering you only one of two outcomes. Would you rather he:
Remove all of your XP from your highest skill and put it onto your lowest 66% (7222 votes)
Fix your XP gain so that you only get half the XP you're supposed to every time you train 34% (3689 votes)
Question 5 - 
You're in the Kharidian desert and you have no water. It's getting critical! Would you rather survive by:
Squeezing out the rancid juice of a recently vanquished Kalphite Worker 69% (7557 votes)
Wringing out the sweat of a Bedabin nomad's robes 31% (3354 votes)
